Space and Culture is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal that covers the field of architecture. The editors-in-chief are Joost van Loon (Catholic University of Eichstätt-Ingolstadt), Justine Lloyd (University of Technology), Michael Schillmeier (Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München), and Rob Shields (University of Alberta). It was established in 1997 by Rob Shields (Lancaster University), who also published the journal. Since 2001, it has been published by Sage Publications. Space and Culture is abstracted and indexed in International Bibliography of the Social Sciences, Scopus, Sociological Abstracts, and ZETOC.
