Songshuhui.net (simplified Chinese: 科学松鼠会; traditional Chinese: 科學松鼠會) is a Chinese collaborative blog focusing on popular science for the general public.  By February 2009 the site had served more than 2 million page impressions.  The articles in Songshuhui.net are published under Creative Commons-BY-NC-ND.  The site was founded on April 28, 2008.
