Meccanoid is a highly intelligent programmable robot.  It was created by Meccano, the model construction system created in Liverpool, United Kingdom, by Frank Hornby and now based in Calais, France. The Meccanoid was described by Popular Science as "The smartest DIY bot to ever spill out of a box"  and the G15 robot featured in Time magazine's 2015 article: "These Are the Gadgets Everybody Wants This Holiday".
