Anthony J. Steinbock is an American philosopher and Professor of Philosophy at Southern Illinois University Carbondale. He is the Director of the Phenomenology Research Center and Editor-in-Chief of Continental Philosophy Review.  Steinbock is known for his research on phenomenology.    • Translation of Edmund Husserl, Analyses Concerning Passive and Active Syntheses: Lectures on Transcendental Logic, Husserliana Collected Works, IX (Dordrecht: Kluwer Academic Publishers, 2001), pp. 659 + lx “Translator’s Introduction.”
