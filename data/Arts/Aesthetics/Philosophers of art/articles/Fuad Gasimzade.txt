Fuad Gasimzade  is an Azerbaijani philosopher and academician. His main area of research area was Azerbaijani philosophic public opinion, social philosophy, ontology, epistemology and aesthetics.  His doctoral thesis was titled "Fizuli’s outlook”. His monograph "Caravan of sorrow" or "Light in the darkness" was printed in 1968. He wrote more than 20 scientific articles about Fizuli.  Fuad Gasimzade wrote the book titled “Sport, beauty and aesthetics”. It was printed in 1970.
