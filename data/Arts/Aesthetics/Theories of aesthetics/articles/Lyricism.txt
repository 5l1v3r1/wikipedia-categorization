Lyricism is a quality that expresses deep feelings or emotions in an inspired work of art.  Lyricism is when art is expressed in a beautiful or imaginative way, or when it has an expressive quality.  Although the term lyricism is often used in conjunction with art composed of sound alone, it can also apply to all forms of art including performance,  architecture,  or film.
