Museum Mile is a collection of museums in London, England, in the area between King's Cross on Euston Road to the north and the Embankment on the River Thames to the south. The area is located in the London Borough of Camden.    The route includes Woburn Place, Russell Square, Southampton Row, Kingsway, and the Aldwych.
