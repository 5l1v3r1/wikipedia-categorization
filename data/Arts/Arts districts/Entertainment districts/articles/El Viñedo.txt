El Viñedo is a neighborhood in northern Valencia, Venezuela. It is home to many of the city's best restaurants and nightclubs; this area is known as "La calle de los cafés" (Café Street). El Viñedo is home to many of Valencia's best hotels: the new Embassy Suites hotel is in El Viñedo and is widely regarded as one of Valencia's best; additionally the Ucaima Hotel is located in El Viñedo. Valencia's Avenida Bolivar borders El Viñedo and once the situation with the city's metro[further explanation needed] is solved the area will become easier to access from other zones of Valencia.
