The 20/21 British Art Fair is a London-based art fair presenting modern, post War and contemporary British art.  A number of galleries exhibit at the fair each year.   The fair has been held at the Royal College of Art in South Kensington, west London, in September each year.  In 2012, the fair celebrated its 25th anniversary.  Due to a loss of availability of the venue, it was cancelled in 2016, but in June 2017 it was held during at the Mall Galleries in St James's, central London.  In 2018, the exhibit moved to Saatchi Gallery at the Duke of York's HQ, which is intended to become the exhibit's new permanent home.
