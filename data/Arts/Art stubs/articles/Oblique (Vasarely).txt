Oblique (in Hungarian: Ferde) is a collage by Hungarian artist Victor Vasarely from 1966 to 1974. Its dimensions are 78 x 77 centimeters.    The picture is part of the collection of the Museum of Fine Arts in Budapest, Hungary.  The Op art work is a grid of squares arranged with strong diagonals.
