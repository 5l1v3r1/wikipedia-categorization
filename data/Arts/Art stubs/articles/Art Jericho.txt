Art Jericho is a contemporary art gallery in Jericho, northwest central Oxford, England.    The gallery is managed by Jenny Blyth Fine Art.  It participates in the annual Oxfordshire Artweeks art festival each May  and holds regular temporary exhibitions for artists.   Concerts are sometimes held at the gallery.
