Charity of St Thomas of Villanova is an etching by Melchiorre Caffà from the 1660s. The print's dimensions are 60 x 40 centimeters. It is in the collection of the National Museum of Fine Arts, Malta, in Valletta . It is an illustration of the sculpture in the church of Sant'Agostino in Rome.
