Georges Lemmen (1865–1916) was a neo-impressionist painter from Belgium.  He was a member of Les XX from 1888.  His works include The Beach at Heist, Aline Marechal and Vase of Flowers. Yvonne Serruys studied in his workshop in Brussels from 1892 to 1894.
