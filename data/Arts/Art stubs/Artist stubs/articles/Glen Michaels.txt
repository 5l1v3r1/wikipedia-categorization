Glen Michaels is a sculptor from Troy, Michigan.  He was born in Spokane, Washington in 1927.  He has had works displayed in the Detroit People Mover Bricktown Station, the NASA Goddard Space Flight Center, and the Ford Motor Company 1964 New York World's Fair exhibit (now installed in the Henry Ford Centennial Library).    He attended Yale, where he contributed to campus humor magazine The Yale Record.  He received a Master of Fine Arts from the Cranbrook Academy of Art.
