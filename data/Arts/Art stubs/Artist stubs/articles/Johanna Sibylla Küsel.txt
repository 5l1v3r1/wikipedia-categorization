Johanna Sibylla Kraus or Johanna Sibylla Küsel (1650 – 1717) was a printmaker from Augsburg. Kraus was born in Augsburg as the daughter of Melchior Küsel. She was taught by her father and assisted his pupil Johann Ulrich Kraus, whom she married and continued to work with.
