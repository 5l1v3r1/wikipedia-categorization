Herbert Page Barringer (1886 – 1946) was an Australian watercolour artist. Barringer was born in North Adelaide on 22 November 1886, the child of Leonard Barringer and his wife Fanny (née Page). His older sister, Ethel Barringer, was another member of this talented artistic family. He married Gwendoline L'Avance Adamson, also a watercolourist, in North Adelaide on 18 Nov 1910. Barringer painted Australian landscapes and was active in the 1920s-1930s. He died in South Australia on 12 August 1946 and a memorial exhibition of his work was held in Adelaide later that year.
