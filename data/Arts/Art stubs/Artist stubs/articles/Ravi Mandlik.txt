  Ravi Mandlik रवि मंडलिक (born 1960, Achalpur, Maharashtra, India) is an Indian artist. He completed his G.D. Art in painting from Sir J. J. School of Art, Mumbai. He was a member of the teaching faculty of L.S. Raheja School of Art, Mumbai and Sir J. J. School of Art, Mumbai, Maharashtra , India.
