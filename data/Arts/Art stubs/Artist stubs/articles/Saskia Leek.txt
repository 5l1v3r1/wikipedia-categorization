 Saskia Leek (born 1970 in Christchurch) is a New Zealand painter. In 2005, Leek spent 12 weeks in New Plymouth as the Taranaki Artist in Residence.  Leek graduated from the Canterbury School of Fine Arts,  and in 2009, was nominated for the Auckland Art Gallery's Walters Prize  for her series Yellow is the Putty of the World.
