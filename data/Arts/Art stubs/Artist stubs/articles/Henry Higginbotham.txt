Henry Higginbotham is an artist whose work has appeared in role-playing games. Henry G. Higginbotham has produced interior and cover illustrations for role-playing game books since 1993. He began his career with White Wolf, on their numerous products for their games Wraith: The Oblivion and Changeling: The Dreaming.
