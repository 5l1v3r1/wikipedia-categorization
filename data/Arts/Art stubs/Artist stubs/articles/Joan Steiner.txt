Joan C. Steiner (October 10, 1943 – September 8, 2010) was an American illustrator and puzzle designer. A graduate of Barnard College,  she was the recipient of numerous art and design awards, including a Society of Illustrators Award and a National Endowment for the Arts fellowship. Her first book, Super Look-Alikes, received glowing reviews and was featured on national television in the USA. Her second book, Amazing Look-Alikes, which was published by Walker Books, has over 700 everyday objects in disguise, ranging from paper clips to refrigerator magnets.  Steiner died of cancer on September 8, 2010 in her home in Claverack, New York, at the age of 66.
