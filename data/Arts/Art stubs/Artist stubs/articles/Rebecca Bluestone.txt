Rebecca Bluestone is an American artist and studio trained tapestry weaver currently residing in Santa Fe, New Mexico.  She studied with Nancy Lubin of Western Maine Weavers and contemporary Hopi weaver Ramona Sakiestewa.  Her work can be found in several museum collections including the Art Institute of Chicago,  the Denver Art Museum, the Albuquerque Museum of Art and History, the New Mexico Museum of Art,   and Museum of Arts & Design  in New York.
