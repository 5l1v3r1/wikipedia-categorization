Kait Rhoads is an American glass artist whose works have been featured at numerous notable exhibitions in the United States and internationally. She has an Atrium Baccalalureate from Rollins College 1989, BFA in Glass from Rhode Island School of Design 1993 and a MFA in Sculpture from Alfred University 2001.
