Ann Phong is a Vietnamese American artist, known for her paintings depicting her experience as a Vietnamese-American Woman    Phong first developed her artist abilities in Saigon, Vietnam. After fleeing the country she ended up in Southern California where she studied at Cal Poly Pomona for her undergraduates and then at Cal State Fullerton for her masters.  As of current, she is a professor who teaches art classes at Cal Poly Pomona.
