Myriam Ben Salah (born 1985) is a Tunisian writer and curator based in Paris.   She became the Editor-in-Chief of Kaleidoscope  Magazine in 2016.  Before that role she worked at the Palais de Tokyo from 2009.   In 2017, Ben Salah curated I Heard You Laughing at the Gregor Staiger Gallery.  Ben Salah was the curator of the 10th edition of the Abraaj Group Art Prize.   Ben Salah curated a collection called The Pain Of Others at the Ghebaly Gallery in Los Angelos in 2018.  Salah also edits F.A.Q. (magazine) .
