 Viliana Valentinova Georgieva (Bulgarian: Вилияна Валентинова Георгиева) (born 20 September 1982), better known as Viliana, was a Bulgarian TV show host, model and a cabin crew. She modeled for designers and was featured on a dozen magazine covers. Viliana runs her advertising business. She was one of the faces of Manhattan Cosmetics and ItalGold. She was featured in a Florina advertising campaign. After her magazine cover appearance, Viliana had been a host of an episode of the TV show 5 minutes in Hollywood.
