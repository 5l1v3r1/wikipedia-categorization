Heidi Kristina Blomstedt (née  Sibelius; Järvenpää, 20 June 1911 – Helsinki, 3 January 1982) was a Finnish designer.  She graduated in ceramics at the Aalto University School of Arts, Design and Architecture in 1932, and worked as a freelance ceramist and for the Arabia company. She married the architect Aulis Blomstedt, and they had four children: Severi Blomstedt (1946–),  Petri Blomstedt (1941-1997), Anssi Blomstedt (1945–) and Juhana Blomstedt (1937-2010).  Her father was the famous composer Jean Sibelius.
