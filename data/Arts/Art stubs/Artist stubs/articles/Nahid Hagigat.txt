Nahid Hagigat or Nahid Haghighat (1943) is an Iranian-American illustrator and artist, located in New York City. She is well known for her paintings and prints with layered imagery.  Born in 1943 in Iran. In the early 1970s she studied Fine Arts at New York University (NYU) and met her husband, artist Nicky Nodjoumi while in school.   She has a Ph.D in Art Education from New York University (NYU) and a Ph.D from Huntington Pacific University in Behavioral Therapy.  Her work is part of the permanent collection at the Metropolitan Museum of Art.
