Kyle Lambert (born July 18) is a British visual artist best known for his photorealistic portraits on Apple's iPad.  He received widespread attention for his time-lapse video of his 2013 painting of Morgan Freeman,  as well as his work creating the original poster art for Netflix's Stranger Things.  Lambert studied Fine Art Painting and Illustration with Animation at Manchester Metropolitan University. Lambert has created the artwork for stranger things, the artwork for WickerMan (SW8) Alton Towers Resort, and the cover of Muse's 2018 album "Simulation theory"
