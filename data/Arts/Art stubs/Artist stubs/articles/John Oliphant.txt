  John Oliphant (d.1905) was a Scottish portrait painter, working in the 19th century. In the 1830s he is listed as operating from a studio at 14 Waterloo Place, at the east end of Princes Street in Edinburgh.  His most productive period seems to have been 1830 to 1860. Oliphant was brother to the stained glass artist Francis Wilson Oliphant. He was first cousin of and brother-in-law to Margaret Oliphant, the author. He died in 1905, probably in Edinburgh.
