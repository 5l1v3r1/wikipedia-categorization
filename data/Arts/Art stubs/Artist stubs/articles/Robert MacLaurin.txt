Robert MacLaurin (born 1961) is a Scottish artist. Robert MacLaurin was born in Scotland in 1961.  He studied art at Edinburgh College of Art, graduating with distinction.  MacLaurin was part of a movement focused around the 369 Gallery in Edinburgh, alongside Fionna Carlisle, June Redfern, Ian Hughes, Caroline McNairn and Gwen Hardie.
