Enas Taleb (Arabic: إيناس طالب‎; February 21, 1980 – ), is an Iraqi   actress.     Enas Taleb was born in Basra  and lived there for five years. During the "Iran-Iraq war",  she moved to live with her family in Baghdad. She wanted to finish her high school at the Institute of Arts to study music and play the violin, but she did not have the opportunity to accept this section.  She had no choice but to accept things. After she accepted to study in the Department of Dramatic Arts,  she received no objection from her family to study but they had no reservations about working on television anymore. She studied at the Institute of Fine Arts.
