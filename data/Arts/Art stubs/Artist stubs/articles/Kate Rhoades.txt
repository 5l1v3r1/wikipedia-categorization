Kate Rhoades is a contemporary artist in the San Francisco Bay Area. She received a Bachelors of Fine Arts at the Colombus College of Art and Design and a  Masters of Fine Arts from Mills College.  She works in video, sculpture, and comics, often exploring "the absurdity and contradictions of the art world with a wry, comedic angle".   She is also the co-host and co-creator of Congratulations Pine Tree, a Bay Area arts podcast.
