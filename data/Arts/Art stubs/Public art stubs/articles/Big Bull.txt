The Big Bull was a large (14 metres x 21 metres) Holstein bull concrete model located near Wauchope, New South Wales, Australia. When open, it contained a gift shop on the ground floor and a beef display. It was located just off the Oxley Highway between Wauchope and the Pacific Highway.
