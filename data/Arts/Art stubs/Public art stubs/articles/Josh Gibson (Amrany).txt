Josh Gibson is a public artwork by sculptor Omri Amrany, located at the Nationals Park in Washington, D.C., United States.  The statue depicts Josh Gibson batting. Omri Amrany is an Israeli-American best known as a sculptor. The sculpture was commissioned by The D.C. Commission for The Arts & Humanities, and was unveiled at Nationals Park, on April 8, 2009, by the Gibson Family.
