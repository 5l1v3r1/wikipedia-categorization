The Uecker Monument is a public art work by artist Brian Maughan.  It is located in front of the Miller Park stadium west of downtown Milwaukee, Wisconsin. The sculpture depicts Bob Uecker, the popular play-by-play announcer for broadcasts of Milwaukee Brewers baseball games.  It was dedicated on August 31, 2012.
