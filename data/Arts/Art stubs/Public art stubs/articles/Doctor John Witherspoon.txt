National Park Service Doctor John Witherspoon is a bronze sculpture by William Couper  of John Witherspoon, Presbyterian minister and a signatory of the United States Declaration of Independence. It was dedicated May 20, 1909, by the National Presbyterian Church,  and relocated in 1966. It is located at Connecticut Avenue and N Street, N.W. Washington, D.C.  As part of American Revolution Statuary in Washington, D.C., the statue is listed on the National Register of Historic Places.
