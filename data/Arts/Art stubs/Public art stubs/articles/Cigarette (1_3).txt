Cigarette is a public artwork by United States artist Tony Smith, located on the grounds of the Albright Knox Art Gallery in Buffalo, NY.  Cigarette is a minimalist piece of environmental sculpture created by  in 1961. The sculpture is over 15 feet tall and made of flat planes of steel in a twisted form.   This is the first in an edition of three (with one artists proof); no. 2 is at MOMA in New York. A small scale version of the piece is on display at the St. Louis Art Museum.
