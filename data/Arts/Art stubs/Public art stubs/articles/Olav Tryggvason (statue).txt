Coordinates: 63°25′50″N 10°23′42″E﻿ / ﻿63.43056°N 10.39500°E﻿ / 63.43056; 10.39500 A statue of Olav Tryggvason  is located in Trondheim, Norway. The statue by sculptor Wilhelm Rasmussen honors King Olav Tryggvason who was the city's 's founder.    The 18-metre (58-foot) high statue is  mounted on top of an obelisk. It stands at the center of the city square (Torvet i Trondheim)  at the intersection of the two main streets, Munkegata and Kongens gate.  The statue was unveiled in 1921. Around the statue base is a cobblestone mosaic, dating from 1930, which forms a gigantic sun dial . The sun dial is calibrated to UTC+1 so that the reading is inaccurate by one hour in the summer.
