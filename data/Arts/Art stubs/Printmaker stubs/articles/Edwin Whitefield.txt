Born in East Lulworth, near Wareham, Dorset, England,  he emigrated to the United States in 1838. Whitefield Township in Kandiyohi County, Minnesota is named for the artist while nearby Lake Lillian was named for Whitefield's wife.  He died in Dedham, Massachusetts.
