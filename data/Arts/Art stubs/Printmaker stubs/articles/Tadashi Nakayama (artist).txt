Tadashi Nakayama (中山 正, Nakayama Tadashi, born 1927 Niigata, Niigata, died 2014) was a Japanese woodblock print artist, working in a style that combines influences from traditional Japanese ukiyo-e prints and Western painting.  From 1962 to 1965 he lived in Italy and then England. It has been claimed that "Tadashi Nakayama is one of Japan's leading woodblock print artists."
