Sevres Outdoors is an international art project: an exhibition of outdoor artworks, in the historical gardens of Sèvres - Cité de la céramique and in the heart of the Paris region. Organized by CERA, the first edition of Sevres Outdoors was held from June 30 to September 31 in 2014 with 30 artworks from 25 Parisian galleries. The second edition gathers 26 artists from 23 galleries, and opens to public from May 20 to October 25 in 2015.
