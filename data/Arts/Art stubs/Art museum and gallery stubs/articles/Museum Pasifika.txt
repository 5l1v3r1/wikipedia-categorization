Museum Pasifika (Nusa Dua Bali) is an art museum in Bali, Indonesia.  It presents the Asian Pacific’s variety of cultural artefacts. The museum was founded in 2006 by Moetaryanto P and Philippe Augier.  The museum's collection includes more than 600 artworks by 200 artists from 25 countries.
