Ars Aevi is a museum of contemporary art in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina.   It was established during the Siege of Sarajevo as a cultural resistance movement to the Bosnian war. It holds a collection of 1,600 pieces, including approximately 130 works by renowned world artists such as Pablo Picasso, Michelangelo Pistoletto, Jannis Kounellis, Joseph Beuys, Marina AbramoviÄ‡ and Joseph Kosuth.
