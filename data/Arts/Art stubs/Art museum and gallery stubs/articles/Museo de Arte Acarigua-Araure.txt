The Museo de Arte Acarigua-Araure (MAAA) is an internationally recognized not-for-profit art institution in Acarigua, Venezuela. MAAA is the only museum of its kind in the region and is dedicated to the exhibition of local and international contemporary art. The museum was founded in 1987 by Ali Cordero Casal as a forum to promote contemporary art, culture and education through exhibitions and public programs that engage the local and international community. Since its inception, MAAA has organized over 40 exhibitions of both local and international artists.
