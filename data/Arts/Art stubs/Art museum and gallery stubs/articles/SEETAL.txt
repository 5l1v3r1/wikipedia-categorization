SEETAL is a Swiss invitational art show and competition organised by Kunst Forum International. Kunst Forum International is an art club for promoting and establishing co-operation and exhibition interchanges with other organisations, both in Switzerland and abroad. SEETAL is held bi-annually in Meisterschwanden, a village in the Seetal valley and the canton of Aargau.
