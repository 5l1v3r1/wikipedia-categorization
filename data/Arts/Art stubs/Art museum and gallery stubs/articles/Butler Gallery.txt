Butler Gallery is an art gallery in Kilkenny, Ireland, with a collection of works by important Irish and international artists from the present day back to the 18th century. Artists represented include Nathaniel Hone, Louis le Brocquy, Jack Butler Yeats, Barrie Cooke, Tony O'Malley, Mainie Jellett, and Stephen Lawlor. Solo exhibitions have been held by numerous prominent artists, including Nick Miller (artist) and Fergus Martin. Located in Kilkenny Castle, the gallery is named in honor of Peggy and Hubert Butler.
