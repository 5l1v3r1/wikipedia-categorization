Pics.io (pronounced "pixy-o") is a digital asset management app developed by TopTechPhoto. It aims to improve work with images/videos/graphics in Google Drive through a centralised library with advanced search options such as keywords and custom metadata fields. For example it can be used to demosaic a DNG image in a web browser.  TopTechPhoto is founded by Yevgeniy Shpika, Konstantin Shtondenko and Vlad Tsepelev who have backgrounds in medical imaging and software development.
