The Pentax Optio series is a line of consumer digital cameras manufactured by Pentax Corporation.  It consists mostly of point-and-shoot cameras, and encompasses the bulk of Pentax's lower-end camera models.  These products typically range in cost from US$200-US$500, with specific "W" models targeted for underwater and outdoor use. Note: current models are highlighted in blue.
