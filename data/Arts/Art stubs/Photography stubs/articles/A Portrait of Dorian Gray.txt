A Portrait of Dorian Gray (2005) is the fashion designer and photographer Karl Lagerfeld's rendition of Oscar Wilde's novel The Picture of Dorian Gray (1891) in photography. Models Larry Scott and Eva Herzigová star as Mr. and Mrs. Dorian Gray. The book shows photographic interpretations of parts of the novel's plot, and it also contain digitally aged portraits of both Scott and Herzigova, done by Pierre-François Letue.
