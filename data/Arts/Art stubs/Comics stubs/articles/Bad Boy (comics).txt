Bad Boy is a one-shot comic book by Frank Miller and Simon Bisley. It was  published by American company Oni Press in 1999, as a 44-page prestige format comic book. It was republished in July 2008 in hardcover format by Dynamite Entertainment with two covers, one by Bisley  and the other by Miller. Bad Boy follows the story of Jason, a young boy who comes to realize that the couple he thinks are his parents are not his parents at all, and his world is not what it seems to be.
