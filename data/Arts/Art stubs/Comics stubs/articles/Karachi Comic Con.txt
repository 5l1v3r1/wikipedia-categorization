Karachi Film & Comic Con (KFCC) is an annual comic con event held in Karachi, Sindh, Pakistan. It is arranged by the organization called Karachi Film & Comic Con.   The event is held every year and attended by fans and artists of popular culture.  The event also includes fan cosplays and the sale of popular culture such as comic books and other related merchandise.  The event also gives fans a chance to display their art, including drawings and costumes.  It also gives fans a chance to participate in stage dramas and video game competitions.
