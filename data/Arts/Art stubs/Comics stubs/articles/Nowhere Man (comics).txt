Nowhere Man is a comic limited series from Virgin Comics created    The series will be an odyssey of futurist science fiction, in an epoch where man has traded privacy for safety.  It will be written by Hugh Jackman and Marc Guggenheim, who wrote stories for Wolf and The Amazing Spider-Man and created Eli Stone, with art from Paul Gulacy. The Australian actor Hugh Jackman affirmed in a bulletin that he hopes that Nowhere Man should reach sufficient popularity as to turn into a movie. Hugh Jackman and Marc Guggenheim: Story and Script Paul Gulacy: Artist Nowhere Man is solicited as launching October 2008.  So far, Virgin Comics has hyped the series with a contest called Be a Real Nowhere Man, where fans can win the prize of having their likeness drawn into upcoming issues of Nowhere Man.
