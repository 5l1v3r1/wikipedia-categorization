Carousel is a long-running multimedia comics slide show hosted by cartoonist Robert Sikoryak (Masterpiece Comics) that has been presented in various venues in the United States and Canada since 1997.   Sikoryak has described Carousel as "a cartoon variety show."  Carousel has been presented at Dixon Place, MoCCA Fest, Parsons School of Design, The Brick Theater, and other venues. The first Carousel slide shows were projected using a slide projector. The show was named after the Kodak Carousel slide projector.
