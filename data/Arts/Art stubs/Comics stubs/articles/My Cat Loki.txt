My Cat Loki is an OEL manga by Bettina M. Kurkoski. It is published by Tokyopop. The first of two volumes were published on July 11, 2006. Although the series was intended to continue, no third volume was published.  After the death of his cat Luka, who he treated like a brother, the artist Ameya goes into withdrawal. His agent Miss Chacha, wants to bring him back into the art world. Ameya finds a cat named Loki, who replaces Luka. Ameya and Loki's bond causes Ameya's sanity to come under question.
