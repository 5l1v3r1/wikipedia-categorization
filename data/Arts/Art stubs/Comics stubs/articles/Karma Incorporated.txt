Karma Incorporated is a three issue comic book limited series about a team of misfits who are paid to cause trouble with the enemies of their employers. The series is written by David Hopkins, illustrated by Tom Kurzanski, colored by Marlena Hall, and published by Viper Comics. The first issue debuted at the San Diego Comic Con in July 2005, and was released on a monthly basis thereafter. In Dallas, Karma Incorporated had a notable release party on July 26, 2005 at the Gypsy Tea Room in Deep Ellum, promoted by Zeus Comics, with the band Happy Bullets performing.
