iCAM is a color appearance model developed by Mark D. Fairchild and Garrett M. Johnson that was published in 2002 at the IS&T/SID 10th Color Imaging Conference in Scottsdale.  It has been recognized that there are significant aspects of color appearance phenomena that are not described well, if at all, by models such as CIECAM97s or CIECAM02.
