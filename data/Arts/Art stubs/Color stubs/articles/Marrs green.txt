Marrs green is a shade of green that in 2017 won 'The World's Favourite Colour', a major global survey by British paper maker G . F Smith.     It is a rich teal hue.  The color was submitted by Annie Marrs, a UNESCO worker from Dundee, who was inspired by the River Tay.  The survey received 30,000 submissions from over 100 countries via online polling since it launched in January 2017.
