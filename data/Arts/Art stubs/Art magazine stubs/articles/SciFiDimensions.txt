SciFiDimensions was an online science fiction magazine published monthly between February 2000 and February 2010, when it went on hiatus.   It was edited and published by John C. Snider, a long-time genre fan who lives in Roswell, Georgia.  SciFiDimensions included interviews, articles and reviews covering books, movies, television shows and comic books.  Beginning in February 2008, the site launched a companion podcast at https://web.archive.org/web/20080501223026/http://www.scifidimensions.com/podcast/ SciFiDimensions received an honorable mention for Best Website in the 2002 Hugo Awards.
