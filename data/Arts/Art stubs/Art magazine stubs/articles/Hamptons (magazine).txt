Hamptons is a magazine founded in 1978 and published thirteen times throughout the year focused on real estate, interior design, fashion, art, culture, dining, entertainment, fitness, and philanthropy. The magazine is published by Niche Media,   which was founded in 1998 by Jason Binn,  and has an approximate circulation of 40,000 – 50,000 copies, distributed all over The Hamptons.
