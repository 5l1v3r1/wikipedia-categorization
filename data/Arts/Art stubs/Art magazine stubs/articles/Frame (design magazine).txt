FRAME magazine (capitalized by its creators; the E in FRAME often appears mirror-reversed on the magazine's cover) is a magazine devoted to interior design, architecture, product design and  exhibition design based in Amsterdam, Netherlands. The magazine was first published in 1997  by Frame Publishers and have about 6 issues a year. Robert Thiemann is the founder and editor-in-chief of the magazine.  Frame magazine is one of the leading interior design publications.
