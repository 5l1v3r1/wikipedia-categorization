NY Arts or NY Arts Magazine, is both a web and a hard copy print publication founded by Abraham Lubelski in 1995.  Though its focus is New York, its mission is to promote the understanding and appreciation of contemporary art worldwide. It provides opportunity to unknown artists, writers and editors. Over 100 print editions have been published since its founding. Its information is updated daily. Contributors have included Arthur Danto, Robert C. Morgan, Achille Bonito Oliva, Valery Oisteanu, Piero Golia, Enrico Pedrini, Lee Klein, Stefano Pasquini, Jamie Dalglish and Dennis Bellone. NY arts is known to demand payment from an article from artists that it covers.
