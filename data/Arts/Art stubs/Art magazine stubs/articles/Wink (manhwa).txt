Wink (윙크) is a popular South Korean manhwa magazine published by Seoul Media Group (Hangul: Seoul Munhwasa, 서울문화사). The first issue was released on 1 August 1993.  It publishes Bride of the Water God, Goong  and Hissing. In July 2012, the magazine moved to digital format and the ongoing series in the magazine are available monthly through online subscription.
