Daruma Magazine was a quarterly English language magazine published in Amagasaki, Japan and devoted to Japanese art and antiques.  It was owned by Takeguchi Momoko and commenced publication in 1993.  In addition to the major articles, each issue contained brief articles on haiku, woodblock prints, and ikebana.  The magazine ceased publication in 2011, with Issue 70 (Vol. 18, No. 2) being its last.
