George Washington is an outdoor bronze sculpture depicting George Washington by Avard Fairbanks, located on the George Washington University campus in Washington, D.C. Copyrighted in 1975 and dedicated on February 16, 1993, the sculpture measures approximately 46 x 32 x 27 inches, with a granite base measuring approximately 68 x 42 1/2 x 33 inches.
