Eleftherios Venizelos is an outdoor sculpture of Eleftherios Venizelos, installed outside the Embassy of Greece, on Massachusetts Avenue between Sheridan Circle and 22nd Street, NW,  in Embassy Row, Washington, D.C., United States. It was erected by the Hellenic Parliament in 2009.
