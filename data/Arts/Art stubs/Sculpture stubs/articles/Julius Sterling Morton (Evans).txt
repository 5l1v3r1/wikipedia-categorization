Julius Sterling Morton, also known as J. Sterling Morton, is a 1937 bronze sculpture of Julius Sterling Morton by Rudulph Evans, installed in the United States Capitol Visitor Center, in Washington, D.C., as part of the National Statuary Hall Collection.  It is one of two statues donated by the state of Nebraska.   The sculpture was accepted into the collection by Congressman Karl Stefan of Nebraska on April 27, 1937.  The statue is one of two that Evans has placed in the collection, the other being William Jennings Bryan, also from  Nebraska.
