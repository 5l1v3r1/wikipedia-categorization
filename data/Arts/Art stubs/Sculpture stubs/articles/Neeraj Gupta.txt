  Neeraj Gupta (born 1969) is an Indian sculptor.   He took second prize in the sculpture section of the Florence Biennale in 2017, one of over a hundred winners in eleven sections.  In 2018 he was one of the two hundred artists  accepted to exhibit at the annual NordArt exhibition in the Carlshütte (de) of Büdelsdorf, in Schleswig-Holstein in northern Germany.  Keshav Malik was his guru.   In 2004 he won the Sahitya Kala Parishad award at the 28th Annual Art Exhibition, Govt. of  N.C.T. of Delhi.  Gupta is also one of the finalists of Woollahra Small Sculpture Prize 2018 out of 666 entries.
