Ethan Allen is a marble sculpture depicting Ethan Allen by Larkin Goldsmith Mead. One carving of a different Meade statue of Allen was installed outside the Vermont State House in Montpelier, Vermont, , dedicated on October 10, 1861 but deteriorated and was later destroyed.  The statue formerly installed outside the Vermont State House Another Meade statue of Allen appears in the United States Capitol as part of the National Statuary Hall Collection. The statue was gifted by the state of Vermont in 1876.  Another carving of the statue is located in the Ticonderoga Museum in Ticonderoga, New York. "The sculpture was previously in the collection of the Ohio County Library in West Virginia, where it had been donated by a local family. The Museum purchased it in 1973 or 1975. It was located outdoors and was moved inside because of deterioration."  A photograph of the statue is featured on the cover and spine of Willard Sterne Randall's biography of Ethan Allen.
