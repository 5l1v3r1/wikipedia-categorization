Wilton Ware, a brand of English pottery, was the brand name of A.G.Harley-Jones factory. Wilton Ware was produced in Fenton, Stoke on Trent, Staffordshire, England between 1904 - 1934. Horace Wain left Carlton Ware and became the designer for the factory in the early 1920s.
