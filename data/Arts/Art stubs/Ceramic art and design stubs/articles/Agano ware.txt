Agano ware (上野焼, Agano-yaki) is a type of Japanese pottery traditionally made in Fukuchi, Tagawa District, Fukuoka.  The beginnings of its production was supported by Hosokawa Sansai,  who was otherwise known as the daimyō Hosokawa Tadaoki.      It was originally associated with the tea ceremony.
