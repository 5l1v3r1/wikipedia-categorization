Louise Averill Cole (1880-1971) was a prominent American book binding designer, noted for her elegant and finely-worked bindings. She was born in San Francisco, CA and was in charge of Houghton Mifflin's Riverside Press bindery in Cambridge, MA.  She studied bookbinding in Brussels under Professor Louis Jacobs.  She won a grand prize for bookbinding at the Panama-Pacific International Exposition of 1915.
