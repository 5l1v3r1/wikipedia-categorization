The Hôtel de Breteuil is a historic building in the 16th arrondissement of Paris, France. The hôtel particulier was built for Henri Le Tonnelier de Breteuil and his second wife.  It was designed by architect Ernest Sanson, and its construction was completed in 1892.  It is home to the Embassy of Ireland in France.
