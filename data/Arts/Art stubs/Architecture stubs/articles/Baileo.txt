 Baileo is a custom house,  in Maluku and North Maluku, Indonesia.  The house is a representation of the Baileo Maluku culture and has an important function for the life of the community.  Baileo is the identity of any community in the Moluccas in addition to a mosque or church.   Baileo serves as a repository for sacred objects,  a place of traditional ceremonies,  as well as a place for community meetings,  The main characteristic of the Baileo home is its large size and difference in appearance when compared to other buildings in its vicinity.
