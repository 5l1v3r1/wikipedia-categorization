Concord Grand is a mixed-use building located at 169/1, Shantinogor, Dhaka-1217, Bangladesh. It is one of the tallest buildings in Dhaka with a height of 80.39 m (263.74 feet) constructed by Concord Group. The building has 25 floors, which are used for commercial and residential purposes.
