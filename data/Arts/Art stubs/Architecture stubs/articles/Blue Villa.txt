The Blue Villa (fr: "Villa Bleue") is a historic mansion in Barcelonnette, Alpes-de-Haute-Provence, Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur, France.  It was built from 1929 to 1931 for Camille Jean, a French businessman who founded Francia Maritima, a store in Mexico City, Mexico.   It was designed in the Art Deco architectural style by Joseph Hiriart,  Georges Tribout and Georges Beau.  It has been listed as an official historical monument since 1987.
