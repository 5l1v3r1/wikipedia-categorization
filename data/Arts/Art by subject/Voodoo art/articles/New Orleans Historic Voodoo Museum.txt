New Orleans Historic Voodoo Museum is a voodoo museum in New Orleans, United States, situated between Bourbon and Royal Streets in the centre of the French Quarter.  Although only a small museum, consisting of two rooms, it is one of few museums in the world dedicated entirely to Vodou art. There is a voodoo priest on site giving readings.
