Le Concert Champêtre ("Woodland Music-makers") is an 1857 painting by French artist Jean-Baptiste-Camille Corot, currently housed in the Musée Condé of Chantilly, France. A reworking of a composition  exhibited by Corot in the Salon of 1844, the painting was shown in the Salon of 1857. The painting depicts three women, one with a cello, in the foreground of a forest landscape.
