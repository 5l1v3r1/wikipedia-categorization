Emeraz is a social networking service to connect people in the music industry.  Emeraz was founded in Dublin, Ireland in 2010, by producer and sound engineer, Stephen Brett.  Emeraz originally started out as a place to collaborate online and copyright music. In September 2012 a new version of Emeraz launched and is now a complete social networking platform.   Users of Emeraz are able to collaborate with other musicians and present songs to well-known artists and producers, as well as getting feedback from leading names in the industry.
