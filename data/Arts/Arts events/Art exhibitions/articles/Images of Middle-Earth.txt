Images from the Middle Earth was an international Tolkien art exhibition conceived and curated by Davide Martini (Martini is now art director at Greisinger Museum ) that started in Italy including more than 170 artworks.[a] The exhibition was first held in August 2002 at the Agolanti Fortress, Riccione, visited by 8,000 people.
