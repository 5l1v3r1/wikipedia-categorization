 Marko Stout is an American contemporary artist known for his gritty industrial pop style with a focus on New York City and modern urban life. Stout produces work in a variety of media including print, painting, sculpture, film, video, photography and new media.   Mark Stout is also known for the International "Edible Apple Film Festival" in downtown NYC with the sole aim of promoting innovative avant-garde art works and experimental film which was founded in 2015 by Stout.   Stout was featured on The Huffington Post after his tour with his art installation Chelsea Girls.
