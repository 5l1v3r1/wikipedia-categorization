David Geiser (born June 28, 1948) is an American painter and creator of several underground comix. David Geiser is an abstract expressionist painter, born in Rochester, New York in 1948. In 1969, he moved to San Francisco and began drawing underground comix and cavorting with other underground comic creators such as S. Clay Wilson.  He created several underground comic titles in the early 1970s, included Saloon, Demented Pervert, Clowns, Uncle Sham, DTs, Pain, and Edge City.  He moved into oil painting in the late 1970s. Geiser moved to New York City in the early 1980s, where he concentrated on oil painting while moving into abstract expressionism.  Geiser's paintings are tactile and deep, often being composed of many layers of shellack, pitch, tar, rope and scrap wood. Geiser is married to actress Mercedes Ruehl and lives in the Hamptons with her and their son Jake.
