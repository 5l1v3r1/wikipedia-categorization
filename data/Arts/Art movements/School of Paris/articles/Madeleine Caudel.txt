Madeleine Caudel (21 May 1879 in Paris – 27 January 1962 in Paris)  was a French woman landscape painter.  Madeleine Caudel was a member of the Société du Salon des Artistes Français. In Paris, she lived at 17e, 75, street Nollet.  About since 1911 she exhibited at the Société du Salon des Artistes Français.  Since 1929 to 1931 Madeleine Caudel exhibited at the Gallery Alexandre Lefranc, 15, street La Ville-l'Evêque (Paris)  and Gallery Ecalle.  In 1934, Caudel exhibited at the Mon Club together with Vera Rockline (1896-1934) and others.
