Constantina Zavitsanos is a conceptual feminist artist.   Her works are organized around themes of planning, contingency, debt, dependency and care.  Zavitsanos is a part of disability community as a care provider and recipient. She lives in New York City and teaches at The New School.  She is a recipient of the 2015 Wynn Newhouse Award presented to disabled artists.
