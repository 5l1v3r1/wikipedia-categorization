The SIDA      is a Chinese non-profit-making  and non-governmental design organization  ,founded in 2008 ,supported by the Shenzhen Government.SIDA is the member of International Council of Societies of Industrial Design . Till April 2017,SIDA has taken in 821 member units from Shenzhen and across the country, including  institutions that provide services for industrial design chains and the relevant organizations,such as,manufacturers,marketing institutions,etc.
