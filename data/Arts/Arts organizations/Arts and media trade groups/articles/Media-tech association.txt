 The MEDIA-TECH Association serves the packaged media manufacturing industry, spanning the range of optical disc, Blu-ray disc, flash media and future formats such as holographic memory and near field recording.  A Committee formed in June 2001 during the REPLIitech exhibition in Geneva, Switzerland to represent the unified voice of package media manufactures.  This 10 member committee was represented by 5 members appointed by the Optical Media Machine Association (OMMA) and 5 Members of the ARIES Group of exhibiting companies.  The MEDIA-TECH Association was later founded 9 November 2001 in Frankfurt, Germany.
