 Mediacorp has given Star Awards to actors and actresses for their performances in drama serials since its inception. Throughout the history of the Star Awards, there have been actors and actresses who have received multiple Star Awards for Best Actor, Best Actress, Best Supporting Actor, or Best Supporting Actress. As of  2018[update], 20 actors and actresses have received two or more Star Awards in acting categories. Chen Hanwei and Xie Shaoguang lead the way with seven awards (five Best Actor awards and two Best Supporting Actor awards), while Xiang Yun has won four Best Supporting Actress awards. Xie was the first to receive four awards in 1998, followed by Xiang Yun in 2009, and most recently Chen in 2010. Xie was also the first to receive five, six, and seven awards in 1999, 2003 and 2004 respectively.
