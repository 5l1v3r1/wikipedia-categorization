 This is a list of novellas that have been recognised as the best examples of the genre. A novella is a fiction work longer than a short story but shorter than a novel.  It is generally thought to be between around 20,000 and 40,000 words in length.  This list contains novellas that have been recognized as the best examples of the genre. The first table contains those books that are widely considered to be the best, through their appearance on multiple best-of lists.      The second table contains other notable novellas, that have appeared on only one best-of list.
