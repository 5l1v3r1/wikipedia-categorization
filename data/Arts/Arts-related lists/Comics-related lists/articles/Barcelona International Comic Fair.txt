Barcelona International Comic Fair is an annual fair and convention of authors, publishers and comic readers, managed by FICOMIC (ca). It takes place in the city of Barcelona since 1981, and is an event of reference on the Spanish comic market.  The Prizes are awarded to the best works published on the year preceding the Convention.
