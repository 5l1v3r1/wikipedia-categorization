Space Group is an architecture office established in 1999 in Oslo, Norway and created by Gary Bates and Gro Bonesmo. Prior to founding Space Group, they worked with OMA/Rem Koolhaas, which they later on collaborated for the Vestbanen project in Oslo. The firm is also known for leading large master plan development and research. One of their current involvement is for the project "8 million city"/Station City, connecting Oslo to Copenhagen by train.  They were the curator of the Nordic Pavilion at the 14th International Architecture Exhibition at the Venice Biennale in 2014.
