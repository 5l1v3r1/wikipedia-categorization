 dhk is a South African-based architecture and urban design studio with offices in Cape Town and Johannesburg. The firm, led by founding partners Derick Henstra and Peter Fehrsen, has designed a number of high profile buildings in Cape Town and has projects across Africa and in Europe.   dhk designed Portside, Cape Town's tallest skyscraper.  In 1983, Derick Henstra established his own architectural practice . dhk was formed when contemporaries KCVR merged with Derick Henstra Architects in 1998. dhk website
