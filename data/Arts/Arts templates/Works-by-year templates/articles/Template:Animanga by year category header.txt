This template is meant to be used on categories in the animanga by year and decade category structure, where the top-level categories are Category:Anime debuts by date and Category:Manga debuts by date. This template accepts several unnamed parameters. The first should be set to either "Anime" or "Manga", depending on what the category in question covers. The second one is the first three digits in the year (for instance, if the category covers 1997, the second parameter's value would be "199"). The third parameter should only be used on individual year categories, and consists of the last digit in the year. In addition, there are three named parameters: Manga series and one-shots first released from January 1, 1990 through December 31, 1999.
