Jessica Page Morrell is an American author. Morrell is a contributor to The Writer and Writer's Digest magazines.  Her book, Thanks But This Isn't For Us (2009), describes mistakes that new authors make and how these writers can fix their writing.   In particular, she lays out the necessity and process of creating a plan or structure for a novel, before starting to write one.  Library Journal states that she "convincingly portrays the craft of writing for publication."  Morrell conducts workshops throughout the Pacific Northwest.
