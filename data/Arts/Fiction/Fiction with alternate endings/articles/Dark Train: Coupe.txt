Dark Train: Coupe is an upcoming visual novel by Paperash Studio. It is  a prequel to Dark Train. The game was announced on 16 May 2017.  The game was announced on 16 May when Steam Greenlight campaign was launched.   The game is opposite to the original Dark Train. While Dark Train was a nontextual adventure game, DarkTrain: Coupe is a visual novel told through dialogues and text. Player is sometimes given opportunity to make a decision that will influence game's progress and eventually the ending. The game itself is non-linear.    Whole gameplay is supposed to be textual.  The main character is Ann Tagrezbung before she became mechanical squid from original Dark Train and still was a human. Whole game is set in Border City.
