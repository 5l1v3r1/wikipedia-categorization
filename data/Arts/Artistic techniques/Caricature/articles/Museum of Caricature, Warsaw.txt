Museum of Caricature (Polish: Muzeum Karykatury) is a museum in Warsaw, Poland. The museum was established in 1978.  It has a collection of over 20,000 pieces by Polish and foreign artists.   The museums premises are an old orangery which was once part of the 18th century Prymas Castle.  As the museum only has limited space it does not have a permanent collection on display but instead shows temporary exhibitions based on its holdings.
