A blockchain game is a video game which is based on blockchain technology. The world's first blockchain game, Huntercoin,  was released in beta in September, 2013,  with the full release in February, 2014.  One of the best known examples of a blockchain collectible game is Cryptokitties, launched in November 2017.  The game made headlines in December 2017 when a cryptokitty character sold for US$100,000.
