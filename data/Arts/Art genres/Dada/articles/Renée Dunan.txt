Renée Dunan (Avignon, 1892–1936), was a French writer, critic and poet. Her production was abundant and it used diverse pseudonyms: Marcelle La Pompe, M. de Steinthal, Jean Spaddy, Louise Dormienne, Renée Camera. She is an author of erotic and science fiction novels and stories. She belonged to the Dada movement and had connections with André Breton, Philippe Soupault, Louis Aragon, Paul Éluard and Francis Picabia. Her ideology included feminism, naturism, anarchism, and pacifism. She collaborated in the magazines of Crapouillot and Le Sourire.
