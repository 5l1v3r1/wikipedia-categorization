The Graveyard is a computer art game developed by Belgian developer Tale of Tales in which the player assumes the role of an elderly woman walking through a graveyard to a bench.  The game ends either with the woman leaving the graveyard or dying of natural causes.  The Graveyard was nominated for the Innovation Award in the Independent Games Festival 2009   The Graveyard was one of the inspirations for the Tibetan village scene in Uncharted 2: Among Thieves.
