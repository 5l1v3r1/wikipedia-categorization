Musée Visionnaire is a private museum dedicated to outsider art located in Predigerplatz 10, 8001 Zurich, Switzerland.     It was founded on 18 November 2013. The Musée Visionnaire is a non-profit association. It is an art foyer where changing exhibitions allow visitors to discover and re-discover contemporary Art Brut | Outsider Art and experience it within the context of related art forms. Special social events are dedicated to topical subjects touching upon the world of art and current public issues.
