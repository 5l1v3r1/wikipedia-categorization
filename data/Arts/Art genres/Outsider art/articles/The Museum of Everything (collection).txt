The Museum of Everything is a non-profit organisation and platform which stages installations of art by private, self-taught and non-academic art-makers (sometimes referred to as art brut or outsider art). The Museum of Everything staged its first exhibition in London in October 2009.  Its first location was a former recording studio in Primrose Hill, London; however the museum has no fixed venue and stages exhibitions around the world. . The museum has described the material on display as "untrained, unintentional, undiscovered and unclassifiable" and rejects the term "outsider art".  Exhibitions are generally surveys of material from makers around the world. The 2017/2018 exhibition at Mona in Tasmania presented almost 2,000 artworks by over 100 artists.
