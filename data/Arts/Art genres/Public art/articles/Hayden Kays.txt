Hayden Kays (born 3 October 1985 in London) is a British artist. He is influenced by the pop art from the '50s, but has most ties with street art. Banksy is a fan.  He often uses a typewriter (font) in his artworks.  In February 2014 he became well known for selling artwork to One Direction popstar Harry Styles.  He has since sold art to celebrities such as Chris Martin   and Noel Fielding.  His works were collected in a monograph book in 2013, named Hayden Kays Is An Artist.
