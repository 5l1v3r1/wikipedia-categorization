Through and through describes a situation where an object, real or imaginary, passes completely through another object, also real or imaginary. The phrase has several common uses: Through and through is used in forensics to describe a bullet that has passed through a body, leaving both entry and exit wounds Example.. In the case that the image can be viewed from the other side, we see the mirror image, just like in the case of a transparent image, such as a drawing on a transparent sheet.
