Namikawa Yasuyuki  (1845–1927) was a Japanese cloisonné artist. For his work he was nominated an Imperial Household Artist.  The Namikawa Cloisonne Museum in Higashiyama-ku, Kyoto exhibits a range of his items.  Many of his works are in museums today such as the Los Angeles County Museum of Art.
