Gambhir Singh Mura was an Indian tribal dancer known for his contributions to the tribal martial dance of Chhau.   He was an exponent of the Purulia school of Chhau.  Mura was born in a tribal family to Jipa Singh Mura  at Pitikiri Bamni village in Purulia district in the Indian state of West Bengal.  He performed in many places such as England, France,  Japan and USA.  The Government of India awarded him the fourth highest Indian civilian honour of Padma Shri in 1981.
