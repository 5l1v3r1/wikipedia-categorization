Thomas More in Prison, Visited by His Wife and Daughter or Thomas More en prison (and various other titles) is a history painting of 1828 by Claudius Jacquand.  It depicts Thomas More in prison, with his wife and daughter. It has been in the collection of the Musée des Beaux-Arts de Lyon since soon after it was completed.     In 2014 it formed part of the exhibition L'invention du Passé. Histoires de cœur et d'épée 1802-1850.
