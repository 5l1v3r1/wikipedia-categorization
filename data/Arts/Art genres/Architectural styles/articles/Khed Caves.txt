  Khed Caves, also Bouddh Caves, are a series of ancient Buddhist caves in the city of Khed, Maharashtra, India.   The group of caves comprises a large vihara, with three cells for monks, and with a stupa in the back located in an oblong room.  There are also four smaller caves in the group.
