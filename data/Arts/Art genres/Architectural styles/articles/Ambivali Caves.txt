The Ambivali Caves, or Ambivali Leni are a group of Buddhist caves, located near Neral, Raigad district, Maharashtra, 8km southeast of Kalyan.   The caves are cut in the low hill located on the concave portion of a river.  They consist in 12 viharas celles with verandah and several water cisterns.
