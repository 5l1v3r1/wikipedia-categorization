Varied subjects included self-portraits: Koons, Neel, Penck, less known figures and some famous names: Joseph Beuys by Warhol, Marlene Dietrich by Finer, President Mitterrand by Organ, Archbishop Desmond Tutu by Marisol, Seamus Heaney by Edwards, and "The Smoking Man" a video portrait by Marty St.James.
