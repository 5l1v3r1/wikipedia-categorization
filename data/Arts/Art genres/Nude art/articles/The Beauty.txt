 The Beauty (Russian: Красавица, translit. Krasavitsa) is an oil on canvas 1915 painting by the Russian artist Boris Kustodiev. The painting measures 141 by 185 centimeters and is  now housed at the Tretyakov Gallery.  The Moscow Art Theatre actress Faina Shevchenko was the model for the painting.  The painting features a nude, fat woman sitting on a bed in a widely decorated room with flowery wallpaper.
