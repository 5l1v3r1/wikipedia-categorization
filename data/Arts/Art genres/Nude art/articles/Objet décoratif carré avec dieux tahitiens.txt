Objet décoratif carré avec dieux tahitiens (Square Ornament with Tahitian gods) is a 1893-1895 terracotta sculpture by Paul Gauguin, now in the Musée d'Orsay, Paris.  It depicts Tahitin goddesses on both sides; one  the woman is alert and confronting the viewer, on the other the goddess appears to be at rest and sleeping.
