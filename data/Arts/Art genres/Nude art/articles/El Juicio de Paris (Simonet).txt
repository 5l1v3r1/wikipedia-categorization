El Juicio de Paris (The Judgment of Paris in English) is an oil-on-canvas painting  of the Greek myth, the Judgement of Paris. It was executed in 1904 by Enrique Simonet, a Spanish painter, and is one of the many works depicting the scene. The composition is 3.31 by 2.15 metres (10.9 by 7.1 ft). It is currently owned by heirs of Simonet, and is in the Museo de Málaga.
