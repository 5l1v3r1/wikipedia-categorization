Young Girl with a Flower Basket (Fillette Ã  la corbeille fleurie or Jeune fille nue avec panier de fleurs or Fillette nue au panier de fleurs or  Le panier fleuri) is a 1905 painting by Pablo Picasso from his Rose period.  It was sold by Picasso to the writer and poet Gertrude Stein and was latterly part of an art collection established by David Rockefeller. It was sold as part of the David and Peggy Rockefeller collection. The Rockefeller's collection was auctioned in May 2018 by Christie's, and Young Girl with a Flower Basket sold for $115 million on 8 May 2018.
