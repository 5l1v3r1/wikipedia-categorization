Annick Ménardo is a contemporary perfumer who worked under the fragrance firm Firmenich since joining in 1991. Although she has a background in a wide base of sciences such as biochemistry and medicine, Annick Ménardo pursued perfumery as her subject of study and is a graduate of the French perfume academy ISIPCA. She was mentored under Michel Almairac while working at Créations Aromatiques and her early work is influenced by his style  Annick Ménardo is also known in her work with sculptor Nobi Shioya in his /7S/ olfactory art installation and created the scent for the sculpture "Anger".
