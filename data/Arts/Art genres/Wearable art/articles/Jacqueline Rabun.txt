Jacqueline Rabun (born 1961) is an American jewelry designer.   Rabun was born in Bitburg, Germany.   She is self-taught.    In 1989, she moved to London and launched her first collection of jewelry.   The collection was picked up by Barneys New York.  Her work was featured on the cover of British Vogue in 1992.  She has created designs for Georg Jensen as well as other brands like Halston.
