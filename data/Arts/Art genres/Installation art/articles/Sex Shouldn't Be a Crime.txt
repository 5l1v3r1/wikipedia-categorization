Sex Shouldn't Be a Crime is a series of artworks created by Clandestine Culture  in 2013, and exhibited for the first time in 2014 in the Clandestine Culture second solo show at the Gregg Shienbaum Fine Art Gallery.   The word touches on topics like human sexuality and prostitution.  The artist is known for his strong social criticism and portrayal of taboo subjects, often in a disturbing or controversial way.
