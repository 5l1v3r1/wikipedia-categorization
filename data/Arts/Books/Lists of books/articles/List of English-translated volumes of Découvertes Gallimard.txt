Découvertes Gallimard is a French encyclopaedic collection of illustrated pocket books published by Éditions Gallimard since 1986. Books of this collection have been selectively translated into English. London-based publisher Thames & Hudson launched its first English-translated titles in 1992,  under the title New Horizons series. Abrams Books of New York produces the collection in United States under the title Discoveries (generally called Abrams Discoveries series).
