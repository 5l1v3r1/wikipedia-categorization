Mascot Books is a full-service, multi-genre, independent book publisher and distributor. It is a hybrid publishing company headquartered in Herndon, Virginia, USA. The company publishes a variety of genres, including fiction, nonfiction, children's, cookbooks, and coffee table books.  Naren Aryal co-founded Mascot Books in 2003 with the publication of Hello, Hokie Bird, a children's book about his alma mater's mascot.  After a successful initial title, Aryal left his job as a corporate attorney to start Mascot Books.  Since inception, the business has published more than 2,500 titles. Mascot Books was recently featured in The Washington Post, USA Today, and The Costco Connection.
