Rosalind Franklin: The Dark Lady of DNA is a biography of Rosalind Franklin, a scientist whose work helped discover DNA.    It was written by Brenda Maddox and published by HarperCollins in October 2002.   A play based in part on the book, Photograph 51 written by Anna Ziegler, was staged in London in 2015 starring Nicole Kidman.  The book received the 2002 Los Angeles Times Book Prizes for Science and Technology.  It was also awarded the Marsh Biography Award in 2003, and was shortlisted for the 2002 Whitbread Biography Award.
