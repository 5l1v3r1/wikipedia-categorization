The British Academy Medal is awarded annually by the British Academy to up to three individuals or groups. It is awarded for "outstanding achievement that has transformed understanding of a particular subject or field of study in ... any branch of the humanities and social sciences". It was first awarded in 2013.   It is the first medal awarded by the British Academy for any subject within the remit of the academy.  Three people were awarded the British Academy Medal in 2013.
