I'm Off Then: Losing and Finding Myself on the Camino de Santiago (German: Ich bin dann mal weg) is a book by German writer Hape Kerkeling written in 2006 and translated into English in 2009.   It has sold over three million copies.  It has also been translated into French, Italian, Dutch, Polish, Latvian, Spanish and Korean. A film adaptation, directed by Julia von Heinz, with Devid Striesow and Martina Gedeck starring, was released on December 24, 2015.
