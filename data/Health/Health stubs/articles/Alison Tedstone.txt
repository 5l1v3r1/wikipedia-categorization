Alison Tedstone RNutr FAfN (born April 1961)  is Chief Nutritionist (National Director of Diet & Obesity) at Public Health England (PHE).  She was born Alison Tedstone. She has a BSc and PhD from the University of London. She conducted research into nutrition at the University of Oxford. She is a Registered Public Health Nutritionist. She is a founding fellow of the Association for Nutrition (AfN).  She was an academic at the London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine. She joined the Food Standards Agency (FSA) in 2001, working there until 2010, becoming Head of Nutrition Science. She became Chief Nutritionist at PHE. Work includes the National Diet and Nutrition Survey. she gives evidence to the Scientific Advisory Committee on Nutrition (SACN). She married Alan Penn in the 1980s in Worcestershire.
