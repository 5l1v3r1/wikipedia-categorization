The Myanmar Dental Association (Burmese: မြန်မာနိုင်ငံ သွားနှင့်ခံတွင်း ဆရာဝန်အသင်း; MDA) is a professional association established in 1979 which has more than 4,000 members.  The association publishes Myanmar Dental Journal every year.  It also arranges Myanmar Dental Conference in which FDI World Dental Federation speakers also make their presentations.
