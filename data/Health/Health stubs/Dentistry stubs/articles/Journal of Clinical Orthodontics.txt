The Journal of Clinical Orthodontics  is a monthly peer-reviewed medical journal covering the practical aspects of orthodontics and practice management. The editor-in-chief is Robert G. Keim (University of Southern California). It was established in 1967 as the Journal of Practical Orthodontics, obtaining its current title in 1970. The journal is abstracted and indexed in MEDLINE/PubMed  and CINAHL.
