Paul S. Wright,  (born 1 May 1946)  is the emeritus professor in prosthetic dentistry at Barts and The London School of Medicine and Dentistry.  He also chair of the General Dental Council's, Specialist Dental Education Board.   He is also consulting editor on the European Journal of Prosthodontics and Restorative Dentistry.   In 1969 Wright graduated from The London Hospital Medical College Dental School.
