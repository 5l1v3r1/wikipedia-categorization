 P. alactolyticus  Pseudoramibacter is a Gram-positive, strictly anaerobic, non-spore-forming and non-motile bacterial genus from the family of Eubacteriaceae with one known species (Pseudoramibacter alactolyticus).     Pseudoramibacter bacteria occur in birds.  Pseudoramibacter alactolyticus is associated with endodontic infections.
