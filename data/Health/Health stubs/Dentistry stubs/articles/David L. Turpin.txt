Dr. David L. Turpin is an American Orthodontist who was the Editor-in Chief for The Angle Orthodontist from 1988 to 1999 and American Journal of Orthodontics and Dentofacial Orthopedics from 1999 to 2010.   He received his dental degree from University of Iowa College of Dentistry in 1962 and received his Masters for Orthodontics from University of Washington School of Dentistry in 1966.  He worked in Private practice for more than 37 years of his life along with being the clinical faculty at the Orthodontics Department at University of Washington.
