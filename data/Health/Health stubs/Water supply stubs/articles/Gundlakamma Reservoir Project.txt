Gundlakamma Reservoir Project is an irrigation project located in Prakasam district in Andhra Pradesh, India.  The dam with 56 million cubic meters live storage capacity is located across the Gundlakamma River.   It now supplies 100 acres out of the total envisaged area of 80,000.
