San Antonio Reservoir is located in Alameda County, California, about three miles east-southeast of Sunol. It was built in 1964  by the City and County of San Francisco. Formed by the James H. Turner Dam  across San Antonio Creek  not far above where it flows into Alameda Creek, its purpose is to store water from the Hetch Hetchy Aqueduct and local wells and watersheds.
