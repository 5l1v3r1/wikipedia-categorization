The Water Resources Department is the government department in the state of Madhya Pradesh responsible for irrigation and flood control.    Water Resources Department, formerly known as Irrigation Department is one of the major establishments of Government of Madhya Pradesh. It protects the right of State in sharing Water of Inter State Rivers/Basins. The field of Operations of Water Resources Department is  responsible for creation and maintenance of irrigation potential through construction of water resources projects. It also looks after Calamity Managements in the form of construction and maintenance of flood control works, reservoir operation and reconstruction of structures damaged by cyclone.construction, maintenance and regulation of Major , Medium & Minor irrigation projects, Flood Control and Drainage works.
