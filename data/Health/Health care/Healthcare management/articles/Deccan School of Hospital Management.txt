  The Department of Hospital Management,Deccan school of management or Deccan School of Hospital Management (DSHM), established in the year 1998, is an affiliated college of Osmania University,  and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). DSHM is in Hyderabad in the state of Telangana, India. Among other management degrees, it offers a master's degree in Hospital Management (MHM). Masters in Hospital management is a programme which is designed to bring out managers in hospitals and healthcare organisations.mrs Manisha saxena is the principal of the college.syed murtuza bakshi sir is the vice principal of the college.
