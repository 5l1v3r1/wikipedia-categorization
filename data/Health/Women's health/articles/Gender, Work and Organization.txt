Gender, Work & Organization is a bimonthly peer-reviewed academic journal. The journal was established in 1994 and is published by John Wiley & Sons. It covers research on the role of gender on the workfloor. The editors-in-chief are Deborah Kerfoot (Keele University), Ida Sabelis (Vrije Universiteit) and Alison Pullen (Macquarie University). In addition to the regular issues, the journal publishes several special issues per year.
