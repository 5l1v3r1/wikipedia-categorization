Reproductive Sciences is a peer-reviewed medical journal that publishes papers in the fields of obstetrics and gynecology. The editor-in-chief is Hugh S. Taylor (Yale University). It was established in 1994 and is currently published by SAGE Publications on behalf of the Society for Reproductive Investigation. The journal is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2013 impact factor is 2.23, ranking it 17th out of 30 journals in the category "Reproductive Biology"  and 26th out of 79 journals in the category "Obstetrics & Gynecology".
