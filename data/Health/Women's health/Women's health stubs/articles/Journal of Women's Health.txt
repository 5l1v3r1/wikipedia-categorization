The Journal of Women's Health is a monthly peer-reviewed healthcare journal focusing on women's health care, including advancements in diagnostic procedures, therapeutic protocols for the management of diseases, and research in gender-based biology that impacts patient care and treatment.  The journal was established in 1992 and is published by Mary Ann Liebert, Inc.. The editor-in-chief is Susan G. Kornstein (Virginia Commonwealth University). It is the official journal of the Academy of Women's Health  and the American Medical Women's Association.
