The International Cannabinoid Research Society (ICRS) is a professional society for scientific research in all fields of the cannabinoids. The ICRS was formally incorporated as a scientific research society in 1992. Prior to that, early ICRS Symposia were organized by various researchers in the field since 1970. Membership in the Society has risen from 50 original members in its first year to 650+ members from all over the world.
