  Subharti College of Physiotherapy formerly Jyotirao Phule physiotherapy college is located in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, India. The college was established in 1999. The college campus is outside the city on an area of over 250 acres of land with playgrounds LTs and labs. There is a 1250 bed hospital attached to the college. The academic programme exposes the students to practical aspects through seminars, group discussions and lectures of experts invited from other institutions. The college has always been proud of its alumni who are working as good physiotherapist all over India. The college runs a free Out-patient department throughout the year. 1.
