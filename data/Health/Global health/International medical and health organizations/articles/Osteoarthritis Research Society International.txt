The Osteoarthritis Research Society International (OARSI) is a non-profit scientific organization.  The mission of OARSI is to promote and advance research for the prevention and treatment of osteoarthritis. OARSI publishes an international fully peer-reviewed multidisciplinary journal, Osteoarthritis and Cartilage. Many specialists, practitioners and researchers use this journal as a forum for idea dissemination related to diseases of cartilage and joints. Each year OARSI hosts a world congress on osteoarthritis.
