The International Headache Society (IHS) is a charity membership organisation founded in 1981 for those with a professional commitment to helping people affected by headache. It has over 1,000 ordinary members (including national society members). The society publishes an international journal Cephalalgia. The society was incorporated in 1994 in England and Wales as a Company Limited by Guarantee and registered in 1995 as a charity. The International Headache Congress (IHC) is held every second year, organized and hosted on behalf of IHS by a member national society.
