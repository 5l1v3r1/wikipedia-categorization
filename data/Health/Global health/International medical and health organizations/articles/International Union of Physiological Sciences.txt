The International Union of Physiological Sciences, abbreviated IUPS, is the global umbrella organization for physiology.  IUPS aims to facilitate initiatives that strengthen the discipline of physiology. IUPS is a scientific union member of the International Council for Science (ICSU),  and is accredited with the World Health Organization (WHO). The Union is composed of 54 National Members, 10 Associate Members, 2 Affiliated Members, 5 Regional Members and 5 Special Members.   IUPS organizes an international congress every 4 years and, in association with the American Physiological Society publishes the review journal Physiology.
