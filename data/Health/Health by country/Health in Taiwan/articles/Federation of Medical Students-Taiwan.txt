The aFederation of Medical Students in Taiwan (FMSTW; Chinese: 台灣醫學生聯合會), as the representative of all the medical students in Taiwan, consists of 12 medical colleges, including 16 departments of medicine, post-baccalaureate medicine, Chinese medicine, post-baccalaureate Chinese medicine. and one observer member. FMSTW also works as a formal member in both International Federation of Medical Students' Associations (IFMSA)   and Asian Medical Students' Association (AMSA),  representing medical students from Taiwan in all forms of international organisations and campaigns.
