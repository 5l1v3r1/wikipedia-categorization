The Ministry of Health (MOH or MoH; Malay: Kementerian Kesihatan) is a cabinet-level ministry in the Government of Brunei which oversees health system in the country. The ministry is headed by the Minister of Health and the incumbent is Md Isham bin Jaafar.   In the fiscal year 2018-19, the budget for Ministry of Health is allocated 345 million Brunei dollars ($262M as of March 2018),    an increase from B$324 million in the previous year.
