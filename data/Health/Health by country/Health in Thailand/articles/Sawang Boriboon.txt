Sawang Boriboon (สว่างบริบูรณ์) is a non-profit humanitarian based organization based and operating in Pattaya, Thailand. Originating as a Thai-Chinese Buddhist community based organization to fulfil the needs of local community, including language education, feeding the needy with vegetarian food, it is well known in the country for its a humanitarian rescue teams.   The language arm is called Sawang Boriboon Wittaya and began operations in 2001.  Its affiliate Sawang operates throughout the nation.
