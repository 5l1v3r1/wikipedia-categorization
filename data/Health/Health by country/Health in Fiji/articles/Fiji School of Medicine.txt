The Fiji School of Medicine is a tertiary institution based in Suva, Fiji. Originally established in 1885 as the Suva Medical School. After merger with institutions in 2010, FSM is now called College of Medicine, Nursing & Health Sciences under Fiji National University. It is located on the main island of Viti Levu in the Fiji Islands. FSM has been educating Health Care Professionals since its establishment in 1885 as Suva Medical School to train vaccinators. The school now provides training in most health science disciplines including medicine, dentistry, pharmacy, physiotherapy, radiography, laboratory technology, public health, health services management, dietetics and environmental health.
