The Ministry of Health and Sanitation is the health ministry of Sierra Leone. As of  2018[update], the Health Minister is Dr. Alpha T Wurrie. Their offices are located on the fourth floor of the Youyi Building, Brookfields, Freetown.  As of  2014[update], the Ministry of Health and Sanitation is engaged in a campaign against the Ebola virus epidemic in West Africa, in which Sierra Leone was one of the most seriously affected countries.
