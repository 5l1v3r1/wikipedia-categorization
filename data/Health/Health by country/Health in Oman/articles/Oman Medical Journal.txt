The Oman Medical Journal is a bimonthly open access peer-reviewed medical journal. It was established in 1984 and published by the Oman Medical Specialty Board.  The journal publishes different types of articles from various medical disciplines: The Oman Medical Specialty Board awards continuing medical education credits to authors and reviewers of the Oman Medical Journal. The journal is indexed in PubMed Central, CINAHL, EBSCO databases, and Expanded Academic ASAP.
