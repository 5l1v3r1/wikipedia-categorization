Genstat (General Statistics) is a statistical software package with data analysis capabilities, particularly in the field of agriculture.   Since 1968, it has been developed by many scientific experts in Rothamsted Research, and has a user-friendly interface,  professional modular design, excellent linear mixed models   and graphic functions. Leading Genstat’s continued development and distribution is VSN International (VSNi),  which is owned by The Numerical Algorithms Group and Rothamsted Research. Genstat is used in a number of research areas, including plant science, forestry, animal science, and medicine,  and is recognized by several world-class universities and enterprises.
