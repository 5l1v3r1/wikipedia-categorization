A Sertoli cell nodule, also Pick's adenoma,  testicular tubular adenoma and tubular adenoma of the testis, is a benign proliferation of Sertoli cells that arises in association with cryptorchidism (undescended testis).  They are not composed of a clonal cell population, i.e. neoplastic; thus, technically, they should not be called an adenoma.
