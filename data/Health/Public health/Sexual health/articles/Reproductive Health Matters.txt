Reproductive Health Matters (RHM) is an organisation promoting sexual and reproductive health and rights (SRHR) globally through its Journal and 'More than a Journal' activities.  It was established in 1993 by Marge Berer.  The editor-in-chief is Julia Hussein and Acting Chief Executive is Eszter Kismodi.  The RHM peer-reviewed journal publishes research, in-depth analysis and critical reviews across the spectrum of SRHR, generating knowledge and evidence and inspiring new thinking and action. In addition, RHM supports advocacy and informs global, regional and local policy discussions to ensure investment in overlooked SRHR issues, research, rights-based and evidence-based policies and programmes. RHM has a presence in London, Geneva and Cape Town, and works with regional partners in Brazil, China, Egypt, India, Peru, and Russia to promote the dissemination of our publications in seven languages.
