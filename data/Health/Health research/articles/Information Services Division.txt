  The Information Services Division (ISD) is the part of NHS Scotland that provides health information, health intelligence, statistical information and analysis.  ISD is part of the Public Health and Intelligence Strategic Business Unit of NHS National Services Scotland.  From January 2015 ISD will publish health statistics weekly, spreading the publications out more evenly across the year.
