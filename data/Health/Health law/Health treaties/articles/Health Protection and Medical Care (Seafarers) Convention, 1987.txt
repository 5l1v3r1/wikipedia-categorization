Health Protection and Medical Care (Seafarers) Convention, 1987 is  an International Labour Organization Convention. It was established in 1987, with the preamble stating: Having decided upon the adoption of certain proposals with regard to health protection and medical care for seafarers,...As of 2013, the convention has been ratified by 15 states. Of the ratifying states, seven have subsequently denounced the treaty. The principles of the convention were revised and incorporated into the Maritime Labour Convention.
