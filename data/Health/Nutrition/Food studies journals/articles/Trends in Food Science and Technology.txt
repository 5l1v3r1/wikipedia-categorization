 Trends in Food Science and Technology is a peer-reviewed review journal covering product development issues in the science and the technology related to food production. It is an official publication of the European Federation of Food Science and Technology and of the International Union of Food Science and Technology. It is edited by Paul Finglas (Institute of Food Research).
