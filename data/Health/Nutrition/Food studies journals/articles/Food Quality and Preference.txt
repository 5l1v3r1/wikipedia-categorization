Food Quality and Preference is a peer-reviewed scientific journal in the field of sensory and consumer science, published in 8 issues per year by Elsevier. Its scope covers consumer and market research, sensory science, sensometrics and sensory evaluation, nutrition and food choice, as well as food research, product development and sensory quality assurance. It is the official journal of The Sensometric Society and The European Sensory Science Society. According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2013 impact factor of 2.272.
