The Eatwell Plate is a pictorial summary of the main food groups and their recommended proportions for a healthy diet. It is the method for illustrating dietary advice by the Department of Health, issued officially by the government of the United Kingdom.   The Eatwell Plate was previously known as The Balance of Good Health.  The Eatwell Plate was superseded by the Eatwell Guide published on the 17th March 2016.
