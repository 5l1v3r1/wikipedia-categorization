Satiety value, is the degree at which food gives a human the sense of food gratification, the exact contrast feeling of hunger. The concept of the Satiety Value and Satiety Index  was developed by an Australian researcher and doctor, Susanna Holt.   Highest satiety value is expected when the food that remains in the stomach for a longer period produces greatest functional activity of the organ   Limiting the food intake after reaching the satiety value helps reduce obesity problems   Foods with great satiety value:
