Translational Behavioral Medicine is a quarterly peer-reviewed medical journal covering behavioral medicine. It was established in 2011 and was originally published by Springer Science+Business Media until January 1, 2018. Since then, it has been published by Oxford University Press.  It is one of two official journals of the Society of Behavioral Medicine. The editor-in-chief is Suzanne Miller-Halegoua (Fox Chase Cancer Center). According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2016 impact factor of 2.989.
