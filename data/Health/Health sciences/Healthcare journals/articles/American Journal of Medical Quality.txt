The American Journal of Medical Quality is a bimonthly peer-reviewed academic journal covering quality control in medicine. The editor-in-chief is David B. Nash (Jefferson School of Population Health). It was established in 1986 and is published by Sage Publications in association with the American College of Medical Quality. The journal is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Science Citation Index Expanded. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2014 impact factor is 1.252.
