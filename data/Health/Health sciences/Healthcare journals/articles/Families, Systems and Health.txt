Families, Systems and Health is a peer-reviewed academic journal published by the American Psychological Association on behalf of the Collaborative Family Healthcare Association. It was established in 1983 and covers research in the areas of families and health, especially collaborative family healthcare. The current editors-in-chief are Colleen Fogarty (University of Rochester Medical Center) and Larry Mauksch (University of Washington School of Medicine). According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2016 impact factor of 1.219, ranking it 107th out of 157 journals in the category "Public, Environmental & Occupational Health."
