Medical Care Research and Review is a peer-reviewed academic journal that covers the field of health care. The editor-in-chief is Thomas D'Aunno (Columbia University Mailman School of Public Health). It was established in 1944 and is currently published by Sage Publications. The journal publishes three types of articles:  review articles on particular research or policy topics that synthesize theoretical and empirical literature across several disciplines; empirical research; and articles that present new data and trends in the health care field. Each year, the journal's editorial board selects a "Best Paper", selected based on the quality of the work and its contribution to the field.
