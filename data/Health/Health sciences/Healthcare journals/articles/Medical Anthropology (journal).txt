Medical Anthropology is a bimonthly peer-reviewed academic journal covering medical anthropology published by Routledge. It was established in 1977. The editor-in-chief is Lenore Manderson (University of the Witwatersrand and (Brown University). The journal is abstracted and indexed in Anthropological Index Online, Current Contents/Social & Behavioral Sciences, EBSCOhost, European Reference Index for the Humanities, International Bibliography of the Social Sciences, Social Science Citation Index, PsycINFO, and Scopus.
