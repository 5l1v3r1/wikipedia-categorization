This is a list of countries by cancer rate, as measured variously by the number of new cancer cases (frequency), or death rate (mortality), per 100,000 population among countries (and dependencies). This is a list of countries by cancer frequency, as measured by the number of new cancer cases per 100,000 population among countries, based on the 2012 and 2014 GLOBOCAN statistics.  It excludes non-melanoma skin cancer. There are databases for "cancer mortality", including WHO's International Agency for Research on Cancer.  The 2014 IARC statistics include 172 countries.
