The Washington State University College of Pharmacy is one of two PharmD granting institutions within the state of Washington. In 2013, the college and its programs were relocated to WSU's Spokane campus, which has a health sciences focus. As of 2015, the school has 415 PharmD students, and 23 PhD students.
