The University of Washington School of Pharmacy was founded in 1894 and is one of two PharmD granting institutions within the state of Washington. Offices are located within the Warren G. Magnuson Health Sciences Building on the University of Washington campus. U.S. News & World Report ranked the School of Pharmacy as tied for the tenth best pharmacy school in the United States in 2012.  Sean Sullivan became the 9th dean of the school in 2014.   As of 2015, the school has 381 PharmD students, 59 PhD students, and 40 Masters of Science students and is 64% female.
