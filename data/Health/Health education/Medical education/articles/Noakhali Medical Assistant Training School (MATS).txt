Noakhali Medical Assistant Training School  provides 3 years course and 1-year internship Medical Assistant Training courses under Directorate General of Health Services (DGHS). Noakhali MATS is one of the eight others MATS along with Bagerhat, Kushtia, Shirajgonj, Tangail, Comilla, Faridpur and Jhinaidah.
