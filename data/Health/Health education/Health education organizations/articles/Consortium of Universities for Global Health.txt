The Consortium of Universities for Global Health (CUGH), established in 2008, is a membership-based nonprofit organization focusing on global health. Its members are primarily institutions, although individuals can also become members. CUGH members currently include over 145 academic institutions and other organizations.  CUGH was established in 2008 with funding from the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation and The Rockefeller Foundation.  Since 2009, CUGH has held an annual conference for global health academics and practitioners.
