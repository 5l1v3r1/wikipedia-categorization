Sharon Fonn is a South African Professor of Public Health at the University of the Witwatersrand. Her work has focused on cervical cancer, health systems and developing African capacity for public health research. Fonn served as the head of the University of the Witwatersrand School of Public Health from 2003-2011.  She is a member of the Academy of Science of South Africa.  Professor Fonn helped to set up Africa's longest running study of children from birth    She has published over 50 scientific articles.
