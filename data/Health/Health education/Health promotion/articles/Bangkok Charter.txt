The Bangkok Charter for Health Promotion in a Globalized World is the name of an international agreement reached among participants of the 6th Global Conference on Health Promotion held in Bangkok, Thailand in August 2005, convened by the World Health Organization.   It identifies actions, commitments and pledges required to address the determinants of health in a globalized world through health promotion.
