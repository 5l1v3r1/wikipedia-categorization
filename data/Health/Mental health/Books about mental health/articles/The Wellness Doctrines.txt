The Wellness Doctrines for law students and young lawyers is a self-help book published by Jerome Doraisamy.  He first wrote The Wellness Doctrines for Law Students and Young Lawyers in 2015.  He published the second book The Wellness Doctrines for high school students in April 2018.  The book has been widely spoken about in the legal communities both in Australia and worldwide.  The book peaked at #2 on iTunes.
