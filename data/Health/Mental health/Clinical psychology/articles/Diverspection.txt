Diverspection - is a blended word (diverse + perspective), which documents a different approach to understanding and dealing with complexity in psychosocial environments. The approach is based on abandoning the traditional view on roles and processes in organizations. A new set of questions destroys the “matrix of routines” and generates new lenses which enable a diverse perspective on complexity. Diverspection builds on clinical approaches to management  which refer to the psychodynamic-systemic way of looking at people in organizations. Diverspective approaches require clinical trained executive consultants in order to address competently the human element in organizations. One of the basic methodological assumptions of the clinical approach is that the key information for assessment and intervention can only be collected in direct contact with the client.
