Abigail Ashley is a Ghanaian television presenter, radio host, health advocate and the project leader for Behind My Smiles Foundation - a non-governmental organization (NGO) focusing on kidney health. She is also the author of the book "A Decade of My Life" Behind My Smiles.    Ashley is the presenter for "My Health, My Life" on United Television Ghana.  In 2017 she was nominated for "50 Young Most Influential people in Ghana".
