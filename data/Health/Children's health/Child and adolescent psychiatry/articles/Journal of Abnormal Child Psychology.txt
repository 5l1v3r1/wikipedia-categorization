Journal of Abnormal Child Psychology is the official publication of the International Society for Research in Child and Adolescent Psychopathology (ISRCAP). This scientific journal publishes 8 issues per year focusing on research in psychopathology in childhood and adolescence. Current Editor: John E. Lochman, University of Alabama, Tuscaloosa, AL Founding Editor: Herbert C. Quay, University of Miami, Coral Gables, FL
