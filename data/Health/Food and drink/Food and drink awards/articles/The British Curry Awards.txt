  The British Curry Awards is an annual awards dinner dedicated to the British curry industry. It was established in 2005 by Enam Ali   and has continued to this day. Each individual award is split for geographic purposes — regional splits of Scotland, North West, North East, Midlands, Wales, South East, South West, London Central and City, and London Suburbs — and expertise. The awards themselves are in association with Kukd.com, an online food delivery service. Categories for each award include Best Casual Dining, Best Delivery Restaurant and Best Newcomer.  The 14th edition of the British Curry Awards is set to take place on the date TBC November 2018 at the Battersea Evolution.
