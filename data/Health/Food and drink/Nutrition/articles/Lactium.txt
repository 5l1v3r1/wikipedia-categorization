Lactium is a αS1-casein tryptic hydrolysate containing bioactive decapeptide that act on GABA receptors. Lactium (150 mg/day) may be helpful in reducing stress and stress associated symptoms (mental function, digestion, cardiovascular health and social interactions), promote relaxation and can be useful in Insomnia.    In clinical trials, Lactium found to reduce stress induced increase in cortisol levels, stress associated symptoms and improve sleep quality in patients with chronic insomnia.    Lactium has health claim of reduction of stress effects.  Lactium exerts tranquilizing and anti-stress activity without any side effects like sedation, habituation, dependence, memory impairment and toxicity.
