The Food Wars is a 2009 book by Walden Bello which examines the food crisis and issues relating to food security.  It was originally published on 25 September 2009. ISBN 1-84467-331-6  ISBN 978-1-84467-331-5 Philip McMichael described that book as "A comprehensive and timely corrective to agribusiness-as-usual scenarios for solving the food crisis ... His solutions are compelling and critical for planetary sustainability."  Naomi Klein praised the book.
