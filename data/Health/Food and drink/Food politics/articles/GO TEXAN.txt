Go Texan day sponsored by the Texas Department of Agriculture, whose purpose is to encourage consumers (both within and outside the state of Texas) to seek and purchase Texas-made products.  The GO TEXAN campaign began in 1999 to focus on Texas agricultural products.  Later, the campaign expanded to include shrimp and Texas wine.
