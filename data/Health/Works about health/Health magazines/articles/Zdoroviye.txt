Zdoroviye (Russian: Здоровье; literally "Health") was a health magazine published in the Soviet Union and then, in Russia.  Founded in 1955,  Zdoroviye had a circulation of 12,600,000 copies in 1986.  It was published monthly  in Moscow by the Pravda Publishing House as a joint edition of the USSR and the RSFSR Ministries of Public Health, and covered popular scientific; medicine and hygiene.  Its chief editor was M. Piradov.
