A mesenteric cyst (MeSH C04.182.473) is a cyst in the mesenterium, and is one of the rarest abdominal tumors, with approximately 822 cases reported since 1507.  The incidence is between 1 per 100,000 to 1 per 250,000 hospital admissions.  Tillaux's triad named after the french surgeon Paul Jules Tillaux can be seen in cases of mesenteric cyst.
