Enteropathy refers to any pathology of the intestine.  Although enteritis specifically refers to an inflammation of the intestine, and is thus a more specific term than "enteropathy", the two phrases are sometimes used interchangeably. Specific types of enteropathy include: If the condition also involves the stomach, it is known as "gastroenteropathy". In pigs, porcine proliferative enteropathy is a diarrheal disease.
