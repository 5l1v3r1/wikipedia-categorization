Blepharoconjuctivitis is combined blepharitis and conjuctivitis.  Blepharitis is inflammation and irritation of eyelids and Conjuctivitis is inflammation and irritation of conjuctiva. Blepharoconjunctivitis occurs when normal bacteria that live on the eyelid skin cause irritation and inflammation to your eyelids.  The irritation and inflammation also spreads to conjuctiva.
