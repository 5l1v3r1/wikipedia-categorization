The peripheral odontogenic fibroma is an uncommon gingival mass.  It affects people across a large age range.  It can be confused with the peripheral ossifying fibroma.  In contrast to the peripheral ossifying fibroma, the peripheral odontogenic fibroma is a rare lesion.
