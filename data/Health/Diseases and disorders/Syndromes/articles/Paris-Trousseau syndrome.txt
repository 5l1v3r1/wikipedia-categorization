Paris-Trousseau syndrome (PTS) is an inherited disorder characterized by mild hemorrhagic tendency associated with 11q chromosome deletion.  It manifests as a granular defect within an individual's platelets. It is characterized by thrombocytes with defects in Î±-granule components which affects the cell's surfeace area and, consequently, its abitlity to spread when necessary.  FLI1 has been suggested as a candidate.
