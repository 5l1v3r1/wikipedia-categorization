In histology, cryptitis refers to inflammation of an intestinal crypt.   Cryptitis is a non-specific histopathologic finding that is seen in several conditions, e.g. inflammatory bowel disease,  diverticular disease,  radiation colitis,  infectious colitis. Cryptitis.
