Tony Gaskins  is a motivational speaker, author and life coach.    Having appeared on The Oprah Winfrey Show, The Tyra Banks Show and TBN's 700 Club.     He speaks on various topics including business, success and self-development but is most known for his love and relationship advice for men  which has garnered him a large Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter following.  In 2011, he made Under30Ceo's  Top 50 Most Motivational People on the Web.
