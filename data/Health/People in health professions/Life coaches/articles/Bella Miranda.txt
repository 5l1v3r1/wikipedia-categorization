Bella Miranda is an American life coach to several famous people.[who?]   Miranda was born in Texas, United States. Miranda is an advisor, executive business coach, executive strategic coach and life coach to a wide array of influential people in Fortune 100 companies, space industries, world leaders, CEOs, boards of directors and the entertainment industry. She is a captivating public speaker and author of her upcoming book based on her training philosophies and success. Miranda is currently working with certain commercial and private space companies to help the human population become an interplanetary species. Miranda is currently a sponsor for Association of Spaceflight Professionals, is on the patrons board and observer on the board of directors for the Commercial Spaceflight Federation.  Miranda sits on the advisory board for Space For Humanity and is an executive advisor for Arch Mission.  Miranda has been featured in Forbes, Huffington Post and more for her teachings and successes.
