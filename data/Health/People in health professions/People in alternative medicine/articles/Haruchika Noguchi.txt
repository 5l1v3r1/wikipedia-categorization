Haruchika Noguchi (野口 晴哉, Noguchi Haruchika, September 1911 – 1976) was the Japanese founder of Seitai.  He established the concept of taiheki. Among his many books, three are now available in English translation. Seitai Inteligencia Vital de Laura López Coto. 2015 ISBN 978-84-944640-0-3 (castellano)
