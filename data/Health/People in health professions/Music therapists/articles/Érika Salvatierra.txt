Érika Salvatierra Durán (born 3 May 1990) is a Bolivian footballer who plays as a defender. Salvatierra played for Universidad in her native Bolivia.  She later moved to Spain.  Salvatierra has capped for Bolivia during the 2006 South American Women's Football Championship.  She has also appeared at the 2008 South American U-20 Women's Championship.  Salvatierra is also a psychologist, a music therapist and a sports psychologist.
