  Sally Diana Poppitt is a New Zealand nutrition academic. She is currently a full professor at the University of Auckland where she holds the Fonterra Chair in Human Nutrition since June 2012.   Poppitt is originally from Cambridge, England. Her 1988 PhD thesis at Aberdeen University was titled  'Energetics of reproduction and overwintering in some insectivorous mammals' . After graduation, she worked for Dunn Human Nutrition, both in Gambia and University of Cambridge and then moved to the University of Auckland, rising to full professor.
