  Barbara A. Burlingame is a New Zealand food and nutrition academic. She is currently a professor at Massey University.  Burlingame did a BSc at University of California, Davis in Nutrition Science and Environmental Toxicology, graduating in 1983; and a PhD at  Massey University in 1997 entitiled 'Food composition harmonisation in international nutrition programme management.' After 16 years working at the Food and Agriculture Organization in Rome and rising to Deputy Director of the Nutrition Division, she returned to Massey as a professor in 2016.
