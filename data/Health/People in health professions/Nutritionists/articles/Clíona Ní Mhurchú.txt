  Clíona Ní Mhurchú is a New Zealand population nutrition academic, she is currently a professor at the University of Auckland.  After training at Trinity College Dublin and a PhD from the University of Southampton entitled  'Effectiveness of motivational interviewing in dietary education for people with hyperlipidaemia' , Ní Mhurchú  stated work at the University of Auckland where she rose to full professor.  In 1999-02 and 2010-13 Ní Mhurchú  received funding from the Heart Foundation of New Zealand  and she is on a number of national and international technical advisory groups.   Her research regularly gets covered in the mainstream New Zealand press.
