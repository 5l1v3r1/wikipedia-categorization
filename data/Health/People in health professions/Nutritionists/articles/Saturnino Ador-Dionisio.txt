Saturnino B. Ador-Dionisio MD (November 11, 1910 San Isidro, Nueva Ecija, Philippines – May 16, 2004) was a Filipino nutritionist who formulated the Ador-Dionisio Rule on 1965 in finding the ideal body weight of the person . He is a graduate of the University of the Philippines College of Medicine (Class of 1935) and is one of the founding fathers of its first medical fraternity, the Mu Sigma Phi. He served as Mu's first Most Exalted Brother from 1933 to 1934. He died at the age of 93 due to cardiac arrest. Mu Sigma Phi1933–1934
