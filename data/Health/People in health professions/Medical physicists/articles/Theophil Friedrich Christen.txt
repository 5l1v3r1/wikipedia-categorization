Theophil Friedrich Christen (1 April 1873 in Basel – 6 May 1920 in Genfersee) was a doctor, mathematician, physicist, economist and pioneer of physical medicine, in particular of X-ray radiation. Zeitschrift für medizinische Physik wrote:  In his honor, the Swiss Society for Radiation Biology and Medical Physics established the Theophil-Christen Medal in 2006,  first awarded to Jean-François Valley.
