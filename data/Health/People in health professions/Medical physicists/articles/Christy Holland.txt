Christy K. Holland is an American scientist and professor of internal medicine and biomedical engineering at the University of Cincinnati. After a B.A. with majors in physics and music at Wellesley College, she obtained her Ph.D.in engineering and applied science from Yale University.  Holland is editor-in-chief of Ultrasound in Medicine and Biology.  Holland's articles in peer-reviewed scientific journals have been cited over 5000 times, giving her an h-index of 41.  Holland is a fellow of the Acoustical Society of America,  the American Institute of Ultrasound in Medicine,  and the American Institute for Medical and Biological Engineering.
