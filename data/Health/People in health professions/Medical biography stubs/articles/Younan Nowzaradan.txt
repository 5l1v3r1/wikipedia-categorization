 Younan Nowzaradan (born 1944/1945 ), also known as Dr. Now, is an Iranian American general and vascular surgeon, known for appearing on the TLC reality television series My 600-lb Life (2012–present).   He has also appeared on Half Ton Teen and World’s Heaviest Man Part II.  Nowzaradan was born in Iran.  In 1970, he graduated from the University of Tehran with a Doctor of Medicine.
