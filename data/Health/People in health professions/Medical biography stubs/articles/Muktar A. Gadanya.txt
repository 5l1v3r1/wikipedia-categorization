Dr. Muktar A. Gadanya  is the youngest recipient of National Honour in Nigeria (Member of the Order of the Federal Republic, MFR) in 2007.   He has initiated a campaign to harness the goodwill of the Nigerian youth for a successful Umaru Musa Yar'Adua presidency.  He is recipient of several other prizes,  and is currently a Consultant Public Health Physician with the Department of Public Health, Aminu Kano Teaching Hospital, Nigeria.  He was among the people recommended for meeting with Stephen Hawking, who was in quest for an "African Einstein".
