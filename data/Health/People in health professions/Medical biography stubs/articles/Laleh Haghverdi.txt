Laleh Haghverdi is an Iranian-born physicist and winner of the 2017 Erwin Schrödinger Prize.  Haghverdi and her colleagues Fabian Theis (mathematicain), Timm Shröder (‌biologist) and Carsten Marr (physicist) won the Erwin Schrödinger Prize for the design and development of new solutions and algorithms in the study of stem cells mechanisms in the process of cell differentiation dynamics.  She also received the Helmholtz Association Doktorandenpreis for her Ph.D. thesis "Geometric Diffusions for Reconstruction of Cell Differentiation Dynamics".
