  Dr. Steven Jon Hambleton MBBS FAMA (born 14 January 1961) is a Brisbane, Australia GPand former federal president of the Australian Medical Association.  Steve Hambleton attended St Joseph's College, Gregory Terrace  from 1969 to 1978. Hambleton studied a Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery at the University of Queensland from 1979 to 1984.  Steve Hambleton served as president of the Australian Medical Association for three years from his unopposed election in June 2011  to May 2014.   Hambleton is the Foundation President of the AMAQ Foundation,  the charity arm of the AMAQ.
