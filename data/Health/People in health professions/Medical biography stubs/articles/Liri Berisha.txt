Liri Berisha, née Ramaj, is an Albanian pediatrician and president of the Albanian Children Foundation, and also an UNICEF ambassador. She is the wife of Sali Berisha, the former President and Prime Minister of Albania, with whom she has two children, daughter Argita, and son Shkëlzen. In 2008, Berisha founded the Mother Teresa Cultural Foundation.  In 2009, the Women's Information Network named Berisha "Woman of the Year".  Berisha joined Autism Speaks in 2011 in celebrating the opening of a new centre for autistic children in Albania, calling the centre a way to improve the lives of children with autism there.
