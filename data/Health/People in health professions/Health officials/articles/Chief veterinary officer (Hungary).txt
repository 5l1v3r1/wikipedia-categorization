The Chief Veterinary Officer of Hungary, as a chief veterinary officer, is the supervisor of the National Food Chain Safety Office, and in general, the governmental veterinary services including animal health, animal protection and welfare and food chain safety control. Besides organizing and operating veterinary public health, he plays a significant role in international agricultural and trade affairs, in which the animal health and food safety status usually an important issue.
