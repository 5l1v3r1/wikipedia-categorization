The Montreal Oral School for the Deaf (MOSD) is a provincial school in Westmount, Quebec, Canada with programs serving deaf and hard-of-hearing students. The school was founded in 1950.  The school has a preschool day program, closed classrooms for elementary school children, and itinerant support for students of all ages mainstreamed into regular hearing classrooms. The mission of the Montreal Oral School for the Deaf (MOSD) is to provide habilitation and education for children with hearing loss from infancy through to early adulthood.
