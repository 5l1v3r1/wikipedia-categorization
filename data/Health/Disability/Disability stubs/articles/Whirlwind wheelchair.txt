The Whirlwind wheelchair is a wheelchair designed to be made in developing countries using local resources, in a sustainable development effort. It was co-designed by Ralf Hotchkiss of Whirlwind Wheelchair International. Hotchkiss, a paraplegic, has traveled extensively, designing wheelchairs that could be built in developing countries. Whirlwind Wheelchair International uses the principle of open source design,  and offers construction classes and consulting services.
