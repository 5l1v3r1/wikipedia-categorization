The Danish Association of Pharmaconomists (Danish: Farmakonomforeningen) is the trade union that represents pharmaconomists (experts in pharmaceuticals) and pharmaconomist students in Denmark (including Greenland and Faroe Islands). The Danish Association of Pharmaconomists has about 5,350 members (i.e. 98% of all Danish pharmaconomists). Christina Durinck has been president of the trade union has since 2014. Sanne Hee Johansen has been vice president since November 2016. The Danish Association of Pharmaconomists cooperates with Pharmakon—Danish College of Pharmacy Practice and the Association of Danish Pharmacies and is affiated with the FTF – Confederation of Professionals in Denmark. The association publishes the magazine The Pharmaconomist 10 times a year.
