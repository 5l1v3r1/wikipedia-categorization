Jon Magnussen (born 12 August 1959) is a Norwegian Professor in health economics and Head of Department of Public Health and General Practice at Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) in Trondheim. Organization and financing of health care services and productivity and efficiency in the health care sector is some of his research topics.
