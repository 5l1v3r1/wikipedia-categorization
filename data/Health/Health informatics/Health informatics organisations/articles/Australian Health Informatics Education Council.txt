The Australian Health Informatics Education Council is the peak forum for the coordination of Health Informatics education in Australia. The Council includes the major stakeholders in health informatics education in Australia, including the senior e-health organisations,   universities,  etc.  AHIEC is auspiced by the major Australian health informatics  organisations.  AHIEC was founded in January 2009 and is undertaking a number of baseline projects  based on the AHIEC Work Plan '2009-10 and Beyond'.  The Council functions are: ACHI supports AHIEC through provision of a secretariat and organising of working meetings.
