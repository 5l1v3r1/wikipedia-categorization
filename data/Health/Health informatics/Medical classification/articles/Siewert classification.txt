The Siewert-Stein classification is a system of anatomical classification used for adenocarcinomas of the esophago-gastric junction. Adenocarcinoma of the distal part of the esophagus. The tumor center is located 1–5 cm above the gastric cardia. Adenocarcinoma of the real cardia. The tumor center is located 1 cm above or 2 cm below the gastric cardia. Adenocarcinoma of the subcardial stomach. The tumor center is located 2–5 cm below the gastric cardia.
