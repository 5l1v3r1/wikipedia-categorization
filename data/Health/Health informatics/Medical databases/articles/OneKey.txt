OneKey is a worldwide healthcare professionals database developed by Cegedim Relationship Management. Cegedim was acquired by IMS Health in April 2015.  The OneKey database is available in 73 countries and contains more than 13.7 million Healthcare Professionals  OneKey is updated by phone operators who validate the database, adhering to ISO 9001 procedures.  OneKey is built according to the same unique data model in all countries.
