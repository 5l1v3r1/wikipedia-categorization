 The ISO/TC 215 is the International Organization for Standardization's (ISO) Technical Committee (TC) on health informatics. TC 215 works on the standardization of Health Information and Communications Technology (ICT), to allow for compatibility and interoperability between independent systems. ISO TC 215 consists of several Working Groups (WG), each dealing with an aspect of Electronic Health Records (EHR).
