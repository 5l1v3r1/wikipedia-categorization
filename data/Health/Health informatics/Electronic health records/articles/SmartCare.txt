The SmartCare electronic health record system (EHR) has been developed and deployed by the Zambia Ministry of Health (MoH) in collaboration with the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) and many other implementing partners. SmartCare is: SmartCare is also the name of a child care management software solution available in the United States. To enable the delivery of cost-effective, confidential, quality health care for everyone, everywhere, every time, by improving health records and related health information systems. In addition to Zambia, SmartCare is also being used in Ethiopia and South Africa.  However, as of  2012,[update] it was only available for use by partner organizations.
