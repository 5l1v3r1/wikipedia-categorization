Doctify is a company based in London - a website and app launched in 2015 which enables patients to search, compare and book private doctors and health professionals in the United Kingdom.  It features reviews written by patients and recommendations by other health professionals.  https://www.doctify.co.uk/ In December 2016 it secured substantial series A funding from Amadeus Capital Partners.  Clinicians can use the software to schedule their appointments.    It covers covers 47 different specialties and is accessible on web or mobile devices. It focuses on around 30,000 consultant doctors who operate within the private sector.   90% of them also work in the NHS.  The company has two founders -  two orthopaedic surgeons named Stephanie Eltz and Suman Saha. The Doctify office is in Camden, with a staff of 20+.
