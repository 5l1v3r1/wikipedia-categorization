The Journal of Innovation in Health Informatics is a quarterly peer-reviewed open-access medical journal covering health informatics. It was established in 1992 and is published by the British Computer Society. The editor-in-chief is Simon de Lusignan (University of Surrey). The journal was established in 1992 as Informatics in Primary Care,  obtaining its current name in 2015.  The journal is abstracted and indexed in: Index Medicus/MEDLINE/PubMed,  Scopus,  and EBSCOhost.
