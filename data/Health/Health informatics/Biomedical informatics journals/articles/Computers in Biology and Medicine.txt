Computers in Biology and Medicine is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal established in 1970. It covers the intersection of biomedical engineering, computational biology, bioinformatics, and computers. Articles are published both in print and online. The journal accepts original research articles, reviews, tutorials, editorials and letters. The Impact Factor is 2.115 (2017). The 5 year Impact Factor is 2.168 (2017). Based on Eigenfactor score (includes quality of citing journals) and Immediacy Index (considers first year citations of published papers), the journal is ranked 23rd of 78 bioengineering journals in 2017 (29th percentile).
