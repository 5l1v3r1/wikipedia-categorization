Source Code for Biology and Medicine is a peer-reviewed open-access scientific journal in the field of bioinformatics, including information systems and data mining. The journal is published by BioMed Central and was established in 2006. The editors-in-chief are Emmanuel Ifeachor (University of Plymouth) and Leif E. Peterson (The Methodist Hospital Research Institute). The journal is abstracted and indexed in Chemical Abstracts Service,  EmBiology, and Scopus.
