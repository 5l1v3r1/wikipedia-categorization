Oil Mines Regulations-1984   (OMR 1984) replaces the Oil Mines Regulations-1933, with effect from October-1984 to deal with matters for the prevention of possible dangers in oil mines in India. OMR 1984 was Published in 1986 by Directorate General of Mines Safety, Ministry of Labour in Dhanbad, Jharkhand. Short Title; Extent; Application and; Definitions.
