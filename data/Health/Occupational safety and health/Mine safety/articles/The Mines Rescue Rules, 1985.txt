The Mines Rescue Rules, 1985  came into force with effect from 2nd April-1985 in India, replacing the previous Coal Mines Rescue Rules-1959, to provide for rescue of work persons in the event of explosion, fire etc. in the Mines.   These rules apply to coal and metalliferous underground mines to provide for the establishment of rescue stations and conduct of rescue work.  In case of  explosion or fire, an inrush of water or influx of gases, services of specially trained men with special rescue apparatuses are required.
