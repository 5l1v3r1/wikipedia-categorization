  This is a list of disasters and tragic events in modern Australia sorted by death toll. The Centaur memorial, in Point Danger, Coolangatta, Queensland, commemorates the sinking of the hospital ship AHS Centaur in 1943, which claimed 268 lives. At least 243 people died in the Japanese air raids on Darwin in 1942.
