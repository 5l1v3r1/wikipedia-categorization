Smallworld is the brand name of a portfolio of GIS software  provided by GE Energy Connections, a division of General Electric. The software was originally created by the Smallworld company founded in Cambridge, England, in 1989 by Dick Newell and others. Smallworld grew to become the global market leader for GIS in utilities and communications and remains in this position today.  Smallworld was acquired by GE Energy in September 2000.   Smallworld technology supports application products for telecommunications, utilities, and public systems organizations.
