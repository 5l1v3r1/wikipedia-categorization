An engraver in wood and stone,  Quad collaborated with the Cologine publisher Johann Bussemacher to publish a quarto atlas of Europe in 1592.  This was expanded into a Geographisches Handtbuch (1599), with more text than maps, and then into a proper atlas, Fasciculus Geographicus (1608).
