Reginald Akindele Cline-Cole (born Freetown, Sierra Leone), is a University lecturer and scholar of Developmental Geography.  Cline-Cole has done extensive work in the field of development issues in rural environments, as well as forestry and its human impact. Now the head of the Centre of West African Studies, University of Birmingham (UK). He has previously taught at Bayero University, Nigeria and Moi University, Kenya.
