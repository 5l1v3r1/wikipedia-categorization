FACSAT-1 is scheduled to be the first Colombian-manufactured  satellite sent into orbit, on behalf of the Colombian Air Force.  FACSAT-1 is a nanosatellite, capable of taking images with a resolution of 30 meters per pixel.  The contract to manufacture FACSAT-1 was awarded to GomSpace A/S in 2014.  FACSAT-1 is scheduled to be launched in July 2018, likely from the Satish Dhawan Space Centre in India.
