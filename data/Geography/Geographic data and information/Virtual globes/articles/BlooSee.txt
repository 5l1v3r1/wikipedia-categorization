BlooSee was a web- and mobile-based social mapping platform for ocean recreation and conservation. The platform was designed to host a combination of crowdsourced and institutional media, forecasts, charts, and location-based points of interest, safety, navigation, conservation, and cruising routes. BlooSee is owned by BlooSee, Inc. a Silicon Valley startup company founded in 2010 by Internet entrepreneurs Pedro Valdeolmillos (originator of the idea) and Marc Puig. It closed in 2015.
