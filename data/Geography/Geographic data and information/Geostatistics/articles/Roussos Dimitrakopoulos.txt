Roussos Dimitrakopoulos is a Canadian Professor with the McGill University. He was selected to receive the William Christian Krumbein Medal in 2018 from the International Association for Mathematical Geosciences.  He was also the recipient of Georges Matheron Lectureship Award 2015 from the International Association for Mathematical Geosciences. Since 2007 Roussos has been the Editor-in-Chief of Mathematical Geosciences.
