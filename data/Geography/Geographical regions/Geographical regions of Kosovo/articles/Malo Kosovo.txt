Malo Kosovo (Serbian Cyrillic: Мало Косово, "Little Kosovo") or Llap (Albanian: Rrafshi i Llapit, "Plain of Llap") is a small basin in northeastern Kosovo. Malo Kosovo is about 16 kilometers long, and five kilometers wide. It is bordered by the mountain ranges of Kopaonik in the north and west, and Goljak in the east. Its main river is the Lab River.
