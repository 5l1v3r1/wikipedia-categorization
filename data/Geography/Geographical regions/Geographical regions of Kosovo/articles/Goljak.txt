Goljak (Albanian: Gollak, Serbian: Гољак) is a mountainous region in the eastern part of Kosovo,[a], bordering the Lab region to the west. The cities of Pristina and Gjilan in Kosovo, and Sijarinska Banja in Serbia are located by the mountains. The highest peak Velja glava has an elevation of 1,181 m (3,875 ft) above sea level.
