The Pratomagno is a mountain range which has the Arno River on both sides: to the west is the upper Valdarno and to the east is the Casentino. It lies north-west of the city of Arezzo, in Tuscany, Italy. Its highest peak has an elevation of 1,592 m.  Coordinates: 43°39′N 11°39′E﻿ / ﻿43.650°N 11.650°E﻿ / 43.650; 11.650
