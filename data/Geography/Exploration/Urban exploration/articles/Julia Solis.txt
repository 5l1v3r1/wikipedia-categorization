Julia Solis is a writer and photographer who investigates ruined urban spaces. She is the founder of two arts organizations: Dark Passage and Ars Subterranea, both of which are dedicated to exploring and exposing New York City ruins and underground spaces.  Solis wrote the book New York Underground and her photography book Stages of Decay (Prestel, 2013) shows abandoned American and European theaters.   She is the executive producer of the film American Ruins.  She received a fellowship from the New York Foundation for the Arts. She is also an officer of the Madagascar Institute and a board member of Place in History.
