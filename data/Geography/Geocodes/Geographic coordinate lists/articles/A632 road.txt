  The A632 is a major road in Derbyshire and Nottinghamshire, England. It starts in Matlock (53°08′05″N 1°32′55″W﻿ / ﻿53.1347°N 1.5485°W﻿ / 53.1347; -1.5485﻿ (A632 road (western end))) and joins the town with Chesterfield. From there, it goes through Bolsover and then onto the A616 at the village of Cuckney (53°14′01″N 1°09′15″W﻿ / ﻿53.2336°N 1.1543°W﻿ / 53.2336; -1.1543﻿ (A632 road (eastern end))).
