  The A801 is a road in Scotland which runs from east of Polmont to the A705 near Whitburn that heads towards Livingston in the other direction. Coordinates: 55°55′47″N 3°40′44″W﻿ / ﻿55.9298°N 3.6789°W﻿ / 55.9298; -3.6789﻿ (A801 road) The A801 provides a link from the M8 to the M9, creating easy access from travellers from Falkirk or Stirling to places in West Lothian like Livingston.
