The 56th parallel south is a circle of latitude that is 56 degrees south of the Earth's equatorial plane.  No land lies on the parallel — it crosses nothing but ocean. At this latitude the sun is visible for 17 hours, 37 minutes during the December solstice and 6 hours, 57 minutes during the June solstice.
