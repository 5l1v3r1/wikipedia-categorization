The 59th parallel south is a circle of latitude that is 59 degrees south of the Earth's equatorial plane.  The only landmass on this parallel is Bristol Island.[a] At this latitude the sun is visible for 18 hours, 30 minutes during the December solstice and 6 hours, 10 minutes during the June solstice.
