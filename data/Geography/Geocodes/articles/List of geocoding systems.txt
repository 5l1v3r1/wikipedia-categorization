This is a list of geocoding systems, in the sense of schemes that assign systematic labels to geographic entities. This is not a list of software systems that can perform geocoding in the sense of turning a geographic name into a latitude and longitude. Some of these code systems are free for use, others have different licences. Codes with only partial coverture of the globe, mainlly for "administrative regions", like countries or biomes.
