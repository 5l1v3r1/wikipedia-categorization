ISO 3166-2:MM is the entry for Myanmar in ISO 3166-2, part of the ISO 3166 standard published by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO), which defines codes for the names of the principal subdivisions (e.g., provinces or states) of all countries coded in ISO 3166-1. Currently for Myanmar, ISO 3166-2 codes are defined for 7 regions, 7 states, and 1 union territory. Each code consists of two parts, separated by a hyphen. The first part is MM, the ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 code of Myanmar.
