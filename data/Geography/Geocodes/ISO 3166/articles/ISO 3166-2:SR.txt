ISO 3166-2:SR is the entry for Suriname in ISO 3166-2, part of the ISO 3166 standard published by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO), which defines codes for the names of the principal subdivisions (e.g., provinces or states) of all countries coded in ISO 3166-1. Currently for Suriname, ISO 3166-2 codes are defined for 10 districts. Each code consists of two parts, separated by a hyphen. The first part is SR, the ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 code of Suriname. The second part is two letters. Subdivision names are listed as in the ISO 3166-2 standard published by the ISO 3166 Maintenance Agency (ISO 3166/MA).
