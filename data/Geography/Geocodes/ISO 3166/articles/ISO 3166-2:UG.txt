ISO 3166-2:UG is the entry for Uganda in ISO 3166-2, part of the ISO 3166 standard published by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO), which defines codes for the names of the principal subdivisions (e.g., provinces or states) of all countries coded in ISO 3166-1. Currently for Uganda, ISO 3166-2 codes are defined for 4 geographical regions, 121 districts, and 1 city. As of 2017, Uganda is divided into 121 districts and 1 city council (the capital of the country Kampala). Each code consists of two parts, separated by a hyphen. The first part is UG, the ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 code of Uganda.
