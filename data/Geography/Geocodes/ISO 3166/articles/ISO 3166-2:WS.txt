ISO 3166-2:WS is the entry for Samoa in ISO 3166-2, part of the ISO 3166 standard published by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO), which defines codes for the names of the principal subdivisions (e.g., provinces or states) of all countries coded in ISO 3166-1. Currently for Samoa, ISO 3166-2 codes are defined for 11 districts. Each code consists of two parts, separated by a hyphen. The first part is WS, the ISO 3166-1 alpha-2 code of Samoa. The second part is two letters. Subdivision names are listed as in the ISO 3166-2 standard published by the ISO 3166 Puta ka Agency (ISO 3166/MA).
