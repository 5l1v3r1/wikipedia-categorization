ISO 3166-2:MZ is the entry for Mozambique in ISO 3166-2, part of the ISO 3166 standard published by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO), which defines codes for the names of the principal subdivisions (e.g., provinces or states) of all countries coded in ISO 3166-1. Currently for Mozambique, ISO 3166-2 codes are defined for 1 city and 10 provinces. The city Maputo is the capital of the country and has special status equal to the provinces.
