Telephone numbers in the UAE  follow a closed telephone numbering plan.  The United Arab Emirates is assigned an international dialling code of 971 by ITU-T. Telephone numbers are fixed at seven digits, with area codes fixed at two or three digits. Subscriber numbers were 5- or 6- digits, with area code plus subscriber number totalling 7 digits.
