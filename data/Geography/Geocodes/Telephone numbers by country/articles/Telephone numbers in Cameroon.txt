On November 21st 2014, numbers were expanded from 8 to 9 digits, by prefixing with 2 or 6.   The last expansion occurred in 2007, when 7-digit numbers were expanded to 8 digits.  (See last the section.) For calls within Cameroon, use just the 9 digits : xxxxxxxxx For calls from outside the country, add the code for Cameroun : +237 xxxxxxxxx
