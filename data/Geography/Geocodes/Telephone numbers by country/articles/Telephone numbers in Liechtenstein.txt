 Country Code: +423 (formerly +41 75) International Call Prefix: 00 Trunk Prefix: None Liechtenstein previously used the Swiss telephone numbering plan (+41) under area code 075 (formerly dialed as +41 75 from outside Switzerland and Liechtenstein), but in April 1999, it adopted its own international code +423, meaning that calls to and from Switzerland now require international dialing. It is dialed internationally as +423 xxx xx xx.
