Telephone numbers in Mauritius follow a closed numbering plan and are regulated by the Information and Communication Technologies Authority. Mauritian phone numbers are seven (landline) to eight digits (mobile) long. Landline numbers begin with 2 (north), 4 (central), or 6 (south). Mobile numbers begin with the digit 5.
