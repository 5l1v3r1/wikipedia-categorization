  Charlestown-Bellahy (Irish: Baile Chathail-Béal Lathaigh) is a census town split between County Mayo and County Sligo in Ireland. It comprises the adjoining villages of Charlestown, County Mayo, and  Bellahy, County Sligo, had a population of 753 at the 2002 census.
