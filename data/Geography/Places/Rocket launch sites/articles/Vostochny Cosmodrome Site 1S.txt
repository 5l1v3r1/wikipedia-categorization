Site 1S is a launch complex at the Vostochny Cosmodrome in Russia. It consists of a single pad for use by the Soyuz-2 rockets.  On April 28, 2016 the first launch from the Vostochny Cosmodrome took place from this pad.  The third launch took place on 1 February 2018.
