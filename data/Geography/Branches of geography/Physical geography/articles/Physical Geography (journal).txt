Physical Geography is a bimonthly peer-reviewed scientific journal covering all aspects of physical geography. It was established in 1980 and is published by Taylor & Francis. It was originally published by Bellwether Publishing until the start of the 34th volume in 2013, when it moved to Taylor & Francis.  The editor-in-chief is Carol Harden (University of Tennessee). According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2017 impact factor of 1.086.
