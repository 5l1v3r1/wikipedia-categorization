Temporal Analyst is an extension of ArcMap (ESRI) for storage, management, processing, plotting and analysis of virtually any time-related data inside ArcGISTM application and was developed by DHI. Temporal Analyst tool for GIS brings time series data management directly into ArcGISTM and provides fully dynamic data handling, modeling and monitoring. Temporal Analyst can be used for storage, management and n of temporal data in a spatial context, multi-disciplinary data analyses and processing (hydrology, ecology, health, etc.), and maps and reports of time series statistics and properties.
