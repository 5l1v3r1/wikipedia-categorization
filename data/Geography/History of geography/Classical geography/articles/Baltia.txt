Baltia, Basilia or Abalus is an island in northern Europe mentioned in Greco-Roman geography in the connection of amber. It presumably corresponds to a territory near either the Baltic Sea or the North Sea, perhaps the coast of Prussia, the island of Gotland, Sweden,  or of the Jutland Peninsula.
