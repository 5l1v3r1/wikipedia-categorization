Stockholm Partnerships for Sustainable Cities is an organization which gathers and disseminates information and technology developed through [[Sustainable urbanism |sustainability]] projects in several countries. The organization attempts to help representatives of national and local governments, industry, non-governmental organizations, academia, media, finance institutions, and international organizations to share their knowledge and experience in applied technology and management.  The group focuses on the respective roles of industry, local government, and communities in sustainable urban development, and how these roles evolve and influence each other.
