Environment and Behavior is a bimonthly peer-reviewed academic journal covering the fields of environmental psychology and environmental studies. The editor-in-chief is Ann Sloan Devlin (Connecticut College). It was established in 1969 and is published by Sage Publications. The journal is abstracted and indexed in Scopus, PsychINFO and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2017 impact factor is 3.549.
