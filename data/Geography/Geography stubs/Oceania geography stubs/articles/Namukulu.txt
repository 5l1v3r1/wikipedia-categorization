Namukulu is one of the fourteen villages of Niue.  With a population of 14 (2001 and 2011 census), it is one of the smallest settlements of the island. The village is located about 5 kilometres from Alofi, and lies between the villages of Tuapa and Hikutavake. There is a single resort, Namukulu Cottages & Spa. The village contains one of the most popular tourist destinations in all of Niue, Limu Pools
