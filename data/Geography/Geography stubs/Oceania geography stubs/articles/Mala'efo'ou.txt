Mala'efo'ou, formerly Mua, is a village in Wallis and Futuna. It is located in Mua District on the southeast coast of Wallis Island.  Mala'efo'ou is the main town in the district, located about 8 km south of Mata-Utu. Its population according to the 2008 census was 224 people. Except a few industrial buildings the village is mostly residential.
