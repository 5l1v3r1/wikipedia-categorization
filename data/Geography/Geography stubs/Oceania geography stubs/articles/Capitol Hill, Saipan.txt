Capitol Hill    (sometimes spelled Capital Hill,  formerly Army Hill under the United States Navy, ) is a settlement (sometimes termed a village or district) on the island of Saipan in the Northern Mariana Islands. It has a population of just over 1,000.  Capitol Hill has been the territory's seat of government since 1962. It lies on the cross-island road between Tanapag and San Vicente. Capitol Hill was originally built in 1948 by the Central Intelligence Agency as a base involved in covert training of Nationalist Chinese guerrillas.  The area is home to various government departments and agencies: Route 31 (Cross Island Road) or Isa Drive is the main road in the area.
