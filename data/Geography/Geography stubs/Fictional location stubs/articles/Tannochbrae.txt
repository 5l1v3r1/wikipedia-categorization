Tannochbrae is a fictional village in Scotland first introduced in the Dr. Finley short stories of A. J. Cronin as a "snug little village on the lochside", and later used in his autobiographical Adventures in Two Worlds.  It is best known as the setting of the television and radio series based Cronin's Dr. Finley stories.   The filming of the original BBC series, Dr Finlay's Casebook, took place in Callander.  The second ITV series, Doctor Finlay, was filmed in Auchtermuchty.
