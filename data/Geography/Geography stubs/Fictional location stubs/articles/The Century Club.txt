The Century Club is a fictional gentleman's club in Manhattan for wealthy executives set in the Marvel Comics Universe, specifically in the Spider-Man titles. The club was first shown in Amazing Spider-Man #23 however was not referred to as The Century Club until Peter Parker, the Spectacular Spider-Man #80. The Club played a major role in the Hobgoblin mystery storyline that ran through the Spider-Man titles in the 1980s.
