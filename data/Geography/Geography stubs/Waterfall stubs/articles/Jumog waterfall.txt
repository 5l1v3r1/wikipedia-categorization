Jumog Waterfall, at Karanganyar Regency, is one of the sites included in the Indonesian tourism program, "INTANPARI" (Industri Pertanian Pariwisata). Grojogan Sewu is located at Mount Lawu (2632 meter), 27 kilometres from Karanganyar Regency. It has a height of approximately 25m which splits into two so-called "Twin Falls".
