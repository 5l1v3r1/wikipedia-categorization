  The Dip Falls, a cascade waterfall over cubic-basalt formed rocks on the Dip River, is located in the North West region of Tasmania, Australia.  The waterfall is situated in the Dip Range Regional Reserve at an elevation of 218 metres (715 ft) above sea level and descends in the range of 22–34 metres (72–112 ft),  near the village of Sisters Beach  and approximately 36 kilometres (22 mi) southeast of Stanley via the Bass Highway.
