The Joranda Falls (ଯୋରନ୍ଦା ପ୍ରପାତ୍) is located in the core area of Simlipal National Park in Mayurbhanj district in the Indian state of Odisha. The Barehipani Falls is located close to the Joranda Falls.  It is the 19th highest waterfall in India.  The Joranda Falls has a total height of 150 metres (490 ft). The water plunges over a lofty cliff in a single drop, spreading out slightly as it falls.
