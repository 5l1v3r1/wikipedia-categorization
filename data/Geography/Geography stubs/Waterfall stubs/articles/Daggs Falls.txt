  The Daggs Falls is a plunge waterfall on Spring Creek that is located in the Darling Downs region of Queensland, Australia.  The falls are located east of the town of Killarney and descend from the McPherson Range, north of the Queensland/New South Wales border.  The falls are situated directly on the roadside and there is a lookout provided.   Four other waterfalls are located in the area surrounding Killarney, including the Queen Mary Falls, Teviot Falls, Browns Falls and Upper Browns Falls.
