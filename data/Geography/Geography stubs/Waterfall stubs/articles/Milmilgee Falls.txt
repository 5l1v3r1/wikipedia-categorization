  The Milmilgee Falls, a waterfall on the Freshwater Creek, is located in the Far North region of Queensland, Australia. From the plateau of the Atherton Tableland at an elevation of 377 metres (1,237 ft) above sea level, the falls descend between 29–53 metres (95–174 ft) and are situated below the Copperlode Falls Dam; approximately 9 kilometres (5.6 mi) west of Cairns.
