  The Ellinjaa Falls is a ledge waterfall that is located on Ellinjaa Creek, on the Atherton Tableland in the North region of Queensland, Australia.  The falls are located on Ellinjaa Creek, and are approximately seven kilometers (ten minute drive) from Millaa Millaa. Access to the base of the falls is via a walking track leading from the picnic area and carpark on Theresa Creek Road.
