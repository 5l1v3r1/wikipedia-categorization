The Badaghagara Waterfall is a waterfall of the Kendujhar district in the Indian state of Odisha. Badaghagara is located at a distance of 9 km from the district headquarters (Kendujhar) of Kendujhar district. Being a perennial source of water, a dam has constructed on the downstream side. It is situated at a distance of 3 kilometers on the downstream of Sanaghagara Waterfall. It is a perennial waterfall. The Machha Kandana, a small river, plunges from a height of 60 metres (200 ft) in a single drop.
