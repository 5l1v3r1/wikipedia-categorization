Aintharuvi is a group of five waterfalls, that is nearby the famous waterfalls of Tamil Nadu, India, the Coutrallam Falls. It is located in Tenkasi. The English meaning of the name is Five falls.The waterfalls is compared with Adisesha, the holy snake since the waterfalls cascades in five directions resembling a 5-headed Cobra.  Two temples dedicated to Swami Ayyappa and Lord Vinayaka are situated nearby.
