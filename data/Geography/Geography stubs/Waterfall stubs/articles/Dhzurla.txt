Dhzurla (Ukrainian: Джурла) waterfall is located on the Dhzurla river in the Crimean Mountains of Crimea.  Waterfall height is about 6 metres (20 ft). Waterfall has a few cascades. The word “Dhzurla” from Crimean Tatar language which means “her, who runs."[unreliable source?] Downstream river is called Soter.
