The Kamianka or the Kamianetskyi waterfall (Ukrainian: Кам'янецький водоспад) is located on the Kamianka river in the National Park Dubyna, Skole Raion, Lviv Oblast of western Ukraine The waterfall's height is 7 metres (23 ft).
