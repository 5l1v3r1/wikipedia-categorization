 Meenmutty Falls is located 29 km from Kalpetta in Wayanad District in the state of Kerala, India.   It is a three-tiered waterfall with a height of 300 metres.  Meenmutty is a combination of Malayalam words Meen (fish) and Mutty (blocked). Meenmutty Falls can be reached thorough a 2 km hike though the jungle from the Wayanad-Ooty road.
