Kozhuun (Tuvan: кожуун) is the Tuvan term of an historical feudal administrative division known as a banner.   The term kozhuun is used today as a unit in the system of the administrative divisions of the Tuva Republic of Russia.   A typical federal subject of Russia is subdivided into raions. Kozhuun is an ethnic name for a raion, used only in Tuva. Tuva historically had nine kozhuun.  Each kozhuun was divided into sumus which was then subdivided into arbans. List of historic kozhunn
