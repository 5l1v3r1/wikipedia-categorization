Palustrine wetlands include any inland wetland that lacks flowing water, contains ocean-derived salts in concentrations of less than 0.5 parts per thousand, and is non-tidal.   The word palustrine comes from the Latin word palus or marsh.  Wetlands within this category include inland marshes and swamps as well as bogs, fens, tundra and floodplains.
