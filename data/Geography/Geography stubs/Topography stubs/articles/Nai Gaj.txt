Nai Gaj (Sindhi: نئي گاج‎, Urdu: نئی گاج‎) is mighty hill torrent in Dadu District, Sindh, Pakistan. Government of Pakistan has constructed Nai Gaj Dam  on its stream. It flows from Balochistan, Pakistan towards Sindh Province of Pakistan and drains out in Lake Manchar flowing through arid zone of Kachho, Dadu District.
