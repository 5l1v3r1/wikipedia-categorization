Coordinates: 51°31′05″N 0°02′40″W﻿ / ﻿51.517972°N 0.044360°W﻿ / 51.517972; -0.044360  The Baptist Academical Institution chapel at Stepney is a largely-destroyed place of worship that was part of an estate built for the Particular Baptists in 1810.  Whilst relocation to larger premises at Holford House took place in 1856,  the building remained until it was damaged during World War 2; only three parts of the estate remain.  The site is currently part of the Stepney City Farm.  Crossrail works are taking place there.
