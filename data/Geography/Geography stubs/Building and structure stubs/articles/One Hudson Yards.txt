One Hudson Yards is a residential skyscraper at 530 West 30th Street  in the Chelsea neighborhood of Manhattan.  Related Companies developed the building, and it is adjacent the much larger Hudson Yards project. One Hudson Yards contains 178 apartments.  The building is clad in brick, reminiscent of the industrial buildings that once occupied the site.   The name "One Hudson Yards" originally referred to 55 Hudson Yards.
