Priority traffic signs indicate the order in which vehicles should pass intersection points. Common examples are the Give Way sign. The priority sign in Belgrade is obsolete from 1993. Stop Stop Give Way Priority road End of priority road Sign indicating the route of priority road (thick line) at an intersection and defining the priority Give way on oncoming traffic Priority over oncoming traffic Dangerous intersection with priority indication (for the next intersection only). Different variants of the sign can be used on both priority- and non-priority roads. Each sign has the thicker line indicating the road or direction that has priority with the viewer's own direction being from the bottom of the sign.
