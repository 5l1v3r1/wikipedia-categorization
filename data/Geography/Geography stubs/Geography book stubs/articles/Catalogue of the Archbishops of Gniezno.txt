Catalogue of the Archbishops of Gniezno is an illuminated manuscript by Jan Długosz. It was illustrated by Stanisław Samostrzelnik. The manuscript page size is 31 cm × 24 cm (12.2 in × 9.4 in). It has 145 pages. It is in the collection of the National Library of Poland. The manuscrupt was commissioned by Cracow Bishop Piotr Tomicki. It was produced between 1531 and 1535.
