Coordinates: 37°43′52.08″N 122°12′24.18″W﻿ / ﻿37.7311333°N 122.2067167°W﻿ / 37.7311333; -122.2067167 Martin Luther King Jr. Shoreline is a regional park located on the shores of the San Leandro Bay in Oakland, California. Part of the East Bay Regional Parks system, it is named after Civil Rights Movement leader Martin Luther King Jr. The park was established in 1993 on a 741 acres (300 ha) tract of land leased from the Oakland Airport.
