Battle of Sakarya National Historic Park (Turkish: Sakarya Meydan Muharebesi Tarihi Milli Parkı), established on February 8, 2015, is a protected area of national historic significance associated with the Turkish War of Independence (Turkish: İstiklâl Savaşı) comprising the battleground of the Battle of Sakarya (Turkish: Sakarya Meydan Muharebesi) in the Greco-Turkish War (1919–22). It is located in the Polatlı and Haymana districts of Ankara Province.
