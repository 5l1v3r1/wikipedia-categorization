Katannilik Territorial Park Reserve[pronunciation?] ("the place of waterfalls") is a territorial park in the Qikiqtaaluk Region of Nunavut, Canada. It was established in 1993 and has an area of 1,262 km2 (487 sq mi). The Soper River, part of the Canadian Heritage Rivers System, flows through the park.
