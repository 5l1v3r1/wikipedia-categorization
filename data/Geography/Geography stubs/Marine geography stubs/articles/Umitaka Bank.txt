Submerged bank Umitaka Bank (67°25′S 167°0′E﻿ / ﻿67.417°S 167.000°E﻿ / -67.417; 167.000) is a submarine bank in the Balleny Islands area. It was named after the research vessel Umitaku-Maru of the Tokyo University of Fisheries, which took soundings in the area (1964–65). Name approved 4/80 (ACUF 201).  This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Umitaka Bank" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
