Submerged bank Pennell Bank (74°30′S 179°59′E﻿ / ﻿74.500°S 179.983°E﻿ / -74.500; 179.983) is a northeast trending submarine bank on the continental shelf in the Ross Sea. Name approved 2/64 (ACUF 201).  This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Pennell Bank" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
