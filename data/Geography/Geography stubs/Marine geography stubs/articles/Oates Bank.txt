Submerged bank Oates Bank (70°15′S 165°0′E﻿ / ﻿70.250°S 165.000°E﻿ / -70.250; 165.000) is a submarine bank off the coast of Oates Land. The name, in association with Oates Coast, was proposed by Dr. Steven C. Cande, Scripps Institution of Oceanography. Name approved 9/97 (ACUF 272).  This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Oates Bank" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
