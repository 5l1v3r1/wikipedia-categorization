Submerged bank Mawson Bank (73°30′S 174°0′E﻿ / ﻿73.500°S 174.000°E﻿ / -73.500; 174.000) is a submarine bank in the Ross Sea off Antarctica named for British Antarctic scientist Sir Douglas Mawson. The name was approved by the Advisory Committee for Undersea Features in June 1988.   This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Mawson Bank" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
