Submerged bank Iselin Bank (72°30′S 179°0′W﻿ / ﻿72.500°S 179.000°W﻿ / -72.500; -179.000) is a submarine bank in the Ross Sea off Antarctica. The name was approved by the Advisory Committee for Undersea Features in April 1980.   This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Iselin Bank" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
