Ajax Reef is a coral reef located within the Florida Keys National Marine Sanctuary and also within Biscayne National Park.  Unlike many reefs within the National Marine Sanctuary, this reef is not within a Sanctuary Preservation Area (SPA).  It is north of Pacific Reef.
