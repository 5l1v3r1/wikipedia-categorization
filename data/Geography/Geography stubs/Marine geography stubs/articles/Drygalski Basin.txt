Drygalski Basin (74°50′S 166°30′E﻿ / ﻿74.833°S 166.500°E﻿ / -74.833; 166.500Coordinates: 74°50′S 166°30′E﻿ / ﻿74.833°S 166.500°E﻿ / -74.833; 166.500) is an undersea basin named as such by the Advisory Committee for Undersea Features (ACUF) in April 1980, in association with Drygalski Ice Tongue. The name was changed to "Von Drygalski" in November 1995, in agreement with the General Bathymetric Chart of the Oceans Sub-Committee on Undersea Feature Names, but was changed back to Drygalski Basin by ACUF in June 2003.   This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Drygalski Basin" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
