Oates Canyon (68°0′S 164°30′E﻿ / ﻿68.000°S 164.500°E﻿ / -68.000; 164.500Coordinates: 68°0′S 164°30′E﻿ / ﻿68.000°S 164.500°E﻿ / -68.000; 164.500) is an undersea canyon on the continental rise east of Iselin Bank in Antarctica. Its name (approved June 1988 (ACUF 228)) is associated with Oates Coast.  This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Oates Canyon" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
