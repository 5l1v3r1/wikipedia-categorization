Submerged bank Shirase Bank (76°40′S 158°0′W﻿ / ﻿76.667°S 158.000°W﻿ / -76.667; -158.000) is a submarine bank located off the Shirase Coast in the Ross Sea. Name approved 6/88 (ACUF 228).  This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Shirase Bank" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
