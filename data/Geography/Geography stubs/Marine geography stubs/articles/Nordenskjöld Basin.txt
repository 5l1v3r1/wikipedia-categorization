Nordenskjöld Basin is an undersea basin named in association with the Nordenskjöld Ice Tongue (itself after explorer Otto Nordenskjöld). Name approved 4/80 (ACUF 201).   This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Nordenskjöld Basin" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
