Submerged bank McDonald Bank (75°30′S 26°35′W﻿ / ﻿75.500°S 26.583°W﻿ / -75.500; -26.583) is a submarine bank in the Weddell Sea named in association with the McDonald Ice Rumples. The name was proposed by Dr. Heinrich Hinze of the Alfred Wegener Institute for Polar and Marine Research, Bremerhaven, Germany, and was approved by the Advisory Committee for Undersea Features in June 1997.   This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "McDonald Bank" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
