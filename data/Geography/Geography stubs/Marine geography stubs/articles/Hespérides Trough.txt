Hespérides Trough (60°21′S 50°50′W﻿ / ﻿60.350°S 50.833°W﻿ / -60.350; -50.833Coordinates: 60°21′S 50°50′W﻿ / ﻿60.350°S 50.833°W﻿ / -60.350; -50.833) is an undersea trough named for the Spanish research vessel Hespérides. The name was approved by the Advisory Committee on Undersea Features in November 1995.   This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Hespérides Trough" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
