Submerged bank Ross Bank (76°45′S 176°0′W﻿ / ﻿76.750°S 176.000°W﻿ / -76.750; -176.000) is a submarine bank located in the Ross Sea in the area of Ross Island. Name approved 6/88 (ACUF 228).  This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Ross Bank" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
