Iselin Seamount is a seamount in the Southern Ocean off Antarctica. It was named for the research ship Iselin II of the Woods Hole Oceanographic Institute, the name being approved by the Advisory Committee for Undersea Features in February 1964.   This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Iselin Seamount" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
