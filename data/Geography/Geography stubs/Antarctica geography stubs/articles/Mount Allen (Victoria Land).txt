Mount Allen (77°24′S 162°32′E﻿ / ﻿77.400°S 162.533°E﻿ / -77.400; 162.533) is a peak, 1,400 m, standing between Clark Glacier and the head of Greenwood Valley in Victoria Land. Charted by the Victoria University of Wellington Antarctic Expedition (VUWAE), 1959-60, and named for A.D. Allen, one of the party's geologists.  This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "Mount Allen (Victoria Land)" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
