The Spire is a prominent rock spire, over 2,600 metres (8,500 ft) tall, surmounting the western end of Rampart Ridge, in Victoria Land of Antarctica.  It was surveyed (78°9′S 161°37′E﻿ / ﻿78.150°S 161.617°E﻿ / -78.150; 161.617)  and descriptively named in 1957 by the New Zealand party of the Commonwealth Trans-Antarctic Expedition of 1956−1958.  This article incorporates public domain material from the United States Geological Survey document "The Spire (Victoria Land)" (content from the Geographic Names Information System).
