Collins Reef (also known as Johnson North Reef/Johnson Reef North, Vietnamese: Đá Cô Lin, Chinese: 鬼喊礁; pinyin: Guǐhǎn jiāo, Tagalog: Roxas) is a Vietnamese occupied and controlled reef and the westernmost feature of Union Banks Atoll near the centre of Dangerous Ground in the Spratly islands in the South China Sea.  It is also claimed by China and Taiwan.
