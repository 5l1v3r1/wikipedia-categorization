Coordinates: 40°12′55″N 46°48′46″E﻿ / ﻿40.21528°N 46.81278°E﻿ / 40.21528; 46.81278 Martakert (Armenian: Մարտակերտ Martakert, also spelled as Mardakert) or  Aghdara (Azerbaijani: Ağdərə also spelled as Agdere or Aqdara) is the de facto administrative center of Martakert Region of the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic and a de jure town in the Tartar District of Azerbaijan. The town is de facto part of Nagorno-Karabakh Republic since the end of the 1991-94 Nagorno-Karabakh War. The climate in Aghdara is classified as Cfa by the Köppen-Geiger system.
