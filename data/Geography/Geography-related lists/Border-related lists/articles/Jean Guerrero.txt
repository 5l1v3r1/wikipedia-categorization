Jean Guerrero (born 1988)  is an investigative journalist   and a former foreign correspondent.  She is the author of the memoir: Crux: A Cross-Border Memoir, (2018, One World: Random House)  which won the PEN/FUSION Emerging Writers Award.   Her KPBS series Americaâ€™s Wall  has won two emmys.  Guerrero is the Vice President of the National Association of Hispanic Journalists for San Diego and Tijuana. Guerrero received her B.A. in Journalism from University of Southern California  and her MFA in creative nonfiction from Goucher College.  Crux: A Cross-Border Memoir was published in 2018 by One World: Random House. The book is an exploration of borders, Guerrero's father, and eventually Guerrero's own sense of self.  Guerrero's mother is a doctor.
