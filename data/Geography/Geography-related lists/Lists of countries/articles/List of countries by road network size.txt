This is a list of countries by total road network size, both paved and unpaved. Length of expressways are also included. Expressways or motorways, are the highways designed for high-speed vehicular traffic with controlled-access, free of signals, delicated right of way, without any at-grade intersections or any crossings except the terminus of a expressway.  This article incorporates public domain material from the CIA World Factbook website https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/index.html.
