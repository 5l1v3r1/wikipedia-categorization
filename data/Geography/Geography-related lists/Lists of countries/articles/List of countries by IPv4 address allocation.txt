This is a list of countries by IPv4 address allocation, as of  2 April 2012[update]. It includes 252 areas, including all United Nations member states, plus the Holy See, Kosovo and Taiwan. There are 232 (over four billion) IP addresses  in the IPv4 protocol. Of these, almost 600 million are reserved and cannot be used for public routing.
