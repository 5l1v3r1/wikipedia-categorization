There are 111 officially notified Inland National Waterways (NWs) in India identified for the purposes of inland water transport,  as per The National Waterways Act, 2016. Out of the 111 NWs, 106 were created in 2016. The NW network covers around 20,275.5Â km. NW-1, 2, & 3 are already operational.  Cargo as well as passenger / cruise vessels are plying on these waterways.  Detailed Project Report(DPR) for development of NW-4 & 5 was completed in 2010. The DPR of NW 5 was updated in 2014.
