This is the list of all the flags used and being used in the Philippines. Among the country's 17 regions, only the Cordillera Administrative Region and the Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao has their own regional flags. Under the final version of the Bangsamoro Basic Law to be reconciled by the Bicameral Committee in July 2018, the proposed Bangsamoro autonomous region is given the right to have its own flag and anthem.
