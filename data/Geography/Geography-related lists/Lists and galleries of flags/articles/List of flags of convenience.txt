The International Transport Workers' Federation maintains a list of countries it considers to be flag of convenience in regard to ship registration. There are currently 35 registries on the list.  In developing the list, the ITF considers "ability and willingness of the flag state to enforce international minimum social standards on its vessels,"  the "degree of ratification and enforcement of ILO Conventions and Recommendations,"  and "safety and environmental record."
