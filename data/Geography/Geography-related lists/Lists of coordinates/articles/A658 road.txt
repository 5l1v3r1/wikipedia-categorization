  The A658 is a road in the UK running from Bradford, West Yorkshire (53°47′42″N 1°44′55″W﻿ / ﻿53.7949°N 1.7487°W﻿ / 53.7949; -1.7487﻿ (A658 road (southwestern end))), to Knaresborough, North Yorkshire (54°00′23″N 1°25′58″W﻿ / ﻿54.0065°N 1.4327°W﻿ / 54.0065; -1.4327﻿ (A658 road (southwestern end))), passing along the way Leeds Bradford International Airport, where it runs through a tunnel directly under the main runway. The road originally terminated south of the village of Pannal at the junction with the A61 but was extended when the south Harrogate and Knaresborough bypass was built and the road now ends east of Knaresborough where it meets the A59.
