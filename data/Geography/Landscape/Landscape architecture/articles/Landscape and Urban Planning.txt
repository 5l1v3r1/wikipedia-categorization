Landscape and Urban Planning is a monthly peer-reviewed academic journal published by Elsevier. It covers landscape science, planning, design, and architecture, urban and regional planning, landscape and ecological engineering, landscape and urban ecology, and other practice-oriented fields. The editors-in-chief are Joan I. Nassauer (University of Michigan) and Wei-Ning Xiang (Tongji University). The journal was established in 1974 as Landscape Planning under founding editor-in-chief Arnold E. Weddle. In 1986 the journal was renamed and merged with the journal Urban Ecology. In 1988 the journal also incorporated Reclamation and Revegetation Research.
