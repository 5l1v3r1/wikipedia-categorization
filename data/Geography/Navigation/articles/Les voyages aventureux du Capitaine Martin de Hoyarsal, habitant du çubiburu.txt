 Les voyages aventureux du Capitaine Martin de Hoyarsabal, habitant du Cubiburu, contenant les règles et enseignments nécessaires à la bonne et seure navigation is a treatise on navigation by Martin de Hoyarçabal,  from the French Basque Country.  It was published in 1547 in Bordeaux, edited by Jean Chouin. It was the world's first pedagogical book on navigation, whose purpose was to teach sailors how to sail.
