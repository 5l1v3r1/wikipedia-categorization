Radio direction-finding station is – according to article 1.91 of the International Telecommunication Union´s (ITU) ITU Radio Regulations (RR)  – defined as «A radiodetermination station using radio direction-finding.» Each radiodetermiantion station shall be classified by the radiocommunication service in which it operates permanently or temporarily. If this station operates in a safety-of-life service, it will be protected for Interferences. In accordance with ITU Radio Regulations (article 1) this type of radio station might be classified as follows:  Radiodetermination station (article 1.86) of the radiodetermination service (article 1.40 )
