Korea University Museum (고려대학교박물관), commonly called The University Museum, is a history, archaeology, and art museum that is part of the Korea University in Seoul, South Korea The Museum is housed in the Centennial Memorial Samsung Hall that is one of the landmarks of the Korea University campus.
