Edmond Lévy (born 1934) is a French classical historian. Originally a pupil of École normale supérieure (promotion 1956), an aggregate of letters, he was a pupil of École française d'Athènes, a school where the French language is educated to students mainly from France in Athens, Greece.  He is a specialist in Greek history, he is a retired professor at Marc-Bloch University in Strasbourg, France.  He directs the Ktèma review.
