The Tiger Warrior is an archaeological adventure novel by David Gibbins. First published in 2009, it is the fourth book in Gibbins' Jack Howard series. It has been published in 18 languages and was a New York Times bestseller  . Among its foreign language editions, The Tiger Warrior is entitled Tigers de Guerre in France (Editions First), and Guardians of the Secret (Φύλακες του Μυστικού) in the Greek edition.
