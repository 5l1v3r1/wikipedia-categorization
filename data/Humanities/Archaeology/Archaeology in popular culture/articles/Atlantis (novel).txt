Atlantis is an archaeological adventure novel by David Gibbins. First published in 2005, it is the first book in Gibbins' Jack Howard series. It has been published in 30 languages and has sold over a million copies, and is the basis for a TV miniseries currently in development.    A TV miniseries based on the novel is in preparation by Akkord Film and Muse Entertainment, with production planned for 2012. [permanent dead link]. David Gibbins's sixth novel in the Jack Howard series, The Gods of Atlantis, published by Headline in 2011, is a sequel to Atlantis.
