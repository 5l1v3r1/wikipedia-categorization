Cuchi Machay (possibly from in the Quechua spelling Khuchi Mach'ay or Khuchimach'ay, khuchi pig, mach'ay cave,  "pig cave") is an archaeological site with rock paintings in Peru.  It is located in the Lima Region, Yauyos Province, Tanta District.
