Mousteroid was a culture occurring during the period 80,000 - 50,000 B.P. of Central,South and East Africa. Examples of this culture are the tool-industry of the Middle Paleolithic in West Africa,    known from sites at Zenebi, Nigeria   and Tiemassas in Senegal.    The most important of the Western Senegal sites is Pte. de Fann in Dakar.  Classified Mousteroid, indicating similarities to industry Mousterian in form but neither akin to, nor at a temporal parallel. Other sites about Dakar and Rufisque, were part of a culture that had not occurred for sufficient duration for  tools to be found at differing depths in the ground (without stratification)
