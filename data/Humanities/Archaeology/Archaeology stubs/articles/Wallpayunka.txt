Wallpayunka (Quechua wallpa hen, yunka warm area on the slopes of the Andes,  warm valley, "hen valley", hispanicized spellings Huallpayunca, Hualpayunca) is an archaeological site in Peru. It lies in the Huánuco Region, Yarowilca Province, Obas District. It is situated above the little village of Wallpayunka at heights between 3,700 metres (12,139 ft) and 3,800 metres (12,467 ft).
