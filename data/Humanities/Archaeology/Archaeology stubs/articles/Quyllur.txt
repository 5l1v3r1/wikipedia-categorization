Quyllur (Quechua for star,  Hispanicized spellings Collor) is an archaeological site in Peru. It is situated in the Cajamarca Region, Cajamarca Province, Namora District.   The site lies at a height of about 2,970 metres (9,744 ft) on a mountain named Quyllur (Coyor), east of San Nicolas Lake.
