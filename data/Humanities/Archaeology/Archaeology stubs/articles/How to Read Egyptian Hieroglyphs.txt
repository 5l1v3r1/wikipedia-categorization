How to Read Egyptian Hieroglyphs is a primer on understanding Egyptian hieroglyphs.  The text was written by Mark Collier (Egyptologist), and Bill Manley, c. 1998.   The standard version of analytic Egyptian hieroglyphs is based upon the 26 categories of the Gardiner's Sign List (about 700 signs), still the basic standard. According to WorldCat, the book is widely known and is held in 1062 libraries.
