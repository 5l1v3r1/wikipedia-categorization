This is a list of the suttas in the Majjhima Nikaya collection of middle-length discourses, part of the Tripitaka Buddhist canon. Also called  "All The Taints"  (Bhikkhu Bodhi) This sutta explains the seven ways to remove the taints by seeing, restraining, using, enduing, avoiding, removing, and by developing. This sutta explains the eleven ways for a bhikkhu to make progress in the path.
