Comic book superheroes and professional wrestling characters have long been viewed in the same vein of entertainment as colorful characters that are larger than life, and numerous wrestlers have appeared or been referenced in comic books. Spider-Man's first costumed appearance was the result of Peter Parker's need for a colorful gimmick, as he planned to enter and win a wrestling challenge. Mexico and Japan have also had characters crossover into comic books. Mexican Wrestling Comic Books Pro Wrestling Superstar Retsuden is a series of biographic manga published in Japan in the 1980s.
