This lists early editions of The Protocols of the Elders of Zion, an antisemitic forgery purporting to describe a Jewish conspiracy to achieve world domination. For recent editions, see Contemporary imprints of The Protocols of the Elders of Zion. 1902 M: Mikhail Osipovich Menshikov (1902) - The first textual reference to  the Protocols, in a far-right newspaper article; claims that they were  stolen by a "French journalist" in Nice, and quotes a line.  L: Hippolytus Lutostansky  - A direct quote of above edition; no revision, but useful chronological evidence.
