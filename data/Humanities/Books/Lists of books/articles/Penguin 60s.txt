To celebrate its 60th anniversary circa 1995, Penguin Books released three boxed sets of "Penguin 60s". The main set (ISBN 0140952721, ISBN 978-0-14-095272-8) contained "classic" works. The set with orange spines (ISBN 0140951792 / ISBN 978-0-14-095179-0) focused on 20th century or contemporary writers. A children's set (ISBN 0140953361) was also released, consisting of 30 volumes.
