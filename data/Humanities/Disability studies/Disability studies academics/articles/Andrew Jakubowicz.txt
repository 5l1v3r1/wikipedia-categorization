  Andrew Jakubowicz is an Australian academic and Professor of Sociology at the University of Technology Sydney. Jakubowicz holds a PhD from UNSW and an Honours Degree in Government from Sydney University. Since the early 1970s, Jakubowicz's work has focused on race relations, theories of cultural and ethnic diversity, disability and media. He was foundation director of the Centre for Multicultural Studies at the University of Wollongong and foundation chair of the Disability Studies and Research Institute. Jakubowicz has taught at universities in the United States, Europe and Asia; he is co-director of the UTS Centre for Cosmopolitan Civil Societies.
