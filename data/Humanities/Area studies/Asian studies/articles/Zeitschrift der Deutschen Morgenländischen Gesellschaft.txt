The Zeitschrift der Deutschen Morgenländischen Gesellschaft (ZDMG), lit. "Journal of the German Oriental Society", is a journal of Oriental studies, published by the Deutsche Morgenländische Gesellschaft since 1847. The journal has been digitized and is available at no cost from  the University of Halle.   The journal is available from 1847–2007, together with various indexes and supplements, including the Deutscher Orientalistentag volumes from 1968–1995.
