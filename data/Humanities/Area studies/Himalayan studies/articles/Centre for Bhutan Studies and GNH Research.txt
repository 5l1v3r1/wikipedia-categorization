The Centre for Bhutan Studies and GNH Research (formerly The Centre for Bhutan Studies) is a research institute located in Thimphu, Bhutan established in 1999 with the purpose of promoting research and scholarship on Bhutan. The President of the Centre is Dasho Karma Ura. Since 1999 the Centre has regularly published an  English language academic journal The Journal of Bhutan Studies.
