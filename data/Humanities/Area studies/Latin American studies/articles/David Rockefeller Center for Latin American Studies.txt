The David Rockefeller Center for Latin American Studies was founded in 1994 and is based at Harvard University.  It was endowed by David Rockefeller. It exists to "increase knowledge of the cultures, economies, histories, environment and contemporary affairs of Latin America; to foster cooperation and understanding among the peoples of the Americas; and to contribute to democracy, social progress and sustainable development throughout the hemisphere."
