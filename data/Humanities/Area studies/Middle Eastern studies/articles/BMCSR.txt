The British Middle East Center for studies and Research (BMCSR) carries out an in-depth analysis of current issues of regional and global level.  The center is based in London and was established in 2008. The program primarily focuses on geopolitics and strategic development in the world, particularly in the Middle East. The BMCSR center seeks to contribute to knowledge sharing and present superior understanding of the complexity of the world. It has a widespread range of networks of distinguished researchers and an extensive range array of experts from across the world. The center aims to promote dialogue and build bridges of mutual understanding and cooperation between cultures, civilizations, and religions. BMCSR center covers a wide range of areas such as media, engineering, business studies, politics, health and social care, science, etc.
