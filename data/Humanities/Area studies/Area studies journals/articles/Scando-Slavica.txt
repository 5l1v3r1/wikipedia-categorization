Scando-Slavica is a biannual peer-reviewed academic journal covering Slavic and Baltic studies. It was established in 1954 and is published by Taylor & Francis on behalf of the Association of Scandinavian Slavists and Baltologists. Its first editor-in-chief was the Danish slavist Adolf Stender-Petersen. The journal is abstracted and indexed in Emerging Sources Citation Index  and Scopus.
