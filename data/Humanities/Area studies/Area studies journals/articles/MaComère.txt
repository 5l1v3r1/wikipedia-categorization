MaComère (ISSN 1521-9968) is a biannual peer-reviewed academic journal covering studies of and creative works by and about women in the Caribbean and its diaspora published by the Association of Caribbean Women Writers and Scholars. Past issues (1998-2009) are available online at the Digital Library of the Caribbean. The journal was established in 1998 and the editor-in-chief is Hyacinth Simpson (Ryerson University).
