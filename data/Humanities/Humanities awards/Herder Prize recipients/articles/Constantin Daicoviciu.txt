Constantin Daicoviciu (Romanian pronunciation: [konstanˈtin ˈdajkovit͡ʃju]; March 1, 1898, Căvăran – May 27, 1973, Cluj-Napoca)  was a Romanian historian and archaeologist. He was rector (president) of Babeş-Bolyai University of Cluj, and a member of the Romanian Academy. His son, Hadrian Daicoviciu (1932–1984) was also a historian. Since his death in 1973, the commune in the Banat where he was born in bears his name.
