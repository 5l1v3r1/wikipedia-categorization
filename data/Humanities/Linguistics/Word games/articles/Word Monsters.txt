 Word Monsters is a word puzzle video game developed by Raketspel and was published by Rovio Stars.  However, as of the middle of 2014 the game is published by Raketspel and is no longer published by Rovio Stars.  The iOS release was on March 27, 2014 and the Android release was on May 7, 2014. The game features creating a custom monster and finding all the words on the screen under a specific time limit.  The player may play challenges against their in-game friends or against players worldwide. The game has received average reviews with a Metacritic score of 69/100 based on 6 reviews.
