An online word game is a word game based in an online venue.  Like many online games, many online word games are accessed via web browsers such as Facebook. Many traditional word games translate to the Internet. The advent of the Internet allows for new possibilities, in particular interactive and multiplayer games. There are a vast array of educational word play websites, useful for learning languages. For example 'Name, Place, Animal, Thing' - Categories game Name, Place, Animal, Thing An area of increasing popularity are multiplayer games different from the traditional family setting, bringing together total strangers. Those with an element of creativity are good examples of this.
