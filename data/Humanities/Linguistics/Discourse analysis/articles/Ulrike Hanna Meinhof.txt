Ulrike Hanna Meinhof is Professor in the Department of Modern Languages at the University of Southampton, previously having worked as a professor and Chair of Cultural Studies at the University of Bradford.  She is a specialist in discourse analysis.  Her main areas of research currently involve ethnographic research in European border communities and a comparative media-project about the 20th century on television. She is the author of Language Learning in the Age of Satellite Television, published by Oxford University Press.
