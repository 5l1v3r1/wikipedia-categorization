Sotaro Kita is a professor in the Department of Psychology at The University of Warwick. Professor Kita's work focuses on the Psycholinguistics properties of co-speech gesture, the relationship between spatial language, developmental psychology and cognition and sound symbolism.   Kita received his PhD from the University of Chicago, working in the lab of David McNeill.  from 1993-2003 he led the Gesture Project at Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics, one of the research foci of the MPI.  From April 2017 he is the editor of GESTURE (published by John Benjamins of Amsterdam).
