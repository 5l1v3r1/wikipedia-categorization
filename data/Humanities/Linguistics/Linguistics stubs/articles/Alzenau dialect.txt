The Alzenau or Haltsnovian dialect (aljznerisch/altsnerisch) was a dialect of German which was spoken in Alzen, now Hałcnów, a district of Bielsko-Biała, Poland. It was spoken until 1945, when the Germans were expelled out of Poland. It was related to the Wymysorys language.
