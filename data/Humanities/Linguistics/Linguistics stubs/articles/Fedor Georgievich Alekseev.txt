Fedor Georgievich Alekseev (Russian: Федор Алексеев, Armenian: Ֆեոդոր Ալեքսեև) is a Russian and Armenian linguist and journalist of Belarusian origin. He is the editor-in-chief of Minority Languages, a Russian academic journal dedicated to endangered languages of Russia,  and Kamysh, a local online magazine based in the city of Astrakhan in Southern Russia.
