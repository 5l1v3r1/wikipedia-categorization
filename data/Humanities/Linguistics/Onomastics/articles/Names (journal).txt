Names is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal covering onomastics. It was established in 1952 and is published quarterly by Taylor & Francis. It is the official journal of the American Name Society. The editor-in-chief is Frank Nuessel (University of Louisville). According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2016 impact factor of 0.333, ranking it 154th out of 179 journals in the category "Linguistics".
