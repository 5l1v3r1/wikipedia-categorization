V. Hovhannes Hagopian was a professor of Ottoman Turkish and Persian in the Anatolia College in Merzifon. He was the author of an Ottoman-Turkish Conversation-Grammar, published in Heidelberg in 1907. Hagopian was deported, probably to his death, from Anatolia College on the 10 August 1916.
