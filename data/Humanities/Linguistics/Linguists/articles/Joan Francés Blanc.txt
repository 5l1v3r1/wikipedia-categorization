 Joan Francés Blanc (Jean-François in French, Agen, December 8, 1961)  is an Occitan writer and lexicographer. In 1996, he, along with Domergue Sumien and others, contributed to the "escrituras descobertistas" manifesto.  He writes short stories in Gascon  as well as in Lengadocian.  He also published a science fiction novel written in Gascon, titled Heisei, in 1998.    He is also an activist in the Paris branch of the Institut d'Estudis Occitans  and participates in multiple lexicography efforts: Gidilòc,  DiGaM,   and the IEO IT glossary.
