  Robert (Bob) E. Johnson is an American linguist. Dr. Johnson is a professor emeritus of Gallaudet University.   Dr. Johnson earned his B.A. at Stanford University and his PhD. in linguistics at Washington State University. Dr. Johnson was a professor in the Department of Linguistics at Gallaudet University until his retirement in 2011, and was one of the founding faculty members of the department. Named in his honor, the Robert E. Johnson Outstanding Graduate Student in Linguistics Award is presented annually to a deserving member of the graduate student body.
