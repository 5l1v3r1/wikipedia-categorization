Writeprint is a method in forensic linguistics of establishing author identification over the internet, likened to a digital fingerprint.  Identity is established through a comparison of distinguishing stylometric characteristics of an unknown written text with known samples of the suspected author (writer invariants).  Even without a suspect, writeprint provides potential background characteristics of the author, such as nationality and education.
