Yongwoo Lee (born in Seoul) is an art historian, critic and curator.  Yongwoo Lee is executive director of the Shanghai Himalayas Museum. He was the president of the IBA (International Biennial Association) from 2014 to 2017.  Yongwoo Lee is the founding director of the Gwangju Biennale, established in 1995, and served as president of the Gwangju Biennale Foundation for six years (2008-2014).
