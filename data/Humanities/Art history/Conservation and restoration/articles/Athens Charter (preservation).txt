The Athens Charter for the Restoration of Historic Monuments is a seven-point manifesto adopted at the First International Congress of Architects and Technicians of Historic Monuments in Athens in 1931. The Athens Charter for the Restoration of Historic Monuments was produced by the participants of the First International Congress  of Architects and Technicians of Historic Monuments organized by the International Museums Office and held in Athens in 1931.
