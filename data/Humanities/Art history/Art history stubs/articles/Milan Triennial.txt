The Milan Triennial (Triennale di Milano) was established in 1933 as a 3 yearly art and design exhibition held in Monza and then in Milan. The event started as a biennial event in Monza in 1923  and then transferred to Milan where was intended to be held every three years, last a few months and to invite global contributions.
