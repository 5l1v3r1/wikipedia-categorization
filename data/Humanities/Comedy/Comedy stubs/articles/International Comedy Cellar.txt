Coordinates: 53°20′35″N 6°15′42″W﻿ / ﻿53.343105°N 6.261779°W﻿ / 53.343105; -6.261779 The International Comedy Cellar was a comedy club in the International Bar on Dublin's South Wicklow Street. It was Ireland's first alternative comedy club, founded by 'Mr. Trellis' members :Irish comedians Ardal O'Hanlon, Barry Murphy and Kevin Gildea.    Dermot Carmody later took on a leading role. Many Irish and UK acts started their careers there including Des Bishop,  Tommy Tiernan and Eddie Izzard.
