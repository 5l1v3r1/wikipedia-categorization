Reno is an American stand-up comedian and actress known for such films as Quiz Show, The Manchurian Candidate, The Hard Way and Kinsey.  She also hosted her own 2001 reality documentary television series on Bravo entitled Citizen Reno which was produced by Lily Tomlin and Jane Wagner.   She also starred in the 2002 concert film Reno: Rebel without a Pause directed by Nancy Savoca.
