BA, California State University, Northridge Ildiko Tabori is an American psychologist practicing in Los Angeles, California. She is best known as the Psychologist-in-Residence  at the Laugh Factory comedy club in Hollywood, California. Tabori is the daughter of Hungarian Olympian and 4 minute miler, Laszlo Tabori, and Kata Preszburger Tabori. Tabori is a native of Los Angeles, California. She continues to live in Los Angeles with her daughter, Mari.
