  Fear of a Brown Planet Returns is a 2011 Australian concert film directed by Danielle Karalus featuring Australian comedy duo Fear of a Brown Planet (Aamer Rahman and Nazeem Hussain). The film features the "best of" material from Aamer Rahman and Nazeem Hussain's 2010 sell-out festival show, Fear of a Brown Planet Returns, as well as content from their debut shows.   It was recorded at the Chapel Off Chapel in Melbourne  on 15 January 2011.  The film was released on DVD and Blu-ray on 31 August 2011.
