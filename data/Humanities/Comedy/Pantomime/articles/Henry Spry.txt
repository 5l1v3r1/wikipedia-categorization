  Henry Spry (born c. 1835) was a playwright and pantomime performer who co-wrote and appeared in nearly 50 productions with George Conquest at the Surrey Theatre and elsewhere. Henry Spry was born around 1835 in Bloomsbury, London. Spry married Eliza. Spry's daughter Alice was an actress.
