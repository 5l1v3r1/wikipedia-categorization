  The ITV Panto is a series of televised pantomimes originally broadcast on ITV in 1998, 2000, and 2002, and have regularly seen Re-runs in the years since. All written by Simon Nye, they included an array of celebrities playing the lead roles. (In order of original broadcast date) All the main roles in each pantomime were headed by well-known celebrities, some of whom became regular players, including some cameos from other major celebrities.
