In addition to the items listed below in respect of Folk-lore Record and its successor, Folk-lore, the journal of the Folklore Society, Crooke also contributed frequently to that journal in the form of letters and brief observations. He was editor of the journal from 1915 until his death.  In addition to these works, Crooke made many contributions to the Encyclopedia of Religion and Ethics. He had been editing the Katha Sagara Kosa, as translated by Charles Henry Tawney, at the time of his death.
