Jann (Arabic: جَنّ / جُنّ‎, jann) is a type of Jinn in  Arabian- and Islam-related lore which has several meanings: Jann from the Semitic root JNN is an Arabic term, whose primary meaning is "to hide" and can also refer to an agile snake.  In the Quran this term appears in Surah 15:27 and Surah 27:10 to designate a supernatural creature or a serpent.
