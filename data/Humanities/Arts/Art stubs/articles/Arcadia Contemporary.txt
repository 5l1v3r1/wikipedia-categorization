Arcadia Contemporary is a contemporary art gallery in Los Angeles. Arcadia Fine Arts Gallery was founded in 1998 by Steve Diamant, showcasing contemporary, representational artwork.  The gallery changed its name in 2013 to the current title, Arcadia Contemporary.  In July 2016 the gallery relocated to Downtown Culver City.
