Founded in 2015, the CTG Collective is a not-for-profit organization focusing on exploring the human experience through boundless configurations of art.   The Collective was founded by Rachel Monosov, Catinca Tabacaru and Justin Orvis Steimer, which has been running international traveling residencies since 2015.
