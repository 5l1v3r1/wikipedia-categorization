Six Flags Zhejiang is an upcoming amusement park at Jiaxing, Zhejiang, China, owned by Six Flags. It will feature original rides and attractions, along with attractions themed around Garfield and Friends.  In addition to a theme park, the park will be accompanied by a Six Flags Hurricane Harbor water park.  Six Flags will be located in Haiyan, on the coast of Hangzhou Bay. The park is approximately 45 miles from downtown Shanghai.  Official website
