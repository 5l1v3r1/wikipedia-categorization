Sora to Umi no Aida (ソラとウミのアイダ, lit. "The Space Between the Sky and Sea") is a Japanese media franchise owned by ForwardWorks, with Oji Hiroi credited as the original creator. It primarily consists of a smartphone game, released for Android and iOS on September 28, 2017. A Microsoft Windows version by DMM will be released in Q2 2018.  An anime television series adaptation by TMS Entertainment is scheduled to air from October 4, 2018. An anime television series adaptation was announced on July 25, 2017.   Atsushi Nigorikawa is directing the anime at TMS Entertainment, and Takashi Yamada is in charge of series composition. Shuuhei Yamamoto is drawing the character designs.  The series is scheduled to air from October 4, 2018 on Tokyo MX and other channels. [a] The opening theme is "Sora to Umi no Aida" (ソラとウミのアイダ) by Karin Takahashi, Honoka Inoue, and Momoko Suzuki under their character names, and the ending theme is "Ao no Kanata" (蒼の彼方, lit. "Beyond the Blue") by Konomi Suzuki.
