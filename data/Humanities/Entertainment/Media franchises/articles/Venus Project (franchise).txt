Venus Project (Japanese: ヴィーナス プロジェクト, Hepburn: Vīnasu Purojekuto) is a Japanese multimedia franchise conceived by software and 3D character studio Galat. The project involves Japanese idols living in Japan in the near future. A video game for the PlayStation Vita was released in April 2015. An anime television series aired in Japan between July and September 2015. A web comic is also planned for release.
