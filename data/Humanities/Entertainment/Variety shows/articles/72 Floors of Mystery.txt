72 Floors of Mystery (Chinese: 七十二层奇楼) is a 2017 Chinese adventure variety show on Hunan Television. The members of the cast are Kris Wu, Simon Yam, Leo Wu, Zhao Liying, Wowkie Zhang, Wang Xiaoli, Liu Chang, Waer.  The team is tasked with solving mysteries that will aid in understanding the secret behind the 72-floor building.  Each episode supposedly was a floor in the 72 story tower that the cast members solved. The show premiered on May 5 via Hunan TV. It will air every Friday at 20:20 for 12 episodes.
