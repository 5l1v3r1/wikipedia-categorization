The Fiction Circus is a Brooklyn- and Austin-based online literary magazine and art collective that publishes short fiction and essays on the arts.  The group also holds staged multimedia fiction readings accompanied by electronic music and incorporating visual art and theater as a frame narrative.  The writers of the site operate under names such as Miracle Jones, Stephen Future, Geoff Sebesta, Goodman Carter, and Xerxes Verdammt, and the main magazine content includes writing about classical and contemporary literature, including fiction in non-traditional media. The Fiction Circus has been featured in Slashdot,  the New York Times  and Wired,  among other online news sources.
