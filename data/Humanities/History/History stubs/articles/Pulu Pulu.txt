In Upin & Ipin characters, Pulu Pulu known as, Raja Pulu is an evil, legendary leader of the Treasure Island. The goal of Pulu pulu is to wear the evil and enchanting mask, fight each other, and stealing crystal ball.  Not so long ago, in 500 B.C. from the Majapahit Empire (now Indonesia), the crystal ball gives its power to give a wish when eclipse of the sun settles. When they invaded the Pulu Pulu, the power is useless after getting the crystal ball and make a wish.
