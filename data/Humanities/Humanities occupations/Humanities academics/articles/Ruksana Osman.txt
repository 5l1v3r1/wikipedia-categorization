Ruksana Osman is Professor and Dean of the Faculty of Humanities at University of the Witwatersrand, Johannesberg, South Africa . She is an elected member of the Academy of Science, South Africa  .  Osman's expertise is in Higher Education, Research Led Teacher Education and Teaching and Learning in Higher Education. Her focus is on equity, access and success in teacher and higher education . She has authored three books. She also serves as convenor of the UNESCO Research Chair in Teacher Education for Diversity and Development .
