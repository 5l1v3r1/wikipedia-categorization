John Morreall (born February 1, 1947 in Rochester, New York) is a Doctor of Philosophy  and Emeritus Professor of Religious Studies at the College of William and Mary in Williamsburg, Virginia.  He has also taught at Northwestern University, Santa Clara University, Pennsylvania State University, University of South Florida and the Rochester Institute of Technology. Morreall has also published over 60 articles, chapters, and reviews on humor, as well as over 40 articles and chapters on other topics in philosophy, religion, and linguistics. Morreall has done over 500 presentations on humor for business and medical groups in the U.S., Canada, Europe, and Japan, under the name Humorworks.
