Men and Masculinities is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal covering men's studies. It was established in 1998 and is published by Sage Publications. The editor-in-chief is Michael Kimmel (Stony Brook University). The journal is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2017 impact factor of 1.863, ranking it 35th out of 146 journals in the category "Sociology".
