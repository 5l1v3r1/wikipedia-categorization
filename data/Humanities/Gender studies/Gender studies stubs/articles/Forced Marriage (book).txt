Forced Marriage: A Study on British Bangladeshi Community is a 2012 book written by Abu Sadik Maruf. The study addresses the international and cross-cultural dimensions of forced marriage. It is mainly focused on British Bangladeshi community to know the circumstances of forced marriage in general and then evaluated the existing social work services in the UK. The main interest of the study was in the statements of particular groups of people including their personal views, perceptions and experiences on forced marriage.
