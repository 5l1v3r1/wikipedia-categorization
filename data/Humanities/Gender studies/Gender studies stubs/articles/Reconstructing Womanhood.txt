Carby received her Ph.D. in 1984 from Birmingham University. Her doctoral dissertation later became the foundation for the book. Reconstructing Womanhood analyzes writings from black women in the nineteenth and early twentieth centuries, as well as examines the social, political, and historical landscapes in which these works were produced.
