Catherine M. Sama is a professor of Italian at the University of Rhode Island. Her research focuses on Early Modern and 18th-Century Italian Women Writers, Correspondence Networks, The Italian Enlightenment, Italian Women Artists, and Gender Studies.  In 2013 she was a recipient of the National Endowment for the Humanities Research Fellowship.  She also serves as a board member of the URI Center for the Humanities. Sama earned her BA from the University of Virginia in 1985, where she majored in French and minored in Italian. She earned her PhD from Brown University in 1995.  She also serves as a board member of the URI Center for the Humanities.
