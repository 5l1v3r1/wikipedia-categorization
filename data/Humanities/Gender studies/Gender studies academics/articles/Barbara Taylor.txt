Barbara Taylor is a Canadian-born historian based in the United Kingdom, specialising in the Enlightenment, gender studies and the history of subjectivity. She is Professor of Humanities at Queen Mary, University of London.  Taylor has written a biography of Mary Wollstonecraft, the early English feminist and republican,   and continues to speak on her life, for example in 2009 at Newington Green Unitarian Church as part of the celebrations of the 250th anniversary of Wollstonecraft's birth. With psychologist Adam Phillips, Taylor is the coauthor of On Kindness (2009).    Taylor's memoir The Last Asylum: A Memoir of Madness in Our Times, describing her years at Friern Hospital, was published in 2014.     It was a finalist for the 2015 RBC Taylor Prize.
