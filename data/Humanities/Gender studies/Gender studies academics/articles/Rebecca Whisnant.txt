Rebecca Suzanne Whisnant  is the associate professor, and the director of the Women's and Gender Studies Program, at the University of Dayton.  Whisnant also sits on the editorial board of the journal, Sexualization, Media, and Society.  Whisnant gained her degree in philosophy from Oberlin College in 1989.  She went on to complete a masters (1992), and a Ph.D (2002), both in philosophy, at University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.   Prior to working at the University of Dayton, Whisnant taught at the University of Southern Indiana.  Book review
