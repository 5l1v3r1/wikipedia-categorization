Gabrielle Houbre is a French historian.  She is a lecturer at the University of Paris VII: Denis Diderot and a member of the Institut Universitaire de France.  Specializing in 19th century history, she pursues research of gender, history of sexuality, the body, sensitivities, and youth.  After her doctoral thesis was completed under the direction of Michelle Perrot (Paris VII, 1990), she supported her accreditation in 2002 to carry out research (Les lois du genre. Identités, pratiques, représentations sociales et culturelles. France, XIXe siècle, Paris I-Sorbonne, dir. Alain Corbin).
