The Center for the Advancement of Women is a not-for-profit institution dedicated to research-based education and advocacy for women.    An independent, non-partisan organization founded in 1995 and led by Faye Wattleton, the organization works for the advancement of women.  The organization conducts national opinion research among women to measure experiences in their daily lives. This research presents a profile of women that is used to educate opinion leaders, policy makers and the general public.
