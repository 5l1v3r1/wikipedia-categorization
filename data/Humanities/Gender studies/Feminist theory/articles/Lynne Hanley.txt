Lynne Hanley (born 1943) is an American feminist author and literary critic.  She is Professor Emerita of literature and writing at Hampshire College. Hanley received a B.A. in English from Cornell University, an M.A. from Columbia University, and a Ph.D. from the University of California, Berkeley.  Before coming to Hampshire, she taught at Princeton University, Douglass College and Mount Holyoke College.
