Sally Gary (born September 19, 1961) is a speaker and author on the Christian approach to homosexuality.  Gary was born in Wichita Falls, Texas. She grew up going to Tenth and Broad Church of Christ in Wichita Falls.  She attended Abilene Christian University and has a law degree from Texas Tech University. In 2006, Gary founded CenterPeace, a nonprofit ministry that creates safe spaces for Christians to talk about homosexuality.  Gary also taught at Abilene Christian University in the department of communication from 2001 to 2011.  In 2013 Gary authored Loves God, Likes Girls, a memoir telling the story of her own journey experiencing homosexuality.
