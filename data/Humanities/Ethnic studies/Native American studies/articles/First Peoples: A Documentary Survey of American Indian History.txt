First People: A Documentary Survey of American Indian History was written by Colin G. Calloway (born 1953), a British historian.  He is the professor of Native American Studies at Dartmouth College.  The book's most recent edition was published by Bedford/St.Martin's on September 2, 2015 and the first edition was published in 2004.  The book provides an overview of perspectives of American Indian history through Calloway's own research of both Native Indians and European colonials along with primary source documents and picture essays.
