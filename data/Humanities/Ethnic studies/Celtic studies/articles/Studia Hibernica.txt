Studia Hibernica is an annual academic journal for Irish studies, focusing on the wide spectrum of Irish language, literature, history, archaeology and folklore.  It is published annually by St Patrick's College, Drumcondra, Dublin.  The journal was established in 1961 by members of St Patrick's Training College, including Gearóid Mac Eoin, Dónall Cregan, Séamus Ó Mórdha, Breandán Mac Aodha and Tadhg Ó Ceallaigh.  The journal is currently edited by Pauric Travers.
