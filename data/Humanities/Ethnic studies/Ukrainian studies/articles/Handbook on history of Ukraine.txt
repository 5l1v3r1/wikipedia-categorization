Handbook on history of Ukraine (Історія України) is a multi-volume comprehensive encyclopedic book about the history of Ukraine. There were at least two editions published by Kiev publisher "Geneza" which specializes in publishing school handbooks. The first edition was published in 1993-99, the second in 1999. The authors of the handbook are Ihor Pidkova and Roman Shust.  The web version also provides electronic copies of Ukrainian historical outlines from such Ukrainian historians like Natalia Yakovenko and Yaroslav Hrytsak.
