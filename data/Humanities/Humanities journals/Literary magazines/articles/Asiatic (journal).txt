Asiatic: IIUM Journal of English Language and Literature is a biannual Malaysian peer-reviewed academic journal based at the International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM), focusing on literary and linguistics works by Asian and Asian diaspora writers. The journal's current and founding editor-in-chief is Mohammad A. Quayum.
