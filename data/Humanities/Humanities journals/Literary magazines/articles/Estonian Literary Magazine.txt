 Estonian Literary Magazine or simply ELM is an English language biannual literary magazine published in Estonia. ELM was established in 1995 and is published by the Estonian Institute.   The goal of the magazine is to promote Estonian literature and it is published twice per year in spring and autumn.  ELM covers articles on former and current Estonian authors and poets as well as book reviews published in English language.   The editor of the magazine is Berit Kaschan.
