tripleC: Communication, Capitalism & Critique is a biannual peer-reviewed open access academic journal covering information and media studies from the perspective of critical theory. It was established in 2003 as tripleC: Cognition, Communication, Cooperation. Journal for a Global Sustainable Information Society, obtaining its current name in 2013. It is published by a United Kingdom-based company, also called tripleC.  The editors-in-chief are Christian Fuchs (University of Westminster) and Marisol Sandoval (City University London).
