The Asia Pacific Media Educator is a blind peer reviewed academic journal that offers a platform for discussion and dialogues among media educators, researchers and journalists. The journal is published twice a year by SAGE Publications (New Delhi) in collaboration with the School of the Arts, English and Media, Faculty of Law, Humanities and the Arts, University of Wollongong in Australia.
