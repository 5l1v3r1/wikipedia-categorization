The International Journal of Press/Politics is a peer-reviewed academic journal that covering the field of journalism, especially the linkages between the news media and political processes and actors. The editor-in-chief is Rasmus Kleis Nielsen (University of Oxford). It was established in 1996 and is currently published by SAGE Publications. The International Journal of Press/Politics is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2017 impact factor of 2.468, ranking it 31st out of 169 journals in the category "Political Science" and 12th out of 84 journals in the category "Communication".
