The Journal of Creative Communications is a blind peer reviewed academic journal that promotes inquiry into contemporary communication issues within wider social, economic, cultural and technological contexts. The journal is published three times a year by SAGE Publications (New Delhi, India) in collaboration with MICA-The School of Ideas,  Shela, Ahmedabad, India.
