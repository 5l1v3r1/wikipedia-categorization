Lingua Nostra is a peer-reviewed academic journal of general linguistics published by Frienze Sansoni. The journal publishes both articles and book reviews. It  publishes two special issues a year. The current Editor-in-Chief is Migliorini S.and Devoto G. Since 2010, it publishes 1400 pages per year. The journal has been published since 1935  .
