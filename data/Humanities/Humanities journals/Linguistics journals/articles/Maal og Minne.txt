Maal og Minne ("Language and Memory") is a Norwegian academic journal of linguistics established in 1909 by Magnus Olsen. It covers research on Scandinavian languages,  focusing mainly on language history and philology. It is a "level 2" journal in the Norwegian Scientific Index.
