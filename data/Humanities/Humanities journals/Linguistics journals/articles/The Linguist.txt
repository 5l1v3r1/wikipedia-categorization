The Linguist (formerly The Incorporated Linguist) is the bimonthly journal of the UK's Chartered Institute of Linguists. The headquarters is in London.  The journal was established in 1962.  The Linguist includes news about the Institute and its members, articles on translation and interpreting, bilingualism and language use, as well as book reviews and current opinions.  The editor is Miranda Moore. The late Professor Peter Newmark was a regular contributor, whose wide-ranging column was entitled "Translation Now", as was Andrew Dalby with "Notes in the Margin".
