Phonology is a British peer-reviewed journal of phonology published by Cambridge University Press, the only journal devoted exclusively to this  subfield of linguistics. The current editors are Prof. Colin J. Ewen (Leiden University) and Prof. Ellen Kaisse (University of Washington). The volumes from 1997 on are available electronically with subscription via the site of the publisher.
