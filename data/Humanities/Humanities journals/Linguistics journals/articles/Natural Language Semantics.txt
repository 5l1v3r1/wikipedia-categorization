Natural Language Semantics is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal of semantics published by Springer Science+Business Media. It covers semantics and its interfaces in grammar, especially in syntax. The editors-in-chief are Irene Heim (MIT) and Angelika Kratzer (University of Massachusetts Amherst). The journal is abstracted and indexed in: According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2012 impact factor of 0.480.
