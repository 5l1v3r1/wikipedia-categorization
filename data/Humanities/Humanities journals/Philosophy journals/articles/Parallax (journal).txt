Parallax is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal publishing work in cultural studies, critical theory and philosophy. Each issue has a theme and issues are regularly compiled by guest-editors. The journal was established in 1995 by Adrian Rifkin, Marq Smith and Joanne Morra, and is now published by Routledge.
