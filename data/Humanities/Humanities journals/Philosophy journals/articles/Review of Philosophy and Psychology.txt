The Review of Philosophy and Psychology is a peer-reviewed academic journal published by Springer  that focuses on philosophical and foundational issues in cognitive science. The journal is hosted at the Jean Nicod Institute (Paris), a research center of the French National Centre for Scientific Research (CNRS). Under its former name, it was published by Stanford University's CSLI Publications and distributed by the University of Chicago Press. It was renamed to its current title in 2010 and volume numbering was started at 1 again.
