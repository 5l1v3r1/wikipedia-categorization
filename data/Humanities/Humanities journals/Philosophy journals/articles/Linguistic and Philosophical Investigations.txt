Linguistic and Philosophical Investigations is a peer-reviewed philosophy journal that publishes articles that treat the foundations of language; the journal deals with linguistic and philosophical issues. Nick Bostrom published in the journal in 2006 (vol. 5) ("The Transhumanist FAQ") and in 2005 (vol. 5) ("What is a Singleton?"). Michael Della Rocca published in the journal of 2008 (vol. 7) ("The Identity of Indiscernibles and the Articulability of Concepts").
