Thought: A Journal of Philosophy is a twice-annual peer-reviewed academic journal published by Wiley-Blackwell for the Northern Institute of Philosophy.  The journal covers logic, philosophy of maths, philosophy of mind, epistemology, philosophy of language, and metaphysics.  The editors-in-chief are Crispin Wright (New York University), John Divers (University of Leeds), and Carrie Jenkins (University of British Columbia).
