The Journal of Social Philosophy is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal of social philosophy covering work of normative and practical significance concerning social life. It was established in 1970 and is published by John Wiley & Sons. The editor-in-chief is Carol Gould (Hunter College). It is an official journal of the North American Society for Social Philosophy.
