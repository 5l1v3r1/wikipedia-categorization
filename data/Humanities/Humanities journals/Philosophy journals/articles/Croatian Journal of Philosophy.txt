The Croatian Journal of Philosophy is a peer-reviewed academic journal of philosophy, publishing articles of diverse currents in English. The journal publishes three issues per year with the support of the Republic of Croatia's Ministry of Culture and the Ministry of Science, Education, and Sports.  All issues are available in electronic format from the Central and Eastern European Online Library and the Philosophy Documentation Center. Between 2002 and 2004, the journal published 61 papers, 57 of which (93%) were written by authors outside Croatia.
