Faith and Philosophy is a peer-reviewed academic journal sponsored by the Society of Christian Philosophers. Since 1984 it has published over 1,300 articles and reviews focused on the philosophical examination of religion and Christian faith. The journal is currently edited by Mark Murphy (Georgetown), and receives support from Asbury Theological Seminary and the University of Notre Dame. The journal is also supported by patrons who pledge annual contributions. Members of the Society for Christian Philosophers receive a print subscription to the journal as a benefit of membership. The journal is published by the Society in cooperation with the Philosophy Documentation Center. All issues are available online. Faith and Philosophy is abstracted and indexed in the ATLA Religion Database, Christian Periodical Index, MEDLINE, Periodicals Index Online, Philosopher's Index, Philosophy Research Index, PhilPapers, Religion Index One, Religious and Theological Abstracts, and Scopus.
