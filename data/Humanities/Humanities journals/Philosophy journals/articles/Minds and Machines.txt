Minds and Machines is a peer-reviewed academic journal covering artificial intelligence, philosophy, and cognitive science.  The journal was established in 1991 with James Henry Fetzer as founding editor-in-chief.  It is published by Springer Science+Business Media on behalf of the Society for Machines and Mentality, a special interest group within the International Association for Computing and Philosophy. The current editor-in-chief is Mariarosaria Taddeo (University of Oxford).  Previous editors-in-chief of the journal have been James H. Fetzer (1991–2000), James H. Moor (2001–2010), and Gregory Wheeler (2011–2016).
