European Transport Law is a scholarly law journal. It is publishes case notes on transport law decisions from courts throughout Europe, as well as articles in Dutch, English, French, German, Italian and Spanish on all areas of transport law. European Transport Law may be abbreviated to ETL for scholarly citations.  The journal was founded in Antwerp, Belgium in 1965 by the Belgian lawyer Robert Wijffels. Mr Wijffels died in 2010,  but his family law firm continues to publish the journal on an annual basis.  2012 will mark the 47th volume of European Transport Law.
