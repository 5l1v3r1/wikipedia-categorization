Lloyd's Maritime and Commercial Law Quarterly ("LMCLQ") is a  legal journal that publishes articles relating to commercial law and shipping law. It is currently edited by Professor Sir Francis Rose of the University of Southampton.  The LMCLQ was established in 1974.
