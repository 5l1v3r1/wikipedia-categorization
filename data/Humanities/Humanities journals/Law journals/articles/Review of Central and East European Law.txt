 Review of Central and East European Law (RCEEL) is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal covering research on legal doctrine and practice in Central and Eastern Europe.  It was established in 1975 under the name Review of Socialist Law and it was renamed to Review of Central and East European Law in 1991. It is published by Brill.
