Reports and Transactions of the Cardiff Naturalists' Society was an annual scientific journal published by the Cardiff Naturalists' Society, containing scholarly articles on geology, archaeology, natural history, and meteorology, as well as book reviews and society notes.  The title started in 1867 and ceased publication in 1986. It is being digitized by the Welsh Journals Online project at the National Library of Wales.
