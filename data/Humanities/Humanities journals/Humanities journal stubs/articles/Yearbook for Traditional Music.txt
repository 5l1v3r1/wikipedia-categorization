The Yearbook for Traditional Music is a peer-reviewed academic journal covering research on folk music and dance. It is published by the International Council for Traditional Music, once a year in December. The editor-in-chief is Kati Szego. The Yearbook was established in 1949 as the Journal of the International Folk Music Council, obtaining its current title in 1981. The journal is abstracted and indexed in the Arts & Humanities Citation Index and Current Contents/Arts & Humanities.
