Theoria: Historical Aspects of Music Theory (ISSNÂ 1554-1312) is a peer-reviewed academic journal specializing in music theory and analysis. It was established in 1985, under the auspices of the University of North Texas College of Music. According to its website, "Theoria is a peer-reviewed journal on all aspects of history in music theory. This includes critical articles representing the current stage of research, and editions of newly discovered or mostly unknown theoretical texts with translation and commentary."  The journal's first editor in chief was Mark McCune. It is currently edited by Frank Heidlberger. In 2017 volume 24 was published. Publisher since volume 13 is the University of North Texas Press.
