 Music Theory Online is a quarterly peer-reviewed open access academic journal covering music theory and analysis. It was established in 1993 and is published by the Society for Music Theory. The initial issues were designated as part of volume 0. Volume 1 began in January 1995. Its founding editor-in-chief was Lee A. Rothfarb. It is currently edited by Nicole Biamonte (McGill University). The journal is abstracted and indexed in the Répertoire International de Littérature Musicale.
