Technological Forecasting and Social Change (formerly Technological Forecasting) is a peer-reviewed academic journal published by Elsevier which discusses futures studies, technology assessment, and technological forecasting. Articles focus on methodology and actual practice, and has been published since 1969. The editor-in-chief is Fred Phillips, National Chengchi University, Taiwan. According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2016 impact factor of 2.625 and a 5-Year Impact Factor of 3.226.
