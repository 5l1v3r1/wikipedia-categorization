Syria, subtitled Archéologie, art et histoire (until 2005 Revue d’art oriental et d’archéologie), is a multidisciplinary and multilingual academic journal covering the Semitic Middle East from prehistory to the Islamic conquest. It is published by the Institut français du Proche-Orient and was established in 1920.  For 19 years (1978–1997), the archaeologist Ernest Will edited the journal. The current editor-in-chief is Maurice Sartre (Institut français du Proche-Orient). From 2011–2014 the journal was abstracted and indexed in Scopus.
