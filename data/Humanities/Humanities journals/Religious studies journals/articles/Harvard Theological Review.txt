The Harvard Theological Review is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal established in 1908  and published by  Cambridge University Press on behalf of the Harvard Divinity School. It covers a wide spectrum of fields in theological and religious studies; its range is not limited to any one religious tradition or set of traditions. The editors-in-chief are Jon D.
