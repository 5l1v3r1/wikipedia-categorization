Sociology of Religion is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal covering the sociology of religion. It was established in 1973 as SA: Sociological Analysis, obtaining its current name in 1993. It is published by Oxford University Press on behalf of the Association for the Sociology of Religion, of which it is the official journal. The editor-in-chief is Gerardo Marti (Davidson College). According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2016 impact factor of 1.370.
