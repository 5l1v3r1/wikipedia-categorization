Modern Theology is a peer-reviewed academic journal published by Wiley-Blackwell. It publishes articles, review articles, and book reviews in the area of theology, with an ecumenical editorial policy.  The journal is abstracted and indexed in ATLA Religion Database, EBSCO, Philosopher's Index, ProQuest, Religious & Theological Abstracts, and Scopus.  The editors-in-chief are Jim Fodor (St. Bonaventure University) and William T. Cavanaugh (DePaul University).
