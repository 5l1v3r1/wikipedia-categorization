The International Association for the Reunification of the Parthenon Sculptures is a campaign group, which joins together various organizations around the world whose primary aim is to bring about the reunification of all the surviving Parthenon Sculptures to Athens, Greece. The Association was founded in November 2005 at a meeting in Athens of twelve national reunification organisations organised by the Greek government.    The founder and current chair of the campaign, David Hill, is also involved with some of the member organizations. At the founding meeting a declaration was released  which encapsulated the common goals of all the members.
