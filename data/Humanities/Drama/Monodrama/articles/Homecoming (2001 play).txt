Homecoming is an Off-Broadway one-person show by Lauren Weedman that premiered at Westside Theatre on September 10, 2001. Following the September 11 attacks, Weedman announced that the show would be postponed till January 21, 2002, with the venue changed to the Arclight Theatre.  The show revolves around Lauren, who is trying to find her mom, her step-mom, her bossy sister, her aging grandmother, and her boyfriend. Homecoming was directed by Maryann Lombardi and it starred Lauren Weedman.  This show is based on true stories from her life.  The following excerpts of reviews were quoted as part of the promotion for a May 29, 2011 performance at Don't Tell Mama in New York City:  "...recalls Carol Burnett in her prime." --Back Stage West, David Edward Hughes
