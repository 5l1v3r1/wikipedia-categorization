 Graeme K Talboys is an English writer and teacher. Graeme Keith Talboys was born at Queen Charlotte's Hospital in Hammersmith on Thursday 26 November 1953.  He is represented by Leslie Gardner of Artellus.  Talboys was nominated for The Guardian's "Not the Booker Prize 2011."  In 2014, he was among 15 "mostly unagented writers" signed by HarperVoyager, the novels including his Exile and Pilgrim to be released digitally, followed by short-run paperback editions.
