The Meeting is a 1987 American play by Jeff Stetson about an imaginary meeting between Martin Luther King, Jr. and Malcolm X in 1965 in a hotel in Harlem during the height of the Civil Rights Movement.   The play was later televised on American Playhouse in 1989.   The Meeting won a Louis B. Mayer Award and eight 1987 NAACP Theatre Awards.   It has been performed throughout Europe and the United States.
