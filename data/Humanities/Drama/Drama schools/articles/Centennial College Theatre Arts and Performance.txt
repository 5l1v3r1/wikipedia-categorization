Theatre Arts and Performance is a professionally oriented acting training program offered within the School of Communications, Media, Arts and Design  at Centennial College, in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Story Arts Centre 951 Carlaw Avenue Performing Arts Commons 585 Dundas St E. Story Arts Centre The primary campus is the Story Arts Centre.   Text-based and seminar classes are held in classrooms on this campus, as are guest appearances and school meetings.  Studio work, including acting, voice and movement classes were originally to be held at the Toronto Centre for the Arts.   In the summer of 2017, the program moved into its new home at Daniels Spectrum  studios in Regent Park.  Theatre Arts and Performance is a 6 semester program, typically taken over 3 years.  Students normally apply through the Ontario College Application Service.   Candidates are invited for auditions and interviews beginning in February of each year, with program commencement in September of the following year.
