Freeadvice is a free US legal information web service that was established in 1997. It has won extensive awards, including having been named by Forbes in 2000 as a "Best of the Web",  by PC World in 2004, as one of the Best 125 Free Sites on the entire Web,  as one of the 100 featured sites in PC Magazine’s Best 1000 websites, as the legal site of 2009 by Access Magazine,  and to have received recognition in the New York Times  in 2001, Wall Street Journal  in 1999, and Washington Post’s reviews of legal websites in 1998.  Freeadvice is an affiliate of the Advice Company located in Sausalito, California  and was founded by attorney Gerry Goldsholle.
