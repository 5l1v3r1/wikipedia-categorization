National Security Law of People's Republic of China (Chinese: 中华人民共和国国家安全法), which was issued on July 1, 2015, is a Chinese law made up by 7 sections containing 84 articles.the authority of the PRC says that this law can protect national security of the PRC effectively and the medias controlled by the authority praise the law, while some analysts think that the authority can restrict Chinese people's freedom by this law.   Annette Lu, the formal Vice President of Taiwan, said that this law is an offense to Taiwan, and some people from Hong Kong concern about their safety while stay in the Mainland China after this law was issued.
