The word gotra means "lineage" in the Sanskrit language. While it is akin to a family name, the given name of a family is often different from its gotra; given names may reflect the traditional occupation, place of residence or other important family characteristic rather than gotra. People belonging to a particular Gotra may not be of same caste in the Hindu social system.  However, there is a notable exception among matrilineal Tulu speakers, for whom the lineages are the same across the castes. People of the same gotra are generally not allowed to marry.
