Landscape was a magazine of human geography founded by J.B. Jackson in 1951 and published three times a year in Berkeley, California until 1999.  The magazine's original subtitle was "Human Geography of the Southwest"; this was later dropped. The first five issues consisted largely of Jackson's own essays.
