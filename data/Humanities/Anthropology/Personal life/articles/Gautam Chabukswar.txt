30 AugustPimpri,Pune Professor Gautam Chabukswar (Marathi: गौतम चाबुकस्वार) is Shiv Sena politician from Pune district, Maharashtra. He is a member of the 13th Maharashtra Legislative Assembly. He represents the Pimpri Assembly Constituency.He defeated sitting Mla  Anna Bansode  who contested from Nationalist Congress Party along with other 23 candidates.
