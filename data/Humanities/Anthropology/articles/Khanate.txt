A Khanate or Khaganate is a political entity ruled by a Khan or Khagan. This political entity is typical for people from the Eurasian Steppe and it can be equivalent to tribal chiefdom, principality, kingdom or empire. After Genghis Khan established appanages for his family in the Mongol Empire during his rule (1206-1227),  his sons, daughters,  and grandsons inherited separate sections of the empire.
