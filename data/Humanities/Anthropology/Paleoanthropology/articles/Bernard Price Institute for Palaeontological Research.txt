  The Bernhard Price Institute for Palaeontological Research was established in 1945 at the University of Witwatersrand, Johannesburg, South Africa. The institute was funded by and named after Dr. Bernard Price.Palaeontologia Africana (a journal)  was issued by them where they published their research,  building on research done by their colleagues (like Schroder   on Diademodon Broomi and Young   on Stramatolite in Boetsap) years before. In 2015 the institute was renamed to the Evolutionary Studies Institute.
