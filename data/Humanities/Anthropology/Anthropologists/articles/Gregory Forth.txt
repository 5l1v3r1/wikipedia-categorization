Gregory Forth is a professor of anthropology at the University of Alberta. He earned his PhD from University of Oxford in 1980. Forth is a fellow of the Royal Society of Canada.  As a social anthropologist, Forth's position is both structuralist and interpretivist.  He is known for his contributions to ethnoscience.
