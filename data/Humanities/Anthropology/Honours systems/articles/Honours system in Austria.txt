The honours system in the Republic of Austria is a means of rewarding individuals' personal achievement, or service to  Austria by state decorations and medals.  The system consists of several types of award which were established by the National Council (Austria) and are conferred by the President of Austria in accordance with the respective laws.  Over time, the National Council of Austria has enacted a series of laws establishing the various decorations and medals which now make up the Austrian honours system.
