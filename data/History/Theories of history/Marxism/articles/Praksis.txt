Praksis is a Marxist interdisciplinary academic journal related to social sciences that has been published in Turkey since 2001. The name of the journal is taken from the Turkish spelling of the word "praxis". The journal supports historical materialism in theory. Among the most discussed topics are class struggle, contemporary Marxist currents, intellectuals, Turkish politics, and international developments. There is also a socialist band called "Praksis" from Turkey/Izmir.
