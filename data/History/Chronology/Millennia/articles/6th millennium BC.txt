 The 6th millennium BC spanned the years 6000 through 5001 BC.  It falls into the Holocene climatic optimum, with rising sea levels. Culturally, Mesopotamia is in the Pottery Neolithic (Halaf culture), and  agriculture spreads to Europe and to  Egypt.  World population grows dramatically as a result of the Neolithic Revolution, perhaps quadrupling, from about 10 to 40 million, over the course of the millennium.   farming, animal husbandry pottery, metallurgy, wheel circular ditches, henges, megaliths Neolithic religion
