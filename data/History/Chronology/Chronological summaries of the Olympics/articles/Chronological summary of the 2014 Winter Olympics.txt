This page contains a chronological summary of major events from the 2014 Winter Olympics in Sochi, Russia. In the following calendar each blue box represents one or more event competition(s), such as a qualification round, on that day. The yellow boxes represent medal-awarding finals for a sport with in each box the number of finals that were contested on that day.   Twelve new events were added for the games.  For this reason competition started a day before the opening ceremony. This marks the first time in the history of the Winter Olympics that competitions were held before the opening ceremony.   Luge
