Peter Kenez (born 1937) is a historian specializing in Russian history and Eastern Europe. He was born in Hungary and a survivor of Holocaust himself.  He also teaches courses on Soviet cinema and an interdisciplinary course on the Holocaust with literature professor Murray Baumgarten.  He has taught at the University of California, Santa Cruz  since 1966, where he is currently Professor Emeritus.  He received his PhD from Harvard where his advisor was Richard Pipes.
