Horace Pauleus Sannon (7 April 1870 - 1938 Pétion-Ville) was a Haitian historian and politician. Born in Les Cayes, Pauleus Sannon studied social-political sciences in Paris, France. He wrote several books on the history of Haiti, including Essai Historique sur la Révolution de 1843 and Histoire de Toussaint Louverture. He was a presidential candidate in 1926 and 1930.
