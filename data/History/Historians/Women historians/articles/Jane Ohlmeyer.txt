Jane Helen Ohlmeyer, MRIA is a Northern Irish historian and academic, specialising in early modern Irish and British history. Since 2003, she has been the Erasmus Smith's Professor of Modern History at Trinity College, Dublin (TCD). She has also served as Chair of the Irish Research Council since 2015. She was President of the Irish Historical Society from 2003 to 2005, and Vice-Provost for Global Relations at TCD from 2011 to 2014.      In 2011, Ohlmeyer was elected a Member of the Royal Irish Academy (MRIA), the premier all-Ireland learned society for the sciences and humanities.
