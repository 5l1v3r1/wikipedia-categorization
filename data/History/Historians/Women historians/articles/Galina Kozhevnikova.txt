Galina Vladimirovna Kozhevnikova (Russian: Галина Владимировна Кожевникова; 16 March 1974 in Kazan – 5 March 2011 in Moscow) was a Russian journalist and human rights activist.  She had been seriously ill for a long time and died age 36. Kozhevnikova was Deputy Director of the Moscow NGO SOVA Center
