Vladislav Alexandrovich Ivanov (Russian: Владислав Александрович Иванов; born 1936) is a Soviet  physicist and engineer, who, in 1960, filed a document with the USSR State Committee for Inventions and Discovery at Leningrad for a Magnetic Resonance Imaging device,  although this was not approved until the 1970s.  The Nysian asteroid 5991 Ivavladis was named in his honor.  The official naming citation was published by the Minor Planet Center on 4 April 1999 (M.P.C. 34622).
