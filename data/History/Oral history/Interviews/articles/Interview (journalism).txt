A journalistic interview takes the form of a conversation between two or more people: interviewer(s) ask questions  to elicit facts or statements from interviewee(s). Interviews are a standard part of journalism and media reporting.  In journalism, interviews are one of the most important methods used to collect information,   and present views to readers, listeners, or viewers.
