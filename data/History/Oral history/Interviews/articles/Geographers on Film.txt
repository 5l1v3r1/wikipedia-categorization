Geographers on Film is an archival series of more than 300 filmed and taped interviews with various distinguished geographers dating back to 1970. The series was created as an educational resource by geographer Maynard Weston Dow, Plymouth State University, and his wife, Nancy Freeman Dow. The series was supported in part by the Association of American Geographers (AAG),  the National Science Foundation, Plymouth State University, and the Marion and Jasper Whiting Foundation, of Boston.
