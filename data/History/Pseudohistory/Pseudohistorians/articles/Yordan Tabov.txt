 Yordan Tabov  (born in 1946 in Sofia) is a Bulgarian mathematician with academic Degrees: Doctor of Pedagogical Sciences (2007), PhD in Mathematics (MGU, Moscow, 1974)   Besides he is also an amateur historian and supports the fringe views on the autochthonous origin of the Bulgarian people, espousing ideas  similar to those of Gancho Tsenov.  Lectures at the Sofia University 1979−1989 Lectures at New Bulgarian University 1993−2009 Lectures at Bourgas Free University 1995−2003
