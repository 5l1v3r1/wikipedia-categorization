Bouches-del'Èbre was a province of the First French Empire. Louis-Gabriel Suchet, Duc d'Albufera was the military governor of Bouches del'Èbre. Bouches del'Èbre was founded on 12 February 1812. The province of Bouches-del'Èbre was in what is now modern Spain or more precisely Catalonia. http://www.worldstatesmen.org/Spanish_autonomous_communities.html
