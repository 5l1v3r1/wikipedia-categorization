The Castellani or 'Castelani', (Greek: Καστελλανοί, Kastellanoi), were an ancient Iberian or Pre-Roman people of the Iberian peninsula. They inhabited the bottom of the eastern Pyrenees in the northern Tarraconense. The Castellani are one of the groups mentioned by Claudius Ptolemy in his Geographia, book 2, chapter 5.
