Sebastian Garro y Sidrac  (14–15?) was a Basque nobleman, Viscount of Zolina, Lord of Rocafort and Sidrac.  Sebastian Garro was born in Navarra, son of a noble family. He was married to María de Gongora, daughter of an eminent Spanish family, descendants of the Marquises of Góngora.  Garro was a possible descended of Pere Arnaut de Garro, a noble knight, who served in the court of Charles II of Navarre.
