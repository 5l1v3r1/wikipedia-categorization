 León de Garro y Javier (?-?) was a Basque nobleman, Count of Javier and  Viscount of Zolina.  León was born in Pamplona, Kingdom of Navarre, son of Jerónimo de Garro and Ana de Javier, belonging to the Basque nobility.  He was married to the noble lady Inés de Coloma, daughter of don Pedro Coloma, Lord of Malon and Maria de Luna.  In 1604, León de Garro was appointed Alcalde ordinario in Pamplona.
