About · The people · The land · Language · Culture · Diaspora · Persecutions · Tribes · Cuisine Politics · Writers · Poets · Military · Religion · Sports · Battles The Flag of Hazaristan (Dari/ Hazaragi : پرچم هزارستان) includes the colors yellow, white and blue, and was originally presented on the cover of the anthology Poems for the Hazara.   The color combination of the flag symbolizes the Hazara people’s root[disambiguation needed] and origin[disambiguation needed], sky, their loyalty,  their long winter in Hazaristan and their future and development.      The Flag of Hazaristan was designed by the Hazara poet and activist Kamran Mir Hazar.
