About · The people · The land · Language · Culture · Diaspora · Persecutions · Tribes · Cuisine Politics · Writers · Poets · Military · Religion · Sports · Battles Tatar Hazara (Persian: تاتار‎) is a tribe of Hazara people. They mainly live in Maymana Kotal area of Faryab Province in Afghanistan mostly with the neighboring of Uzbek people, the same as other hazaras they have maintained all their Hazaragi cultural habits. Tatar Hazaras speak Hazaragi dialect of Persian.
