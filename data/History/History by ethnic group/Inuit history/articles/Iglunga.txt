Iglunga (previously: Iglungayut) was an Inuit settlement, now uninhabited, in Qikiqtaaluk Region, Nunavut, Canada. It is located on a Baffin Island offshore island at the southern entrance into Bon Accord Harbour,  southwest of Auyuittuq National Park Reserve. The nearest community is Pangnirtung.
