Li Kuge (Chinese: 李窟哥) was one's people of old Khitan people in Tang Dynasty period. During the Tang Dynasty period, Li Kuge (李窟哥) was just one people is old Khitan people at Tang Dynasty, he get surname Li (李) from Tang Government because distinguish oneself at a battle about Babarian in East Manchuria. Li Jinzhong was grand child of Li Kuge.
