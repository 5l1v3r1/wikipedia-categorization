A list of the monuments and the memorials at the Mount Herzl national cemetery and the Yad Vashem Holocaust memorial in Jerusalem. The Garden of the Missing in Action is a memorial garden with memorial board and memory stones. It is planned that the National Memorial Hall will be built at the entrance to the cemetery.
