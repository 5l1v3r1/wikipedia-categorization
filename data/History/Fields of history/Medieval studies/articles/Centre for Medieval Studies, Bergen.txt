The Centre for Medieval Studies at the University of Bergen, Norway was founded in 2003 by the Research Council of Norway.  The organization's site defines its goal as follows: "to enhance our understanding of Europe as a whole, in the Middle Ages as well as today, from a peripheral point of view".
