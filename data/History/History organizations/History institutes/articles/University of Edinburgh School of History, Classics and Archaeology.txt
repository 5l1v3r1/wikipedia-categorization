  The School of History, Classics and Archaeology at the University of Edinburgh is a school within the College of Humanities and Social Science.  The school is located in the William Robertson Wing of the Old Medical School buildings on Teviot Place. Classics have been taught at the university since its foundation in 1583. The school has the oldest established Chair in Scottish History. Several well-known archaeologists have graduated and taught at the school.
