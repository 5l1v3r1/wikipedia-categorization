Museum of Photography in the old town jail (Town Jail Photography Museum) - is a branch of the Tallinn City Museum, opened on 21th of July in 1980. The museum is located in the Old Town directly behind Tallinn Town Hall in the late XIV century building, where the city prison used to be.  The museum presents both permanent exhibitions and temporary exhibitions. A permanent exhibition gives an overview of photography history in Estonia from 1840 to 1940. In addition to photo collections, there is a collection of a photography equipment of the 20th century. Temporary exhibitions are held in the galleries: Lee, Project room, Annual exhibition and Secret staircase. Temporary exhibitions reflect both the contemporary fine-art photography and works of photographers of the 19-20th centuries.
