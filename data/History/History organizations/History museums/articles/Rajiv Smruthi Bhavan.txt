Coordinates: 17°43′02″N 83°19′41″E﻿ / ﻿17.717200°N 83.328175°E﻿ / 17.717200; 83.328175 Rajiv Smruthi Bhavan is a memorial and cultural center at Beach road, Pandurangapuram, Visakhapatnam. It was established in the year 2008 by the then Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh Y. S. Rajasekhara Reddy.  This memorial and cultural center is dedicated to the former Prime Minister of India Rajiv Gandhi. This venue is permanent photo exhibition of Rajiv Gandhi   and is also a center for classical and carnatic music.
