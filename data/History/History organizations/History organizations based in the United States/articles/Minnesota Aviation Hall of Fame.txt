The Minnesota Aviation Hall of Fame, Inc. is a nonprofit corporation, recognized by the State of Minnesota as a means of honoring aviation pioneers.  Minnesota Aviation Hall of Fame, Inc.  collects and maintains a biographical file on each significant Minnesota aviation personality. These files include interview tapes, photos and other memorabilia. Replicas of the award plaques given to the Minnesota Aviation Hall of Fame inductees are on display in the passenger waiting area of the Duluth International Airport Terminal.
