Clash of the Gods is a one-hour weekly mythology television series that premiered on August 3, 2009 on the History channel. The program covers many of the ancient Greek and Norse Gods, monsters and heroes including Hades, Hercules, Medusa, Minotaur, Odysseus and Zeus. Clash of the Gods: The Complete Season 1 DVD Set was released on February 16, 2010.  The Blu-ray version was released on March 16, 2010.
