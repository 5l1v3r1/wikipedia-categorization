Born in Vockenhausen, he was a major proponent of the Enlightenment in Bavaria.  He died in Waldsassen.  He was a godfather to Adam Weishaupt. Regarding personal names: Freiherr is a former title (translated as Baron). In Germany since 1919, it forms part of family names. The feminine forms are Freifrau and Freiin.
