Bergepolis (Ancient Greek: Βεργέπολις) was an ancient Greek  city located in Thrace, in the region between the river Nestos to the river Hebros. It was founded by colonists from Abdera. Its citizens were called Bergepolites.Bergepolis was an urban center dependent of Abdera.  Bergepolis survived in the Roman times.
