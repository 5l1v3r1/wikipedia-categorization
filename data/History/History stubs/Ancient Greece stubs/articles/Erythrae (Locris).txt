Coordinates: 38°24′27″N 21°56′40″E﻿ / ﻿38.40741°N 21.94436°E﻿ / 38.40741; 21.94436 Erythrae or Erythrai (Ancient Greek: Ἐρυθραί) was a town of the Locri Ozolae, probably the harbour of Eupalium.   The site of Erythrae is tentatively identified with the modern site of Monastirakion.    This article incorporates text from a publication now in the public domain: Smith, William, ed. (1854–1857).
