Kastelli Hill (also Kasteli; Greek: Λόφος Καστέλλι or Καστέλι) is a landform at the city of Chania on the island of Crete in the present day country of Greece.  The Minoan city of ancient Cydonia was centered on Kastelli Hill,  which later was selected by the Romans as the site of an acropolis.
