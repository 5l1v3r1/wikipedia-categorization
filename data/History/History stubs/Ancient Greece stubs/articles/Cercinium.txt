Coordinates: 39°37′27″N 22°41′07″E﻿ / ﻿39.6241°N 22.68522°E﻿ / 39.6241; 22.68522 Cercinium or Kerkineion (Ancient Greek: Κερκινείον) or Kerkinion (Κερκινέον)  was a town in Magnesia, in ancient Thessaly, near the Lake Boebeis.
