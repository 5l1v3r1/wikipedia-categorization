Delium (also called Delion Greek: Δήλιον Dêlion, Latin: Delium) was a Greek city  famous for its important temple dedicated to Apollo, similar to the one in Delos, thence its name. Founded by colonists of Tanagra, it was located approximately 2 km from Oropos and 8 km from Tanagra.
