Isaura Nova or Isaura Nea (Greek: Ἴσαυρα Νέα, "New Isaura") was a town of the Roman and Byzantine era,  so called in juztaposition with the settlement of Isaura Palaea.  Along with Isaura Palaea, the city was one of the two major settlements of the region of Isauria (Ἰσαυρία), now southern Turkey, and is identified with Aydoğmuş, formerly Dorla.
