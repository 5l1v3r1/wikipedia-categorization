Coordinates: 38°22′41″N 22°23′14″E﻿ / ﻿38.378004°N 22.38728°E﻿ / 38.378004; 22.38728 Chalaeum or Chalaion (Ancient Greek: Χάλαιον) or Chaleion (Χάλειον) or Chaleos (Χαλεώς)  was an ancient town on the coast of the Locri Ozolae, near the borders of Phocis.   Pliny the Elder erroneously calls it a town of Phocis.  Its site has been located near modern Galaxidi.    This article incorporates text from a publication now in the public domain: Smith, William, ed. (1854–1857). "Chalaeum".
