Cynoscephalae (Ancient Greek: Κυνὸς κεφαλαί) was a village of ancient Boeotia in the Cynoscephalae Hills. It was noted as the birthplace of Pindar.   This article incorporates text from a publication now in the public domain: Smith, William, ed. (1854–1857). "Cynoscephalae". Dictionary of Greek and Roman Geography. London: John Murray.
