Coordinates: 38°57′29″N 22°06′47″E﻿ / ﻿38.95814°N 22.11292°E﻿ / 38.95814; 22.11292 Macra Come or Makra Kome (Ancient Greek: Μακρά κώμη) was a fortress mentioned by Livy along with Sperchiae in Ainis, in ancient Thessaly.  It may have been a town of the Aenianes. Its site is at a place called Varybopi in modern day Makrakomi.    This article incorporates text from a publication now in the public domain: Smith, William, ed. (1854–1857). "Macra Come".
