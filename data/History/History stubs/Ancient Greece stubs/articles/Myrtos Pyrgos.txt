Pyrgos (also Myrtos-Pyrgos) is an archaeological site of the Minoan civilization near Myrtos in the municipality of Ierapetra on the south coast of Crete. This Minoan settlement on the road to Ierapetra boasts several Minoan features:  a drain, paved floors and footpaths and ashlar foundation blocks for its central building.
