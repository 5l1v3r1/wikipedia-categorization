Hierophylakes, also known as hierodidaskaloi, hieronomoi, or hierophantai,  were priests for the Eumolpidai  involved in performing acts of sacrifice.  Pausanias stated that new hierophantai were elected every quadrennial, and so individuals did not maintain an existence within this role for their life-time.
