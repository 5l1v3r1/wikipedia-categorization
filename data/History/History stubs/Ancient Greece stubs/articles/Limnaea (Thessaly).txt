Coordinates: 39°30′37″N 22°05′17″E﻿ / ﻿39.51028°N 22.08803°E﻿ / 39.51028; 22.08803 Limnaea or Limnaia (Ancient Greek: Λιμναία) or Limnaeum or Limnaion (Λιμναίον) was a town of Histiaeotis in ancient Thessaly, taken by the Romans in 191 BCE.  It's location is within the bounds of the modern town of Vlochos.    This article incorporates text from a publication now in the public domain: Smith, William, ed. (1854–1857). "Limnaea".
