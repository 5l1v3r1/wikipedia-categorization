Coordinates: 39°08′57″N 22°34′07″E﻿ / ﻿39.14917°N 22.56873°E﻿ / 39.14917; 22.56873 Chalai or Chalaion was a town in Phthiotis in ancient Thessaly.  The town's name is not attested directly, but s extrapolated from its demonym, Χαλαῖος.    Its site has been located at a place called Tsournati,   where archaeological remains from the Archaic and Classical periods have been found.
