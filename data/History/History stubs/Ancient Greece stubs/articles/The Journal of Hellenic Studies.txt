The Journal of Hellenic Studies is a peer-reviewed academic journal covering research in Hellenic studies. It also publishes reviews of recent books of importance to Hellenic studies. It was established in 1880 and is published annually by  Cambridge University Press on behalf of the Society for the Promotion of Hellenic Studies.
