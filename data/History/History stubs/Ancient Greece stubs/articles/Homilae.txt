Coordinates: 38°45′54″N 22°24′14″E﻿ / ﻿38.7651°N 22.4038°E﻿ / 38.7651; 22.4038 Homilae or Homilai (Ancient Greek: Ὅμιλαι) was a town in Oetaea in ancient Thessaly.  The town's name appears in an epigraph dated to 206/5 BCE as providing a hieromnemone to the Amphictyonic League on behalf of the Aetolians.  Its site is at the modern site of Kouvelo Kastro/Kastro Orias.
