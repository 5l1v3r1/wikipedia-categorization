  Manauli Fort is located in the Manauli village of Mohali district, Punjab, India. It is 11 km from Mohlali city and nearly 4 km away from International Chandigarh Airport Road Mohali. Manauli is a small village with a population of 3,919 and 693 households (2011 Census). As per land record area of the village is 738 hect. (s.no. 85).  The fort is 20 feet above the village habitation level.
