Andrew Robertshaw was born in Sheffield, England, and is an English military historian, curator, author and educator.   He is best known for his television appearances, in programmes such as Two Men in a Trench and Time Team.   He was a military advisor on the film War Horse.   During his career Robertshaw has worked as Curator/Manager of the Royal Logistic Corps Museum in Deepcut, and previously as Head of Education at The National Army Museum in London.
