 World War II Order of the Red Banner The 65th Rifle Division was an infantry division of the Soviet Union's Red Army. It fought in World War II. Poirer and Connor say it was established at Chita in February 1941. Began Barbarossa with the 12th Rifle Corps, Transbaikal Military District.
