The Marine Cryptologic Support Battalion is a military intelligence battalion of the United States Marine Corps.  The battalion headquarters are located in Fort George G. Meade, Maryland. Marine Cryptologic Support Battalion mission statement The battalion's stated purpose is "to enable and execute Signals Intelligence, Information Assurance and National-Tactical Integration activities in order to satisfy NSA/CSS, Marine Air-Ground Task Force and joint force intelligence requirements."  The Marine Cryptologic Support Battalion headquarters are located in Fort Meade, Maryland.
