IA, The Journal of the Society for Industrial Archeology is a biannual peer-reviewed academic journal published by the Society for Industrial Archeology, currently edited by Fredric L. Quivik (Michigan Technological University). IA publishes scholarly research, essays, and reviews of books published in the field of industrial archeology.  The first issue of IA was published in 1975, followed by one issue per year through volume 11 in 1985. The current biannual publication frequency began with volume 12 in 1986, although there have been several double issues.
