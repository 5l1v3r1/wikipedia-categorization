Qaqapatan (Quechua) is an archaeological site with rock paintings in Peru. It lies in the Pasco Region, Pasco Province, Yarusyacán District, at a height of 3,787 metres (12,425 ft).  Qaqapatan is situated at a height of 3,782 metres (12,408 ft) at the foot  of the mountain Qayakuna in the village Quchaq (Cochac), north of the town Cerro de Pasco.
