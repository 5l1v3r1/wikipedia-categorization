Borkowo megalithic cemetery is a prehistoric burial ground situated in the county of SÅ‚awno, in Western Pomerania, Poland.  The site consists of a passage grave, unchambered long barrows, and other tumuli.  The most impressive monument, Borkowo 1, is passage grave made with heavy boulders and capstones.  It is around 4.5 metres long, with a width of 1.5 metres.  Excavations revealed a cremation and a few pottery sherds.  The grave is assigned to the Funnelbeaker culture of the Chalcolithic Age.
