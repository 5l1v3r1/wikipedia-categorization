 Allt om Historia is a Swedish language history magazine based in Malmö, Sweden.  Allt om Historia was established in 2005.  The first issue published in September 2005.  Based in Malmö, the magazine covers articles and book reviews about historical events.  It was based in Lund and was part of Historiska media  until 1 June 2016 when it was acquired by the Bonnier Group.  It is published by the Bonnier Publications fourteen times a year.  The circulation of Allt om Historia was 26,700 copies in 2014.
