Renaissance Magazine is a glossy American magazine published bimonthly. Each issue comprises approximately 90 pages, and includes articles about the contemporary renaissance faire experience, medieval and renaissance history, castles, heraldry, cooking, and interviews with key individuals in the renaissance fair industry. It was founded in 1996 by "Lady Kimberly," a graduate of Yale University, and has its headquarters in Bridgeport, Connecticut.  The magazine is owned by Lionheart Entertainment LLC in Houston, Texas.  The magazine is the sponsor of the Annual Renaissance Festival Awards together with Celtic Music magazine.
