Aeroplane (formerly Aeroplane Monthly) is a British magazine devoted to aviation, with a focus on aviation history and preservation. Issue 1 of Aeroplane Monthly was published in May 1973 at a cover price of 30p,  in association with Flight International, by IPC Media. The founder was Richard T. Riding.  The magazine is now owned by Key Publishing Ltd and headquartered in Stamford, Lincolnshire.  The magazine is the successor to an earlier, weekly publication called The Aeroplane, founded in 1911.
