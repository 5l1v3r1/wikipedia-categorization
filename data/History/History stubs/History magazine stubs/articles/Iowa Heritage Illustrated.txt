Iowa Heritage Illustrated is the historical magazine of the State Historical Society of Iowa and is published in Iowa City.  It contains stories about Iowa history written for a lay audience, along photographs, reproductions of historic documents, and other media.  Iowa Heritage Illustrated was first published in 1920 as The Palimpsest and renamed in 1996.
