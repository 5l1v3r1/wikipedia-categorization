Jonathan Sawday (born 13 November 1956) is a British historian and author. He was born on 13 November 1956 in Leicester, United Kingdom.  He completed his PhD in literature from University College, London.  He is currently the Walter J. Ong professor of humanities at the Saint Louis University.  He is a fellow of the English Association (FEA), the Royal Society for the Arts (FRSA), and of the Royal Historical Society (FRHistS).  His books have received mostly positive reviews.
