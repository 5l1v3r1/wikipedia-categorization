  Phillip Bradley (born 18 May 1955) is an Australian military historian who has written five books as well as numerous articles for Wartime and After the Battle magazine.  He has been described as "One of the finest chroniclers of the Australian Army's role in the New Guinea campaign".  Bradley has travelled many Australian battlefields, including sites in Papua New Guinea, Turkey and France.
