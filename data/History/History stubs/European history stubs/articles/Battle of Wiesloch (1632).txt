Palatinate War (1620, 1622, 1623) 1620 campaign 1622 campaign 1623 campaign Transsylvanian invasions of Hungary (1619–21, 1623–24, 1626) Danish War (1626–1628) Swedish War (1630–1635) French-Swedish War (1635–1648) The Battle of Wiesloch (German: Schlacht bei Wiesloch) occurred on 16 August 1632 during the Thirty Years' War near the German city of Wiesloch, south of Heidelberg. A Swedish army led by Count Gustav Horn fought an army of the Holy Roman Empire led by Count Ernesto Montecuccoli. The battle resulted in a Swedish victory.
