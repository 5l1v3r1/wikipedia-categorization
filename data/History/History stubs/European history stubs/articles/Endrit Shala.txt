  Endrit Shala (Serbo-Croatian: Endrit Å alja) (born 2 December 1981 in Pristina, SFR Yugoslavia) is a Kosovo politician. He is a member of the Democratic Party of Kosovo (PDK). Shala is considered to be Fatmir Limaj's right hand and close political partner. After the Kosovo War of 1999, Shala became a member of the Democratic Party of Kosovo. Shala is Chief of Cabinet in the Ministry of Transport and Telecommunication in the Government of the Republic of Kosovo.
