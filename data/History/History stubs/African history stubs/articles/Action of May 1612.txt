This raid took place on the night of 23  or 25 May 1612 when a force of Sicilian and Neapolitan galleys attacked some Tunisian vessels at La Goulette, northern Tunisia. 7, or perhaps 9 or 10, Tunisian sailing ships were destroyed, while several smaller vessels were captured. Ships involved: Sicily (Ottavio de Aragon) 6 galleys Naples (Santa Cruz) 7 galleys 7 or more sailing ships destroyed, several smaller captured
