Zemianske Sady (old form: Nemeškert; Hungarian: Nemeskürt) is a village and municipality in Galanta District of  the Trnava Region of south-west Slovakia. In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1156. The municipality lies at an altitude of 150 metres and covers an area of 8.053 km². It has a population of about 880 people.
