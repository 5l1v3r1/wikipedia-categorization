Medgyesegyháza (Slovak: Medeš) is a town in Békés County, in the Southern Great Plain region of south-east Hungary. It covers an area of 64.32 km² and has a population of 3558 people (2015). The current mayor of Medgyesegyháza is Dr. Béla Nagy (Independent).
