Lucien Lublin, born 1909 in Brest-Litovsk and employed as electrical engineer.  A socialist Zionist who became a leader of the French Jewish Resistance during World War II with Abraham Polonski and David Knout.  Lublin had been a member of the Zionist Labor Movement before the war.  After the World War II, Lublin created the Society for Protecting Jewish Children, a charity which helped Jewish children who had survived the Holocaust and took them to Palestine.
