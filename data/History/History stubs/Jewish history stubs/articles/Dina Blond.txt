Dina Blond (1 March 1877 - 1985) was a prominent member of the Jewish Labour Bund in Poland and a prolific Yiddish translator. She was born Shayne-Feygl Szapiro in Vilna,  then a part of the Russian Empire. In the mid-1920s Blond became chairwoman of the Bund's women's organisation, the Yidisher Arbeter Froy (YAF). She was also editor of the women's page of the party newspaper, Folkstsaytung.
