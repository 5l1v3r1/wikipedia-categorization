 Aline Caroline de Rothschild, Lady Sassoon (21 October 1867 – 28 July 1909)  was a French socialite and daughter of Cécile Anspach and Baron Gustave de Rothschild of the prominent Rothschild family. She was born in Paris, where her parents had a house on Avenue Marigny.  In 1887, aged 19, she married Edward Sassoon (1856–1912).  They had the following children: Her great-great-grandson is actor Jack Huston.
