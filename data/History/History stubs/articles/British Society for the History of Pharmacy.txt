The British Society for the History of Pharmacy is the organisation in the United Kingdom devoted to the history of pharmacy.  It was established in 1967, although its roots date back to 1952, when the Council of the Pharmaceutical Society established a history of pharmacy committee. The society has published a journal, the Pharmaceutical Historian, since 1967.  It is affiliated to the British Society for the History of Medicine. The President of the society is Peter Homan.
