BAANO TV (Persian: تلویزیون بانو‎) is a commercial television station operated by Baano Media Group in Afghanistan. Launched in July 2017  , it became one of the first terrestrial women's defecated TV station in the country.  Baano tv is avialable in Kabul, Parwan, Kapisa, Logar & Maidan Wardak Province of Afghanistan   and they start thire transimision in Mazar-e-sharif in August 2018. On 1st week of Sep 2018 the channel also start their transmission on AlYahSat satellite  . Baano Media Group also operate a FM radio station for women under the name of Baano FM (Women FM).
