Joann Jorgensen (born May 1, 1957) was the United States Libertarian Party candidate for vice-president in the 1996 U.S. presidential election, the running mate of presidential candidate Harry Browne.  She was also a candidate for South Carolina's 4th congressional district in 1992 receiving 4,286 votes for 2.2%. She received a BS in Psychology at Baylor University in 1979 followed by a Master's in Business in 1980. She began her career at IBM working with computer systems.  She received a PH.D. in Industrial/Organizational Psychology from Clemson University in 2002.  She is now a Psychology lecturer at Clemson University in Clemson, SC.
