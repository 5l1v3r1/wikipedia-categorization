Jeanne Albertine Colin-De Clerck (born 9 January 1924) is a Belgian composer who also uses the name Jeanne Colin. She was born in Brussels, Belgium, and studied at Royal Conservatory of Music in Brussels.   After completing her education, she began to compose without formal instruction. She took a position teaching at the Music Academy in Anderlecht, married composer Georges Colin (1921-2002) and has two sons and two daughters. She sometimes collaborates with her husband.
