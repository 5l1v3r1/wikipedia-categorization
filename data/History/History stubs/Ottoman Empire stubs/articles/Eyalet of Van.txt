 The Eyalet of Van (Ottoman Turkish: ایالت وان‎; Eyālet-i Vān)  was an eyalet of the Ottoman Empire. The capital was Van. It was formed in 1548 as the one of the Beylerbeyliks of the Ottoman Empire.
