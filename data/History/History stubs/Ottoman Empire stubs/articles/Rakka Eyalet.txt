The eyalet of Rakka or Urfa  (Ottoman Turkish: ایالت رقه; Eyālet-i Raqqa‎)  was an eyalet of the Ottoman Empire. Its reported area in the 19th century was 24,062 square miles (62,320 km2).  The eyalet was created in 1586 on territory previously under the jurisdiction of Diyarbekir.  In the 16th century, the town of Raqqa again entered the historical record as an Ottoman customs post on the Euphrates.
