Black Stockings FC is a Turkish "defunct" football club from Istanbul, Ottoman Empire, closed in 1901. Black Stockings FC was founded by Fuat Hüsnü Kayacan, Mehmet Ali, Reşat Danyal, Rıza Tevfik, Daniş, Tahsin Nahit  in Istanbul in 1899. The club was dissolved after its first match on 26 October 1901, because the players were indicted after the sultan's detective Ali Şamil Bey and police raided the field.  The first Turkish Football player Fuat Hüsnü Kayacan played for Black Stockings FC.
