Paléorient is a French journal which is dedicated to the prehistory of Near East and published in French, German and English.  It was first published in 1973  (ISSN 1957-701X).  It is based in Paris and the publisher is Association Paléorient.  Its frequency was annual until 1974 and from 1981 is published biannually.  This journal has a high rank  among those studying Near Eastern prehistory.
