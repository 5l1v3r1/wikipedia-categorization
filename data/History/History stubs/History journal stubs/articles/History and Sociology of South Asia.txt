History and Sociology of South Asia is a forum for scholarly interrogations of significant moments in the transformation of the social, economic and political fabric of South Asian societies. The focus of the journal is to incorporate diverse areas of research, including political economy, social ecology, and issues of minority rights, gender, and the role of law in development. It is published twice a year by SAGE Publications in association with Centre for Jawaharlal Nehru Studies, Jamia Millia Islamia.
