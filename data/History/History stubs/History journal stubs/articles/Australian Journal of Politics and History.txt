The Australian Journal of Politics and History is an academic journal that includes articles about history, political studies, and international affairs, concentrating on Australia, the Asia-Pacific region, and modern Europe. It was established in 1955 and was published triennially until 1997. In 1998 it began publishing quarterly. It is currently edited by Ian Ward and Andrew Bonnell. It is published by Wiley-Blackwell.
