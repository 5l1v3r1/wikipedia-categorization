Antiguo Oriente is an annual peer-reviewed academic journal published by the Center of Studies of Ancient Near Eastern History (CEHAO) (Pontifical Catholic University of Argentina, Buenos Aires). It covers the history of societies of the Ancient Near East and the Eastern Mediterranean from the Paleolithic through the Greco-Roman period. Antiguo Oriente publishes articles and book reviews in Spanish, English and French.  The journal is indexed and/or ranked en several bibliographic databases, including the prestigious rankings Scimago , Emerging Sources Citation Index (ESCI) , and MIAR .
