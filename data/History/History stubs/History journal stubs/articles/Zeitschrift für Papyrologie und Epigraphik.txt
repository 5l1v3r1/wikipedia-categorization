The Zeitschrift für Papyrologie und Epigraphik (ZPE) is a peer-reviewed academic journal which contains articles that pertain to papyrology and epigraphy. It is published four to five times annually by the Verlag Rudolf Habelt and was established in 1967. The current editors of ZPE are Werner Eck, Helmut Engelman (de), Jürgen Hammerstaedt (de), Rudolf Kassel (de), Ludwig Koenen (de), Wolfgang Dieter Lebek (de), Klaus Maresch, Georg Petzl (de), and Cornelia Römer (de).
