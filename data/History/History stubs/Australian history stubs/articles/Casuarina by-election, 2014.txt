A by-election for the seat of Casuarina in the Northern Territory Legislative Assembly was held on 18 October 2014.  The by-election was triggered by the resignation of Labor MP Kon Vatskalis, who retained the seat at the 2012 election with a 59.3 percent primary and two-party vote.
