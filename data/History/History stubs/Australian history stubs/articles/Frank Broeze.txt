Born Franklin Jan Aart Broeze in Rijswijk, Netherlands, Broeze was a maritime historian, and was one of the founders of the Australian Association for Maritime History. The Frank Broeze Memorial Maritime History Book Prize was created by the Australian National Maritime Museum in his memory.  In addition,  a memorial lecture was inaugurated.
