 Grieving Hearts Talk: The Matthew Churchill Story is a book written by TC Badcock about the tragedy    that happened on the Bauline Line in Portugal Cove, a town just outside St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada.  It has been featured in many local newspapers and magazines.  In 2005, Matthew Churchill was with a friend when he was struck by a drunk driver. Grieving Hearts Talk: The Matthew Churchill Story takes you through the lives of his family after the accident and shows another side the media didn't show to the trials in court.
