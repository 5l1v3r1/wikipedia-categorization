  The Camera Museum (Malay: Muzium Kamera, Tamil : நிழற்படக் கருவி அருங்காட்சியகம்) is a museum in George Town, Penang, Malaysia.     The museum started its operation on 31 August 2013 and was officiated on 9 October 2013 by Penang Chief Minister Lim Guan Eng.  The museum is housed in a two-story shop house building, which is divided into Dark Room, Obscura Room and Pinhole Room.  It also features a cafe and souvenir shop.  The museum displays up to 1,000 vintage cameras and accessories. The museum regularly holds exhibition and festival events every month.
