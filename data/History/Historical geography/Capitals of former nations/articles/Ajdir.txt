Ajdir (Arabic: أجدير; Berber: ⴰⵊⴷⵉⵔ) is a small town in Morocco near Al Hoceima. It was the capital of the Republic of the Rif from 1922-1926 under the leadership of Abd el-Krim (d.1963).
