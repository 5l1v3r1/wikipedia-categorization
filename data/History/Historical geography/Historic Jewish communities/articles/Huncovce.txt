Huncovce (Slovak pronunciation: [ˈɦuntsɔʊ̯tsɛ]; Hungarian: Hunfalva, til 1902: Hunfalu, German: Hunsdorf, Hunszdorf, Hundsdorf in der Zips, Hunzdorf, Hunesdorf,  Yiddish: אונסדאָרףֿ‎ Unsdorf, Hebrew: אונסדורף‎, Latin: Villa Canis, Hunisvilla) is a village and municipality in Kežmarok District in the Prešov Region of north Slovakia. The municipality lies at an altitude of 639 metres and covers an area of 13.262 km². It has a population of about 2400 people.
