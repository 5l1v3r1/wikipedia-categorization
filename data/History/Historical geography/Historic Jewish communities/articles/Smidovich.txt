Smidovich (Russian: Смидович) is an urban locality (an urban-type settlement) and the administrative center of Smidovichsky District of the Jewish Autonomous Oblast, Russia. Population: 5,120 (2010 Census);  5,905 (2002 Census);  6,646 (1989 Census).  It is an early Jewish settlement, which was founded in 1928  named after Pyotr Smidovich who, along with Mikhail Kalinin, came up with the idea to found the Jewish Autonomous Oblast.  Classifies by Köppen-Geiger system as humid continental (Dwb). Summers are warm and rainy, while winters are very cold and dry. The bandy club Urozhay  played in the Russian Bandy Supreme League, the second highest division, until 2015-16. Its home arena was the Lokomotiv Stadium.
