Gerald A. Danzer (born 9 November 1938) is professor emeritus of history at the University of Chicago. He is a specialist in historical geography, world history, and the use of old maps as historical sources. Gerald Danzer was born on 9 November 1938. He received his Ph.D from Northwestern University in 1967 for a dissertation on the subject of "America's Roots in the Past: Historical Publication in America to 1860".  Danzer is professor emeritus of history at the University of Illinois at Chicago  where he has taught an MA program for teachers of history for over thirty years.  He is a specialist in historical geography, world history, and the use of old maps as historical sources. He is a former director of the Chicago Neighborhood History Project.
