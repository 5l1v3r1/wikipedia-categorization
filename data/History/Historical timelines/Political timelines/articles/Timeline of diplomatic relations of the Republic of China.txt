Numerous states have ceased their diplomatic recognition of the Republic of China during the last 60 years - since the founding of the People's Republic of China. Timeline of diplomatic relations of the Republic of China begins between from 1912 to 1949 (the establishment of the People's Republic of China), and the year of the Taipei Economic and Cultural Representative Office (TECRO) establishments as the replacement in the absence of embassies and official diplomatic relations with the ROC.
