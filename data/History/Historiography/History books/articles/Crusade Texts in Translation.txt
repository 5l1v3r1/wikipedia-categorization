Crusade Texts in Translation  is a book series of English translations of texts about the Crusades published by Ashgate in Farnham, Surrey and Burlington, Vermont. Publication began in May 1996.   The editors of the series, all from the United Kingdom, are Malcolm Barber, University of Reading; Peter Edbury, Cardiff University; Bernard Hamilton, University of Nottingham; Norman Housley, University of Leicester; and  Peter Jackson, University of Keele.  It contains a large corpus of texts concerning the history of the Crusades and the Crusader states. Many of the texts have not been translated into English before.
