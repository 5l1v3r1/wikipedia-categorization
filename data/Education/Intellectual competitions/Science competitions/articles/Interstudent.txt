INTERSTUDENT is a competition for the best foreign student in Poland, which is organized by Perspektywy Education Foundation. It is open to all foreign students studying at Polish universities and other higher education institutions.  Currently, about 72 thousand foreign students from 166 countries are studying in Poland .
