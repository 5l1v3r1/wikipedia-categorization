Time for Kids (or TFK) is a division magazine of Time magazine that is produced especially for children. It contains some national news, a "Cartoon of the Week", and other features in its weekly eight pages. The headquarters is in Tampa, Florida.  It is distributed in various schools across the United States. The magazine also runs special edition issues, and a website which offers daily news coverage and is the home of the TFK "Kid Reporter" program. There is a TFK edition of the trivia game Don't Quote Me, which has won several awards.
