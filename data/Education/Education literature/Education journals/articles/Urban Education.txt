Urban Education is a bimonthly peer-reviewed academic journal that covers the field of urban education. The journal's editor-in-chief is H. Richard Milner (University of Pittsburgh). It was established in 1965 and is published by Sage Publications. The journal is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2017 impact factor is 2.1, ranking it 14th out of 40 journals in the category "Urban Studies"  and 49th out of 238 journals in the category "Education & Educational Research".
