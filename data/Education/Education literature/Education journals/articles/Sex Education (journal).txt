Sex Education: Sexuality, Society and Learning is a triannual peer-reviewed academic journal covering sex education. It was established in 2001, with Michael Reiss (University of London) as the founding editor-in-chief.  It is published by Routledge and the current editor-in-chief is Peter Aggleton (University of New South Wales). According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2015 impact factor of 0.505, ranking it 179th out of 230 journals in the category "Education & Educational Research".
