The Journal of Cases in Educational Leadership is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal covering the field of education administration. The editors-in-chief are Zorka Karanxha, William Black, and Vonzell Agosto (University of South Florida). It was established in 1998 and is published by Sage Publications in association with the University Council for Educational Administration. The journal is abstracted and indexed in Educational Administration Abstracts, ERIC - Education Resources Information Center, and NISC.[clarification needed]
