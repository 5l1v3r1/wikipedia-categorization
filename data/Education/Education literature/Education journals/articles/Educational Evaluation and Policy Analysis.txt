Educational Evaluation and Policy Analysis is a peer-reviewed academic journal covering all aspects of educational policy analysis. It was established in 1979 and is published by Sage Publications on behalf of the American Educational Research Association. The 2016 - 2018 editors are Joshua Cowen (Michigan State University), Cassandra Guarino (University of California, Riverside), Spyros Konstantopoulos (Michigan State University), and Peter Youngs (University of Virginia). The 2019 - 2021 editors are Joseph R. Cimpian (New York University), Julie A. Marsh (University of Southern California), Paco Martorell (University of California, Davis), and Morgan Polikoff (University of Southern California).  Educational Evaluation and Policy Analysis (EEPA) publishes scholarly manuscripts of theoretical, methodological, or policy interest to those engaged in educational policy analysis, evaluation, and decision making. EEPA is a multidisciplinary policy journal, and considers original research from multiple disciplines, theoretical orientations, and methodologies.  The journal is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2017 impact factor is 2.486, ranking it 30th out of 238 journals in the category "Education & Educational Research".
