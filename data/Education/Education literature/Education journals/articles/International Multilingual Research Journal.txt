The International Multilingual Research Journal is a peer-reviewed academic journal in linguistics and education, focusing on the study of multilingualism. The current editorial team consists of Jeff MacSwan as Editor-in-Chief and Associate Editors Jeff Bale, Mileidis Gort, and Megan Madigan Peercy.  The journal was established in 2007 with Terrence G. Wiley and Alfredo Artiles as founding editors. The journal is abstracted and indexed in: Linguistics and Language Behavior Abstracts and ERIC.
