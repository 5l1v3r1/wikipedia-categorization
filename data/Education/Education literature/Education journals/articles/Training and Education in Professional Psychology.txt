Training and Education in Professional Psychology is a peer-reviewed academic journal published by the American Psychological Association on behalf of the Association of Psychology Postdoctoral and Internship Centers. It was established in 2006 and "is dedicated to enhancing supervision and training provided by psychologists."  The current editor-in-chief is Michael C. Roberts of the University of Kansas. The journal is abstracted and indexed by the Social Science Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2016 impact factor of 0.810, ranking it 50th out of 58 journals in the category "Psychology, Educational".
