Reading Research Quarterly is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal published by Wiley-Blackwell. The current editors are Linda Gambrell (Clemson University) and Susan Neuman (New York University). The journal is one of three journals published on behalf of the International Literacy Association. According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2011 impact factor of 2.697, ranking it 5th out of 203 journals in the category "Education & Educational Research"  and 5th out of 51 journals in the category "Psychology, Educational".
