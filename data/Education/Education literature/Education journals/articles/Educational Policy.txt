Educational Policy is a bimonthly peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes papers in the field of education policy. The editor-in-chief is Ana M. Martinez Aleman (Boston College). The journal was established in 1987 and is published by Sage Publications. The journal is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2017 impact factor is 1.586, ranking it 93 out of 238 journals in the category "Education and Educational Research".
