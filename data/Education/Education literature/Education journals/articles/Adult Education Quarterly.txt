Adult Education Quarterly is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal covering the field of education. It was established in 1950 and is published by Sage Publications on behalf of the American Association of Adult and Continuing Education. As of  2014[update], the editors-in-chief have been Jeff Zacharakis (Kansas State University), Leona M. English (St. Francis Xavier University), Catherine A. Hansman (Cleveland State University), and Qi Sun (University of Wyoming). The journal is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2017 impact factor is 1.257, ranking it 138th out of 238 journals in the category "Education & Educational Research".
