 Comunicar is a quarterly peer-reviewed open access academic journal covering research on education and communication. It is bilingual in Spanish and English and has summaries in Chinese and Portuguese. The journal was established in 1993 and is published by Grupo Comunicar. Since 2016 it is published quarterly. Its editor-in-chief is Ignacio Aguaded. The journal is abstracted and indexed in the Social Sciences Citation Index  and Scopus.  According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2016 impact factor of 2.212.
