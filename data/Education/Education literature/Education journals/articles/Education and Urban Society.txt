Education and Urban Society is a bimonthly peer-reviewed academic journal that covers research on the role of education in contemporary society. The editor-in-chief is Charles J. Russo (University of Dayton). It was established in 1968 and is published by Sage Publications. The journal  is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2017 impact factor is 0.658, ranking it 202 out of 238 journals in the category "Education and Educational Research"  and 35 out of 40 journals in the category "Urban Studies"
