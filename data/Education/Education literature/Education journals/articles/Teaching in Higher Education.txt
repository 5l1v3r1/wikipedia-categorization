Teaching in Higher Education is a peer-reviewed academic journal publishing articles on teaching in the setting of higher education. It was established in 1996 and is published eight times per year by Taylor & Francis. The founding editor-in-chief was Len Barton (UCL Institute of Education),  and the current one is Penny Jane Burke (University of Newcastle). According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2017 impact factor of 1.316, ranking it 126th out of 236 journals in the category "Education & Educational Research".
