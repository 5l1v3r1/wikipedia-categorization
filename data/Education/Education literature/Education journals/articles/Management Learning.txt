Management Learning is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes papers in the field of management. The journal's editors are Emma Bell (Keele University) and Todd Bridgman (Victoria University of Wellington). The journal was established in 1970 and is published by SAGE Publications. The journal is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2015 impact factor is 1.393, ranking it 90 out of 192 journals in the category "Management".
