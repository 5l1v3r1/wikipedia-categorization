 Teaching Boys and Young Men of Color: A Guidebook (2016) is a teaching and learning guidebook by J. Luke Wood and Frank Harris III and published by Lawndale Hill. The publisher says: "This guidebook reports on recommendations from a national study of teachers with a proven record of success in teaching boys and young men of color. Recommendations for relationship-building, teaching and learning practices, and classroom management are extended."
