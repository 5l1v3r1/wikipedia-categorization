The Board of Secondary Education, Karachi (BSEK) is a government board in Karachi for secondary education examination. It was established in 1974 by the Government of Sindh through the Sindh Boards of Intermediate and Secondary Education amendment act No. 20 of 1973 along with Board of Intermediate Education, Karachi. BSEK controls and organizes the secondary education examinations in Karachi.
