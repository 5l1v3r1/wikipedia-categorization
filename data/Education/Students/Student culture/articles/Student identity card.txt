Student identity card is any identity card which may be used to verify aspects of a student's personal identity. In Poland, the school student ID (Polish: legitymacja szkolna) is the document certifying that the person is attending a primary or secondary school in Poland. For student of age at which no ID card or passport is issued, this document certifies his identity. With this you can have official discounts on public transport. Polish student ID contains first name, last name, birth date, address,  PESEL and photo. Document size: 72×103 mm and photography: 30×42 mm. Students of higher education (e.g., university) are issued the student ID (Polish: legitymacja studencka). The preschool students have their own type of card as well.
