(Mid-High/Highschool)Calgary, Alberta, T2N 4Y3 (Mid-High),  Westmount Charter School is a charter school based in Calgary, Alberta, Canada which educates students from kindergarten to grade 12. It was founded in 1996 as ABC (Action for Bright Children) Charter Public School, a school for gifted children. The school started with 116 students, from grades one to three, and is now hosting a student body of approximately 1,200 in grades kindergarten through 12.  There are also 73 teachers currently at the school . The name was officially changed from ABC Charter Public School to Westmount Charter School in 2002, though the school remains publicly funded. Westmount's first graduation occurred in June 2006.  As of 2017, 25.1% of students at the elementary campus are ESL and 13.5% have special needs.  19.9% of students at the mid-high campus are ESL and 13.4% have special needs.
