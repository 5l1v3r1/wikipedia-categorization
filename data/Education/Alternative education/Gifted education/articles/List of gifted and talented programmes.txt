List of gifted and talented programs is a list of gifted education programs located all across the world. Alberta British Columbia Ontario Quebec Saskatchewan As of 2002, only 37 US states have laws requiring that some services be made available for the gifted.  Of these, approximately 28 require that the services must be adequate to meet to the educational needs of every gifted student. There is one federal law with respect to gifted education. The Jacob K. Javits Gifted & Talented Student Education Act of 1988 was renewed as part of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act in 1994 and as part of the No Child Left Behind Act of 2001.
