Menlo Park Academy is a Kindergarten-8th Grade gifted school (With advanced high school classes) located in Cleveland, Ohio. It is a tuition-free community school that serves the area of Northeastern Ohio. When it was founded on September 23, 2008, the number enrolled was 38. In 2016 the amount enrolled is about 350. It is located at 2149 West 53rd Street.  It is currently located at the former Joseph and Feiss warehouse building on West 53rd St. in Cleveland, Ohio, in the United States.  It recently renovated the Joseph & Feiss building.
