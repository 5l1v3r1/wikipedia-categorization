Shahid Beheshti school is located in Sabzevar, Iran. The school, which include middle and high schools, is among nationwide schools developed specifically to provide a superior education for exceptionally talented students in Iran, and is managed by the  National Organization for Development of Exceptional Talents (NODET). Students are selected through a nationwide entrance exam that is designed to measure intelligence. Students study subjects in depth, similar to college courses. Shahid Beheshti Schools are boys-only, while Farzanegan is the equivalent school for girls.
