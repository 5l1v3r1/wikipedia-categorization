Wendy Priesnitz is a Canadian alternative education and environmental advocate.   She was leader of the Green Party of Canada from July 1996 to January 1997, when she abruptly resigned. She is known for her advocacy of homeschooling/unschooling and home-based/green business.  She founded the Canadian Alliance of Home Schoolers in 1979, and is the author of numerous books on homeschooling. Since 1976, she has co-owned and edited Natural Life, an award-winning sustainable lifestyles magazine. In 2002, she founded Life Learning Magazine, which she owns and edits. She is listed in Canadian Who's Who and Who's Who of Canadian Women.
