Tracy Packiam Alloway is a British cognitive psychologist known for her research on working memory. She is an associate professor of psychology at the University of North Florida, where she is also the director of the graduate program in psychology.  She is the developer of the world's first working memory test designed for use by educators.  Previously, she was the director of the Center for Memory and Learning in the Lifespan at the University of Stirling in the United Kingdom.
