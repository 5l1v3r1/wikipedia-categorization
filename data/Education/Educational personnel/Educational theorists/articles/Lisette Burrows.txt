  Lisette C. Burrows is a New Zealand physical education academic. She is currently a full professor at the University of Waikato. After a 1999 PhD from the  University of Wollongong titled  'Developmental discourses in school physical education,'  Burrows worked at the University of Otago from 1991,   rising to full professor in 2015  before moving to the University of Waikato in 2017.
