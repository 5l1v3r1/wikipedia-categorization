The Task teaching style is an option available to students under Student-Directed Teaching, a progressive teaching technology that aims to give the student a greater sense of ownership in his or her own education. This teaching style is "for those students who required formal instruction and yet are capable of making some choice as to the appropriate practice for them to master the objective."  This formal instruction happens at the same time as the Command students.
