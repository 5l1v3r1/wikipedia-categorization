Coordinates: 25°1′56.79″N 121°30′41.16″E﻿ / ﻿25.0324417°N 121.5114333°E﻿ / 25.0324417; 121.5114333 The National Education Radio (NER; Chinese: 國立教育廣播電台; pinyin: Guólì Jiàoyù Guǎngbō Diàntái) is a government owned radio station in the Taiwan (ROC) and is located in the Zhongzheng District of Taipei City, Taiwan next to the Taipei Botanical Garden and National Museum of History.  The radio station was founded on 29 March 1960. The Headquarters building has been a registered historic landmark since 2009.
