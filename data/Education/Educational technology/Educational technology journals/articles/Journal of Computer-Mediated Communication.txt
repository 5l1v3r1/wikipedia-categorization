The Journal of Computer-Mediated Communication JCMC is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal that covers the interdisciplinary field of computer-mediated communication. It was established in 1994 and is published by Oxford University Press on behalf of the International Communication Association. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2017 5 year impact factor was  5.629, placing it as one of the highest ranked communication journals.
