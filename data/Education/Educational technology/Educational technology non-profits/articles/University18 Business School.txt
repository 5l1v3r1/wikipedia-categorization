University18 Business School is a 'not for profit' Online Management Education Institution based out of India. A collaboration between the Sampuran Prakash Foundation and University18,  UBS in partnership with the Karnataka State Open University, India, provides accredited post graduate degree programs to working professionals and other distance learners.
