Jabbertalky is a 1981 video game published by Automated Simulations. Jabbertalky is programmable word game which includes three games and a method of creating a vocabulary for use in these games.  Ron Boerger reviewed Jabbertalky in The Space Gamer No. 48.  Boerger commented that "Unless you are (a) crazy about word games, (b) want to buy every game for the Apple, or (c) both of the above, don't waste your money on this one."
