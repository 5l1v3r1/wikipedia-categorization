Digital learning is any type of learning that is accompanied by technology or by instructional practice that makes effective use of technology. It encompasses the application of a wide spectrum of practices including: blended and virtual learning.   Sometimes confused with online learning or e-learning, digital learning encompasses the aforementioned concepts.
