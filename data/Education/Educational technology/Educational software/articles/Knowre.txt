Knowre is an education technology company founded in Seoul and headquartered in New York City. Knowre launched a nationwide campaign with schools to pilot its online adaptive math program for Pre-Algebra and Algebra I in 2013.  In 2012, Knowre was awarded the grand prize at Global K-Startup, a competition sponsored by Google, the Korean Internet & Security Agency, and the Korea Communications Commission.  Knowre was called one of four “South Korean Startups to Watch.”  In May 2013, Knowre won first place in the GapApp Challenge sponsored by the New York City Department of Education.
