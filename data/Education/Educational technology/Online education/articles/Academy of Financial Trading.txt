Academy of Financial Trading is an online education company that provides education, training, cyber tutorials and learning resources in the academic field of trading in the world’s financial markets.   Academy of Financial Trading was formed in early 2012 by James Egan and Adrian Murphy in Dublin, Ireland. The Academy of Financial Trading provides several tailored courses in online financial trading. It offers a range of courses in the discipline of financial trading depending on the student in questions level of experience. The courses focus on providing the technical information and psychological foundation required for trading on the world’s financial markets.
