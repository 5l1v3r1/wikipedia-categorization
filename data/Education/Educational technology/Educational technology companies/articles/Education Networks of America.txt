Education Networks of America (ENA) is a private company providing internet services to public schools and libraries. It is based in Nashville, Tennessee, in the United States.  ENA was founded in 1996, and as of 2014 is estimated to generate $100 million in revenue annually.  It is now owned by Zelnick Media Capital, an investment company based in New York.   On January 18, 2018, TeleQuality Communications (TeleQuality) of San Antonio, Texas became a subsidiary of ENA   On June 28, 2018, CatchOn became part of ENA family of companies
