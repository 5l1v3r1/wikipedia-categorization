Intellipaat is an e-learning and professional certification company for software developers, IT administrators, and other professionals, headquartered in Bangalore, India.  The training is offered in three major modes: Online instructor Led Trainings, Self-paced e-learning trainings, and Corporate Trainings. The company offers more than 150 courses on skills like Big Data, Data Science, Business Intelligence, Mobile Development, Databases, and Cloud Computing. Intellipaat conducts corporate training programmes for clients including Ericsson, Genpact, Wipro, Cognizant and TCS, and has trained more than 7,00,000 professionals .
