The Commission for Higher Education was established in 1985[where?] by an Act of Parliament, Universities Act, CAP 210B as a body corporate to make better provisions for the advancement and quality assurance of university education. Quality university education that is accessible and sustainable. To oversee quality assurance and expansion of university education ensuring sustainability, affordability and relevance. The Commission is committed to ensuring increased access to sustainable quality higher education and training through planning, coordination, resource mobilisation, quality assurance enhancement and information service.
