Established in 1965, Greater Lawrence Technical School is a four-year regional technical high school, located in Andover, Massachusetts, United States. It serves the communities of Andover, Lawrence, Methuen, and North Andover. As of September 2017, GLTS offers 21 CTE programs, organized into three academies. Business, Consumer Services and Transportation Academy (B.C.S.T.) Construction Technology Academy  Medical, Engineering, Technology and Arts Academy (M.E.T.A.)
