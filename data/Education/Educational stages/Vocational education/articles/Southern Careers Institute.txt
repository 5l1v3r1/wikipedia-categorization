Southern Careers Institute is a private, for-profit school with seven locations across Texas. The school was founded in 1960. The school is accredited by the Commission of the Council on Occupational Education. Southern Careers Institute is approved by the Texas Workforce Commission and the Texas Department of Assistive and Rehabilitative Services. The programs are approved  for VA funding by the Veterans Administration. Between 1992 and 2014, Southern Careers Institute has increased the number of campuses across Texas.
