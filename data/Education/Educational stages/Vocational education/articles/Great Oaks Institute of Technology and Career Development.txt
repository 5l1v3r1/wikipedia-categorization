The Great Oaks Institute of Technology and Career Development, or Great Oaks, is a joint vocational school district that has operated in parts of southwestern Ohio since 1970. During their junior and senior years of high school, students from 36 Ohio school districts have the option to attend one of four Great Oaks campuses, known as Career Development Centers: In addition, the district operates the Center for Economic Opportunity in Washington Court House and the Highland County Area IDEA Center in Hillsboro.  Great Oaks also provides satellite programs to their affiliate schools. The district owned a student-run FM radio station, WJVS, until 2012.
