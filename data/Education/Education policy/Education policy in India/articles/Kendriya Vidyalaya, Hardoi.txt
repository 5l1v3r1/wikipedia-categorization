The Kendriya Vidyalaya is a central government school in Hardoi that is instituted under the aegis of the Ministry of  Human Resource Development (MHRD). The literal of Kendriya Vidyalaya is "central school". It is affialiated Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. The school came into existence in 2015. This school belongs to one of world's largest chains of schools, Kendriya Vidyalayas. The Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan, which literally translates to 'Central School Organisation', oversees the functioning of the school. Shri Santosh Kumar Mall is the commissioner of the school. Shri Pulkit Khare (District Magistrate of Hardoi District) is the head of the thirteen membered Vidyalaya Management Committee (VMC) of the school. Sh.K.L Kishore is the Principal of the school. Sanskrit is taught as a compulsory subject from class VI-VIII.
