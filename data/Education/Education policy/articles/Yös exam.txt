The YÖS Examination is an entrance examination designed for foreign students wishing to study in higher education institutions in Turkey.  This examination was set and administered by the Student Selection and Placement Centre (ÖSYM) until 2010, when universities began conducting their own versions of the exam.  The results of this exam apply only for students wishing to enrol in undergraduate programmes. Students presently enrolled in undergraduate programmes who seek a transfer, and also those who wish to follow postgraduate programmes, should apply directly to the institutions of their choice. The YÖS Examination is composed of two tests: Approximately 9,200 candidates from various countries take the YÖS Examination.
