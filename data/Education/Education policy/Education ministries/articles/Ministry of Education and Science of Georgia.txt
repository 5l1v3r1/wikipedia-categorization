 The Ministry of Education and Science of Georgia (Georgian: საქართველოს განათლებისა და მეცნიერების სამინისტრო) is a governmental body responsible for education system and children's services in Georgia. Ministry of Education works under the Minister of Education and Science of Georgia. The ministry is located on Uzandze street in Tbilisi in a historical building built in Mauritanic style. Tamar Sanikidze's resignation was announced on 3 June 2016. Her proposed replacement is Aleksandre Jejelava.
