The Ministry of Education of the Republic of China (MOE; Chinese: 中華民國教育部; pinyin: Zhōnghuá Mínguó Jiàoyùbù) is the ministry responsible for incorporating educational policies and managing public schools in the Republic of China (Taiwan) and has Overseas Education Divisions all over the world.
