The Universities and University Colleges Act 1971 (Malay: Akta Universiti dan Kolej Universiti 1971), is a Malaysian laws which enacted to provide for the establishment, maintenance and administration of Universities and University Colleges and for other matters connected with it. The Universities and University Colleges Act 1971, in its current form (1 August 2012), consists of 5 Parts containing 27 sections and 2 schedules (including 6 amendments).
