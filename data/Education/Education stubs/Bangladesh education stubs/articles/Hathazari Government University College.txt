 Hathazari Government University College (Bengali: হাটহাজারী সরকারি বিশ্ববিদ্যালয় কলেজ) is a government-run, honors-level, degree college in Hathazari Upazila, Chittagong District, Bangladesh.  It was founded in 1968.  In 2017, the Directorate of Secondary and Higher Education ordered that the college, which had been private, be nationalized.  The campus is located on the south side of the Chittagong-Rangamati Highway in Hathazari. Total enrollment for the three divisions of the college is 3,375.  As of 2017, the principal is Mohammad Kafil Uddin.
