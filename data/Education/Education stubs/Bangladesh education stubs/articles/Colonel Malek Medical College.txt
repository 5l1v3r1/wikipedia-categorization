Colonel Malek Medical College, Manikganj  (Bengali: মানিকগন্জ মেডিকেল কলেজ) is a government medical school in Bangladesh, established in 2014. It is located in Manikganj.  Academic activities began in 26 January 2015 with 51 students.  It offers 5 years of MBBS course and admits 51 students every year.
