University Laboratory School and College also known as ULAB, is a school and college located in Dhaka, Bangladesh.   It is situation in the campus of Institute of Education and Research, Dhaka University, Nilkhet Road, Dhaka and is under the institutes management. It is a part of Institute of Education and Research(IER). Institute of Education and Research control this school and college. website:- http://ulab.du.ac.bd/
