Mainamoti Medical College is a private medical college in Baropara, Comilla, Bangladesh.  The college was established on 21 February in 2011.  It was fined 10 million taka in November 2016 by Bangladesh High Court for admitting students that did not meet the admission requirements for medical college set by health ministry.
