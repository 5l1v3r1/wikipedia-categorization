The Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Mymensingh is an autonomous organization that is responsible for holding public examinations (Junior School Certificate (J.S.C.), Secondary School Certificate (S.S.C.), and Higher Secondary (School) Certificate (H.S.C)) in four districts of Mymensingh Division.
