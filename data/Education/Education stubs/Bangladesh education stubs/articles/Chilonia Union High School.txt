Chilonia Union High School (Bengali: ছিলোনিয়া ইউনিয়ন উচ্চ বিদ্যালয়) is a secondary school located in Chilonia Bazaar, Arjuntala Union of Senbagh Upazila, Noakhali District, Bangladesh.   The school offers education for students ranging from 6 to 10. Usually students are admitted in the sixth grade. This high school follows the  National Education Curriculum of Bangladesh (NCTB).
