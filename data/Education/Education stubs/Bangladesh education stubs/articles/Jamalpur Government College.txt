Jamalpur Government College, commonly referred to as Government Ashek Mahmud College Jamalpur or Government Jaheda Safir Women college Jamalpur, is an institution of higher education in Bangladesh, located in the Jamalpur district. Govt. Ashek Mahmud college was established on August 10, 1946. It currently offers HSC, Graduate and post Graduate courses. The college offers many courses under three main categories of faculty.
