Shahid Syed Nazrul Islam College (Bengali: শহীদ সৈয়দ নজরুল ইসলাম কলেজ) is a college in Mymensingh, Bangladesh, founded in 1999. The college was named after Shahid Syed Nazrul Islam, who was the temporary President in 1971 when Sheikh Mujibur Rahman had been captured by Pakistanies. The college is close to the old Bramhaputra river and Circuit house. The college has a reputation that every year its students get more GPA 5.00 than those admitted students who got GPA 5.00.
