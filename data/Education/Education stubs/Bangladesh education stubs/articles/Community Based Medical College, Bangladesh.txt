English Community Based Medical College Bangladesh (CBMCB) (Bengali: কমিউনিটি বেজড মেডিকেল কলেজ বাংলাদেশ) is a private medical school in Bangladesh, established in 1995. It is one of the best private medical school in Bangladesh.  It offers a five-year course of study leading to a Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) degree. A one-year internship after graduation is compulsory for  all graduates. The degree is recognised by the  Bangladesh Medical and Dental Council and outside the country,  globally it is also recognised,as there are so many foreigner students in Cbmcb.
