 Gohira Degree College (Bengali: গহিরা ডিগ্রী কলেজ) is a private, pass-level, degree college in Raozan Upazila, Chittagong District, Bangladesh.  It was founded in 1968 by writer Mahbub Ul Alam Choudhury.  The campus is located on the south side of the Chittagong-Rangamati Highway in Gohira. The college sports ground has been selected as the site for the construction of Sheikh Russell Mini Stadium.  Another project underway is the construction of a BDT 12.1 million (USD 150,000) four-story information technology building.  The college transitioned to an online admissions process in 2015.
