  Coordinates: 19°11′07″N 72°51′15″E﻿ / ﻿19.185312°N 72.854285°E﻿ / 19.185312; 72.854285 Children's Academy is a Mumbai-based chain of private co-educational English-medium day schools at Malad and Kandivali in Mumbai. The first school was founded in 1970 by Shri V.V. Bhat  in Malad. Currently the schooling of pupils is through affiliations by choice of SSC of MSBSHSE, Pune and ICSE of Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations, New Delhi. The school caters for pupils from nursery levels to the Std. 10th grade examinations.It has more than 2000 students studying in the school.Malad branch is ranked 7th best school in western mumbai. Founded in 1970, the Children’s Academy group of schools owes its inception to the profound vision of its founder, the late Shri V.V. Bhat (15 June 1930 – 15 August 2001). The school aims at providing a learning environment that leads to joyful learning and holistic development amongst students.
