Tabani's School of Accountancy (Urdu: تابانی اسکول آف اکاؤنٹنسی‎, abbreviated as: TSA) is an accountancy academy situated in Karachi, Pakistan. It was established in 1998 to provide accounting and financial study in Pakistan. It is registered with ICAP as Registered Accounting Education Tutor (RAET).    Till date, TSA has 2 main divisions: "CA" and "ACCA" plus a subsidiary "Tabani's College" previous known as "Institute of Business Education (IBE)".
