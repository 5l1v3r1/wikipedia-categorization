Puranik was born in Bilagi, a village in the Bagalkot district. He was a creative fiction writer. Some of his writings were made into successful Kannada and Tamil movies. They include "Dharma Devate" that was made into Karuneye Kutumbada Kannu, Kulavadhu, Sanaadi Appanna, Muttaide, and Halunda Tavaru. Puranik was a poet and also a short story writer.
