 Anastasios Lavrentzos (Greek: Αναστάσιος Λαυρέντζος, born 25 June 1966 Piraeus, Greece) is a Greek physicist, economist, and writer. Lavrentzos holds a BSc in Physics from National Kapodistrian University (Athens, Greece); a MSc in Physics  from the University of Crete (2002); and an MSc in Business Mathematics from National Kapodistrian University of Athens. Lavrentzos has worked as a risk management director at the National Bank of Greece Securities SA and at Millennium Bank. He currently teaches Banking Management in the post graduate Program "Mathematics of Production and Capital Markets" at the Athens University of Economics and Business.
