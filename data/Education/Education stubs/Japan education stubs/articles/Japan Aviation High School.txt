The Japan Aviation High School (日本航空高等学校, Nihon Kōkū Kōtō Gakkō) is a private high school in Kai, Yamanashi, Japan. It was established by Japan Aviation in partnership with Japan Airlines as a corporate educational institution to promote the teaching of industrial technology. Most graduates go to a special advanced school established as an annex. Very few continue to the university level.
