  Tsuen Wan Public Ho Chuen Yiu Memorial College (TWPHCYMC; Chinese: 荃灣公立何傳耀紀念中學 or 何傳耀 in short) is an English-language (EMI) secondary school located at Shek Wai Kok Estate, Tsuen Wan, Hong Kong. The school was founded in 1977 and moved to its current campus in 1978. Computer-assisted Learning Centre, Computerized Music Room, Computerized Art Room, Computer Room, Student Union Room, Student Activity Centre, etc. All classrooms are equipped with computers and projectors. All rooms are air-conditioned. There are 4 houses, Integrity, Elegance, Loyalty and Benevolence. More than 10 clubs and societies of academic, service, sports or other interests are also organized.
