Tung Wah Group of Hospitals Kap Yan Directors' College (Chinese: 東華三院甲寅年總理中學) is a Hong Kong secondary school. Situated in Choi Yuen Estate, Sheung Shui, New Territories, the grammar school is fully subsidized by Government of Hong Kong. The school adopts English as the medium of instruction (EMI). Established in 1982 and governed by Tung Wah Group of Hospitals, the oldest and largest charitable organization in Hong Kong, the school is the eleventh secondary school established by the organization.
