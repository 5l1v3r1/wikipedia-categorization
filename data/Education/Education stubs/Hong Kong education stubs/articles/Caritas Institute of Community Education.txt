  Caritas Institute of Community Education (Chinese: 明愛社區書院) is a self-financing institution of higher education based in Hong Kong.  It was created in January, 2014, by combining all community education schools managed by Caritas Hong Kong. The Institution aims to provide a combination of sub-degree and vocational training programmes.  Courses offered by CICE are recognised by the Hong Kong Council for Accreditation of Academic and Vocational Qualifications up to QF Level 4
