  Tung Wah Group of Hospitals Wong Fut Nam College (Chinese: 東華三院黃笏南中學) is a secondary school in Hong Kong. Entirely subsidized by Government of Hong Kong, the day school is located in Oxford Road, Kowloon Tong, Kowloon. It was established in 1961, making the school become the first secondary school established by Tung Wah Group of Hospitals, the oldest and largest charitable organization in Hong Kong. It was initially named as Tung Wah Group of Hospitals Number One School upon the school's foundation in 1961. It was then bestowed the current name after Wong Fut-nam donated HK$500,000 in autumn 1971. In 2017, the group operated a two-weeks Joint-School trip for students to engage in English learning environment in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.
