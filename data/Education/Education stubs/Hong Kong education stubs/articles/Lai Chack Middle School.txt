  Lai Chack Middle School (Chinese: 麗澤中學) is a secondary school in Tsim Sha Tsui, Kowloon, Hong Kong.  Founded in 1929, it started as a girls' school in Wan Chai and a branch in Jordan Road.  With various relocation in its history, its secondary school section finally settled in the current premises at No. 180 Canton Road in 1955.
