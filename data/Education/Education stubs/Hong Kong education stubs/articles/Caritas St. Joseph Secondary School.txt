  Caritas St. Joseph Secondary School (Chinese: 明愛聖約瑟中學) is vocational training secondary school in Tsing Yi Estate, Tsing Yi Island, Hong Kong.  It was formerly, Caritas St. Joseph Prevocational School (明愛聖若瑟職業先修學校), a prevocational school established in Tsuen Wan by Caritas Hong Kong in 1961 and moved to the island in 1989.
