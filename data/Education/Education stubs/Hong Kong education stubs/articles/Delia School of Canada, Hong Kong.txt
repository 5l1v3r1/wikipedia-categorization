Coordinates: 22°17′07″N 114°13′06″E﻿ / ﻿22.28528°N 114.21833°E﻿ / 22.28528; 114.21833   The Delia School of Canada, Hong Kong (Chinese: 地利亞加拿大學校), is a Canadian International school in Tai Koo Shing, Hong Kong Island, Hong Kong. The school is registered in the province of Ontario as an international private school, and has students from over 48 countries. It offers kindergarten, primary and secondary education in 3 separate buildings. The school was first intended as a local school for the new residential development in Tai Koo Shing.
