  Buddhist Wong Fung Ling College (BWFLC) is a secondary school in Causeway Bay, Wan Chai District, Hong Kong. It first opened in 1959.  A government-aided school, it is sponsored by the Hong Kong Buddhist Association.  The school campus is about 4,600 square metres (50,000 sq ft) in size.  It has a student activity centre, a Chinese room, two halls, and an interactive laboratory.  Its students include Chinese and those from other countries, such as India, Indonesia, Pakistan, the Philippines, Thailand, and the United Kingdom.
