Tai Po Methodist School (Chinese: 大埔循道衛理小學; Jyutping: daai6 bou3 ceon4 dou6 wai6 lei5 siu2 hok6), founded in 1992, was the first Protestant, full-time primary school belonging to the Methodist Church, Hong Kong. There are 30 classes in total, and about 1,000 students and 47 teachers.
