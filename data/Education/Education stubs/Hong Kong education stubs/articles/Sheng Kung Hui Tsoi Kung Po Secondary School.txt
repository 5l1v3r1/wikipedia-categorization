  Sheng Kung Hui Tsoi Kung Po Secondary School (Chinese: 聖公會蔡功譜中學) is a secondary school in Homantin, Kowloon, Hong Kong. It is managed by the Hong Kong Sheng Kung Hui, Hong Kong. This school was founded in 1982. It is a Co-educational secondary School. Sheng Kung Hui Tsoi Kung Po Secondary School is an Anglican church school. School motto is: "Faithful to God and Charitable to Man." They are committed to providing students with a Christian education that develops students spiritually, morally, intellectually, physically, socially and aesthetically; to instill in them a spirit of self and lifelong learning, a civilized temperament, good character and a sense to serve their families, society, nation and the world.
