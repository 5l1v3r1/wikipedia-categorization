Chen Cheng-siang (simplified Chinese: 陈正祥; traditional Chinese: 陳正祥; pinyin: Chén Zhèngxiáng) (1922 – 2003)  was a Chinese geographer. He was Professor of Geography and Director of the Geographical Research Center at the Chinese University of Hong Kong. He wrote the entry on China for Encyclopædia Britannica.
