  Hong Kong Federation of Education Workers (HKFEW; Chinese: 香港教育工作者聯會) is a pro-Beijing teachers union in Hong Kong.   Established in 1975, it is the second-largest teachers union in Hong Kong, after Hong Kong Professional Teachers' Union. The union had over 26,000 members, as of early 2017.  The incumbent president is Wong Kwan-yu, also the supervisor of HKFEW Wong Cho Bau Secondary School.
