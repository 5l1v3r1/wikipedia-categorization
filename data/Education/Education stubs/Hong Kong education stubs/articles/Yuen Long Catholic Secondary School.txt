  Yuen Long Catholic Secondary School  (Chinese: 元朗天主教中學),  which was established in 1995 by Catholic Diocese of Hong Kong, is a subsidized co-educational school located in Hong Kong. Being a vocational school since 1995, it was changed into a grammar school in 2002. It held 22 classes in 2007-2008.
