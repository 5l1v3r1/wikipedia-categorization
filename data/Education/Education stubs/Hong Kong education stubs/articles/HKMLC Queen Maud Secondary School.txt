  HKMLC Queen Maud Secondary School (Chinese: 慕德中學) is located in Hau Tak Estate, Tseung Kwan O, New Territories, Hong Kong. It is a memorial school for the Norwegian queen Maud of Wales. The school was founded in 1950. The Houses are named admiration, righteousness, faith, love, virtue and benevolence.
