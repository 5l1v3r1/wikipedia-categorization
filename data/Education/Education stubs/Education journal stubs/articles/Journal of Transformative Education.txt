The Journal of Transformative Education is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes papers four times a year in the field of Education. The journal's editor is John M. Dirkx (Michigan State University). It has been in publication since 2003 and is currently published by SAGE Publications. The Journal of Transformative Education focuses on advancing the understanding, practice and experience of transformative education. The journal publishes articles that may test, build on and elaborate existing theoretical perspectives. The Journal of Transformative Education aims to explore the international and cross-cultural issues of the theory and practice of transformative learning.
