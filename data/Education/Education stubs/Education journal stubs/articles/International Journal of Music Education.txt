International Journal of Music Education is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal covering the field of music education. The editors-in-chief are Ruth Brittin (University of the Pacific), Beatriz Ilari (University of Southern California), Scott Harrison (Griffith University), and Bo Wah Leung (Hong Kong Institute of Education). It was established in 1983 and is currently published by Sage Publications on behalf of the International Society for Music Education.
