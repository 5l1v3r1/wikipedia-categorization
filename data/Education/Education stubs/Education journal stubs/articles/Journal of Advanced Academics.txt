The Journal of Advanced Academics is a quarterly  peer-reviewed academic journal covering the field of gifted education, including broader issues in the education of academically advanced learners, such as advanced placement and international baccalaureate programming, academically focused charter and magnet schools, and honors programming in the college or university setting. The editors-in-chief are Michael S. Matthews (University of North Carolina at Charlotte) and Matthew McBee (East Tennessee State University).
