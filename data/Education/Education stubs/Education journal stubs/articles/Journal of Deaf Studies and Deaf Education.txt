The Journal of Deaf Studies and Deaf Education is a peer-reviewed academic journal covering basic and applied research relating to individuals who are deaf, including developmental, cultural, educational, and linguistic topics. The journal is indexed in CINAHL, Current Contents/Social and Behavioral Sciences, Education Research Abstracts, E-psyche, Linguistics & Language Behavior Abstracts, ProQuest, PsycLIT, Social Sciences Citation Index, and Studies on Women and Gender Abstracts.Accorsding to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2009 impact factor of 1.375, ranking it 12th out of 29 journals in the category "Education, Special" and 18th out of 52 in the category "Rehabilitation".
