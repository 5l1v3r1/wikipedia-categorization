The Foundation Review is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes articles for foundation staff. It covers evaluation results, tools, issues confronting the philanthropic sector, and reflective practice. The journal's editor in chief is Teri Behrens of Grand Valley State University. It was established in 2009 and is published by the Dorothy A. Johnson Center for Philanthropy. The Foundation Review article abstracts are included in these databases Scopus, ProQuest, Gale, and Emerging Sources Citation Index.
