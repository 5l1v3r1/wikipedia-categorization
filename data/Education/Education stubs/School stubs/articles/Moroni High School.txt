Moroni High School  is a private Mormon secondary school in South Tarawa, Kiribati, serving forms 1 through 7. The mascot is the warrior.  It opened in 1972 as the Ataria Kokoi Auriaria School (AKAS) and originally had primary school grades. It became the Moroni Community School after the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints became the school's owner. The name changed as the school transitioned into being a secondary school.
