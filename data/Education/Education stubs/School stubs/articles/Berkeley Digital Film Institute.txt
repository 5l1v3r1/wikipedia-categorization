Coordinates: 37°51′30.5″N 122°17′25.84″W﻿ / ﻿37.858472°N 122.2905111°W﻿ / 37.858472; -122.2905111 The Berkeley Digital Film Institute is a San Francisco Bay Area film school founded in early 2007 by Patrick Kriwanek, Dean of Education. The program is a 16-month program with emphasis on Producing and Directing for Motion Pictures, Television, Commercials and Music Videos.    Kriwanek, who was the head of the Academy of Art's Motion Picture division was instrumental in growing the program from 79 to 900 students before leaving in 1999.
