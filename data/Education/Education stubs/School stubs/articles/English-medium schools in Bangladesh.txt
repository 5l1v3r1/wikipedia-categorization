English medium school is one of the education systems in Bangladesh that follows mostly the curriculum of Edexcel and Cambridge of UK and other curriculums of other countries also. All the textbooks of every subject are taught in English except for Bengali and religious subjects which is taught in Arabic in some schools.  The exams of the O-level and A-level students of Edexcel and Cambridge are conducted through British Council Bangladesh.  St Francis Xavier's Green Herald International School is the first English medium school in Bangladesh which was established in 1912.
