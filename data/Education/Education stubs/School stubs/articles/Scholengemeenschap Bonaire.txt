Scholengemeenschap Bonaire (SGB) is a secondary school in Bonaire with multiple campuses in the capital Kralendijk; one for Liseo Boneriano Bovenbouw, and another for Liseo Boneriano Onderbouw, VMBO Vakcollege, and SLP - Pro.  Scholengemeenschap Bonaire serves as Bonaire's secondary school, for ages 12-18.  It is a Dutch-medium school. As of  2018[update] it has over 1,500 students including those of Dutch Caribbean, European Dutch, and other origins.
