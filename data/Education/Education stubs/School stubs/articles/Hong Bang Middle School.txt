Hong Bang Middle School was established in 1933 by French colonialists as a pension school for French children studying with local Vietnamese students in Saigon (Ho Chi Minh City), THCS Hong Bang (Hong Bang Middle School) has since become one of the oldest and most well-known public middle schools in Ho Chi Minh City.   Apart from the normal classes for all students, the school also offers intensive classes specialized in the sciences, mathematics and foreign languages for students upon a rigorous competitive exam at the entrance of grade 6 (start of middle school). The school is known today for its long-tradition excellence in teaching and academics, as well as its historical architecture dated back to the French time.
