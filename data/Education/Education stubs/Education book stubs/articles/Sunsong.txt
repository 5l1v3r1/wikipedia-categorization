Sunsong is an anthology series of secondary school textbooks published by Longman Caribbean, from 1987. The last book in the series, Sunsong: Tide Rising has been discontinued, but the Books 1-3 remain in use in Caribbean schools. The books consist of a vast selection of poems from some of the literary genre's most famous writers, from the Caribbean and abroad. The books' editors were Pamela Mordecai and Grace Walker Gordon.
