Ryan C. Bailey  is an American professor of chemistry at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.  Bailey joined the Department of Chemistry in 2006 as Assistant Professor and was promoted to Associate Professor with tenure in 2012.  In 2011, he was received the Sloan Research Fellowship from the Alfred P. Sloan Foundation.
