Kyari Mohammed is a Nigerian academic, a professor and Vice chancellor of Moddibo Adama University of Technology (MAUTECH), which was formerly Federal University of Technology (FUT), Yola, Adamawa State. He resumed office as the replacement for Prof. Bashir Usman Hanna  Mohammed had his PhD in 1995 at the University of Ibadan, Nigeria.
