Markus Nikolaus Andreas Bockmuehl (born 29 December 1961) is a Biblical scholar specialising in Early Christianity. He has been the Dean Ireland's Professor of the Exegesis of Holy Scripture at the University of Oxford since 2014, and a Fellow of Keble College, Oxford since 2007. He previously taught at Regent College, University of British Columbia, at the University of Cambridge, and at the University of St Andrews.
