Eli Hirsch is an American philosopher and the Charles Goldman Professor of Philosophy at Brandeis University.  He is best known for his work in meta-ontology.  He coined the phrase "soft ontology" and has authored numerous books such as The Concept of Identity and Dividing Reality. Hirsch received a Ph.D. and M.A. from NYU, and a B.A. from CUNY Brooklyn College. He has written 10 published works.
