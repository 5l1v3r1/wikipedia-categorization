Rose McDermott is the David and Marianna Fisher University Professor of International Relations at Brown University; she has also taught at Cornell, UCSB, and Harvard.  She is a member of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences and the recipient of numerous awards and fellowships.  She is the author of more than 100 academic articles, 25 book chapters, and five books;  her work is situated at the intersection of several disciplines including political science, psychology, biology, methods, and development and gender studies. In November 2016, McDermott gave a TEDx talk entitled 'The Genetics of Politics'.
