Raluca Radulescu is professor of medieval literature at Bangor University. She is a specialist in Arthurian and non-Arthurian romances including Sir Thomas Malory and pious romances, medieval chronicles, political culture and gentry studies.  Radulescu received her BA at the University of Bucharest and her MPhil and PhD at the University of Manchester.
