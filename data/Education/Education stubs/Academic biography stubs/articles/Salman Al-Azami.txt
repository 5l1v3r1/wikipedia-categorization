Salman Al-Azami is a British-Bangladeshi author and academic at Liverpool Hope University, where he is a seniorlecturer in English language. He obtained his BA, MA, and PhD at Aligarh Muslim University in India.  Dr Al-Azami is the author a number of works including Religion in the Media: A Linguistic Analysis, published by Palgrave Macmillan.  He frequently publishes articles on matters pertaining to religion, media, and his native Bangladesh, in a variety of online media outlets including The Huffington Post, OpenDemocracy and Anadolu Agency.    He has also published several academic articles.
