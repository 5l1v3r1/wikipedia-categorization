The Rev. Canon Henry Thomas Bowlby, M.A.,  (1864â€“1940), was headmaster of the English public school Lancing College, 1909-25. Bowlby was educated at Charterhouse School and was an Exhibitioner of Balliol College, Oxford. Oxford University Athletics Club 1884-87, President 1886. Assistant Master at Eton College 1887-1909 Canon of Chichester 1925-30. Father of Capt. Henry Russell Bowlby, also a Master at Lancing.  He was succeeded as Headmaster of Lancing by Cuthbert Harold Blakiston. Died 7 February 1940.  He was the son of Henry Bond Bowlby, Bishop Suffragan of Coventry.
