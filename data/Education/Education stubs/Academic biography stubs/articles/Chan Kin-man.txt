Chan Kin-man (Chinese: 陳健民, born 1959  in Hong Kong) is an associate professor of the Chinese University of Hong Kong.  He is one of the founders of Occupy Central Campaign to strive for universal suffrage of Hong Kong Chief Executive Election in 2017.  He graduated from Bachelor of Social Science in Sociology of the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 1983, and Master of Arts, Master of Philosophy and Doctor of Philosophy in Sociology of Yale University in 1990, 1991 and 1995 respectively.
