Jean Robillard is the vice president for medical affairs at the University of Iowa. He is also the dean of the Roy J. and Lucille A. Carver College of Medicine. Following the retirement of Sally Mason, he served as the interim president from August  to November 2015 before Bruce Harreld took over. A pediatric nephrologist, he earned bachelor’s and medical degrees from the Université de Montréal.  # denotes an acting or interim president
