Luciano Rebay (April 23, 1928 - July 14, 2014), known especially for his work on the poet Giuseppe Ungaretti, was one of the leading post-war critics of Italian literature in America. Rebay had a long affiliation with Columbia University, where he was the Giuseppe Ungaretti Professor of Italian and, from 2005 until his death, the Giuseppe Ungaretti Professor Emeritus.
