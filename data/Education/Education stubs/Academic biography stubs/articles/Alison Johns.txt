Alison Johns is the chief executive of the Leadership Foundation for Higher Education (LFHE),  and a past president of the Association of University Administrators (AUA).  In December 2017 she was named as the CEO-designate of a new organisation which will consist of the former LFHE, the Equality Challenge Unit (ECU) and the Higher Education Academy (HEA).   Johns has a masters in management learning from Lancaster University where she studied part-time in the 1990s.
