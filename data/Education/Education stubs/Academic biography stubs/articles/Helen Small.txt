Helen Small FBA is a Professor of English Literature at the University of Oxford and a Fellow of Pembroke College, Oxford. Helen W. Small was awarded a B.A. in English from Victoria University of Wellington  and a Ph.D. from the University of Cambridge. She was the recipient of a Leverhulme Research Fellowship from 2001 to 2004. She attended Queen Margaret College in 1970-1982 and was Prefect and Dux in her final year.
