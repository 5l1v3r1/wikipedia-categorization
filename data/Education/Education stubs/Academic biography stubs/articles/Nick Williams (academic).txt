Nicholas Edward Williams is a management scholar at the University of Leeds Business School where he is an Associate Professor in Enterprise.  He is based at the Centre Enterprise and Entrepreneurship Studies where he is Director of the MSc Entrepreneurship with Study Abroad and Director of the Research in Enterprise and Entrepreneurship Studies (REES) group. Williams' research interests centre on entrepreneurship and economic development, including entrepreneurship in crisis economies, new born states and transition countries. He is also interested in enterprise policy and economic resilience.
