Bogomil Ferfila (born 7 June 1951) is a Slovenian political scientist, economist, and publicist. Ferfila is founder and head of the American, German and Global Studies graduate program, chair of the "Policy Analysis and Public Administration" and a member of the Centre for Political Science Research at the Faculty of Social Sciences of University of Ljubljana.
