 Professor Simon Ho Shun-man is the current President of the Hang Seng Management College. He took over the office from the founding President of the college, Chui Hong-sheung, in 2014.  Before that, he was Vice Rector (Academic Affairs) of the University of Macau in 2009-2014 and Dean of School of Business of the Hong Kong Baptist University, where he founded the Centre for Corporate Governance and Financial Policy (CCGFP).
