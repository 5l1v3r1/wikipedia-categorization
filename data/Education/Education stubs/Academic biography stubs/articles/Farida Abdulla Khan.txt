  Dr. Farida Abdulla Khan is a professor and Dean of Educational Studies at Jamia Millia Islamia, Delhi, India.  She has a Ph.D. in developmental psychology and had taught in many other institutes, including Delhi University, JNU, etc.  Besides that she is also a member of National Commission for Minorities since 2013.  She actively write on Kashmir issues and educational issues of backward communities in general and minorities in particular.
