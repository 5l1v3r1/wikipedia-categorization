Frank H. Berkshire is a retired professor of dynamics at Imperial College London.  He is known for being the co-author of Classical Mechanics. Amongst his students, he is well-known for his charismatic charm, and entertaining anecdotes during his lectures. He also supervised a very successful M2R project in Summer 2018 on Orbital Precession, in which the students adknowledged his willingness to provide a great deal of support and advice throughout.
