Edward Scott Geller (born February 7, 1942) is a behavioral psychologist, and currently an Alumni Distinguished Professor of Psychology at Virginia Tech and Director of the Center for Applied Behavior Systems.   Stemmings work in behavior-based safety, he is founder of the idea of "Actively Caring".
