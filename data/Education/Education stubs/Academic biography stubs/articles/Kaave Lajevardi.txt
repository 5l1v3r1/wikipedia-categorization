Kaave Lajevardi (Persian: کاوه لاجوردی‎) is an Iranian philosopher. He is known for his expertise on metaphysics of modalities  and translating some classic philosophy books into Persian.   Lajevardi was a faculty member of Institute for Research in Fundamental Sciences (2009-2012).  On 18 May 2011, he had a debate with Bijan Abdolkarimi about continental-analytic divide in Mofid University.  Lajevardi has published some papers on academic dishonesty in Iranian universities.
