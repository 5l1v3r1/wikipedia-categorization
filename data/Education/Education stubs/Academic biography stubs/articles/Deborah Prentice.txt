Deborah Prentice is a scholar of psychology and a university administrator. She serves as provost at Princeton University  and Alexander Stewart 1886 Professor of Psychology and Public Affairs.  Prentice began at Princeton in 1988, becoming assistant professor in 1989.  Prior to becoming provost on July 1, 2017, Prentice served as Dean of the Faculty from 2014 to 2017.  Her research focuses on the social norms.  Prentice is married with three children.
