Burt Goldberg is a  Professor of Chemistry at New York University. Goldberg received his B.S. from Pace University, an M.Phil from Mt. Sinai School of Medicine, and a Ph.D. from the University of Cardiff. He has received the  S.H. Hutner Award for Research Contributions to Protozoology (2004).  He has been a visiting faculty member at Barnard College.    He is one of the faculty at NYU to plan to deliver all of his lectures on the web in a general course, with the class time used instead for discussions.  His course,  "The Body: How It Works" will be first taught in this format in 2011, but the lectures are expected to be available to the public in 2010.
