The Morelia Institute of Technology (in Spanish: Instituto Tecnológico de Morelia), also known as Morelia Tech (Tec de Morelia), is a public university in Morelia, Michoacán, México founded in 1964. Students can create student groups and participate in a number of events running every year. They can choose to take many recreational activities in the areas of athletics and the arts.
