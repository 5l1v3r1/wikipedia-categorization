CEB (or Centro de Estudios de Bachillerato) is a chain of Mid to high-level Schools since these schools offer programs to upgrade the regular degree to a technical-professional level (In Mexico known as "Preparatoria") in 21 States and the Federal District. All CEB jointly with the PFLC and all PREFECO high schools are part of the Technical school of the DGB, and are dependent of Secretaría de Educación Pública of Mexico.  There are 34 CEB in México
