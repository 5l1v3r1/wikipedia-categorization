Centro de Estudios Tecnológicos en Aguas Continentales (CETAC) is a chain of mid to high-level schools in Mexico. These schools offer programs to upgrade the regular degree to a technical-professional level, in Mexico known as "Preparatoria", in two States. All CETAC and CETMAR high schools are part of the technical school of the DGECYTM and are dependent of Secretaría de Educación Pública.  There are two CETAC in México
