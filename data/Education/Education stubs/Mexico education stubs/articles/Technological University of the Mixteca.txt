The Technological University of the Mixteca (Spanish: Universidad Tecnológica de la Mixteca) (UTM), is a Mexican public university belonging to the SUNEO (Spanish: Sistema de Universidades Estatales de Oaxaca). UTM is located in Huajuapan de León, Oaxaca, region of the Mixteca, Mexico. Its main areas of focus include: teaching, research, cultural diffusion and economic development.
