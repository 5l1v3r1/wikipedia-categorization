The Autonomous University of Campeche (in Spanish: Universidad Autónoma de Campeche, UACAM) is a Mexican public university based in the city of Campeche, Campeche, that has several campuses across the state. It was founded in 1965 as the Universidad del Sudeste (University of the Southeast).  Its library holds over 18,000 volumes.
