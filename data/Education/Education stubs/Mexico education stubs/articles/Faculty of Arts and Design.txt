The Faculty of Arts and Design (formerly known as the Escuela Nacional de Artes Plásticas or ENAP),  is a college of art in Xochimilco, Mexico City.  The school is part the Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México, and is responsible for teaching painting, sculpture and graphic design, with undergraduate and graduate studies in San Carlos.  It is regarded as one of the best art schools in Mexico.  The Escuela Nacional de Artes Plásticas was spun off from the Academy of San Carlos.
