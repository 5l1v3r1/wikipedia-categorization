The Monterrey Institute of Technology and Higher Education, Colima Campus (in Spanish: Instituto Tecnológico y de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey, Campus Colima) commonly shortened as Tecnológico de Monterrey, Campus Colima or ITESM Campus Colima, is a campus of the Monterrey Institute of Technology and Higher Education private university system in Colima, Colima, Mexico. The campus is sponsored by Educación Superior de Colima: a non-profit organization founded in 1980 by Guillermo Ramos Brun.  It has an extension of 5.2 hectares (13 acres)  and concentrates on high school education while offering post-graduate courses through the institute's Virtual University.
