CETMAR (or Centro de Estudios Tecnológicos del Mar) is a chain of Mid to high-level Schools since these schools offer programs to upgrade the regular degree to a technical-professional level (In Mexico known as "Preparatoria") in 16 States. All CETMAR jointly with all CETAC high schools are part of the Technical school of the DGECYTM, and are dependent of Secretaría de Educación Pública of Mexico.  The CETIS offers technical careers is several fields like accountancy, construction, education, cosmetology, tourism, etc. These careers differ in each school. There are 30 CETMAR in México
