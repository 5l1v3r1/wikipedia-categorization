The National Catholic Student Coalition is an organization made up of Catholic Campus Ministry groups at colleges or universities throughout the United States. The National Catholic Student Coalition is a partner with regional, national, and international associations whose missions are aligned with its own.  Its partners include: The NCSC is divided into four regions.  The West, Midwest, South, and Northeast.  Each region has a color associated with it, those being yellow, blue, green, and red respectively.  Each region has its own leadership team, headed by a regional chairperson who reports to the national team.
