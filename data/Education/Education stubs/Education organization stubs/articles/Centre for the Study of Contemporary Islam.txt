The Centre for the Study of Contemporary Islam (full official name in Italian Centro Studi sull'Islam Contemporaneo, CSIC) is an Italian institute specialised in Islamic studies. It is a private association and its headquarters are in Rome. The Centre's activities branch out in two main directions: MENA and APAC programmes, making research, publications, and events. CSIC is an impartial scientific benchmark for all those interested in international issues: from members of the political and cultural worlds to operators in the business community, public administrations, international bodies and non governmental organisations.
