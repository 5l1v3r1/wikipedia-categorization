The Supreme Student Councils Confederation or abbreviated as "SSCC" is the highest student governing body of Batangas State University. It was composed of different Supreme Student Council Officers from its 10 campuses that also represent the students of said University. The SSCC just amended its own Constitution and By-Laws (CB-L) last January 24, 2016, and now undergoes the process of ratification for full implementation of this CB-L.
