The Test Assessing Secondary Completion, or TASC, is a high school equivalency exam. It was chosen by the states of New York and Indiana as a replacement for the GED exam, effective January 2, 2014.     The TASC is administered by Data Recognition Corp. and was created by McGraw-Hill Education.  As of July 2016, the TASC is approved in 13 states. (in the United States).
