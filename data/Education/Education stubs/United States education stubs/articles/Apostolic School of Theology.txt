Apostolic School of Theology (AST) is a private online Apostolic Bible college based in Elk Grove, California.  It has an enrollment of about 375 students. It provides accredited undergraduate and graduate programs to Apostolic Pentecostals.  Currently, AST is the only Apostolic school that provides a regionally accredited degree program in the world  and has been endorsed by the Worldwide Pentecostal Fellowship. Since 1984, AST has worked to enable students to fulfill their potential and purpose in the church and in the world and has a special concern for bringing the benefits of Christian higher education to apostolic Pentecostals. From 2009 through 2016, AST was affiliated with Hope International University.  It is now affiliated with Wilson University, a division of Patten University.
