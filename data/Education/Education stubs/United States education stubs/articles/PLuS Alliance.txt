PLuS Alliance describes the educational cluster model formed by three academic research universities in different parts of the world, aiming to undertake cross-border research collaborations to tackle globally-important issues related to social justice, health, innovation and sustainability   The PLuS Alliance is a collaborative effort of three universities: King's College London, Arizona State University,  and UNSW Sydney (the University of New South Wales. ) Together these three institutions comprise a body of over 15,000 staff, 150,000 students and more than one billion dollars in research funds.
