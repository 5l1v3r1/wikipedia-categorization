Family Album, U.S.A. is a 1991 book by George Lefferts that later became a television course teaching English on an example of American everyday life. It was published by Maxwell Macmillan International Publishing Group. The television series was directed by Jo Anne Sedwick and Merrill M. Mazuer and produced by Alvin Cooperman. The storyline narrates the Stewarts' family life in New York City. Richard Stewart is a professional photographer who decided to make a book of pictures about the USA, called "Family Album USA". The show ran 26 episodes, each preceded by a short summary with an open question over the possible end of the episode and concluded by educational material. Each episode was separated into three acts. Episode titles: Family Album, USA
