The Idaho State Department of Education, also known as the Idaho State Board of Education is the state education agency of Idaho. It is headquartered in Boise. They own and operate Idaho Public Television.  Sherri Ybarra is the Idaho Superintendent of Education. Current  members of the board include: Emma Atchley Linda Clark Debbie Critchfield David Hill  Andrew Scoggin Don Soltman Richard Westerberg
