Las Cruces Public Schools (LCPS) is a school district headquartered in Las Cruces, New Mexico. The District covers the city of Las Cruces as well as White Sands Missile Range, the settlement of Do√±a Ana, and the town of Mesilla. The system has 24 elementary schools, eight middle schools, and seven high schools. Of the high schools, San Andres High School and Arrowhead Park Early College High School are alternative high schools, and Mesilla Valley Training Center is a vocational high school. There are nearly 25,000 students and 3,600 employees.  LCPS is the second largest school district in New Mexico.
