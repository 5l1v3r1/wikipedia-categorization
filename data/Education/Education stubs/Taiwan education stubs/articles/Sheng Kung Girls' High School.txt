The Sheng Kung Girls' High School (Chinese: 聖功女子高級中學) is an all-girl junior and senior high school located in Tainan City, Taiwan. It was founded in Tienjin, China, by the Catholic Sheng Kung Concent, and moved to its present location in 1964. It was founded as an elementary school in 1941 in the former French Protectorate of Tien Jing.
