Coordinates: 24°15′54.4″N 120°34′28.4″E﻿ / ﻿24.265111°N 120.574556°E﻿ / 24.265111; 120.574556 The National Qingshui Senior High School (Chinese: 國立清水高中; pinyin: Guólì Qīngshuǐ Gāozhōng) is the only one high school in Qingshui District, Taichung, Taiwan. It has 1961 pupils in 42 classes.  The school was founded in March 1946.  The school is accessible within walking distance east of Qingshui Station of Taiwan Railways Administration.
