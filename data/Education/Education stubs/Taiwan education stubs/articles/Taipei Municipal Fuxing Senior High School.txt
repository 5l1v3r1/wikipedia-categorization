The Taipei Municipal Fuxing Senior High School (Chinese: 臺北市立復興高級中學; pinyin: Táiběi Shìlì Fùxīng Gāojí Zhōngxué) is a high school in Beitou District, Taipei, Taiwan. The school was established in 1953. The school is accessible within walking distance north of Xinbeitou Station of Taipei Metro.
