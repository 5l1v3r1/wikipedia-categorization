Tsoying Senior High School (TYHS; traditional Chinese: 左營高中; simplified Chinese: 左营高中; pinyin: Zuǒyíng Gāozhōng) is a senior high school in Zuoying District, Kaohsiung, Taiwan. Lin Hwai-min established the Tsoying Dance Division In 1984.  The division has studies for Chinese opera and for Western styles of dance, including ballet and modern dance.
