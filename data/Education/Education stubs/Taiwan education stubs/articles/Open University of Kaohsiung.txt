The Open University of Kaohsiung (OUK; Chinese: 高雄市立空中大學) is an open university in Siaogang District, Kaohsiung, Taiwan. OUK is an agency of the Kaohsiung City Government. The OUK was originally established in 1997 as the Opening University of Kaohsiung. In 2008, it was renamed to Open University of Kaohsiung.  Open University of Kaohsiung
