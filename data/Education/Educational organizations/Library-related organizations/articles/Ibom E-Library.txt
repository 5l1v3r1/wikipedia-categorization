The Ibom E-Library  is a library located in Uyo, Akwa Ibom State, Nigeria.  The library is the first of its kind in West Africa.  Ibom E-Library was built on September 25, 2007, by Akwa Ibom State Government under the leadership of Godswill Akpabio  and commissioned by Nigerian President in July 27, 2012. The E-Library has a multi-functional Conference centre used for conferences and summits.  The Library also accommodates small startups as an incubation system.  Official Website
