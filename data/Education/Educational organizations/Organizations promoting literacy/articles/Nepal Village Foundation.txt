NVF (Nepal Village Foundation) is a UK based registered charity.  (1158504) The main aims of the charity is to improve the well-being of poor and Dalit in rural villages of Nepal by enabling access to education and learning. Nepal is a developing country with high unemployment and low female literacy,  particularly in rural villages. NVF was established in November 2007 to improve female literacy in Nepal. NVF focuses on educating girls from poor and Dalit families, working in close partnership with Nepalese local groups and NGOs.
