The Global Indian Foundation (GIF) is a Singapore-based non-profit foundation which was established in 2002 to provide quality education and allied services to the Indian diaspora across the world. GIF has established over 20 Global Indian International Schools, Global Indian Cultural Centres, Mahatma Gandhi Centre for Values and Thoughts (MGCVT), Global Indian Resource Centres and Global Indian Publications. It is led by Prof M. G. K. Menon, Justice C S Dharmadhikari, Chandu Borde and Atul Temurnikar. Mr. Rajeev Katyal is Country Director, Global Indian Foundation and GIIS Schools India. He overseas and manages all GIIS operations in India.
