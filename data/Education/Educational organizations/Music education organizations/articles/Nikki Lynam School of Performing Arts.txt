Coordinates: 53°25′51″N 1°21′14″W﻿ / ﻿53.43073°N 1.353873°W﻿ / 53.43073; -1.353873  NLSPA (Nikki Lynam School of Performing Arts) is a street dance school based in Rotherham, South Yorkshire, England.  NLSPA was set up by Nikki Lynam in 2001.  This studio is well known in the United Kingdom through their Britain's Got Talent 2010 semi-finalist group ICE, and the Future Dance Group.   The NLSPA provides classes in singing, drama, gymnastics, and street dances such as breakdance and lyrical hip hop.
