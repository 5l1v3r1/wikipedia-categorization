The Pink Palace Family of Museums is a group of museums maintained by the City of Memphis and Memphis Museums, Inc. The Pink Palace Family of Museums display collections of historical, educational, and technological significance.  The following museums are part of the Pink Palace Family of Museums: The Pink Palace Museum and Planetarium houses a museum of local cultural and natural history;  the Crew Training International (CTI) 3D Giant Theater;  and the Sharpe Planetarium.
