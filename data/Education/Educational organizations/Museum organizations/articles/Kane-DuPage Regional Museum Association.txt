KDRMA (Kane-DuPage Regional Museum Association)  is an association of more than 60 museums   and historical societies  in Kane and DuPage counties in Illinois.   "The organization serves its members through collaborative programming, promotion, and professional development and networking".  KDRMA also sponsors the annual "Passport to Adventure" designed to encourage children and their families to visit a variety of museums in the two counties. A passport booklet can be picked up at participating museums and libraries in Kane and DuPage counties. Choosing from over 100 sites to visit children can complete an activity and have their passport stamped at each site. Children visiting 7 or more sites will receive a prize. The Summer Passport To Adventure begins on Memorial Day and ends on Labor Day.
