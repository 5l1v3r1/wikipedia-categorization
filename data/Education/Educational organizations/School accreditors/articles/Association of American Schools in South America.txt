The Association of American Schools in South America (AASSA) is a non-profit, 501-c3 organization that was established in 1961 "to discover and develop ways and means for improving understanding through international education. "  All schools in the organization are private, college preparatory schools that offer a predominantly American curriculum taught in English.  All Full Member schools must meet accreditation standards set by the AASSA board.  Currently, they have 50 full members and 33 invitational members.
