The Association of MBAs (AMBA) has accredited MBA, DBA and MBM programmes at 257 graduate business schools in 54 countries and territories (as of July 2018). Some of the accredited institutions offer programs in a further 33 countries, which expands AMBA's global presence to 85 countries.
