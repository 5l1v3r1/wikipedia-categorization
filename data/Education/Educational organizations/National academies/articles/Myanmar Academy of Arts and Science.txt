The Myanmar Academy of Arts and Science (Burmese: မြန်မာနိုင်ငံဝိဇ္ဇာနှင့် သိပ္ပံပညာရှင်အဖွဲ့, abbreviated MAAS) is Myanmar's national academy of sciences and humanities. The academy convenes an annual research conference.   MAAS also publishes a research journal, the Journal of the Myanmar Academy of Arts and Science.  MAAS' chairman is Than Oo.  Its vice-chairman is Kyi Kyi Hla.
