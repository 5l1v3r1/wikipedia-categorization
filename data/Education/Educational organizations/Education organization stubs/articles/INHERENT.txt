The Indonesia Higher Education Network is an inter-university educational network in Indonesia.  For the first phase of development, the network consists of 32 universities. The main ring of this network is located on the island of Java, five universities as a backbone network connected using STM-1 line with a total 155 Mbit/s of bandwidth capacity. Those universities are the University of Indonesia, the Bandung Institute of Technology, the Institute Technology Sepuluh November, Gadjah Mada University and the Diponegoro University.  It was active since 2006.  Since 2013, INHERENT no longer active. INHERENT Network eventually replaced by Indonesia Research and Education Network (IDREN) in 2015.
