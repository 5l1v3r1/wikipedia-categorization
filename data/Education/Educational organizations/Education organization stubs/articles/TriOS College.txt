triOS College is a private career college in the province of Ontario, Canada.  triOS College has 9 locations in Ontario, offering diplomas in Business, Technology, Healthcare and Law, with programs varying in length from 26 to 60 weeks, some including an internship. The college was established  in 1992 by Frank Gerencser and Stuart Bentley. The school is a member of Career Colleges Ontario.  triOS has 9 campus locations across Southern Ontario In: Windsor, London, Kitchener, Hamilton, Brampton, Mississauga, Toronto, Scarborough and Oshawa. A sister college called Eastern College, which is owned by triOS has 5  locations throughout Nova Scotia, New Brunswick and Newfoundland.
