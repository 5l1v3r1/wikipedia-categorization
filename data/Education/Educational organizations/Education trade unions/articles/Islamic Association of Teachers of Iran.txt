The Islamic Association of Teachers of Iran (Persian: انجمن اسلامی معلمان ایران‎) is an Iranian reformist  teacher's political organization/labor union. Most members of the association are employees of Ministry of Education. According to its official website, the union was established by Mohammad Ali Rajai, Mohammad Beheshti and Mohammad Javad Bahonar before the revolution. Former MPs Goharolsharieh Dastgheib, Ateghe Sediqi, Morteza Katiraie, Abbas Duzduzani are members of the association. Former Minister of Education Hossein Mozaffar is also a member.
