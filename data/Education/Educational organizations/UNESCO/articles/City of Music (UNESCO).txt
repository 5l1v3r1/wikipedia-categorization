A UNESCO City of Music is a city that has joined the United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) as notable in the field of music. The City of Music programme is part of UNESCO's Creative Cities Network, which is aimed at promoting cooperation with and among cities that have identified creativity as a strategic factor for sustainable urban development.  Currently, 30 cities from 23 countries located on six continents bear the title of UNESCO City of Music. To be approved as a City of Music, cities need to meet a number of criteria set by UNESCO .
