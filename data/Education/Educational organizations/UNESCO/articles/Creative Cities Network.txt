The UNESCO Creative Cities Network  (UCCN) is a project of UNESCO launched in 2004 to promote cooperation among cities which recognized creativity as a major factor in their urban development.    As of  2017[update], there are 180 cities from 72 countries in the network. The Network recognizes the concept of creative tourism, defined as travel associated with creative experience and participation.
