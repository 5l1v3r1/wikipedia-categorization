Established on September 18th, 2002 in Chicago, the USA, the International Association of Educators is a non-profit, non-partisan and non-governmental international organization. It aims to develop new pedagogies and alternative languages for multi-cultural communication and understanding. The association provides the global communications spheres for its members from 46 different countries and 1250 different universities in order to contribute to world peace through education.
