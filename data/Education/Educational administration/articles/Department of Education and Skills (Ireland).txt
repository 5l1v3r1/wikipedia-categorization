The Department of Education and Skills (Irish: An Roinn Oideachais agus Scileanna) is a department of the Government of Ireland. It is led by the Minister for Education and Skills who is assisted by two Ministers of State. The official headquarters and ministerial offices of the department are at Marlborough Street, Dublin 1.
