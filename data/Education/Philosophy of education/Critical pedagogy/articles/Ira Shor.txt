Ira Shor (born 1945) is a professor at the College of Staten Island, City University of New York, where he teaches composition and rhetoric.  He is also doctoral faculty in the Ph.D. Program in English, at The Graduate Center, CUNY.  Shor grew up in the working class area in the South Bronx of New York City. According to Shor, coming from a working class area had a powerful influence on his thinking, politics and feelings. In collaboration with Paulo Freire, he has been one of the leading exponents of critical pedagogy.  Together they cowrote A Pedagogy for Liberation.
