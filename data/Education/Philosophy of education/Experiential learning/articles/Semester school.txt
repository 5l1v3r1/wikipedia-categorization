A semester school is a school that complements a student's secondary education by providing them with the opportunity to step out of their regular school for half an academic year and step into a uniquely different educational setting while continuing their required academic studies.   The academic curriculum at semester schools tends to be college preparatory, interdisciplinary, and experiential.
