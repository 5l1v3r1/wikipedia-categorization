Municipal Library Lalamusa The central library Bahawalpur was built by Sir Sadeq Muhammad Khan Abbasi (V) and Rufus Daniel Issacs on 8th March, 1924.  It contains more than 1lac books. It is the oldest library in Pakistan and second largest in Punjab. There are Three public libraries in the city.  Govt. Public Library, Iqbal Bazaar, Kamalia.
