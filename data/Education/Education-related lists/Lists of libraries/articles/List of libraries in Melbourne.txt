This is a list of libraries in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia, the second UNESCO City of Literature.  Melbourne has a rich and varied books-and-reading culture and municipal councils throughout Melbourne provide funding and support for a rich network of libraries.  Membership of these libraries is free. Melbourne's public library services are operated by local government municipalities through a range of grants funding and service agreements. In addition to branch libraries a number of library service providers also operate a mobile service to outer Metropolitan areas.
