Pub Philosophy is a term sometimes used to describe organised gatherings in public houses for philosophical discussion.  Several series of events in the United Kingdom and elsewhere offer pub philosophy in a variety of formats, typically involving an invited speaker and some degree of open discussion. Such alliances have included Tate Liverpool and University of Chester working with Philosophy in Pubs,  the Brighton Science Festival 2010 working with PIPS Brighton  and the London School of Economics producing an event in collaboration with Big Ideas.
