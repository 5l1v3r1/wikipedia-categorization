The Product Development and Systems Engineering Consortium (PDSEC) is a joint industry/academic research group based at Wayne State University in Detroit, Michigan.  The PDSEC focuses on collaborative, globally oriented product development and systems engineering for the automotive industry.  Founded in 2005, the PDSEC has sponsored several presentations and workshops with contributors from General Motors, Ford Motor Company, PTC and other universities.
