The Leadership Quarterly is a bimonthly peer-reviewed multidisciplinary social science journal. It is dedicated to the scientific study of leadership. The journal has a broad focus and publishers papers from various fields of social science (psychology, economics, political science) as well as of biological science (e.g., evolutionary psychology). The journal also publishes methodological advances.    The journal was established in 1990 and is published by Elsevier . The current editor-in-chief is John Antonakis (University of Lausanne). According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2017 impact factor of 3.307, which puts it in the first quartile in management and applied psychology.  It has a CiteScore of 4.41  According to various journal quality lists, The Leadership Quarterly is highly ranked.  Its acceptance rate is below 10%.
