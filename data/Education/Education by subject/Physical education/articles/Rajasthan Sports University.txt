  Rajasthan Sports University (Hindi: राजस्थान क्रीड़ा विश्वविद्यालय)  is a newly established public sports university situated in the Jhunjhunu district of the Indian state Rajasthan to promote the sports education in state Rajasthan. It is stabilised on the lines of Tamil Nadu Physical Education and Sports University and Swarnim Gujarat Sports University. The university was established in the year 2013 by Rajasthan Legislative Assembly by passing The Rajasthan Sports University, Jhunjhunu Bill, 2013.  The jurisdiction of the university comprises whole Rajasthan state.
