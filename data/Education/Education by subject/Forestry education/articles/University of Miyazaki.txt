University of Miyazaki (宮崎大学, Miyazaki Daigaku) is a national university in Miyazaki, Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan. The predecessor of the school was founded in 1884, and it was chartered as a university in 2003.  The university has undergraduate faculties in education and culture, medicine, engineering, and agriculture.  The university also has a graduate program in medicine and veterinary medicine, as well as a graduate program in agriculture and engineering.
