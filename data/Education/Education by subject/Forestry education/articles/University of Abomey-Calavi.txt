The University of Abomey-Calavi ((in French) Université d'Abomey-Calavi) is the principal university in the country of Benin, in Abomey-Calavi.  The school is composed of 19 institutions and six campuses.  The university was founded in 1970 as the Université du Dahomey.    In 1975 the name was changed to Université Nationale du Bénin.  In 2001, the university took its current name.    Enrollment at UAC was over 16,000 in 1999, including over 3,300 women. The Université d'Abomey-Calavi is located in Abomey-Calavi.
