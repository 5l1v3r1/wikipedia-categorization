Fujian Agriculture and Forestry University (Chinese: 福建农林大学) is a public university in Jinshan, Fuzhou, Fujian, China. Fujian Agriculture and Forestry University's library collection contains more than 1.63 million volumes.  The university was founded in 1936. It is now known as the Fujian Agriculture and Forestry University is public university focused on the development of agriculture and forestry.  The main campus is in the rural part of Fuzhou and most of the institutions are located there. Apart from its major campuses, the university owns and operates an independent college named Oriental College. Oriental College is located in Mawei, Fuzhou.
