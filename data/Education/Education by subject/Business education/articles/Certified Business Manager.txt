The Certified Business Manager (CBM) is a credential created and administered by the Association of Professionals in Business Management (APBM).  It was designed to validate the mastery of business management knowledge, skills, and abilities.  An individual is eligible to take the examinations with either an undergraduate degree from an accredited college or university or international equivalent and a minimum of four years full-time work experience, or a graduate degree from an accredited college or university or international equivalent and a minimum of three years full-time work experience.
