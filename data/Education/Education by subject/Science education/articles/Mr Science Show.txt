Mr Science Show is an Australian science podcast.  The show originated on China Radio International  in 2006, and was broadcast on China Drive until 2008, at which point it became a podcast. The show in China was broadcast in English and produced in Beijing, with creator Marc West interviewed over the phone. Currently, the podcast is produced in Australia by Marc West.  The show was originally produced by James West. Archived versions of the show are available on the Mr Science Show site and through the podcast on iTunes.
