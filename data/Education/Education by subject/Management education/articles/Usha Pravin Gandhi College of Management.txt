Usha Pravin Gandhi College of Management, also known as UPG College, is a college in Vile Parle, Mumbai, India that is affiliated with the University of Mumbai. The College was established by Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal in 2003. Since its inception in 2003, Usha Pravin Gandhi College has introduced many courses. The college has introduced un-aided courses like Bachelors in Management Studies (BMS), Bachelors in Mass Media (BMM), Bachelors in Information Technology (BSc.IT) and Masters in Information Technology (MSc.IT).
