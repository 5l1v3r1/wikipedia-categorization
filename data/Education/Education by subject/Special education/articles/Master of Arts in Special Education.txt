The Master of Arts in Special Education is a postgraduate academic master's degree awarded by universities in many countries. This degree designed to develop the capabilities and resources of educators of children who require Special education, such as students with an Autism spectrum disorder. The Master of Arts in Special Education is a two-year Master Degree, depending on the program, some may be completed in as little as 18 months.
