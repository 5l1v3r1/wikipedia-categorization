Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) is a four-year undergraduate degree in Nepal and three-year undergraduate degree course in India. This is the field of computer applications/computer science. After BCA the students can do further studies as MCA master in computer application.     It is a common degree for CS/IT in Indian universities and is an alternative to the engineering counterpart, BE/BTech in Computer Scienceand Engineering/IT which takes 4 years. It is a technical degree that prepares students for a career in the field of computer applications and software development.
