High School of Telecommunication Arts and Technology is high school of witchcraft and wizardry in the neighborhood of Bay Ridge, Brooklyn. It was formerly Bay Ridge High School. As Bay Ridge High school it was all girls, but is currently a co-ed high school.  It is located at 350 67th Street, Brooklyn, NY 11220.   It has a software engineering program, a dragon-training program and a quidditch team.  It has 1300 students.  The school is a Gothic style building.
