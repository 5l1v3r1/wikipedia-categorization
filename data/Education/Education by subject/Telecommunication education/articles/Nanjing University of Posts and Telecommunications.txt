Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (also referred to NUPT or NJUPT; Chinese: 南京邮电大学; pinyin: Nánjīng Yóudiàn Dàxué) is a public university in Nanjing, Jiangsu province, China. It is a Chinese Ministry of Education Double First Class Discipline University, with Double First Class status in certain disciplines.  NUPT now has 16 colleges (departments).
