The Azra Naheed Medical College (Urdu: عذرا ناہید طبی کالج‬‎, abbreviated as ANMC), established in 2011 and named after Azra Naheed, is a private college of medicine, pharmacy and allied health professions. It is located in Lahore, Punjab, Pakistan. The college is affiliated with University of Health Sciences and accredited by the Pakistan Medical and Dental Council (PMDC) for MBBS degree. It is a constituent colege of the Superior University. Surayya Azeem Hospital , and Ch. M. Akram Hospital are teaching hospitals of the college for training.
