The Marc Fitch Lectures are a series of lectures first started in 1956 by Marc Fitch,  historian and philanthropist. The lectures were started by Marc Fitch in 1956, and are funded by the Marc Fitch Fund,  an educational charity also set up in 1956.   The lectures were held at the Institute of Historical Research until 2012,  when the series moved to a tour of the counties with three held a year.
