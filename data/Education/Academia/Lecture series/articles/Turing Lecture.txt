The BCS / IET Turing Lecture is an annual lecture given by a noted speaker on the subject of Computer Science. The lecture is delivered in several different locations annually including: The Royal Institution in  London, Cardiff University, The University of Manchester, Belfast City Hall and the University of Glasgow.   The lecture is named in honour of Alan Turing, and is co-hosted by the British Computer Society (BCS) and the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET). The event should not be confused with the Turing Award lecture organised by the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM).
