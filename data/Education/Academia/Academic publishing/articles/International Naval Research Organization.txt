The International Naval Research Organization was founded in 1964 and incorporated in the US state of Massachusetts.  The  organization is the publisher of the quarterly journal, Warship International; the journal is mainly about warships in the era of iron and steel (about 1860 to date). The International Naval Research Organization is a non-profit organization. Warship International is a quarterly magazine published by the International Naval Research Organization  that was established in 1964.
