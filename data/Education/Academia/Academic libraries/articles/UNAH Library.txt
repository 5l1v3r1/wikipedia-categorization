UNAH Library System at National Autonomous University of Honduras, founded in 1968, is a free support for students, teachers and researchers. UNAH Library began as the library for CURN in 1970, but later was renamed UNAH Library. Every campus of UNAH has its own library that forms part of the library system.  UNAH has more than 16,000 volumes that include, books, magazines and ebooks. The library system of UNAH is composed of three parts: The central library is located in the administrative building, at the center of university city in Tegucigalpa, it is composed of six rooms: Central library is in the same building of learning source center (CRA), which has movies, films, and rooms for presentation and exhibitions.
