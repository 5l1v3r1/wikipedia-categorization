Advanced Placement Comparative Government and Politics (AP Comparative Government and Politics or AP Comp Gov) is an Advanced Placement comparative government or comparative politics course and exam offered by the College Board. It was first administered in 1987. To better match a typical introductory college course, changes were made to the course and the exam in the fall of 2005.
