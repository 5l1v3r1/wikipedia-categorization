James G. Greeno (born May 1, 1935) is an American educational psychologist. He received the E. L. Thorndike Award in 1994.  He was born on May 1, 1935 in Sioux Falls, South Dakota.  He received his Ph.D. from the University of Minnesota in 1961.  He married Noreen H. Greeno on 15 June 1957. They have two children.
