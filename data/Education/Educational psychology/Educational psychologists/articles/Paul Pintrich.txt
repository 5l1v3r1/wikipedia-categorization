He was a professor of education and psychology at the University of Michigan where he also completed his PhD and MA. Pintrich published over 140 articles, book chapters, and books on topics related to educational psychology. Paul Robert Pintrich was born 4 November 1953 in Wilmington, Massachusetts, the only son of five children born to Paul and Teresa Pintrich.  On 15 October 1988 he married Elizabeth DeGroot.  Pintrich was a prolific author of works on educational psychology. He died of a stroke on 12 July 2003 while on a cycling tour.
