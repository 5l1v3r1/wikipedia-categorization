The Journal of Educational Psychology is a peer-reviewed academic journal that was established in 1910 and covers educational psychology. It is published by the American Psychological Association. The current editor-in-chief is Steve Graham (Arizona State University). The journal publishes original psychological research on education at all ages and educational levels, as well as occasional theoretical and review articles deemed of particular importance.  According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2016 impact factor is 3.459, ranking it 5th among 58 journals in the category "Psychology, Educational".
