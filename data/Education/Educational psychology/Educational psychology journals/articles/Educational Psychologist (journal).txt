The Educational Psychologist is a quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal published by Routledge on behalf of Division 15 (Educational Psychology) of the American Psychological Association. It was established in 1963 and the editor-in-chief is Clark A. Chinn (Rutgers University). The journal publishes conceptual and review articles rather than empirical studies on all aspects of educational psychology. It is considered one of the "big five" educational psychology journals (along with Cognition and Instruction, Journal of Educational Psychology, Educational Psychology Review, and Contemporary Educational Psychology).  The journal practices double-blind peer review (since at least 1979 ).
