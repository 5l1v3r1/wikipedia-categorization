Conductive pedagogy is pedagogy especially useful for disabled pupils like those with cerebral palsy. The main principles are: There are brilliant people that had disabilities like the blind Homer who wrote the Iliad and the Odyssey, the deaf Beethoven who is one of the most famous and influential of all composers. and the physically disabled Toulouse-Lautrec who is the most well-known painters of the Post-Impressionist period. In a 2005 auction one of his paintings sold for 22.4 million USD
