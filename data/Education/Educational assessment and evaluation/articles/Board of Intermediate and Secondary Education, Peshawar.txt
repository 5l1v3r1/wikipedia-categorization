The Board of Intermediate and Secondary Education, Peshawar or BISE Peshawar is a government board for intermediate and secondary education examination located in Peshawar, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan.      BISE Peshawar was established in 1961 by the Government of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. Previously, it was conducting the examination of intermediate and secondary education for the whole Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province but later, new educational boards were established in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa by the government and now its jurisdiction is Peshawar Division.
