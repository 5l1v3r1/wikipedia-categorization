The Prairie State Achievement Examination (PSAE) was a two-day standardized test taken by all High School Juniors in the U.S. state of Illinois. On the first day, students take the ACT, and on the second day, a WorkKeys examination and Illinois State Board of Education-developed science examination.  The test is no longer administered in Illinois schools; however, it was required for all Illinois High School Students from 2001-2014.
