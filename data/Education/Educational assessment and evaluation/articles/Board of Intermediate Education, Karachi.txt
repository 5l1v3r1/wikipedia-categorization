The Board of Intermediate Education, Karachi (BIEK) is a government board in Karachi for intermediate education examination. It was established as a separate entity in 1974 through the "Sindh Boards of Intermediate and Secondary Education" amendment act No. 20 page 31 of the 1973 book of documentation. The Board has the power to organize, regulate, develop and control Intermediate Education. The Board of Secondary Education, Karachi has similar authorities for secondary education.
