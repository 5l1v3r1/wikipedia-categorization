Multiverse is an Initial Teacher Education (ITE) Professional Resource Network (funded by the Training and Development Agency for Schools created to meet the challenge of raising the achievement of pupils from diverse backgrounds.  Issues in relation to Social class, religious diversity, Refugees and Asylum Seekers, and Travellers and Romani are also explored.
