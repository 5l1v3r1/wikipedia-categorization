A Program Supervisor is the chief administrator of a school program, such as the high school, elementary school, middle school or pre-school. A Program Supervisor is comparable to a Principal (school), with the responsibility of enrolling students, hiring new teachers, placing students in their classes, and other duties of a principal. A Program Supervisor is responsible for reporting to a top executive, comparable to the Superintendent (education), or another higher administrator. The Program Supervisor is usually aided by the Program Coordinator. The Program Coordinator is comparable to the Assistant Principal.
