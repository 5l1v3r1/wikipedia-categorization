The Chollima (Chosŏn'gŭl: 천리마; Hancha: 千里馬) is a monthly educational magazine of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.  It was first published in January 1959,  initially to support the Chollima Movement. The publisher is the Chollima Editing Committee of the  Art and Literature Publishing House. Unlike most other magazines in the country, it is targeted at the general public.
