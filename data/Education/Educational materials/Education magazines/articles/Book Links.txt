Book Links is a quarterly magazine and is the supplement of another magazine Booklist, which are based in Chicago. Book Links was launched in 1990.  It began as a magazine published by the American Library Association that helps teachers, librarians, school library media specialists, and parents connect children with high-quality books. Barbara Elleman was the founder of the magazine and edited it during the 1990s.  The magazine was published on a monthly basis.   Book Links is now "published as a quarterly print supplement to Booklist, at no additional cost to subscribers, rather than as a stand-alone magazine."
