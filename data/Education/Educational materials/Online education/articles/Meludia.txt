Meludia is a French-based company that offers an online interactive education platform to master the fundamentals of music. The Meludia Method is based upon progressive interactive listening exercises, using two main frameworks designed by Vincent Chaintrier.  Bastien Sannac and Vincent Chaintrier co-founded Meludia in June 2012. Meludia was further developed in the Cent Quatre, a public cultural incubator in Paris, before being commercially launched in 2014.  Meludia is used at The Curtis Institute of Music in Philadelphia.  Meludia has also been deployed to entire populations by several countries, like Estonia , Malta  and Canada.
