Teaching Games for Understanding is an approach to physical education developed by Peter Werner, David Bunker, and Rod Thorpe  is a model which was adopted in the year 2002 by a group of representatives, associations and individuals from all around the world. It is a global agenda for scholarly inquiry in the field of teaching with the help of games. This international task force evolved in 2008 and became the first special interest group of AIESEP.
