Educartis is an education platform connecting African students with training institutions. Currently present in 9 countries (Angola, Cameroon, DR Congo, Ghana, Ivory Coast, Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa and Zambia), it is the leading education platform in Sub Saharan Africa. The website enables students to find, compare and access both local and international education solutions, allowing them to choose the training they need in an easy, fast and convenient manner. Educartis also supports local institutions to showcase their products and services in a manner which may otherwise not be possible due to limited resources. By leveraging the power and growth of the internet in Africa, Educartis seeks to democratize the way students and institutions connect with each other. The Educartis platform offers access to over 2,700 institutions and 20,000 educational programs and courses.
