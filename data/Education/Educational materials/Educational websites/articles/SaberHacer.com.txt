SaberHacer.com launched in 2008    and provides expert-driven "how-to" videos on a range of topics  relevant to Latinos in both English and Spanish.  SaberHacer.com content is distributed to Hispanic focused online portals and media companies including Yahoo en Espanol and Univision. The content topics include Citizenship, College Education, Computers and Internet, Personal Finance, Careers, Health and other topics.  Experts in relevant fields are included in the videos, which are offered in both Spanish and English. SaberHacer.com was founded by former broadcast journalist Michele Ruiz as a resource for U.S. Hispanics.
