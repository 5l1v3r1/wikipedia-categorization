The Last of the Street Survivors Farewell Tour is a concert tour by the Southern rock band Lynyrd Skynyrd. This will be the final tour conducted by the band.  It began May 4, 2018, at the Coral Sky Amphitheater in West Palm Beach, Florida  and will end January 8, 2019 after the Southern Rock Cruise concludes.  The band will utilize a plethora of opening acts, including Bad Company, Kid Rock, and Hank Williams Jr.,throughout the tour.
