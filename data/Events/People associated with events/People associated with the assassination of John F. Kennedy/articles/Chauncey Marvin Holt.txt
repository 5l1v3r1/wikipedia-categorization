Holt died of cancer at the age of 75, and was survived by a daughter and granddaughter.  In a 1991 Newsweek article about Oliver Stone's JFK, Holt received national attention for various claims he made regarding the assassination of President Kennedy.   According to Holt, he was a CIA operative sent to Dallas to deliver phony Secret Service credentials.   He also claimed to have worked as an accountant for Meyer Lansky.
