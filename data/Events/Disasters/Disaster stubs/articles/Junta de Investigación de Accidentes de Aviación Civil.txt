The Junta de Investigación de Accidentes de Aviación Civil (JIAAC), in English the Civil Aviation Accident Investigation Board, formerly the Junta de Investigaciones de Accidentes de Aviación Civil ("Civil Aviation Accident Research Board"), is an agency of Argentina that investigates aviation accidents. Its headquarters is in Buenos Aires.  The JIAAC has four administrative regions.  As of 2011 Gustavo Enrique Graziani is the president of the JIAAC.
