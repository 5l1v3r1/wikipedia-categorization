The Sugarloaf Fire is a wildfire currently burning near Silverthorne, Colorado in Grand County in the United States. The fire has burned a total of 1,280 acres as of August 14, 2018. It is currently 35% contained.  The Sugarloaf Fire started near Ute Pass.   On June 28, 2018, at around 12:00 A.M, the fire started 13 miles southwest of Fraser, CO.
