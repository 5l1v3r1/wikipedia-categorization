Fight and Smile (相信希望fight & smile賑災募款晚會) is a fund raising campaign held in Taiwan for the victims of the Japan 2011 Tōhoku earthquake and tsunami.  The event was held from March 17 to March 18, 2011.  About NT$788 million were raised by the event.  The event was hosted by Jacky Wu, Chang Fei (張菲), Chang Hsiao-yen (張小燕) and Patty Hou.
