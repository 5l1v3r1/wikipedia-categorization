 Klewer Market is the largest textile market in Solo City, Indonesia.  The market which located beside Keraton Surakarta is also shopping center for batik traders from Yogyakarta, Surabaya, Semarang and other cities in Java.  This market have two floors and can accommodate 1,467 traders with a number of stalls around 2,064 units.   Klewer market not only as a center of economy, but also a tourist destination and a symbol of Solo city.  On 27 December 2014 evening, the whole market was burned down.
