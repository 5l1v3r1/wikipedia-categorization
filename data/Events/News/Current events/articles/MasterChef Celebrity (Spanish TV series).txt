MasterChef is a Spanish competitive reality television cooking show based on the British television cooking game show of the same title. It's a celebrity special spin-off of the series Masterchef Spain, that premiered on La 1 on 10 April 2013. The show is hosted by Eva González. Jordi Cruz, Samantha Vallejo-Nágera and Pepe Rodríguez repeat as the judges of the show. The first season premiered the 6 of November 2016.  On February 28, 2018, RTVE renewed the program for a third season to be aired in the period 2018-2019.  Each episode follows the same script, and it's divided in three challenges. At the end of every episode, one contestant is evicted. Throughout the third season of MasterChef Celebrity, the contestants were visited by Loles León.
