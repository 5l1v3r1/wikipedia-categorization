There have been 75 National Rover Moots in New Zealand. The first was the Dominion Rover Scout Moot at New Brighton Racecourse, Christchurch in 1936 although there was a North island Moot in 1935. List based on the New Zealand Badgers Club "The Histories Book: A History of New Zealand Scout District Badges" 4th Edition 2014 (preprint edition) The 77th National Rover Moot, Once Upon A Moot is being hosted by Rovers from the Lower North Island Region. It will be held over Easter Weekend 2019 at the Brookfield Outdoor Education Center, Wellington, New Zealand.
