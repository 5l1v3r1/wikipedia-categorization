The Scottish International Airshow is an annual Air Show taking place at Ayr, Scotland. Founded in 2014, the air show features a variety of different aircraft such as Biplanes, Fighter Jets, Aerobatic aircraft, Seaplanes, and much more.  TSIA (The Scottish International Airshow) is held at Ayr Low Green. At the airshow, there are food stands, stalls, Various Funfair Rides, exhibitions from the British Armed Forces and British police such as The British Army, Royal Air Force and the Royal Navy.  TSIA is created and hosted by the Scottish International Airshow. Inside of the air show area, it is patrolled by police and security guards. Typically, the order of the aircraft's are the Typhoon, older planes such as the Douglas DC3 and The Beech 18's. Followed by the Seaplanes and then the F18 and the red arrows.
