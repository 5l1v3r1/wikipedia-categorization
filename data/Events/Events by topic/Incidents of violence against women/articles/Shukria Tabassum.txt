Shukria Tabassum (Dari: شکریه تبستم‬) was the daughter of Ramazan Ali from Afghanistan born in Jaghori district of Ghazni Province.  She belonged to the Hazara ethnic group of Afghanistan. She used to go to school. At the age of 9 she was kidnapped by ISIS in Afghanistan. During a journey from Jaghori to Quetta, Pakistan all 7 ethnic Hazara passengers were captured in the valley of Zabul, Afghanistan.  After beheading all 7 kidnap victims, around 1000,000 demonstrators gathered in Kabul to protest against government policies and the administration.
