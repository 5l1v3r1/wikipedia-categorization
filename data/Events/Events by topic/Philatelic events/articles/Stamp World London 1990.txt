  Stamp World London 90 was an international stamp exhibition held 3-13 May 1990 at the Alexandra Palace, London.  There was a special handstamp issued each day of the exhibition for use on First Day Covers (FDC), there was 11 in total issued. 1990 was also the 150th anniversary of the introduction of the Uniform Penny Post and the Penny Black and a number of stamps and exhibition souvenirs were produced in that connection.
