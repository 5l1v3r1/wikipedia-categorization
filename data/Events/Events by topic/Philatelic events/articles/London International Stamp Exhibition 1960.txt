The London International Stamp Exhibition was held at the Royal Festival Hall from 9 to 16 July 1960. Souvenir labels were produced for the event depicting  Colonel Henry Bishop, as it was the tercentenary of the General Post Office.   A non-postal souvenir sheet showing six rare stamps was also produced by Harrison and Sons. The principal awards went to the following exhibits:  The Grand Prix went to Reginald M. Phillips (U.K.) for ‘Great Britain’. Special Awards went to Gerald E. Anderegg (Switzerland) for ‘Switzerland’ and Mario Tomasini (Italy) for ‘Italian States’.
