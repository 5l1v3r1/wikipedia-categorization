SHIFT is a two-day international business festival held every year in Turku, Finland, at the Turku Castle. It was first held on May 31 and June 1, 2016. The attendees included over 1,000 visitors, 80 partner companies, 150 startups, and over 20 investors. Around 100 volunteers were part of this event. SHIFT 2017 is slated to be held on May 31 and June 1, 2017. The SHIFT is backed by companies and the city of Turku, as well as Turku Chamber of Commerce  and The Federation of Finnish Enterprises,  while the event itself is put together by local JCI  members, start-up actives and students.
