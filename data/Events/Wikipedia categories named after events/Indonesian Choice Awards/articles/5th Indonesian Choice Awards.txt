The 5th Indonesian Choice Awards ceremony (Official name: Indonesian Choice Awards 5.0 NET.), presented by the NET., honored the best entertainment of the year in 2018, and took place on April 29, 2018, at the Sentul International Convention Center in Bogor, West Java, at 7:00 p.m. WIB. The awards ceremony aired live on NET. coinciding with the fifth anniversary celebration, entitled NET 5.0: Upgraded. The annual awards have presented by Sarah Sechan, Vincent Rompies and Desta (3rd time overall). In addition to local musicians, NET. also featuring to international musician and artists, such as Craig David, Hailee Steinfeld, and Light Balance.
