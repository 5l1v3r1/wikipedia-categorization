The 11th World Science Fiction Convention, also known as Philcon II, was held in September 1953 at the Bellevue-Stratford Hotel in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA.   It was the first Worldcon to present the Hugo Awards.  The supporting organization was the Philadelphia Science Fiction Society.  The guest of honor was Willy Ley.    The chairman was Milton A.
