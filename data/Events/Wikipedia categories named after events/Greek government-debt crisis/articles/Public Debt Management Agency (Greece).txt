The Public Debt Management Agency (PDMA) is a government agency in Greece. Its stated purpose is the: "improvement of the cost of funding and the achievement of the best possible structure (composition and maturity) of the public debt according to the needs of the Hellenic State and the prevailing international economic conditions."  The PDMA was founded in 1999.  It is headed by a Board of Directors, appointed by the Minister of Finance.
