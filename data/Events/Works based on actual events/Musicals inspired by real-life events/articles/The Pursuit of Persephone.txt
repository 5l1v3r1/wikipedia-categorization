The Pursuit of Persephone is a musical with music and lyrics by Peter Mills and book by Cara Reichel.   The show details F. Scott Fitzgerald's time at Princeton University, and his love for Ginevra King.  Elements of the plot are loosely adapted from This Side of Paradise (Fitzgerald's own fictionalized account of his time at Princeton).
