Siluri umani (internationally released as Human Torpedoes) is a 1954 Italian war film credited to Antonio Leonviola, who abandoned production and was substituted by director Carlo Lizzani (uncredited).  The movie depicts WWII 1941 Crete bay attack by Italian frogmen to Royal Navy's York heavy cruiser and an allied Norway oil tanker. One of its military advisors was former admiral Marcantonio Bragadin.
