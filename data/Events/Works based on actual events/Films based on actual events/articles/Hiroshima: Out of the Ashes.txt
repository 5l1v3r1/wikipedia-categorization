Hiroshima: Out of the Ashes is a 1990 historical television film about the Atomic bombings of Hiroshima.  It was nominated for 2 Primetime Emmy's and other awards.  Hiroshima: Out of the Ashes was directed by Peter Werner.  The story follows the bombing and aftermath of the Nuclear bomb drop on the city of Hiroshima, told from several different perspectives. Hiroshima: Out of the Ashes was released on DVD on April 24, 2007.
