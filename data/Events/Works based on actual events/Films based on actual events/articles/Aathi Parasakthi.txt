  Aathi Parasakthi is a 1971 Tamil Indian feature film directed by K. S. Gopalakrishnan and produced by Chitra Productions. It stars Gemini Ganesan and Jayalalithaa in the lead roles.  The film was dubbed in Hindi as Jai Jagat Janani (1976).  The music composed by K. V. Mahadevan.  Aathi Parasakthi on IMDb
