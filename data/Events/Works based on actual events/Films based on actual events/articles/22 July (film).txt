 22 July is a 2018 crime drama film, based on the 2011 Norway attacks and the aftermath. The film was written, directed and co-produced by Paul Greengrass and features a Norwegian cast and crew. The film was selected to have its world premiere on September 5, 2018  in the main competition section of the 75th Venice International Film Festival.   It will be released on October 10, 2018 by Netflix. The film is scheduled to be released on October 10, 2018.
