Crime in Connecticut: The Story of Alex Kelly (also known as The Return of Alex Kelly) is based on the life of Alex Kelly.  It was released on March 16, 1999. A rape victim recounts her traumatic story. Her attacker, a popular young wrestler named Alex Kelly, ran away to Europe for seven years when charges were brought against him. After eight years in Europe he was returned to the United States and faced trial, with the first trial ending in a hung jury. Kelly was found guilty in his second trial.
