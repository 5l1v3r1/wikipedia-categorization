Rita Lakin (teleplay)Helen Klaben Kahn (from the book by) (as Helen Klaben) & Hey, I'm Alive is a 1975 American made-for-television adventure drama film starring Edward Asner and Sally Struthers.  It recounts the true story of Helen Klaben and Ralph Flores as described in Klaben's book of the same name. The director, Lawrence Schiller, photographed the rescue in 1963 for Life magazine.  It premiered on ABC on November 7, 1975.  Two plane crash survivors who spend 49 days of winter in the Yukon before they are rescued.
