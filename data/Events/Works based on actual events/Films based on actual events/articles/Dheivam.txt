  Dheivam is a 1972 devotional Tamil film with Gemini Ganesan, R. Muthuraman, A.V.M. Rajan, Sreekanth, K. R. Vijaya, Sowkar Janaki, Nagesh, Thengai Srinivasan in lead roles.  The film is directed by M. A. Thirumugam and produced by Dhandayuthapani Films.  Music by Kunnakudi Vaidyanathan and lyrics kannadasan. Music by Kunnakudi Vaidyanathan and lyrics by Kannadasan.
