Half a Dozen Babies (Life's Little Struggles) is a 1999 American drama ABC Family TV film. It stars Scott and Melissa Reeves. It is rated TV-PG. A young couple have been struggling to have a baby for years, unsuccessfully. Then, suddenly, one day, they find out that they are having sextuplets. The movie is based on the true story of Keith and Becki Dilley and the Dilley sextuplets, born in 1993.
