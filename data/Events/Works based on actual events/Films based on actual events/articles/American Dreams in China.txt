Mandarin  American Dreams in China (Chinese: 中国合伙人; pinyin: Zhōngguó héhuǒ rén; literally: "Chinese (business) partners") is a 2013 Chinese film directed by Peter Chan and starring Huang Xiaoming, Deng Chao, and Tong Dawei.  It was shown at the 2013 Toronto International Film Festival.  It is based on the story of New Oriental. Three young men from poor backgrounds achieve success by establishing a reputable English teaching school.
