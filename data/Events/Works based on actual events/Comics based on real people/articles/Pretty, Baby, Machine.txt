Pretty, Baby, Machine (also known as Pretty Baby Machine) is a three-issue comic book limited series written by Clark Westerman with art by Kody Chamberlain, and released by Image Comics through their ShadowLine studio in 2008.  The title refers to the three protagonists: Pretty Boy Floyd, Baby Face Nelson, and Machine Gun Kelly. In 1933, three famous outlaws come together to fight Al Capone.
