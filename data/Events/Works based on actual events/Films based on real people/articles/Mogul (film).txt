  Mogul - The Gulshan Kumar Story is an upcoming 2019 Indian biographical film written and directed by Subhash Kapoor and produced by Bhushan Kumar and Aamir Khan and is also starring Aamir Khan as Gulshan Kumar The film is based on the life of Gulshan Kumar who was founder of the T-Series music label (Super Cassettes Industries Ltd.), and a Bollywood movie producer.   The film is set to release in 2019.
