  Tishnagi is a Bollywood film directed by Sameer Khan and produced by Jayant Ghosh. It stars Qais Tanvee, Aryan Vaid, Rajpal Yadav,Anushka Srivastava in lead roles.   The film was released on 4 May 2018.   An honest policeman and a software engineer investigate a series of murders that appear to have been performed by a psychotic killer.  The music for the film is composed by Gufy. The lyrics are penned by  Deepak Agrawal and Manzer Baliyavi. The music of the film is acquired by the music label Zee Music Company
