  Super 30 is an upcoming Indian biopic drama film co-produced and directed by Vikas Bahl, based on the life of mathematician Anand Kumar and his educational program Super 30.  The film stars Hrithik Roshan as Kumar and Mrunal Thakur. The principal photography of the film began in February 2018.    The film was also shot for 10 days in Sambhar Lake Town which will be shown as Kota, Rajasthan.  Super 30 is now scheduled to release on 25 January 2019 after several delays.
