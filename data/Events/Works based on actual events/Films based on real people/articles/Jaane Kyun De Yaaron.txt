  Jaane Kyun De Yaaron is a Bollywood film written and directed by Akshay Anand and produced by Vishal Bhatia and Raghu Raja. It stars Raghu Raja, Abhishek Sharma, Chetna Pande, Heena Panchal in lead roles. The film was released on 16 February 2018.    The film is a story about Two boys with the dreams to change their ordinary lives change the entire corruption system as they take a stand against police brutality with the little help of a benevolent guardian.   The music for the film is composed by Satya Manik Afsar, Deepak Agrawal, Chand Sadhwani and Sikandar Singh Kahlon. The lyrics are penned by Deepak Agrawal, Atique Allahbadi, Manik Batra and Prashant Ingole. The music of the film is acquired by the music label T-Series.
