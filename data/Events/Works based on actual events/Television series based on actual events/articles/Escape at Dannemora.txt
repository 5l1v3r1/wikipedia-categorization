Escape at Dannemora is an upcoming television limited series based on the 2015 Clinton Correctional Facility escape that is set to air on Showtime.  Ben Stiller will direct all eight episodes of the series.  "Escape at Dannemora is based on the headline-making true story of a 2015 prison break in upstate New York, which spawned a massive manhunt for two convicted murderers who were aided in their escape by a married female prison employee with whom they both became sexually entangled."  Principal production commenced in the fall of 2017, in Upstate New York.
