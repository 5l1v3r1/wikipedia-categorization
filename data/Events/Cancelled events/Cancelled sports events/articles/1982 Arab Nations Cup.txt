The 1982 Arab Nations Cup was the fourth staging of the Arab Nations Cup, an association football tournament held between Arab countries by UAFA. After the championship ended 16 years, it returned in 1982 and was scheduled to be held in Lebanon. However, after the participation of Arab teams in the preliminary qualifiers, the final tournament was cancelled because of the 1982 Lebanon War.
