The 1990 Arab Club Champions Cup is a tournament held between Arab clubs by UAFA. The preliminary round began, but the final tournament was cancelled because of the Gulf War. MC Alger advanced to the final tournament. Preliminary round tournament held in Amman, Jordan from 7 to 15 July 1990. Al-Faisaly and Al-Zawra'a advanced to the final tournament. The final tournament was cancelled because Gulf War.
