  This is a list of serving Members of the Parliament of the United Kingdom who were assassinated. Spencer Perceval is the only British prime minister to be assassinated, having been shot on 11 May 1812 by John Bellingham, a merchant who blamed the government for his debt. From 1882 to 1990, six MPs were assassinated by militant Irish republicans. The murder of Jo Cox on 16 June 2016 was committed by Thomas Mair, a white supremacist. She was the first female or Labour MP to be assassinated.
