  This is a list of international trips made by Maithripala Sirisena as President of Sri Lanka. Sirisena, the 7th, and current President took office on 9 January 2015. As of September 2018, he has made 30 foreign trips. Most of them were state visits to Asian countries. Maithripala Sirisena made 10 trips to 10 countries throughout 2015. Maithripala Sirisena made 12 trips to 9 countries throughout the year 2016. Maithripala Sirisena made 7 trips in 2017.
