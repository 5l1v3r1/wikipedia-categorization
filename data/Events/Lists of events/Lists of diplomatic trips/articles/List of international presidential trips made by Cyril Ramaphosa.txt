This is a list of international trips made by Cyril Ramaphosa while President. Ramaphosa made his first international trip as the President of South Africa to Luanda, Angola. March 21, signs the Kigali Declaration  18 April. Addresses African leaders the 'Africassee Leaders Round table' of the Commonwealth Business Forum. 19 April. Attends the Commonwealth Heads of Government Meeting.  10 Aug. Signs an Agreement on the Establishment of Binational Commission between South Africa and Zambia. 18 Aug. Opens the 30th Ordinary SADC Heads of State and Government Summit as the outgoing chairperson of the SADC.
