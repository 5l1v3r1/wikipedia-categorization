Party princess is a term associated with a person who entertains  children at birthday parties, often dressed as different Disney  I characters.     The most common party princesses costumes are Elsa, Cinderella, Sleeping Beauty and The Little Mermaid. Party princesses generally perform at private birthday parties for young girls - they sing, dance and play with the girls, all-while maintaining a party theme based on the  character they're dressed up as.
