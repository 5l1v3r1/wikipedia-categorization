  The Great Hip Hop Hoax is a documentary film by Jeanie Finlay about a Scottish hip-hop duo called Silibil N' Brains, who pretended to be Americans to secure a Â£250,000 record deal with Sony.  The film premiered at South by Southwest  and was later shown at the Edinburgh International Film Festival,  before a national theatrical release in Scotland and broadcasts on BBC Two Scotland, BBC Four, and Danish station DR2.  It was pitched at Sheffield Doc/Fest's MeetMarket in 2008. The movie has received positive reviews.    Both members of Silibil 'N Brains are reportedly happy with how the film turned out, though find it "bizarre" to watch with other people.
