Sean K (ショーン K, Shōn Kei, born 21 March 1968)  is a former Japanese radio personality, narrator and tarento.  His business name is Sean McArdle Kawakami (ショーン･マクアードル川上, Shōn Makuādoru Kawakami).   His real name is Shinichi Kawakami (川上 伸一郎, Kawakami Shin'ichirō).  He is represented with Sunday. Sean K's career as a Japanese news and business commentator abruptly ended in 2016 after weekly magazine Shukan Bunshun exposed his fabricated academic background including claims of an MBA from Harvard Business School.
