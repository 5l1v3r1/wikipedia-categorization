 Josef "Harry" Jelinek (1905-1986) was a Czech con artist and publisher of Nazi propaganda, who is reputed to have fraudulently sold the Karlštejn Castle to American industrialists in 1945.    Jelinek was born 14 October 1905 in the town of Vlašim, Czechoslovakia (now a part of the Czech Republic).   He died in Guarene d'Alba, Italy in 1986.
