The BWF Grand Prix Gold and Grand Prix is a series of badminton tournaments, sanctioned by Badminton World Federation (BWF) since 2007. A Grand Prix Gold tournament offers minimum prize money of US$120,000; a Grand Prix tournament offers minimum prize money of US$50,000. The formula of prize money distribution is identical to Super Series tournament.  The BWF Grand Prix Gold and Grand Prix series offers third only to BWF tournaments (after BWF events and Super Series), according to World Ranking system.
