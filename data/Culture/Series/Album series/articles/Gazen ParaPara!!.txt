Gazen ParaPara!! (Japanese: 俄然パラパラ！！ also "Gazen ParaPara!! Paradise" or "Gazen ParaPara Gakuen") is the new wave of ParaPara merchandise from avex trax focused on Japanese teenagers. Several CD compilations are available, as well as ParaPara lesson DVDs, which are the first commercially released DVDs since the Para Para Paradise series. Due to some special campaigns, these DVD releases were either only available through contests or in limited quantity at retail stores.
