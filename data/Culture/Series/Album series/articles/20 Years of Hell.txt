20 Years of Hell is a series of EPs released by Anti-Flag. In honor of the band's 20th anniversary, they released the EPs via a subscription service.  Each EP, which was released digitally and as a 7", is a split, featuring two songs by Anti-Flag, both of which are re-recordings of previous Anti-Flag songs, with the exception of the last EP which features one re-recording and one new song, and two songs by a new band.  The first volume was released on July 21, 2013. The second volume was released on October 3, 2013. The third volume was released on July 21, 2013. The fourth volume was released on February 18, 2014. The fifth volume was released on June 4, 2014. The sixth and final volume was released in July 2014
