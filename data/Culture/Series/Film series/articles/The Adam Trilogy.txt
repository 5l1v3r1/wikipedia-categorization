Adam (Turkish for man). The trilogy is one of the best examples of Turkish Psychedelic Cinema of 1970s directed by Remzi Aydın Jöntürk starring Cüneyt Arkın. The trilogy includes: Yarınsız Adam (Man Without Tomorrow) Satılmış Adam (Sold Man) Yıkılmayan Adam (Indestructible Man) The films tell the story of the class struggles in Turkey in the late 1970s with an open style.[clarification needed]
