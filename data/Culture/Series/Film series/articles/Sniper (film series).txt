The Sniper series is a series of action and war films beginning with Sniper in 1993. All seven films feature the United States Armed Forces. The series portrays MGySgt. Thomas Beckett (Tom Berenger) and GySgt. Brandon Beckett (Chad Michael Collins) in their service to the United States Marine Corps as Force Reconnaissance Scout Snipers. Master Gunnery Sergeant Thomas Beckett, an experienced sniper, and Richard Miller are sent on a mission to assassinate a Panamanian General. Thomas Beckett and Jake Cole are tasked with assassinating a Serbian general responsible for ethnic cleansing attacks. Sniper Thomas Beckett is hired by NSA officials William Avery and Richard Addis to perform a covert operation to eliminate a suspected terrorist kingpin in the People's Republic of Vietnam who was providing support for Jemaah Islamiyah. With the assistance of Richard Miller, Marine Sergeant Brandon Beckett, son of Thomas Beckett, takes up the mantle set by his father and goes on a mission of his own. After military leaders are assassinated, Gunnery Sergeant Brandon Beckett receives word that his father is one. Attempting to track down the assassin, Brandon finds out that his father is not dead, realizing that he is being used as bait. Gunnery Sergeant Brandon Beckett is given yet another mission. Master Sergeant Brandon Beckett is given the ultimate mission.
