Seasoned Players is an award-winning pornographic film series directed and produced by Tom Byron. The series is focused on "milf" performers, and often includes some relevant comebacks.       This series has 17 official parts, one special release (Seasoned Players Meets Asseaters Unanimous) and one compilation (Best of Seasoned Players). In 2009 the director Tom Byron started a spin-off series, Unseasoned Players, that has three parts.
