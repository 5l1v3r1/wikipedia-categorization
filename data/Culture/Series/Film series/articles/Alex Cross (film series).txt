The Alex Cross film series is an American series of thriller films, based on the fictional character of Alex Cross, who originally appeared in a series of novels by James Patterson. In the series of three films, two actors have portrayed Cross. Prior to the film Alex Cross (2012) release, it was announced that Double Cross would be adapted into a film, with Tyler Perry reprising the role.  However, the film was cancelled after Alex Cross did not perform well at the box office.
