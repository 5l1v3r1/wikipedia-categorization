Terrifying Girls' High School (恐怖女子高校, Kyofu Joshi Koko) is a 4-film series of Pinky violence pink films made by Toei during 1972 and 1973. Reiko Ike was the star of all four films, and Miki Sugimoto co-starred in the first two. Currently, only the second episode of the series, Terrifying Girls' High School: Lynch Law Classroom has been released on region-1 DVD. This film was included in the 4-disc Pinky Violence Collection released by Panik House in 2005.
