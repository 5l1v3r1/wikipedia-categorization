Cults and Extreme Belief (also known as A&E Investigates: Cults and Extreme Belief) is an American documentary series on A&E.  The show premiered on May 28, 2018 and is hosted by Elizabeth Vargas.   Each episode examines the world of organized organizations, alongside former members.  Eli Holzman and Aaron Saidman executive produce for IPC along with executive producer and showrunner Alex Weresow and executive producer Rachelle Mendez.
