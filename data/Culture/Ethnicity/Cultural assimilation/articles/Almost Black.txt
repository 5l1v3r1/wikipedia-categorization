Almost Black: The True Story of How I Got Into Medical School By Pretending to Be Black is a 2016 non-fiction book written by Vijay Jojo Chokal-Ingam, the brother of comedian Mindy Kaling.         The true story of how writer Vijay Jojo Chokal-Ingam, an Indian American, posed as an African American in order to use affirmative action to get into medical school.
