Mirdadzai or Meerdadzai is a brother of Kakar Khudiadadzai. The warrior Saifullah Khan after whom District Killa Saifullah has been named belonged to this tribe. Mirdadzai tribe lives in District Killa Saifullah, Pishin and Loralai areas. The fort of Saifullah Khan exist in Killi Rabat Kariaz, Killa Saifullah main city.
