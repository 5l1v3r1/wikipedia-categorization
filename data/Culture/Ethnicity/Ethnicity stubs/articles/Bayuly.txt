Bayuly (Rich Son) is one of three tribal confederation of Little jüz which generally occupies western Kazakhstan. Bayuly as tribal consideration consist of 12 tribe or clans. Bai-Uly includes 12 tribes – Alasha, Bersh, Adai, Taz, Alt’n, Baibakty, Zhappas, Kzylkurt, Esentemir, Maskar, Sherkesh, Tana. The battle cry of Alasha is "BaiBarak!" Population of Bai-Uly in 1897 was 600,000 people. (around 16,2% of all Kazakhs).
