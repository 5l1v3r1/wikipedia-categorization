Katawar or Katir is the name for the northern mountainous regions of Kafirstan in  Afghanistan and Pakistan. The Black-Robed (Siah-Posh) tribes living in these regions are also known as Katawars or Katirs. Other names for the tribes  are Kata, Kator and Katawer. The Kafirs of Siah-Posh groups are descended from the ancient Kambojas.
