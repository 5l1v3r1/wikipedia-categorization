Pakistanis in the Netherlands formed a population of 35,000 individuals as of  2004[update], according to the statistics of Pakistan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs.  However, the figures of the Netherlands' Centraal Bureau voor de Statistiek showed only 17,990 persons of Pakistani origin (persons born in Pakistan or with at least one parent born there) that year. Their most updated data for 2011 show 19,408 persons of Pakistani origin, little increase from the 2004 number.
