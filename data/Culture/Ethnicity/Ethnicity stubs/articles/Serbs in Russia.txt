There is a community of Serbs in Russia (Russian: Сербы в России; Serbian: Срби у Русији, translit. Srbi u Rusiji), also known as Russian Serbs (Russian: Российские сербы; Serbian: Руски Срби, translit. Ruski Srbi), which includes Russian citizens of ethnic Serb descent or Serbian-born people residing in the country.
