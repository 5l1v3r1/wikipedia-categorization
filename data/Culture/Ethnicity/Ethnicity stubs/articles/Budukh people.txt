The Budukhs (Budukh: Будад, Budad) are an ethnic group primarily from the mountainous village of Buduq in northwestern Azerbaijan, one of the Shahdagh peoples. They speak the Budukh language, which is a Northeast Caucasian language of the Lezgic branch. The Azerbaijani language is widely spoken. The Budukh people are overwhelmingly Sunni Muslims.
