Ella Habiba Shohat (Hebrew: אלה חביבה שוחט; Arabic: إيلا حبيبة شوحيط; born 1959) is an American cultural-studies academic. She is a Mizrahi Jew from a Baghdadi family.  Her main research interests are post-colonialism and representation in the Middle East. She has worked at City University of New York  and New York University where she is now professor of cultural studies.
