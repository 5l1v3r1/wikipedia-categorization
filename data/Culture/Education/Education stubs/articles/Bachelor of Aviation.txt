The Bachelor of Aviation (BAvn) is a bachelor's degree for studies in the field of aviation. It takes 3 years to complete the degree. This degree also trains students in engineering, and electronics, teaching students to run, fly, design, manage, and operate aviation systems all over the world. Careers are available in all fields of the aviation sector, including flight planning, support and operations, airport and airline management, government and regulatory oversight, and aviation research and development.  Candidates who have passed 12th from any stream (Science, Commerce, Humanities or Arts) could opt in for the course. Though minimum marks criteria exist, it varies from institute to institute. In most of the colleges, it is around 50%.
