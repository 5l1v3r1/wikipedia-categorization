Golaghat Engineering College was established in 2018 by Assam Government at Golaghat district, Assam. On 31st March 2012, Chief Minister Tarun Gogoi laid the foundation stone of the Golaghat Engineering College in Golaghat district, which was proposed by the Assam Government officially.   The college starts to offer BTech degree from academic year 2018-19. The college is accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). The institution is affiliated to Assam Science and Technology University. It is the sixth state government engineering college of Assam.
