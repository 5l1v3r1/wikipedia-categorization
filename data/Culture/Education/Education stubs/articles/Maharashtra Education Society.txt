The Maharashtra Education Society (MES) is one of the oldest private education institutions in Pune, India. It was founded in 1860 as the Poona Native Institution by the revolutionary leaders Vasudeo Balwant Phadke, Waman Prabhakar Bhave, and Laxman Nahar Indapurkar.    At present the society runs over 77 institutions including schools, colleges, vocational training institutions and a hospital in various places within Maharashtra like Saswad, Baramati, Panvel, Belapur, Kalamboli, Shirwal, Kasar Amboli, Navi Mumbai, Ahmednagar, Pune and Chiplun.
