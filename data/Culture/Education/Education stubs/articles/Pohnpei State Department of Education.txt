Pohnpei State Department of Education (PDOE) is an agency of Pohnpei State, Federated States of Micronesia that operates public schools.  As of  217[update] Churchiil B. Edward is the department's director.  As of  2007[update] it had 31 elementary schools with 556 preschool students and 8,112 elementary students, and three high schools with 2,713 students.
