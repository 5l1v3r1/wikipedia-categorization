The Mesopotamian Social Sciences Academy is a co-educational university in Qamishli, the capital of the de facto autonomous region, the Democratic Federation of Northern Syria.  The academy opened on September 2014 as the first university in Rojava .   The school year has three terms, lasting 3-4 months each, comprising mainly of history and sociology classes.  Teaching is mainly in Kurdish.  There was a book donation drive organised early in the life of the university, which, amongst others, was donated to by AK Press.
