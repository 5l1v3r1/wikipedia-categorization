The Freie Waldorfschule Wetterau is a private Waldorf school located in Bad Nauheim, Germany. The Freie Waldorfschule Wetterau is a general-education school starting with class 1, with an officially recognised senior high school until the Abitur in class 13 (diploma from German secondary school qualifying for university admission or matriculation). The school is based on the Waldorf education.  Parents and teachers are the financiers of the school. Therefore the parents are highly integrated into all themes concerning the school.
