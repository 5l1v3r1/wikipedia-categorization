This is a list of laundry topics. Laundry is the washing of clothing and linens (e.g. sheets and towels).  Laundry processes are often done in a business, room or area in a home or apartment building, reserved for that purpose; this is referred to as a laundry room. The material that is being washed, or has been laundered, is also generally referred to as laundry.
