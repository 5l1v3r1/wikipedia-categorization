In sewing and haberdashery, notions are small objects or accessories, including items that are sewn or otherwise attached to a finished article, such as buttons, snaps, and collar stays. Notions also include the small tools used in sewing, such as thread, pins, marking pens, and seam rippers. The noun is almost always used in the plural.  The term is chiefly found in the United States, and was formerly used in the construction Yankee notions.
