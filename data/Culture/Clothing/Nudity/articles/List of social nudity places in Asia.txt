This list of social nudity places in Asia is a list of places where social nudity is practised for recreation in Asia. It includes free beaches (or clothing-optional beaches or nude beaches) and some resorts. Hong Kong does not officially allow nude or topless bathing, and most local bathers are quite conservative in their beach dress (e.g. many women wear T-shirts over swimsuits).  Nevertheless, more remote parts of Hong Kong's extensive coastline do offer opportunities for nudity: The village of Kaji-Say has a beach with a section for nudists
