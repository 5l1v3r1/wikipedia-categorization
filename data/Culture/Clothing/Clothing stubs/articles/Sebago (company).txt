Sebago is an American Michigan-based company that produces a variety of upscale boating and deck shoes and outdoor shoes as well as some dress shoes. Sebago was founded in 1946.  In 2017, BasicNet acquired Sebago from Wolverine World Wide.  During the 1980s, the Sebago "Docksides" become a fashion trend at universities and high schools across the United States, as it was mentioned in The Official Preppy Handbook.  It also sponsored the Single-Handed Trans-Atlantic Race in 1984 and the America's Cup in 1992.
