Shearling coats are made from processed lambskin, sheepskin, or pelt. This "shearing" process creates a uniform depth of the wool fibers for a uniform feel and look. Shearling coats and garments are made from pelts by tanning them with the wool of uniform depth still on them. The result is a soft, natural fleece material that is lightweight and extremely comfortable. Shearling coats often contain insulated lining and are lightweight, warm, and stylish. Due to the high quality and uniqueness of Shearling, coats and garment are considered luxurious. The lighter the Shearling is, the higher quality it is considered to be.
