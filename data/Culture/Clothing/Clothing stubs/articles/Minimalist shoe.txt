Minimalist shoes are shoes intended to closely approximate barefoot running or walking conditions in comparison to traditional shoes.   They have reduced cushioning, thin soles, and are of lighter weight than other running shoes, allowing for more sensory contact for the foot on the ground while simultaneously providing the feet with some protection from ground hazards and conditions (such as pebbles and dirt).  Research shows that wearing a minimalist shoe can help improve foot strength and arch function.
