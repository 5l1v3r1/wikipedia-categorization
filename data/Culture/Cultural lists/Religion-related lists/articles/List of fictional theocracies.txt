Depictions of a fictional theocratic societies recur in science fiction, speculative fiction and fantasy. Such depictions are mostly dystopian, and in some cases humorous or satirical; positively presented theocracies are very much the exception. Some fictional theocracies are based on a future or alternate history development of actual religions, whilst in others the religions depicted are wholly fictional.
