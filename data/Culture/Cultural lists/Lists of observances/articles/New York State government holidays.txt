There are twelve observed New York State holidays for state employees.  Under the Attendance Rules, a holiday that falls on a Sunday is observed on the following Monday.  Lincoln's Birthday and Election Day are deemed floating holidays and can optionally be observed on a later date.  A holiday is observed by employees scheduled to work that day; it is not available as a regular or floating holiday for other employees.
