This list of pest-repelling plants includes plants believed for their ability to repel insects, nematodes, and other pests. They have been used in companion planting for pest control in agricultural and garden situations, and in households, although most research indicates that there are no plants that actually deter pests.  The essential oils of many plants are also well known for their pest-repellent properties.
