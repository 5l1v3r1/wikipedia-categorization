There are many universities that offer fashion design throughout the United States, usually within the context of a general liberal arts degree.  The major concentration incorporating fashion design may have alternative names like Apparel and Textiles or Apparel and Textile Design, and may be housed in departments such as Art and Art History, or Family and Consumer Studies.
