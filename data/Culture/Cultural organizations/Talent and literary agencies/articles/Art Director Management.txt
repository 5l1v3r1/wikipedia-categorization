Art Director Management (ADM) is an agency founded in 1998 by Hervé Morel,  representing freelance art directors specialized in the fashion and luxury industry.  Based in Paris, ADM represents : These art directors work for various brands, among which : Diptyque, Juicy Couture, Kenzo, De Fursac, Cartier, Hermès, Givenchy, Equipment, Piaget, Louis Vuitton, Guerlain, Issey Miyake Parfums, Jean-Paul Gaultier Parfums, Kenzo Parfums, Lancôme, Pucci Parfums, Balmain, Céline, Isabel Marant, M de Missoni, New Man, Giuseppe Zanotti, Krug, Shu Uemura.
