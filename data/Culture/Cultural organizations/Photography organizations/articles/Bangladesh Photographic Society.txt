Bangladesh Photographic Society is a national photographic society in Bangladesh and is located in Dhaka, Bangladesh.    It had the first photography gallery in Bangladesh with its own dark room. It is a member of the International Federation of Photographic Art.  Bangladesh Photographic Society was established on 1 January 1976 by photographers based in Bangladesh. The society owns and operates the Bangladesh Photographic Institute where photography training is provided. The society also organises annual national and biannual international photography competitions. The institute organises symposiums and seminars in Bangladesh.
