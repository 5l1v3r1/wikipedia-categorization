In-Public (sometimes written iN-PUBLiC) is an international group of street photographers that operates as a collective.   It was established in 2000 by Nick Turpin     with the intention of bringing together like minded photographers to hold exhibitions, produce books and conduct workshops and promote street photography.  It was the first international collective of street photographers.  Its geographically disparate membership is facilitated by the Internet.
