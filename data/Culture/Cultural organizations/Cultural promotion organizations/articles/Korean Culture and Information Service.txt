The Korean Culture and Information Service (KOCIS) is an affiliated organization of the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism  of the South Korean government and runs 32 Korean cultural centers in 27 countries. The goal of the organization is to further enhance the image of Korea's national brand by promoting Korean heritage and arts through these cultural centers. 造 To upgrade the country's nation brand and to publicize government policies 造 To promote the spread of Hallyu and to expand cultural exchanges 造 To produce promotional content and to facilitate its use 造 To foster cooperative media relations
