Culture Ireland (Irish: Cultúr Éireann) is the Irish State Agency established to promote and advance Irish Arts internationally.  It was set up in 2005 and is funded by the Department of Arts, Heritage and the Gaeltacht.  Its budget for 2010 was €4.083m.
