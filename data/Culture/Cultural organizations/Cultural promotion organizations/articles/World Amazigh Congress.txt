The World Amazigh Congress (Berber: ⴰⴳⵔⴰⵡ ⴰⵎⴰⴹⵍⴰⵏ ⴰⵎⴰⵣⵉⵖ Agraw Amaḍlan Amaziɣ, French: Congrès mondial Amazigh) is an international organization which aims to protect and promote awareness of the Berber people's identity and culture, across North Africa.  In October 2011, Fathi Ben Khalifa was elected president of the Congress, replacing Belkacem Lounes.   In July 2015, Kamira nayt Saïd was elected as a new president for the next 3 years.
