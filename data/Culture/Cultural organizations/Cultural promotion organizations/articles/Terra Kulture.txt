   Terra Kulture is an arts and culture center in Lagos with an attached restaurant.   Nigerian lawyer Bolanle Austen-Peters founded Terra Kulture in 2003.  The center is a restaurant, serving Nigerian cuisine, bookstore and cultural venue,  hosting exhibitions of Nigerian art,  theater,  and book readings as well as language classes in the three main Nigerian languages, Hausa, Ibo and Yoruba.   Annual events at Terra Kulture includes an art auction  and the Taruwa festival of Performing arts.
