MAGIS Italian Jesuit Missions Network (Movimento e Azione dei Gesuiti Italiani per lo Sviluppo) was founded in 1988 by the Italian Province of the Society of Jesus to coordinate the province’s international activities in solidarity with the most disadvantaged peoples, promoting intercultural and interreligious dialogue and integral development.
