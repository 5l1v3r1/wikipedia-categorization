Casa Fernando Pessoa is a cultural center in Campo de Ourique of Lisbon, Portugal. It was created in honor of the poet Fernando Pessoa, and conceived as a "home of poetry". It is located in the building where Pessoa lived between 1920 and 1935. The cultural center opened on November 30, 1993. Coordinates: 38°43′00″N 9°09′45″W﻿ / ﻿38.7166°N 9.1624°W﻿ / 38.7166; -9.1624
