University College of Opera (Swedish: Operahögskolan i Stockholm), is a Swedish institution in Stockholm offering higher education in the field of opera music and related arts. Operahögskolan is tasked with educating singers, répétiteurs and opera directors and has approximately 40 students, including 36 singers, enrolled.
