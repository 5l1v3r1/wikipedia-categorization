Club Dance Studio (Club Dance) is a dance studio located in Phoenix. Club Dance is widely known for its prestige within the dance community and for producing many successful professional and award-winning dancers. The owner of Club Dance is Kelli Wilkins. Some of the notable past and present dancers are award-winning dancers Bostyn Brown, Jaycee Wilkins, JT Church, Simrin Player, as well as TV personalities Sarah Reasons, Kalani Hilliker and Brynn Rumfallo.
