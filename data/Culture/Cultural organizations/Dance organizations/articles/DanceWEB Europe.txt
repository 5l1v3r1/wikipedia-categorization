 danceWEB Europe is a non profit association of dance organisations based in Vienna, Austria led by "danceWEB/Vienna". Funded under the "Culture 2000 programme of the European Union"  in 2002 to 31 May 2005 with a community grant of €900.000,00  and in 2005 up to 31 May 2008 with a community grant of € 900.000,00 .  to develop contemporary dance throughout Europe and to link the international dance scene and education, enhancing dialogue between cultures.
