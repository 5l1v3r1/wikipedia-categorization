The Proxecto Trasno is an open community dedicated to localise open-source software to Galician language.      Since it was founded at the end of the 1999, its volunteers have been engaged in the Galician translation of the main FLOSS projects, from desktop environments to Linux distributions   and in creating spell-checkers    or developing localization tools.
