Urdu Hall is a Urdu language promotion institution located in Hyderabad, India.    It has a library and archives of old Urdu literature. Urdu Hall was inaugurated by Independent India's first Prime Minister, Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru in December 1955 at Himayathnagar, Hyderabad. Noted Urdu poet, Makhdoom Mohiuddin was associated with the center. A chapter of Anjuman Taraqui Urdu is located here. There is an day and evening college running on its premises.
