Anjuman-e-Tarraqui-e-Khowar (Urdu: انجمن ترقی کھوار) is an Khowar language organisation founded by Hissam ul Mulk in 1965 for the promotion of Khowar language spoken in Chitral district of the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa province of Pakistan.  The organisation publishes Khowar books, and supports research and creative work in Khowar linguistics and literature.
