This is a list of Renaissance fairs worldwide. Included below are the fairs in the United States which are either notably long running or which regularly have had at least a two-week or better run. Generally, renaissance fairs are open weekends-only during the periods indicated.  States are listed first and dates are codified to facilitate searching by state and by date. Most Canadian events are only one weekend in duration.  Those notable and older than 10 years are listed here. Most Australian events are only one or two days in duration.
