The Dynacord ADD-One (Advanced Digital Drums) is a german Drum Machine that was first released in 1986. It uses samples to produce its sounds. It comes with 1Mbyte of memory and can be upgraded up to 8Mbs. The unit can sample at 50 khz at 12-bits   for up to 20 seconds. The unit featues a 80-character backlit LCD. Bass drums, congas, snares and Hihats.  The Chain mode  allows these to be called up in any order and stepped through by a footswitch. The unit also features MIDI which allows it to be controlled from an external deivce such as a synthesizer or electronic drums. Jean-Michel Jarre
