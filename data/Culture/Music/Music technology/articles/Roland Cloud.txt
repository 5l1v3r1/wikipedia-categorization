Roland Cloud is a subscription  based collection of VST instruments released by Roland  , using software called Cloud Manager  to download the instruments. After 12 months subscription to the service you are rewarded with an instruments of your choice via the loyalty program. There are features to the software versions of hardware synthesizers that weren't available in the originals, thanks to the feedback of the users over the years, Roland have been able to develop this.  Roland Cloud Manager manages your instrument library and sound sources and is auto updating by default.   Roland Cloud service is available for both PC and MAC and requires a 64-bit DAW.
