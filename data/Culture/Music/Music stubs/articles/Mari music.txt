Mari music is the music of the Volga-Finnic Mari people of Russia. Mari music is generally pentatonic.  Mari songs are generally short and lyrical.  Common themes include the Volga river, and a love of nature, to include forests and rainbows.  Mari instruments include the kusle or karsh, a type of zither. The Mari also play the tumyr (drum), birch-bark horn, accordions, and a Mari bagpipe, the shuvyr.
