  The Ballad of Angel's Alley is a musical set in Melbourne's 'push' wars of the 1890s, with book and lyrics by Jeff Underhill and music by Bruce George.  First seen at Melbourne's New Theatre in 1958, The Ballad of Angel's Alley received a professional premiere in July 1962 at the Russell St Theatre in Melbourne presented by the Union Theatre Repertory Company. Subsequent professional revivals included Sydney's Old Tote Theatre Company in September 1963 and Melbourne's St Martin's Theatre in April 1973.  The script was published by Yackandandah Playscripts in 1989.
