New Dreams Ltd. is a studio album by American electronic musician Vektroid under the alias Laserdisc Visions released on July 1, 2011.  The album is an early example of the genre vaporwave. The album was released through the independent label Beer on the Rug  and Vektroid's own label PrismCorp. The album has good reviews and is one of Vektroid's more popular releases.
