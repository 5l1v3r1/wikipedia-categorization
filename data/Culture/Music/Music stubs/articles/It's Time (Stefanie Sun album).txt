It's Time (simplified Chinese: 是时候; traditional Chinese: 是時候) is the tenth studio album by Singaporean singer Stefanie Sun (simplified Chinese: 孙燕姿; traditional Chinese: 孫燕姿), released on 8 March 2011 by Wonderful Music.  The album sold more than 75,000 copies in Taiwan alone, and became the second best-selling album of the year in Taiwan.
