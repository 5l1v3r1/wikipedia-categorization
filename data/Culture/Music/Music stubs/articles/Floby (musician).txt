Florent Belemgnegré, also known by his stage name Floby, is a musician from the Kouritenga Province of Burkina Faso. He sings in the French and Mòoré languages.   His music uses African sounds such as the kora and djembe but also rap and electric guitar.  He received two Kundé awards each in 2007,  2015  and 2016  and won the Golden Kundé in 2010.  He is also known by the nicknames le King Zodanga, le Papa des Orphelins and le Kirikou d’Afrique.
