Prelude and Fugue in B-flat minor, BWV 891, is the 22nd Prelude and Fugue in the Well-Tempered Clavier by J.S.Bach, written in 1738.   The prelude is in cut time and has 83 measures. It is made up mainly of eighth notes, quarter notes, half notes and whole notes.   The fugue is in 3/2 time and has 101 measures and 4 voices. It is made up mainly of eighth notes, half notes, dotted half notes, quarter notes, whole notes, and dotted whole notes.
