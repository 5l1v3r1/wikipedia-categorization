Olowex is a French electronic and cinematic music duo, founded by Holeg Spies and Yannis Kamarinos in 2016. Holeg Spies is best known for his film-scoring duo Savage & Spies (The Human Centipede (First Sequence), The Outsider...) and Yannis Kamarinos, electro composer and producer, notably acclaimed for his Giorgio Moroder remixes.
