"Mangiko" (Greek: Μάγκικο,Greek: Σκερτσοπεταχτό,Yiddish: Yoshke Fort Avek‎)  is a Greek and Yiddish folkloric tune hasaposerviko in the meter 24.  Greek music and lyrics are by Kostas Karipis. The Yiddish music and lyrics are by Aaron Lebedeff.  The song was also performed under the name "Chiftilareasa" by the Romanian music group Trei Parale.
