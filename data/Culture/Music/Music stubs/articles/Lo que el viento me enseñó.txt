Lo que el viento me enseñó is the eighth studio album by contemporary Christian music duo Tercer Cielo.  The album was released digitally on April 24, 2012 was released in physical format to the shops of music, produced and distributed under the record labels Universal Music Latin/Vene Music/Fé y Obra Music/Kasa Productions/Mucho Fruto respectively.  All tracks written by Juan Carlos Rodríguez.
