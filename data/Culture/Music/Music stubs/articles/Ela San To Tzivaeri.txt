Ela San To Tzivaeri,' Έλα σαν το τζιβαέρι (el)   is a Greek and Turkish folkloric tune (Tsifteteli). The meter is 44. Its music was composed Greek Vasilis Kazoulis. Greek lyrics written by Vasilis Kazoulis. There are similar song known as Κράτα Για Το Τέλος(Krata gia to telos).(Hebrew:Bakafe Shel Stelios) Its music was composed Greek Pantelis Thalassinos. Greek lyrics written by Elias Katsulis. Hebrew Lyrics written by Zion Kedem. Turkish name Sebahat Abla. Turkish lyrics written by Murathan Mungan.   There are similar song known as Добре Дошъл(Dobre Doshul).Its music was composed Bulgarian Gloria. Bulgarian lyrics written by Gloria.
