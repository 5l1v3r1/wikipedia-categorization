"Paluke Bangaaramaayena" is a popular Telugu composition of Kancherla Gopanna, popularly known as "Bhakta Ramadasu" or "Bhadrachala Ramadasu" who lived in the 17th century. He had written hundreds of thousands of songs on Lord Sri Rama. His music is not available. India's most famous classical musician Dr M. Balamuralikrishna gave music to his songs and popularized his compositions in 1950s. This song is composed in raga Anandabhairavi of Carnatic music set to Adi tala.  This became one of the most famous renditions of Sri Ramadasa, and is sung by millions people of all ages throughout the world.
