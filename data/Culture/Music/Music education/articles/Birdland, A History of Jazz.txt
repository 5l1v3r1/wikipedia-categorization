Birdland is a Danish animated television series produced and directed by Jannik Hastrup. A flamingo couple dance under the moon at night, only to awaken a grumpy parrot who steals a crescent moon. The birds fight over the moon over the course of the episode, until the parrot gets the moon only to turn out to be their egg with two baby flamingos inside it which hatches and the parrot flies off to the sunrise with the flamingos farewell him. A fat female cat attempts to eat a small bird, but fails. Soon she starts singing to the bird and the bird starts singing along with her. Though over the course of the song she tries to eat him, but instead falls in love.
