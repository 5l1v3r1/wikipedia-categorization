Rich Benjamin is an American cultural critic, anthropologist, and author. Benjamin is perhaps best known for the non-fiction book Searching for Whitopia: An Improbable Journey to the Heart of White America.    Benjamin's investigation of Whitopia was the subject of a 2015 TED Talk, which has garnered 2 million views across media platforms and earned translation into 25 languages.  This groundbreaking study is one of few to have illuminated in advance the social-political forces propelling the political rise of Donald Trump.  He is also a lecturer and a public intellectual, who regularly discusses issues on NPR, PBS, MSNBC, and CNN.   His writing frequently appears in The New York Times, The New Yorker, The Guardian and Salon.
