Southerneastern Chakavian (Serbo-Croatian: južnoistočni čakavski dijalekt) or Ijekavian accent is a subdialect of the Chakavian dialect of Serbo-Croatian. It is spoken by the peoples of the diaspora of Lastovo, and on the islands of  Janjina on Pelješac, Bigova on the south of Montenegro.
