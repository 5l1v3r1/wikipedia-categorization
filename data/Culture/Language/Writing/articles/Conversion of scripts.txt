The conversion of scripts or writing is a procedure of replacing text written in one script or writing system with the characters of another script or system in order to make the text (e.g., proper names) legible for users of another language or script. It has two basic forms and many practical implementations of each: A special case is re-conversion, i.e., returning to the original form from a form written while using one of the above-mentioned systems. This is used, among other things, as a text input method.
