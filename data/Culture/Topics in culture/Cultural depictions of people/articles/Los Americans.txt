Los Americans is an eight-part series that focuses on a multi-generational, middle-income Latino family living in Los Angeles. The show was written and directed by Dennis Leoni and released in 2011.  The Valenzuela family deals with many issues facing American families today, including unemployment, cultural identity and alcoholism.  The Imagen Foundation - Best Drama Web Series, 2012  Alan Greenlee, Vice President of Programs, One Economy stated "this series is an engaging drama that will help millions of riders take action to improve their lives and make informed decisions."
