  The Aziz Bagh Villa is a historic residence in Hyderabad, India, owned by the writer and former senior civil servant Dr. Hasanuddin Ahmed. It was built in 1899 by the Persian and Urdu poet Aziz Jang Vila in what is described as an Indo-German style. In 1997 it was given a Cultural Heritage Award by INTACH, the Indian National Trust for Art and Cultural Heritage.  Aziz Bagh Http://www.azizbagh.com
