Torawati Rajput Sabha is an annual event organized in the Torawati region of India to commemorate the festival of Dussera and Shastra Pujan. In Dussera being celebrated on Ashtami (eighth) instead of Dashami (10th) as main festivities were held in Jaipur. In 2013, former Chief of Army Staff Gen. V.K. Singh was the chief guest at the festival held in Neem-ka-thana. Other dignitaries to be felicitated this day were Member of Parliament Narendra Singh Tomar, Dr. L.S. Rathore, Dr. L.K. Shaktawat, Rao Digvijay Singh of Patan.
