  Aadivasi Lok Kala Academy is a cultural institution established by Government of Madhya Pradesh in 1980 with the objective of encouraging, preserving and developing the tribal arts.  It conducts surveys, organizes programs and publishes texts and materials on tribal folk arts. It also organises many festival related to the tribal arts and folk theatre, main being Lok Rang, Ram Leela Mela, Nimad Utsav, Sampada and Shruti Samaroh.
