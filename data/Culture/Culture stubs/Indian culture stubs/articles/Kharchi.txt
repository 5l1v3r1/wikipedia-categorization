Kharchi is the Gods of the Tripuri people. The following are the names of various gods worshipped by the Tripuris and other major tribes of the state. The word Matai  means god and Katar mean great or supreme. The twin deity of sky and sea. Lam means sky or earth and Pra means sea or water. Deities personifying the sky, the sea and the mountains. Tuima means water-mother or water-goddess. the goddess of corn. the goddess of cotton plant. The two brothers Goraiya and Kataiya worshipped on the last day of the Hindu year (chaitra sankranti).
