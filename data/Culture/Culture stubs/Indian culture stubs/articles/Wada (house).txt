A wada (Marathi वाडा) is a traditional mansion typically in the western part of India. Some examples are the Mangalgad in Dudhanewadi, the wada at Menawali, the Shaniwar Wada in Pune, Maharashtra,  the Vishrambaug Wada in Pune  and the Raste wada Gadhi - a castle-like structure (also called a 'Big Wada)
