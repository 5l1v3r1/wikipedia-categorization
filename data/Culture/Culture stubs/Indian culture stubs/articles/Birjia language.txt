The Birjia language, also known as Binjhia or Bijori, is a language of India.  It is commonly assumed to be a Munda language closely related to the Asuri language.  However, Anderson,  based on Prasad (1961:314), suggests that Birjia (Binjhia) may be an Indo-Aryan language, although the Birjia are a tribe of the Asuri nation.
