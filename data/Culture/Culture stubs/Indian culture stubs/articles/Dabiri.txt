Dabiri is an Indian ethnic fashion brand founded by Divya Bindra Kapoor  and Ambika Jain in 2001   in New Delhi, India. It has multiple outlets across India. Dabiri is a member of Fashion Design Council of India (FDCI). Dabiri specialises in bridal wear. Divya Bindra Kapoor  started the company in New Delhi. Dabiri opened its first standalone store in Mumbai. Ambika Jain acquired stake in Dabiri. Dabiri increased investment to 25.8%. Re-branded Dabiri as Dabiri Couture. In 2002, Dabiri tied up with Akriti arts to replicate designer costumes from four of its movies thus tapping the Merchandising movie memorabilia of Indian market.
