Hindi children rhymes is called Bal Kavita. These are poems written for children in Hindi. Example of a bal kavita is the following poem by Suryakumar Pandey- Ek paan ka patta, ja pahuncha kalkatta, pakde apna mathha, Mila wahan par kathha.  Aage mili supari, sabne ki taiyarri, pahunche fir sab poona, laga wahaan par choona.
