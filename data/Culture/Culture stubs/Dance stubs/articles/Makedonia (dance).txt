Makedonia (Greek: Μακεδονία, translit. Macedonía or Greek: Μακεδονικός Χορός, translit. Macedonicós Chorós) is a form of the Greek folk dance Hasapiko (Greek: χασάπικο, translit. chasápico) that has evolved over the years to the patriotic song "Makedonia Xakousti" (Famous Macedonia), unofficial anthem of the Greek region of Macedonia.
