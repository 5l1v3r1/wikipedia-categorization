The Harbor Conservatory for the Performing Arts is a performing arts center in Spanish Harlem, New York City, USA.  It provides performance space and instruction in the disciplines of theatre, music, and dance.   It has been profiled in People Magazine, The New York Times, New York Daily News and in the public television documentary, Mi Mambo!.
