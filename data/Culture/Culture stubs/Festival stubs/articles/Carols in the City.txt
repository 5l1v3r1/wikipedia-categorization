  Carols in the City (also known as IGA Lord Mayor's Carols in the City), is an annual free Christmas concert event held at Riverstage in Brisbane, Australia. It is broadcast around Queensland on the Nine Network since 1970s until 2010 and returned 2014, formerly on Network Ten in 2011 until 2013. It is a major supporter of the Royal Flying Doctor Service of Australia.  The concert features many established, as well as up and coming talent. Royal Flying Doctor Service of Australia
