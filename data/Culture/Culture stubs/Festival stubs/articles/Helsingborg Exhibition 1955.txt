Helsingborg exhibition of 1955, also known as H55, was a BIE recognised world's fair held in Helsingborg, Sweden  held between 10 June and 28 August 1955.     Its theme was arts and crafts, with an aim of showing how modern design could be used in commercial as well as luxury goods.  Exhibitors came from 10 countries  with items including Nisse Strinning's String design bookshelf
