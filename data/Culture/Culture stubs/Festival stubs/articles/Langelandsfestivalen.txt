Langelandsfestivalen, also known as Denmark's largest garden party, is a Danish family festival. The festival began in 1991 and over time has grown into one of Denmark's biggest festivals with 30,000 to 35,000 visitors in 2008. It is usually held in the last week of July and typically lasts for four days, although in 2010 it will be held for a full week, from July 24 to July 31.
