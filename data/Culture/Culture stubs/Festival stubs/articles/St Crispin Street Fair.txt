The St Crispin Street Fair is an annual fair held the town centre of Northampton, England organised by Northampton Borough Council though it is not held every year. A travelling funfair with over 100 amusement rides operating from 10:30am to 10:30pm on several days of the October half-term school holiday with the precise days varying from year to year. The fair was also held from October 28-31 in 2012. Shop owners raised concerns about how the new fair could affect their business during Halloween weekend.  In 2014 the fair was held from October 25th to 29th with a large part of the fair held on a nearby car park. this is because one of the streets, The Drapery, is now used as an overflow bus station.
