Coordinates: 42°7′46″N 80°5′7″W﻿ / ﻿42.12944°N 80.08528°W﻿ / 42.12944; -80.08528 The Roar on the Shore is motorcycle rally that takes place annually in Erie, Pennsylvania. The Roar on the Shore was started in 2007.   Every year the rally is kicked off with "Bringin' In The Roar": a motorcycle parade from Presque Isle Downs and Casino in Summit Township to Perry Square in downtown Erie. Other major events are the "Thunder On The Isle", a motorcycle ride around Presque Isle State Park led by Erie mayor Joe Sinnott, and the "Roar To The Vineyards Poker Run" to North East.
