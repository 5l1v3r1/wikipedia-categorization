The 8th International Architecture Exhibition at the 2002 Venice Biennale of Architecture, titled Next, was directed by Deyan Sudjic. Held at the Arsenale and Giardini exhibition venues, it took place from September 8 to November 3. The exhibition attracted over 100,000 visitors. The awards went to:  World Trade Center.  Commissioner: Robert Ivy, Chief Executive Officer of the American Institute of Architects (AIA)
