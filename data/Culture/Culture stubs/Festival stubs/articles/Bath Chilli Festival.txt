The Bath Chilli Festival  is a chilli and music festival that takes place annually in the city of Bath.  The festival was held at the University of Bath on the weekend of the 15 and 16 July.  The 2015 event was held at the University of Bath campus on Saturday 26 September.  The 2014 event was held at the University of Bath on Saturday 27 September. The festival grew impressively from the previous year, with several thousand in attendance,  including the Mayor of Bath who was there to hand out prizes in the awards ceremony. The 2013 event was held at the University of Bath on the weekend of the 5 and 6 October.
