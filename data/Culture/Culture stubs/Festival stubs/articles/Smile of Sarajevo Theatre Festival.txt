The Smile of Sarajevo Theatre Festival (Bosnian: Festival komedije Osmijeh Sarajeva / Фестивал комедије Осмијех Сарајева) is an international comedy theatre festival that is annually held in Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina.  It was established in 2017 by a team of theatre professionals headed by Nedžad Podžić "Poćko". The festival is organized in May and lasts for 9 days.  It showcases comedy theatre performances from Bosnia and Herzegovina and the Former Yugoslavia.
