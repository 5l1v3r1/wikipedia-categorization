Mahjong has been a common culture of China, Hong Kong, Japan, and other Asian regions.  It shows a high degree of influence from Chinese culture.  In Hong Kong: However, Mahjong games also create problems.  Addiction to Mahjong is a common type of problem gambling.  Mahjong is also a favourite medium for bribery - the person giving the bribe will intentionally lose large sums of money to the person being bribed. Recent studies also suggest Mahjong can cause epilepsy
