Talking shit is a term and type of trash-talk that refers to various types of derogatory language  aimed at an individual or any type of entity, such as a group or organization. Talking shit can be used as a tactic in fighting or brawling, used to draw attention to the matter among onlookers.  This is a term that has been coined more recently and is used in reference when someone talks negatively or spreads false ideas about another person, concept, organization, or entity. Shitposting is the act of posting "content of aggressively, ironically, and trollishly poor quality"  to an online forum.
