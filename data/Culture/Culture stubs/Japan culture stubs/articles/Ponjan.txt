Ponjan (ポンジャン), also known as Donjara, is a kids version of the table game mahjong played in Japan. Ponjan (also spelled Pom Jong in English) has three types of tiles: cars, boats and airplanes. Donjara is the Bandai registered trademark version of this game. The game is played with 2 to 4 players. Ponjan, is also a Malayalam word for ganja, cannabis.
