 Kimodameshi (きもだめし or 肝試し, lit. "test one's liver" ), or test of courage is a Japanese activity in which people explore scary places to build up courage.   Kimodameshi is usually played in the summer, in group activities such as school club trips or camping. at night, group of people visit scary places such as a cemetery, haunted house, or a forest path to carry out specific missions there.
