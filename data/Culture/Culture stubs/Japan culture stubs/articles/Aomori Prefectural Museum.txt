The Aomori Prefectural Museum (青森県立郷土館, Aomori Kenritsu Kyōdokan) is a museum located in Aomori, Aomori Prefecture, Japan.  The museum has a collection of artifacts from the Jōmon Period, as well as exhibits detailing Aomori's culture and history.  The museum opened in 1973.
