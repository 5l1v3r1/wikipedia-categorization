The Association for the Study of American Indian Literatures (ASAIL) is a professional academic organization. It was founded in 1971   to promote the study, criticism, and research of American Indian literary traditions, both written and oral.  Its journal, Studies in American Indian Literatures, has been the primary journal for the study of North American indigenous literature for over thirty years.
