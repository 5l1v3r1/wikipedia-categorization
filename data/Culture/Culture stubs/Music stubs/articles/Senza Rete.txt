Senza Rete was a music show created by Giorgio Calabrese and broadcast by Rai 1 (at the time  Programma Nazionale) from 1968 to 1975. The first episode was aired June 27, 1968. Initially broadcast on Thrursday night, thanks to the high ratings (up to 18 million viewers) starting from the third edition it was moved to the more prestigious Saturday night slot. The show consisted on two or three popular musical guests performing live several hits of their repertoire at the Auditorium Rai in Naples.
