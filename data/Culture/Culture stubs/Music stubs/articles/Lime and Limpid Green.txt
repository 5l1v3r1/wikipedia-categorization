Lime And Limpid Green is a 2017 E.P. of covers released by the psychedelic rock duet of Les Claypool and Sean Lennon, under the name The Claypool Lennon Delirium. The album comprises four covers of songs originally by Pink Floyd, The Who, King Crimson and Flower Travellin' Band. It was originally released for Record Store Day in 2017, but it was eventually made available streaming.
