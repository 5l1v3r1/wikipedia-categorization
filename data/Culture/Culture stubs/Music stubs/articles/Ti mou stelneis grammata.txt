Ti mou stelneis grammata,( Greek: Τι μου στέλνεις γράμματα)    is an anonymous Cappadocian Greek  folkloric tune (Kasik Havasi).The meter is 44. There are similar folkloric tunes known as Pınar başı burma burma. There are similar folkloric tunes known as  Sut ictim dilim yandi. The original form of the song was popular in Cappadocia by Cappadocian Greeks.
