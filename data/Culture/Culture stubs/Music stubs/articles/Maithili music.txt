  Traditional Modern Maithili Music is one of the most ancient types of music in South Asia . It originated from Mithila region which is now divided between India  and Nepal.  No on knows exactly when Maithili Music came  into existence, probably due to the length of its history, but its age indicates that it might have helped other music develop and flourish in India and Nepal. Although Maithili music is usually played by classical instruments, it has been modernized and now uses various modern instruments. Some significant contributors to this music style are Maha Kavi Vidyapati Thakur, Udit Narayan Jha and Sharda Sinha. The region's folk songs are associated with the various events in the life of an ordinary person.
