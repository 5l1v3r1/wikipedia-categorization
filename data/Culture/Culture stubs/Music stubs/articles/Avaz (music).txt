Avaz, or Awaz (Persian: آواز) is unmetered vocal section of a mode in Persian music. In the years 1965 and 1966 Mahmoud Karimi (maestro of Persian vocal music) performed the whole Avazs which were recorded and transcribed by Mohammad-Taghi Massoudieh.
