Barbayianakis or Μια μαύρη πέτρα του γιαλού (Mia mavri petra tou gialou)(el)  and Μπαρμπαγιαννακάκης(el)  is an anonymous Greek folkloric tune (Syrtos or Tsifteteli). The meter is 44.There are similar folkloric tunes such as the Cyprus tune "Loulla mou maroula mou"(Λουλλα μου μαρουλα μου). The original form of the Syrtos   was popular in Urla.
