Leave is the fifth studio album by Singaporean singer Stefanie Sun (simplified Chinese: 孙燕姿; traditional Chinese: 孫燕姿), released on 21 May 2002 by Warner Music Taiwan.  The song, "We Will Get There" is the theme song of the Singapore National Day Parade 2002.
