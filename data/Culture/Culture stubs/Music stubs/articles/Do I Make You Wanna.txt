 "Do I Make You Wanna" is a song written by Ashley Gorley, Zach Crowell, Matt Jenkins, and Jerry Flowers and recorded by American country music artist Billy Currington. It was released in November 2016 as the fourth single from Currington's 2015 album Summer Forever. "Do I Make You Wanna" reached No. 1 on the Country Airplay chart on August 1, 2017, and stayed at that position for three consecutive weeks.
