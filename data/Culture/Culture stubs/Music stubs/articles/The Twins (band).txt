  Brooke Kelaart and Ellie Kelaart (born 18 February 1987) are an Australian EDM, electro house and progressive house duo, better known by their stage name The Twins (styled The Twins). After signing with Sony/ATV Music Publishing at 18 years of age, the sisters pursued careers as songwriting partners and, in 2008, they signed with Fredrik Olsson and his Swedish music publishing company Razor Boy Music Publishing.
