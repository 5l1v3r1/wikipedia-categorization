Asetrad (abbreviation for the "Asociación Española de Traductores, Correctores e Intérpretes" or the "Spanish Association of Translators, Copy-editors, and Interpreters") is an association based in Spain to support and facilitate the work of translators, copy-editors, and interpreters. It is a member of the International Federation of Translators (FIT).  Asetrad has an online publication, "La Linterna del Traductor".
