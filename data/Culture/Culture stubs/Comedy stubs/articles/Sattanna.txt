  Sattanna (Telugu:సత్తన్న) Satish Kumar is a Telugu humorist anchor and comedian. He acted in more than 94 (upto10.4 2018) films as a character artist. He is best known as Nizam Babu and hosts TV news and entertainment shows. He is presently doing a program in ETV plus called FUNDAY. which cones every tuesday atv10.30 pm, He also interviews senior artists in various Telugu channels and occasionally appears in Telugu films.
