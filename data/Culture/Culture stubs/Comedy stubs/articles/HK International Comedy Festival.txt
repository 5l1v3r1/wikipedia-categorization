The HK International Comedy Festival is an annual comedy festival in Hong Kong. Founded by The TakeOut Comedy Club Hong Kong in 2007 for English and Chinese-speaking comedians resident in Hong Kong, the festival has expanded to include comedians from all over the world.  There are separate English and Chinese language competitions.  The festival also includes a night featuring improvisational comedy. The 2016 festival will take place between October 13 and November 5.
