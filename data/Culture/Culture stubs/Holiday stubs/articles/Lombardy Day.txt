The Lombardy Day, officially "Festa Regionale della Lombardia", in Lombard language "Festa Regional de la Lombardia", is the Lombardy's holyday. It celebrate the victory of the Lombard League against the Emperor in the Battle of Legnano, and it's on May 29.
