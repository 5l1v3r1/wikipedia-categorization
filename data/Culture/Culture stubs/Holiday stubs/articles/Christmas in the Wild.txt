Christmas in the Wild is an upcoming American Christmas romantic comedy film directed by Ernie Barbarash from a screenplay by Neal Dobrofsky and Tippi Dobrofsky. The film stars Rob Lowe, Kristin Davis, and John Owen Lowe. In May 2018, it was reported that Ernie Barbarash would direct Christmas in the Wild for Netflix from a screenplay by Neal Dobrofsky and Tippi Dobrofsky. Rob Lowe and Kristin Davis and local extra, Thelma Oelofse were set to star in the film.  Filming will take place in Hoedspruit and Drakensberg, and on sets in and around Cape Town.  Principal photography began in June 2018.
