A Christmas pageant is a public event conducted in celebration of the Christmas holiday, typically involving a entertainment in the form of a procession (such as a  Santa Claus parade), or a Nativity play or other performance. (Reference 1)   The Santa Claus Parade is also known as the Thanksgiving Day Parade which is held on Thanksgiving yearly.
