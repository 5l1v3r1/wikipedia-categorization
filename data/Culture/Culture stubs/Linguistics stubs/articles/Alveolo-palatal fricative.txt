Alveolo-palatal fricative is a class of consonants in some oral languages.  The consonants are sibilants, a variety of fricative. Their place of articulation is postalveolar. They differ in voicing. The voiceless alveolo-palatal fricative is written ɕ in the International Phonetic Alphabet.
