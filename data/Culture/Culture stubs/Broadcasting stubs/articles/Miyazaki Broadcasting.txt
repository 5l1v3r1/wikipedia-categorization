MRT Miyazaki Broadcasting Company (株式会社 宮崎放送, MRT miyazaki hoso kabushiki gaisha) is a broadcasting station in Miyazaki Prefecture, Japan, and it is affiliated with Japan Radio Network (JRN), Japan News Network (JNN) and TBS Network. It is owned by the government of the prefecture, Mainichi Shimbun, the Bank of Miyazaki and various investors with fewers stocks. The name MRT comes from Miyazaki Radio and Television, the name was adopted in 1961 upon changing from RMK (Radio Miyazaki K.K.).
