Shizuoka Broadcasting System, Inc.  (SBS, 静岡放送株式会社) is a Japanese broadcaster in Shizuoka. Its radio station is affiliated with Japan Radio Network (JRN) and National Radio Network (NRN), and its TV station is affiliated with JNN (Japan News Network). SBS Radio JOVR-DTV - SBS Digital Television JOVR-TV - SBS Television (analog ended July 24, 2011) The abbreviation, SBS, is being used since September 22 1960.
