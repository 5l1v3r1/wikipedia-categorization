Committee of Radio and Television "Polish Radio and Television" (Polish: Komitet do Spraw Radia i Telewizji "Polskie Radio i Telewizja") (also called as a Radiocomittee - Radiokomitet) was an institution, which managed all stations of Polish Television and Polish Radio in People's Republic of Poland (PRL) and in Republic of Poland. It was replaced by two separate entities in 1993 - Polish Radio and Polish Television.
