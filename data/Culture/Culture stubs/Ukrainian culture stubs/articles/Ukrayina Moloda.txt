Ukrayina Moloda (Ukrainian: Україна молода, Young Ukraine) is a daily Ukrainian-language newspaper based in Kiev with a circulation of 99,000. It represents the political opposition and caters to a younger audience.  Initially created as a newspaper of the Central Committee of Communist Party of Ukraine for young generation 1991, but already in August of the same year it became independent. It supports pro-Western policies, president-elect Viktor Yushchenko, and is highly critical of president Leonid Kuchma. It offers both domestic and international news reporting, analysis, and interviews. Ukrayina Moloda is a member of the Ukrainian Association of Press Publishers (UAPP).
