Chicago Fusion Theatre (CFT) was founded in Chicago, Illinois in 2007 by a collective of artists and business associates committed to physical based theatre that fuses different genres, mediums and styles in an effort to foster a greater appreciation of diversity and the arts in the community.   The company has put on several productions since its conception including Hugging the Shoulder and bobrauschenbergamerica.   CFT's next production will be J.B. by Archibald MacLeish and opens on March 12th.
