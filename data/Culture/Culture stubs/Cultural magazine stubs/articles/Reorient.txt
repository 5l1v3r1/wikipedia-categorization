Reorient is a Canadian online magazine focusing on contemporary Middle Eastern arts and culture. Founded in 2012 by Joobin Bekhrad,  it is a non-profit, non-religious, non-political, non-partisan, and non-ideological publication made possible by the efforts of contributors around the world. The areas covered primarily include visual art, music, books and literature, and film.
