Mosaic  is an online journal of Jewish ideas. Mosaic was established in June 2013.  A free online magazine, it offers daily summaries and links to stories around the web of interest to readers, original essays, and an extensive "... full-length monthly essay on an issue or theme of pressing significance for Jews, Judaism, or the Jewish state".  Issue topics range from cultural or religious questions through to social and philosophical topics with in depth responses appended throughout the month for the benefit of both Jewish and Gentile readers.  In addition, the Magazine carries the popular etymology column Philologos.   The editor is Neal Kozodoy.
