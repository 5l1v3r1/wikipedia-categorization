Chhatra Sangram (meaning Student's Struggle in English) is a Bengali magazine, the organ of the Students' Federation of India, West Bengal state committee.  It has been published since 1964 and played a great role in developing the leftist student movement in West Bengal. Neo Prints is the publisher of the magazine.
