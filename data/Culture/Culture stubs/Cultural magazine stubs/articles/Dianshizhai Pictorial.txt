Dianshizhai huabao or Dianshizhai Pictorial (《點石齋畫報》, 1884–1898) was a Chinese language magazine published in Shanghai in the late 19th century. The profusely illustrated supplement of the Shen Bao newspaper became "wildly popular" among readers.   Contributors included graphic artist Wu Youru.  Cover and fold-out Advertisement, 1889 1890
