Thelitcham Monthly is an Islamic cultural magazine in the Malayalam languages published by Darul Huda Islamic University   from Kerala State of India. The magazine was launched on October 1998 as a literary contribution of the students at the University. Bahauddeen Muhammed Nadwi is the Chief Editor.  Besides research articles on Islam and Muslims, Thelitcham analyzes the burning current issues from an Islamic perspective. Most of the readers are educated Muslim youth.  The magazine has addressed social issues such as the rights of transgender people in Islam.
