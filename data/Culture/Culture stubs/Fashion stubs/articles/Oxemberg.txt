  Oxemberg is a formal & casual clothing brand for men from the house of Siyaram's.  Its corporate headquarters are located in Mumbai, India. Oxemberg operates mainly through multi-brand  outlets with approximately 2000 outlets pan India.   The apparels are available at all large format outlets like, Central, Reliance Trends, Max, Hypercity, More & LuLu’s; at 140+ exclusive Siyaram’s Shops and 20 exclusive Oxemberg outlets spread across India. Saif Ali Khan has been signed as the latest brand ambassador for Oxemberg in May 2013.
