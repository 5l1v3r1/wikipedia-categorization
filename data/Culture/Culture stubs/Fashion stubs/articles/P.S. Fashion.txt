P.S. Fashion (stylized as P...S.... fashion) is an international brand from Serbia. It is the flagship chain store of the P.S. Fashion Design, clothing company headquartered in Čačak, Serbia. P.S. Fashion has more than 100 stores (including company-owned, franchises and wholesale stores) in different locations, predominantly in Serbia and countries of the former Yugoslavia. It also has two stores in Paris and Toronto.  P.S Fashion is one of the largest Serbian clothing companies and one of the largest companies in the city of Čačak. P.S. Fashion has sponsored local basketball club KK Borac Čačak which plays in top-tier of Serbia - Basketball League of Serbia.  Company's owner and director Predrag Pantović has been the president of the club since December 2017.
