GirlsAward 2016 Spring/Summer (ガールズアワード 2016 Spring/Summer) is a fashion and music event held on April 9, 2016 at Yoyogi National Gymnasium 1st Gymnasium in Tokyo, Japan.  The main MCs were Minami Takahashi and Ryōta Yamasato.  This is the first job for Minami Takahashi since she left AKB48.  The video of the runway show was distributed through Vine.
