GirlsAward 2014 Spring/Summer (ガールズアワード 2014 Spring/Summer) is a fashion and music event held on April 19, 2014 at Yoyogi National Gymnasium 1st Gymnasium in Tokyo, Japan.  In this event, Dewi Sukarno, known as one of the wives of the first President of Indonesia and Japanese tarento, appeared as a secret guest.  Funassyi, the mascot character of Funabashi city and Japanese cartoon character Crayon Shin-chan appeared as a run model.  The main MCs are Japanese comedy duo Cocorico and Fuji Television newscaster Mio Matsumura.
