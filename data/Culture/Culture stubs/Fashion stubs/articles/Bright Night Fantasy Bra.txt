The Bright Night Fantasy Bra was designed for Victoria's Secret and shown at the Victoria's Secret Fashion Show 2016. Angel Jasmine Tookes had the honours this year to wear the million dollar bra, designed by jewellery designer Eddie Borgo The Bright Night Fantasy Bra is worth $3,000,000, it is made of 9000 precious emerald gemstones and It took 700 hours to make. The gemstones are set in more than 450 karats of 18-karat gold. Scanning Jasmine's body and utilising plaster molds were part of the design process to ensure it fits her body perfectly. The design includes handcrafted jewels by AWMouzannar.
