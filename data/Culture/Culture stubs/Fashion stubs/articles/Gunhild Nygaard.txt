Gunhild Nygaard (born February 22, 1965) is a Norwegian fashion designer. She has her education from the Esmod fashion school in Paris, France. Since 1990 she has lived in Paris, and has previously worked as designer at both Givenchy og Christian Dior. In 2007 she presented her first solo collection gunhild, and received the award Créateur de l'Année from the Mayor of Paris in 2009.
