From 2009 onwards the fashion week has been held annually in the capital city of Kathmandu. TGIF Nepal Fashion Week 2011 had 34 designers showcasing their collections on the catwalk. The fashion week lasts for 4 days with all the glitz and glamor on the catwalk.
