BonWorth is an American retail clothing chain based in Hendersonville, North Carolina.  Founder Loren Wells started BonWorth in 1966 in Hendersonville, and expanded throughout the 1980s. Mr. Wells, the sixth owner of Seely Castle, sold the company in 2013 and has retired to Asheville NC.  By 1992, it had 65 stores in 28 states,  and by 2007, it had more than 290.  Its clothing lines are targeted at women 50 and older.
