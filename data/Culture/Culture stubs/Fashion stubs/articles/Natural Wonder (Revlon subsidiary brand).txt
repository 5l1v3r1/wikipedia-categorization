Natural Wonder Cosmetics the United States cosmetics brand, was operated by Revlon Consumer Products Corporation from 1963  to 2000, when Revlon sold the division to CVC Capital Partners.  One of the first products to use the brand was a medicated foundation.  Natural Wonder provided Revlon with an economy cosmetics brand throughout the 1980s and 1990s.
