Andreea Stancu is a Romanian fashion model.  She has modeled for Alberta Ferretti, Christian Dior, Dolce & Gabbana, John Galliano, Giorgio Armani, Gianfranco Ferré, Givenchy, Fendi, Kenzo , Dsquared2, Karl Lagerfeld, Emilio Pucci, Gattinoni, Laura Biagiotti, Fausto Sarli  and many others. She has appeared in fashion advertisements for Extè, Francesco Scognamiglio, Wolford, Kai Kuhne and others.  Her agencies are Ford Models (New York) and Women Management (Milan, Paris).
