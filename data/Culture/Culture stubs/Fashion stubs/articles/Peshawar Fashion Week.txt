Peshawar Fashion Week (PFW) is an annually fashion event held in Peshawar, Pakistan.  It represents the culture of Pakistan, especially Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. Events is organised by the KHYBER PAKHTOONKHAW Fashion DESIGN and Arts Council (KPFDAC).  First event was held in 2011.
