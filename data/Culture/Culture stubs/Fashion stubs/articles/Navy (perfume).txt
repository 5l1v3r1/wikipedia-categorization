Navy is a perfume originally created by CoverGirl and now owned by Dana Classic Fragrances. It was released as a women's fragrance in 1990  and a later version was released for men in 1995  (FragranceX however places the release in 1996 ). The women's fragrance is considered oriental floral   and contains a blend of peach, orange blossom, jasmine, ylang-ylang, rose, amber, musk, vanilla, coriander and cinnamon. The men's fragrance contains juniper berry, leather, tangerine, nutmeg, geranium, sage, water lily, water mint, lavender, siam wood, balsam fir and musk.
