Electric Youth was a celebrity scent designed by Deborah Gibson.  Manufactured by Revlon Consumer Products Corporation and distributed by the latter's Natural Wonder Cosmetics subsidiary (sold 2000 by Revlon to CVC Capital Partners), Electric Youth was marketed concurrently with Gibson's Atlantic Records album, single and dance maxi-single of the same title.
