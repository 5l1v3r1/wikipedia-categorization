GirlsAward 2016 Autumn/Winter (ガールズアワード 2016 Autumn/Winter) is a fashion and music event held on October 8, 2016 at Yoyogi National Gymnasium 1st Gymnasium in Tokyo, Japan.   The main MCs were Minami Takahashi, Ryōta Yamasato and Yurika Mita.  In this event, Nogizaka46 made their 8th appearance and became the most participants.  The event was partially streamed on Line Live.
