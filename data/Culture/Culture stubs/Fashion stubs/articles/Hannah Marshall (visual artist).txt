  Hannah Marshall (born 6 July 1982) is a British visual artist, creative director, and former fashion designer,.  Marshall grew up near Colchester, and graduated from Colchester School of Art and Design with a first class honors degree. Marshall has worked with The xx, Florence and The Machine, Goldfrapp, Róisín Murphy, Janet Jackson, Jessie Ware,  and Savages.
