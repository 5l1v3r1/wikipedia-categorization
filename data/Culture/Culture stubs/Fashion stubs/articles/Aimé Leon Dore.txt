Aimé Leon Dore is a high-fashion streetwear brand  founded in 2014  by Teddy Santis  and based in Queens, New York City.   Highsnobiety describes the brand as “like the Big Apple’s answer to A.P.C.”.  Its concept store is on Mott Street in Manhattan's Chinatown.   The brand collaborated in 2017 with Kith on hoodies, t-shirts, pullover sailing jackets, pants, and caps with a nautical theme and  anchors, boat paddles, and evil eye iconography inspired by Mykonos.   In 2015 the brand collaborated with Puma on an update of  Puma's classic State sneaker.
