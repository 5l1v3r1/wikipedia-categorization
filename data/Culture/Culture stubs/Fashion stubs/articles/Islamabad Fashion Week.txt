Islamabad Fashion Week (IFW) is a fashion week annually held in Islamabad, Pakistan.  IFW was first held in 2011, run by Triple-E (Pvt.) Ltd.  Tariq Amin is the creative head of IFW and Karachi Fashion Week.  First IFW was held in 2011 at Pak-China Friendship Centre, Islamabad.
