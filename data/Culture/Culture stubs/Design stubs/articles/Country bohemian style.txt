Country bohemian style is a fashion style synthesizing rural elements with the bohemian style,  creating a bohemian approach to life in the country.  The country bohemian style can refer to both fashion  and interior design.  The country bohemian style is a deliberate blending of two seemingly disparate styles, country and bohemian. It incorporates local, rural features into bohemian sensibilities, favoring sustainability and rustic features while also embracing modern contributions.
