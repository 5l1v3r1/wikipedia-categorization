The World of Interiors magazine is published by Condé Nast with a total readership of 152,000.  The glossy monthly magazine features articles and photographs about interior design. The magazine began  as Interiors in November 1981.  It was founded in London, England, by Kevin Kelly, with Min Hogg as editor. In 1983, the magazine was bought by Condé Nast and it began publishing internationally under the name The World of Interiors.  Its current editor is Rupert Thomas.
