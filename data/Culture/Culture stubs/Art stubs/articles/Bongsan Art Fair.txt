  Bongsan Art Fair is a festival in Daegu, South Korea. The festival, which is held in mid-October, is also the county's only art festival. The Bongsan Art Festival is held simultaneously at 16 art galleries within Bongsan Culture Street and offers a wide range of genres(Oriental paintings, Western paintings, sculpture, crafts, calligraphy works, and installations) for more than 30 artists. The festival also offers a wide range of attractions, including a celebration performance, a lecture meeting, and a contact event with a writer.
