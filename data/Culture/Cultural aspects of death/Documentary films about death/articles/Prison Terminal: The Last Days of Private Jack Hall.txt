Prison Terminal: The Last Days of Private Jack Hall is a 2013 documentary film by  Edgar Barens. This film tells the story of Jack Hall, a terminally ill octogenarian lifer at Iowa State Penitentiary. The film looks at the last six months of Jack's life and the creation of a hospice run by other inmates serving life sentences.
