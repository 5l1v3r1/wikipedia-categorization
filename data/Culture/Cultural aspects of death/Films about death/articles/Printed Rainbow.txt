  Printed Rainbow is an award-winning 2006 Indian animated short film directed, animated and produced by Gitanjali Rao. It was first screened in International Critc's Week 2006 film festival held in Cannes, France.    A lonely old phillumenist woman lives in a little flat and uses her collection of matchboxes covers to dream away to more adventurous and fantastical worlds. Background score for the film is composed by Rajivan Ayappan. It also features one folk song Na Ja Balam Pardes sung by Begum Akhtar.
