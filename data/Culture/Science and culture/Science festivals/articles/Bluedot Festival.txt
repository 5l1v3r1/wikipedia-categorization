The Bluedot Festival is a  music festival held annually in July since 2016 at Jodrell Bank Observatory in Cheshire, combining music, live science experiments, expert talks and immersive artworks.    The festival is named after the Pale Blue Dot photograph taken from Voyager 1 and popularised by Carl Sagan.  In the photograph planet Earth is a tiny blue dot against the vastness of outer space.  Since 2016, acts performing at the bluedot festival have included New Order, Orbital, Underworld, Alt-J, Goldfrapp, Jean-Michel Jarre,    The Chemical Brothers,  Gary Numan, Hot Chip and The Flaming Lips.  The festival has included talks and demonstrations from Richard Dawkins, Brian Cox, Jim Al-Khalili,  Philippa Browning,  Timothy John O'Brien,  Sarah Bridle,  Lisa Harvey-Smith,  and Teresa Anderson.
