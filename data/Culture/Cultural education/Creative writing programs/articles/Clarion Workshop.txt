Clarion is a six-week workshop for aspiring science fiction and fantasy writers. Originally an outgrowth of Damon Knight and Kate Wilhelm's Milford Writers' Conference, held at their home in Milford, Pennsylvania, United States, it was founded in 1968 by Robin Scott Wilson  at Clarion State College in Pennsylvania. Knight and Wilhelm were among the first teachers at the workshop.  In 1972, the workshop moved to Michigan State University.  It moved again, in 2006, to the University of California, San Diego.
