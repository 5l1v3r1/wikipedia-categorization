The NYU Veterans Writing Workshop at New York University in Manhattan, New York is a free, non-partisan outreach program offered to the veteran community in and around New York City  and is considered by some as the pre-eminent writing workshop for veterans.  The workshop began in 2008,  founded by former United States Ambassador to Ireland Jean Kennedy Smith.
