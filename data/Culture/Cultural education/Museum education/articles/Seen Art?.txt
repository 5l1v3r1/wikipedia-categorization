Seen Art? is a children's picture book written by Jon Scieszka and illustrated by Lane Smith.  It was published in 1995 by Viking Press, and is aimed at a reading age of 4 to 8. It depicts a child's view of the art collection at the Museum of Modern Art in New York City via a storyline that follows a young boy's quest for his friend called Art. Asking people whether they have seen Art, and where Art is, leads him on a journey around the Museum. At the end of the book he finds his friend waiting for him outside the Museum.  The book has been published in Spanish translation as En Busca de Arte.
