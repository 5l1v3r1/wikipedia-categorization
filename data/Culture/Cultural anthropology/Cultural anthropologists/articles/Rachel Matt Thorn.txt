Rachel "Matt" Thorn  (born May 12, 1965) is a cultural anthropologist and an associate professor in the Department of Manga Production at Kyoto Seika University's Faculty of Manga in Japan.    She  is best known in North America for her work dealing with shōjo manga. She has appeared at multiple anime conventions, including Otakon 2004.   She chose to translate shōjo manga into English after reading Thomas no Shinzō by Moto Hagio in the mid-1980s.
