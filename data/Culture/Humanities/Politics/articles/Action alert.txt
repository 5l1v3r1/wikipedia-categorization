An action alert is a message that an organization sends to mobilize people, often members of the group and supporters of a specific point of view, calling on them to take action to influence public policy.   Typically, action alerts are in reference to a timely issue, where prompt action is needed in order to affect upcoming decisions.  Action alerts are considered a cost-effective and efficient grassroots organizing tool and are employed widely by many advocacy organizations.   A well-designed action alert can be a "powerful way to invite people to participate in the processes of a democracy."
