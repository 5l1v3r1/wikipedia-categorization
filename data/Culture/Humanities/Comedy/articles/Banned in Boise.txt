Banned in Boise, originally titled Made in Boise,  is an American online-streaming comedy pilot produced by Sony Interactive Entertainment.  The pilot was released on August 29, 2018 on the PlayStation Network, along with 4 other original TV pilots with intentions of a full series order from at least 1 of the 5. Banned in Boise was created by finalists Max Cannon and Brandon Sullivan of the Playstation Emerging Filmmakers Program.
