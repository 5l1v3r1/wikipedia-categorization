The MOFA (Ministry of Foreign Affairs) Huayu Enrichment Scholarship (HES), is a competitively awarded international scholarship for studying the Mandarin language in Taiwan. The award is not limited to countries with diplomatic ties with Taiwan.  Huayu Scholars may study at any university or college-affiliated Mandarin training center.  The scholarship may be awarded for a maximum of one year; although applications for shorter study periods of three, six and nine months are accepted.
