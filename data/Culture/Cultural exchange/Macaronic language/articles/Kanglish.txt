Kanglish (Kannada: ಕಂಗ್ಲಿಷ್) is a macaronic language of Kannada and English; the name is a portmanteau of the names of the two languages. En sir samachara? : whats the matter sir? Hello, tiffin aytha? : Hello, have you had your tiffin? Swalpa move madi : Just move a bit. Swalpa break haki : Please break Yav movie hakiddare? : Which movie is playing?
