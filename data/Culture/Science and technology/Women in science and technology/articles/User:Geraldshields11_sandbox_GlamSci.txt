 GlamSci is a British  non-profit  which focuses on helping STEM education and training  to be more relatable to the general public.   GlamSci provides educational discussions to promote STEM fields for women and minorities.  GlamSci was founded, in 2013, after the founder was "repeatedly told "pure science isn’t for girls"".  Members have attended "educational events for educators and educational leaders, such as WorldTeachIn, and Athena Swan and Bett events".  As of  2018[update], it is lead by Amy King  , who was London’s Adult Learner of the Year in 2014.  Official website www.glamsci.org
