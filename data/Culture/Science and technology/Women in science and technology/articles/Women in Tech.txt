Women In Tech: Take Your Career to the Next Level with Practical Advice and Inspiring Stories  is a 2016 anthology written by Tarah Wheeler, an entrepreneur,  keynote speaker  and scientist,  published by Sasquatch Books. The book began as a Kickstarter project, with 772 backers and $32,226 in funding.  It includes advice for women in developing careers skills such as salary negotiations, networking, and finding work-life balance,  as well as personal stories from female tech professionals.  The book contains a foreword by Esther Dyson. In addition to Wheeler's writing, the anthology includes chapters by Kamilah Taylor, Miah Johnson, Kristin Toth Smith,  Keren Elazari,  Brianna Wu,  Katie Cunningham, and Angie Chang
