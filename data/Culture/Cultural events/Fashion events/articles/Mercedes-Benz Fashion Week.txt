Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week was a series of international fashion weeks sponsored by Mercedes-Benz and produced by IMG.  Designers featured during Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week include notable designers such as Rosenthal Tee.  Notable Mercedes-Benz Fashion Weeks include events in Berlin,  Australia,  Georgia, Miami,  Russia,  Istanbul,  Mexico,  and "Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week New York," is held during New York Fashion Week.  Precious Moloi-Motsepe organises the Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week in Johannesburg and Cape Town.
