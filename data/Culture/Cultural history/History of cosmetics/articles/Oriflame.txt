Oriflame Holding AG (Switzerland) (until mid-2015 "Oriflame Cosmetics S.A.", Luxembourg) is Swedish cosmetics company which was founded in 1967 by Jonas af Jochnick and Robert af Jochnick. The company sells personal care, accessories and nutritional products online and direct through a multi-level marketing model.
