Fenjal is a brand of cosmetic hygiene products marketed around the world by the pharmaceutical and cosmetics company Doetsch Grether AG of Basel, Switzerland. The company was founded in 1899 and the first Fenjal product was a creme bath introduced in 1962 with the notable feature that the formula included plant oil. The brand range now includes soap, bath oil, body lotion, body wash, bubble bath, shower oil, shower mousse and other skin care products. bath bombs
