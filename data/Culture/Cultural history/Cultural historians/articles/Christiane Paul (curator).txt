Christiane Paul is an Associate Professor in the School of Media Studies at The New School,  and Adjunct Curator of New Media Arts at the Whitney Museum of American Art in New York City.  Paul received both her MA and PhD from the University of Düsseldorf.  She has written extensively on new media arts and lectured internationally on art and technology.
