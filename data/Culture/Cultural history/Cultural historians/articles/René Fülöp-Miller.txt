René Fülöp-Miller, born Philip Müller (17 February 1891 – 17 May 1963) was an Austrian cultural historian and writer.   He was born to an Alsatian immigrant and a Serbian mother in Caransebeş (in what was then the Austro-Hungarian Empire) and died in Hanover, New Hampshire.  During his career as a journalist and editor he resided in various places such as Vienna, Paris, Lausanne, Budapest, Moscow, London, Los Angeles and New York.
