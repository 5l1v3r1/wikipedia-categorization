Khasnor Johan is a Malaysian author and historian. Johan married Australian political scientist and author Harold Crouch in 1973.  Johan studied at the University of Malaya (in Kuala Lumpur).  Her master's thesis, The Malay College, Kuala Kangsar, 1905-1941: British Policy of Education for Employment in the Federated Malay States. was published in 1969.  Like her husband,  Johan attained her PhD at Monash University in Melbourne. Her thesis, published in 1974, was The Administrative Elite in the Federated Malay States: An Aspect of Malaysian Social History.   From 1974 to 1992, Johan taught history at National University of Malaysia Crouch also taught at the university, his tenure from 1976 to 1990. In 1991 he began teaching at Australian National University in Canberra.   Johan retired from teaching in 1992 and moved to Canberra.  She was living in Canberra in 2002.  Johan published works between 1969 and 2005 in English.
