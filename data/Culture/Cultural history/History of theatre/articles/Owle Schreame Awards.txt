  The Owle Schreame Awards were 2014 theatrical awards honouring innovation in classical theatre. They were awarded by Brice Stratford and his Owle Schreame theatre company in 2014 to commemorate the 450th anniversary of Shakespeare's birth.  The award itself was an engraved glass skull, in reference to the famous graveyard scene in Shakespeare's Hamlet. Thirteen were awarded, and the award speeches were published.
