The Devonshire Collection of Period Costume  is a collection of historic costumes housed in the Totnes Fashion and Textiles Museum in the town of Totnes, south Devon, in southwest England.  The collection includes clothing for men, women, and children, dating from the 18th century to the end of the 20th century.  An annually updated exhibition is displayed in Bogan House, a Tudor merchant's house at 43 High Street, Totnes.
