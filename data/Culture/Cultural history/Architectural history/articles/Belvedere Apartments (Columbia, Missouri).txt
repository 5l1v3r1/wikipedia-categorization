Belvedere Apartments is a well-preserved Spanish Colonial Revival-style apartment building located at 206 Hitt Street in downtown Columbia, Missouri. Built in 1927, the building was designed by notable architect, Nelle Peters.  The building was added to Columbia's Notable Buildings List in 2008  and is a possible candidate for the National Register of Historic Places.
