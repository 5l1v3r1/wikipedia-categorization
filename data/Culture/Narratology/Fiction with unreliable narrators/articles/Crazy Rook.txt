Crazy Rook or Crazy Castle (Persian title: Rokhe Divaneh- Persian: رخ دیوانه‎) is a 2015 Iranian film directed by Abolhassan Davoudi and starring Tannaz Tabatabaei, Bizhan Emkanian, Saber Abar, Nazanin Bayati and Gohar Kheirandish.  The film won the Best Director and the Best Film awards in the 33rd Fajr International Film Festival.  Crazy Rook was screened in the state of Florida America from 6 until 14 April. The story of a young couple who met in an internet dating and looking for a fun and gambling complex and daunting fell on the path, the path for each one brings a new understanding of life and society .
