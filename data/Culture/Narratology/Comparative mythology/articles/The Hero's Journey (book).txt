The Hero's Journey: Joseph Campbell on His Life and Work is a biography of the mythologist Joseph Campbell 1904-1987. In the form of a series of conversations, the book was drawn from the film, The Hero's Journey: A Biographical Portrait This book was originally published by HarperCollins in 1990. This edition was the seventh title in the Joseph Campbell Foundation's Collected Works of Joseph Campbell series. Publishers Weekly said that readers of Campbell's previous works would enjoy The Hero's Journey  while Library Journal said the book built upon previous known information about Campbell.
