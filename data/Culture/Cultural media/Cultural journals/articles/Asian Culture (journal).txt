Asian Culture (Yazhou Wenhua, 亞洲文化) is an international refereed bilingual academic journal of the Singapore Society of Asian Studies. It was inaugurated in 1983 as a bi-annual periodical and became an annual publication in 1990. Asian Culture publishes quality research papers in all aspects of Asian literature, history and philosophy. It is a well-regarded journal in the field of Southeast Asian studies. Asian Culture has published a total of 39 issues by 2015. The current editors are Leo Suryadinata and Neo Peng Fu.
