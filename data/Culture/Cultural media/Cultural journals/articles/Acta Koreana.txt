Acta Koreana is a biannual peer-reviewed academic journal covering Korean studies published in English by Academia Koreana (Keimyung University). It was established in 1998 as an annual publication; since 2002 it has been published biannually. The journal is abstracted and indexed in the Arts & Humanities Citation Index, Current Contents/Arts & Humanities,  CSA Sociological Abstracts, Bibliography of Asian Studies, Korea Citation Index, SocINDEX, and Scopus.
