The Korea Journal is a peer-reviewed, English-language academic journal focusing on Korean Studies. It was founded in 1961 and is currently published quarterly by [The Academy of Korean Studies].<ref> <ref> It is indexed in Thomson Scientific's Arts and Humanities Citation Index (A&HCI). The publisher is Byung-wook Ahn(General of the Academy of Korean Studies) and the Editor-in-Chief is Kyeong-il Kim(Professor, the Academy of Korean Studies, the Graduate School of Korean Studies).
