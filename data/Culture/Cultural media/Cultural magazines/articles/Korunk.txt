Korunk (meaning Our Age in English) is a Hungarian language monthly cultural-literary-scientific magazine published in Cluj-Napoca, Romania.   Korunk was founded by Laszlo Dienes in Cluj-Napoca in 1926.  The magazine ceased publication in 1940,  and began publication in 1957.  In 1929 Gabor Gaal became its editor-in-chief.  Ernö Gáll is the long-term editor-in-chief of the magazine who served in the post between 1957 and 1984.  The magazine has a Marxist political stance  and progressive literary approach.  It is published on a monthly basis.
