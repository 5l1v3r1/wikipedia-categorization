Arena is a Swedish-language bi-monthly left-leaning  magazine.  It publishes reports, interviews and essays on politics and culture. Arena was founded by Håkan A. Bengtsson and Per Wirtén. It was first published in 1993 under the name Politikens, kulturens & idéernas arena. In 2003 the name was shortened to Arena.  It is one of the four Swedish magazine members of the European magazine-network Eurozine.  Arena publishes six issues per year and has a circulation of approximately 3,600.
