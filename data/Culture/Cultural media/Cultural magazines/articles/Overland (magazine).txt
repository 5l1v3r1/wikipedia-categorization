Overland is an Australian literary and cultural magazine. It was established in 1954,  under the auspices of the Realist Writers Group in Melbourne, Australia, with Stephen Murray-Smith as the first editor-in-chief.  It has been edited by: • Barrett Reid, 1988-1993   • John McLaren, Spring 1993–Autumn 1997   • Ian Syson, Winter 1997–Summer  2002   • Nathan Hollier and Katherine Wilson, Autumn 2002–Spring 2004   • Nathan Hollier, 2005–2006 • Jeff Sparrow, 2007–2014 The current editor is Jacinda Woodhead. The magazine has a left-wing orientation.
