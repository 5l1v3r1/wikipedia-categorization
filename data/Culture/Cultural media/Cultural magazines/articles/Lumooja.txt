Lumooja is a Finnish cultural and literary magazine based in Turku, Finland.  It features poetry and other literary work as well as articles on arts and book reviews.   The magazine was started in 2000.   The magazine is published by a Turku-based organization, Kapustarinta (The Golden Plover Association).
