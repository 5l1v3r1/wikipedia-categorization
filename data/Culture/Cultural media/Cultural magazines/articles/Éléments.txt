 Éléments is a French bi-monthly magazine founded in 1973, subtitled Pour la civilisation européenne, For the European civilization. With a social and European inspiration, the magazine defends a critical vision of liberal modernity and promotes topics such as rooting, ecology, respect for cultural diversity and the defense of European identity. Dealing with cultural, political, social and scientific subjects, Éléments has long been the general public showcase of the Groupement de recherche et d'études pour la civilisation européenne (GRECE), founded in 1968. The editor of the magazine is from the beginning Robert de Herte, a pseudonym for Alain de Benoist.
