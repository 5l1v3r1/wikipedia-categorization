Celtic Family Magazine was a Los Angeles, California-based print and electronic publication, serving Celtic communities and their descendants around the world.  Celtic Family Magazine published special features and articles on art, history, culture, entertainment, and lifestyle.  Celtic Family Magazine was produced by A Raven Above Press and was distributed throughout North America and select areas of the United Kingdom.   It was announced through social media that after four years of publication Celtic Family Magazine would be going on hiatus starting in 2017.
