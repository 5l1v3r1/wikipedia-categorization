Gonzo (circus) is a Dutch-language bi-monthly culture  and music magazine, first published in 1991 in Belgium.  It is based in Amsterdam, Netherlands.  The magazine primarily focuses on contemporary musical genres such as jazz, improvised music, modern classical music, rock, noise, electronic music and world music.  Since 1995 each publication is accompanied by a compilation CD under the name Mind the Gap, featuring new artists. Gonzo (circus) is published by the publisher Virtumedia.
