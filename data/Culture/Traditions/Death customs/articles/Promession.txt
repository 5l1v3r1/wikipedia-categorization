Promession is an idea of how to dispose human remains by way of freeze drying. The concept of promession was developed by Swedish biologist Susanne Wiigh-Mäsak, who derived the name from the Italian word for "promise" (promessa).   She founded Promessa Organic AB in 1997 to commercially pursue her idea.  The company was liquidated 2015 without being able to produce a functioning facility.
