Talempong orchestra is an Indonesian orchestra that uses a diatonic tone and played with Minang instruments.  This orchestra is formed by the Conservatory Minang in Padang Panjang in 1960.   Most of the instruments used in this orchestra is metallophone and idiophone (similar with kolenang in West Java gamelan).   Talempong orchestra plays Minang song like Tak Tontong and other traditional songs.
