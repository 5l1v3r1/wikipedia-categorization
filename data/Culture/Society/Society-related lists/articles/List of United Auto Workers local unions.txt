The following is a list of local unions belonging to the United Automobile, Aerospace and Agricultural Implement Workers of America, better known as the United Auto Workers (UAW). The UAW is a labor union which represents workers in the United States, Canada, and Puerto Rico.  Founded in order to represent workers in the automobile industry, UAW members in the 21st century work in industries as diverse as health care, casino gaming, and higher education.  Headquartered in Detroit, Michigan, the union has approximately 800 local unions, which negotiate 3,100 contracts with approximately 2,000 employers.
