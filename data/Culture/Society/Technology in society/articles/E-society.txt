E-society is a society that consists of one or more e-Communities involved in the areas from e-Government, e-Democracy, and  e-Business to e-Learning and e-Health,     that use information and communication technologies (ICT) in order to achieve a common interests and goals. The first areas of e-society that emerged were e-Learning and e-Business. The development of e-Society is relying and depending on the development of virtual reality (VR) technologies that insure interaction between participants of an e-Society in a more acceptable and tangible way.
