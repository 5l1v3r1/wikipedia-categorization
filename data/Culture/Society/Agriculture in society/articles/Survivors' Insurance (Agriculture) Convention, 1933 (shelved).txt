Survivors' Insurance (Agriculture) Convention, 1933 (shelved) is  an International Labour Organization Convention. It was established in 1933: Having decided upon the adoption of certain proposals with regard to compulsory widows' and orphans' insurance,...The concepts included in the convention were revised and included in ILO Convention C128, Invalidity, Old-Age and Survivors' Benefits Convention, 1967. Prior to it being shelved, the convention was ratified by ten states.
