Media involvement and tropical cyclones have been carried out in various ways over the past centuries. The passages of hurricanes, typhoons, cyclones, and tropical storms have been reported by newspaper, word of mouth and by telegraph. Since the early to mid-20th century and since 2000, advances in technology have included the radio, television, newsreel footage shown in theaters and mass media, and recently the internet which includes blogs and online news sites that report the coming, passing and effects of tropical cyclones.
