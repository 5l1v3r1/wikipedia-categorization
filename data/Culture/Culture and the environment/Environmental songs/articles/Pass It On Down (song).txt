4:53 (album version)  "Pass It On Down" is a song written by Randy Owen, Teddy Gentry, Ronnie Rogers and Will Robinson, and recorded by American country music group Alabama. It was released in March 1990 as the first single and title track from the album of the same name.  It peaked at number 3 in the United States, and number 2 in Canada.  The music video was directed by Jack Cole.
