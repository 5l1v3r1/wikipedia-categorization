 "When You Made the Mountain" is an "environmentally-themed" electronica/dance single recorded by Opus III with vocals by Kirsty Hawkshaw released in 1994 as the lead single from their second album, Guru Mother. The track got as far as number 75 in the UK Pop singles chart, but it would go all the way to number 1 in the Billboard Hot Dance Club Play Chart, giving the act their second and final chart topper in the United States.
