The Force Traveller, also called Tempo Traveller, is a light commercial vehicle (LCV) made and distributed by Force Motors. This vehicle range was based on the popular Mercedes-Benz T1 range. There are different configurations for different uses (commercial minibus, school bus, ambulance, Quick Response Vehicle, etc.). Other options include high roof / low roof versions, the short chassis / long chassis versions and the Excel series of commercial transport vehicles.
