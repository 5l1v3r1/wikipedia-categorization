Regrading is the process of grading for raising and/or lowering the levels of land. Such a project can also be referred to as a regrade.  Regrading may be done on a small scale (as in preparation of a house site)  or on quite a large scale (as in major reconfiguration of the terrain of a city, such as the Denny Regrade in Seattle).   Regrading is typically performed to make land more level (flatter), in which case it is sometimes called levelling. ) Levelling can have the consequence of making other nearby slopes steeper, and potentially unstable or prone to erosion.
