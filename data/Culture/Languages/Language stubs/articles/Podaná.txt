Podaná (Greek: ποδανά) is a Greek argot based on rearranging syllables, similar to Verlan and Vesre. Podaná itself is a reversal of anápoda (ανάποδα), meaning "upside-down."  Examples include: tsosbá (inverted bátsos, slang for "cop"), zakipré (inverted prezáki, slang for "junkie"), dafoú (inverted foúnda, "hashish"), fosbá (inverted báfos, "joint").
