Siane (Siani) is a Papuan language spoken in the eastern highlands of Papua New Guinea. Named dialects are Hakoa, Kolepa, Yamofowe, Komongu, Komoigaleka, Kemanimowe, Ona, Keto, Laiya, Fowe, Olumba, Lambau, Alango, Yandime, Wando. Komongu and Lambau are the literary standards. Like many Papuan languages Siane has a regester tone system.
