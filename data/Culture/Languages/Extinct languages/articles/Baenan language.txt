Baenan (Baenã, Baenán, Baena) is a poorly attested language of Brazil.  The last remaining speaker lived in Bahia, Brazil in 1940.  The language of this speaker was associated with the Baenan language as the last members of the Baenan tribe lived in Paragaçú, Bahia, near where the language was attested.
