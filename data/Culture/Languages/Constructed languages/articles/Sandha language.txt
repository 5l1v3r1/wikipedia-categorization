Sandha bhasha, the "language of twilight" (Hindi: संधा भाषा), is a literary style used to express inter-instrumental cognition[clarification needed] by Siddha people. In Sandha, symbols[clarification needed] are used to present inter-instrumental expressions[clarification needed].   Most of the literature written in Sandha style by the Siddha people are in Apabhraṃśa.[dubious  – discuss][The sources suggest it's Bengali or related to Bengali]
