Team San Jose (also known as San Jose CVB) is a non-profit management corporation launched in 2004 to promote tourism in San Jose, California. It operates the San Jose Convention Center and cultural venues such as South Hall, Parkside Hall, San Jose Civic Auditorium, California Theatre, San Jose Center for the Performing Arts, and Montgomery Theater, and serves as the convention and visitors bureau for San Jose. Team San Jose is an economic driver in Silicon Valley, evolving into a $20 million company with more than 300 employees.
