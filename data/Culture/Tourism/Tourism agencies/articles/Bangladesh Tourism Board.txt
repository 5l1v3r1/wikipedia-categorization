Bangladesh Tourism Board is the National tourism organisation of Bangladesh, responsible for promotion of tourism and providing necessary training and is located in  Dhaka, Bangladesh.  Akhteruzzaman Khan Kabir is the present CEO of the board.   The board is a statutory body established in 2010 Located at Kazi Nazrul Islam Avenue , Dhaka.  It is responsible for the promotion of tourism in Bangladesh.  It is under the Ministry of Civil Aviation and Tourism of Bangladesh.
