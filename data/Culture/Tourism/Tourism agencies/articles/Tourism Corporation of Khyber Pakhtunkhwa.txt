Tourism Corporation Khyber Pakhtunkhwa set up as a limited company in 1991 to develop Khyber-Pakhtunkhwa's tourism potential. It offers a variety of services in key tourist sites in Khyber-Pakhtunkhwa, as well as visits to attend the Shandur Festival as well as to famous Buddhist monasteries like Takht Bhai and Peshawar's old city historical sites like Sethi Mohallah.
