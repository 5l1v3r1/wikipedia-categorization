Where is a series of magazines for tourists, distributed at hotels, convention centres, regional malls and other tourist areas. The original edition was founded in 1938. Throughout most of the world, the magazine's editions are published by Morris Visitor Publications; it is published in Milan (Italy) by Where Italia Srl, in Rome by Tourist Media Srl, in Canada by St. Joseph Media and in Asia by Asia City Media Group.
