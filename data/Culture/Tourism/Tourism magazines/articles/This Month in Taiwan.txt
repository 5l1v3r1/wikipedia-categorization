This Month in Taiwan, founded by E. Kirk Henderson and first published in 1974, is the most widely distributed tourism magazine in Taiwan, with an annual estimated circulation of 200,000 copies. The publishers authorizes advertisers or their agents to physically count and verify the quantity of magazines published at its printing plant at any time. This Month in Taiwan is distributed free of charge  and available at leading hotels in Taiwan as well as in airports, clubs, government offices and other tourism-related locations.
