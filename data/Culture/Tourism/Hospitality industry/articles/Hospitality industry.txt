The hospitality industry is a broad category of fields within the service industry that includes lodging, event planning, theme parks, transportation, cruise line,travelling  and additional fields within the tourism industry.  The hospitality industry is a multibillion-dollar industry that depends on the availability of leisure time and disposable income. A hospitality unit such as a restaurant, hotel, or an amusement park consists of multiple groups such as facility maintenance and direct operations (servers, housekeepers, porters, kitchen workers, bartenders, management, marketing, and human resources etc.).
