The Ministry of Natural Resources and Tourism is the government ministry of Tanzania that is responsible for the management of natural resources and cultural resources and for the development of the tourism industry.  It has a wide range of investments in various tourist resources and tourism industry projects.   Ministry offices are located in Dodoma. The Ministry's motto is "Land of Kilimanjaro & Zanzibar".
