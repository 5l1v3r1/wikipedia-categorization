The Lao National Tourism Administration (LNTA) is the government agency responsible for governing, promoting and developing the tourism industry of Laos. The LNTA is a ministry-level agency, reporting directly to the prime minister's office.     The Chairman of the LNTA is H.E. Mr.Somphong Mongkhonvilay.  The agency is located in Vientiane.
