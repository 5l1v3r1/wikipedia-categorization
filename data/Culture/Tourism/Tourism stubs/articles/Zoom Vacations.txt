Zoom Vacations is a gay travel provider based in the Chicago, Illinois, United States  specializing in high-end group travel for gay men and lesbians. Zoom was founded in 2003 by Bryan Herb,   Joel Cabrera, and Bill Gudenau as the gay equivalent to popular mainstream high-end tour companies.  Zoom Vacations received Out Traveler's Reader's Choice Award   for Best Small Gay Tour Provider in 2007 and 2008. It also won PlanetOut's 2008 Best Small Tour Operator Award.  In 2007, Zoom Vacations won the International Gay and Lesbian Travel Association (IGLTA) Award for Best Website Promoting Gay Travel.
