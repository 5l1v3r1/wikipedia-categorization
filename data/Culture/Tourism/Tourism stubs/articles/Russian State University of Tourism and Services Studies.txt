Russian State University of Tourism and Services Studies (RSUTS) is a public university in Russia and CIS countries which provides higher education in tourism and services studies. The university is based in Pushkino, Moscow Oblast, near Moscow, and has campuses in Russian cities of Smolensk, Kaliningrad, Samara, Volgograd, Sochi, Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky, Pyatigorsk, Yakutsk, Perm, Yekaterinburg, Makhachkala, Kazan, and Yerevan, Armenia.
