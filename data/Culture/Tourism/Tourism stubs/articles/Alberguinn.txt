Alberguinn is a Youth Hostel, located in Barcelona, Spain. It was opened in March 2005 and has since catered to low-budget travelers visiting Barcelona. Alberguinn features 50 beds as shared dorm rooms. It is located at the nice typical Sants district, 5 min walk from the main train  Station Barcelona Sants in Barcelona and 10 min walk from Barcelona's famous FC Barcelona soccer team stadium, Camp Nou
