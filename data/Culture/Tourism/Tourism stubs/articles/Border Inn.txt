The Border Inn is a motel on the Utah/Nevada border in Baker, Nevada on U.S. 6/U.S. 50.  It is located near Great Basin National Park. The motel offers round-the-clock service and the regular hours are 12:00 am - 11:59 pm from Monday to Sunday. This motel is unique because while the motel rooms are in Utah, and on Mountain Time, the office, restaurant, and casino are in Nevada, and Pacific Time.  The motel also serves as the housing facilities for the seasonal park rangers of the nearby Great Basin National Park.
