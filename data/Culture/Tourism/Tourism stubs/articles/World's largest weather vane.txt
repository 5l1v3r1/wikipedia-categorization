According to the Guinness World Records, the world's largest weather vane is located in Jerez, Spain.  According to the explore north website   a challenger for the title of world's largest weather vane is located in Whitehorse, Yukon. The weather vane is a retired Douglas DC-3 atop a swiveling support. Located beside the Whitehorse airport the weather vane is used mainly by pilots to determine wind direction.
