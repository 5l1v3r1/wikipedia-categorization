JacTravel is a supplier of hotel accommodation for the global travel industry and provider of specialist inbound travel services to the UK and Ireland. With headquarters in London, regional offices throughout the UK, Europe, Asia, the Middle-East and North America.  In June 2014, JacTravel was acquired by private equity firm Vitruvian Partners   In March 2015, JacTravel acquired TotalStay Group  In August 2017, JacTravel was acquired by Australian-based travel firm WebJet
